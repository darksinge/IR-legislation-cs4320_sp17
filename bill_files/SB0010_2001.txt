 SUNSET REAUTHORIZATIONS



            

    
2001 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    


 Sponsor:  Millie M. Peterson

            

    

 This act reauthorizes certain state entities and programs that would otherwise sunset before
            

    
 the 2002 Annual General Session of the Utah Legislature.
