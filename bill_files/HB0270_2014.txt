  Daniel W. Thatcher

 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Peace Officer Standards and Training Act regarding certification
            
10

    
of peace officers.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    clarifies that a peace officer certification becomes inactive if a peace officer has not
            
14

    
been actively engaged in performing the duties as a certified and sworn peace
            
15

    
officer for 18 consecutive months;
            
16

    
    .    provides that a peace officer certification be designated as lapsed if a peace officer
            
17

    
has not been actively engaged in performing the duties as a certified and sworn
            
18

    
peace officer for four continuous years; and
            
19

    
    .    makes a technical correction.
            
20

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
21

    
    None
            
22

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
23

    
    None
            
24

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
25

    
AMENDS:
            
26

    
     
53-6-208
, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2011, Chapter 58
            
27

    
     
53-6-211.5
, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2010, Chapter 313

            
28


    

            
29

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
30

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
53-6-208

 is amended to read:
            
31

    
     53-6-208.  Inactive certificates -- Lapse of certificate -- Reinstatement.
            
32

    
    (1) (a)  The certificate of a peace officer who has not been actively engaged in
            
33

    
performing the duties [of a] as a certified and sworn peace officer for 18 consecutive months or
            
34

    
more, but less than four consecutive years, is designated "inactive."
            
35

    
    (b)  A peace officer whose certificate is inactive shall pass the certification examination
            
36

    
and a physical fitness test before the certificate may be reissued or reinstated.
            
37

    
    (2) (a)  The certificate of a peace officer who has not been actively engaged in
            
38

    
performing the duties [of a] as a certified and sworn peace officer for four continuous years or
            
39

    
more is designated as "lapsed."
            
40

    
    (b)  A peace officer whose certificate is lapsed shall pass the basic training course at a
            
41

    
certified academy, the certification examination, and a physical fitness test before the
            
42

    
certificate may be reissued or reinstated.
            
43

    
    Section 2.  
Section  
53-6-211.5

 is amended to read:
            
44

    
     53-6-211.5.  Voluntary relinquishment of peace officer certification.
            
45

    
    (1)  A peace officer may voluntarily relinquish the peace officer's certification to the
            
46

    
division at any time when a disciplinary issue regarding the peace officer has been referred to
            
47

    
the division.
            
48

    
    (2) (a)  A peace officer who voluntarily relinquishes certification under this section may
            
49

    
not subsequently be certified as a peace officer in this state.
            
50

    
    (b)  This section does not apply to a peace officer whose certification has become
            
51

    
inactive or has lapsed as provided in Section 

53-6-208


.
