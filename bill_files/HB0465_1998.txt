H.B. 465





1     COUNTY SERVICE AREA CHANGES




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor: Brian R. Allen


5    AN ACT RELATING TO SPECIAL DISTRICTS; EXPANDING THE SERVICES THAT A

6    COUNTY SERVICE AREA IS AUTHORIZED TO PROVIDE; PROVIDING A SPECIAL

7    PROCEDURE FOR CREATING A COUNTY SERVICE AREA; AND MAKING

8    TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.

9    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

10    AMENDS:

11         17A-2-403, as renumbered and amended by Chapter 186, Laws of Utah 1990

12         17A-2-404, as last amended by Chapter 227, Laws of Utah 1993

13    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

14        Section 1.  Section  17A-2-403 is amended to read:

15         17A-2-403.  Services which may be supplied by a county service area.

16        Whenever an unincorporated area in a county requires one or more of the following

17    extended services which are not provided on a countywide basis: extended police protection; fire

18    protection; culinary or irrigation water retail service; water conservation; local park, recreation,

19    or parkway facilities and services; cemeteries; public libraries; sewers, sewage and storm water

20    treatment and disposal; flood control; garbage and refuse collection; street lighting; airports;

21    planning and zoning; local streets and roads; curb, gutter, and sidewalk construction and

22    maintenance; mosquito abatement; health department services; hospital service; or, as provided

23    in Subsection 17A-2-404(2), erecting or burying utility lines, such services may be supplied by

24    a county service area.  If the provision of said services shall require the issuance of bonds or the

25    creation of long-term obligations, said services may be supplied by means available at law as

26    herein provided.

27        Section 2.  Section  17A-2-404 is amended to read:


1         17A-2-404.  Establishment of service area -- Special procedure for creation of county

2     service area to erect or bury utility lines.

3        (1)  A county service area shall be established if:

4        [(1)  The] (a)  the county legislative body determines that such service should be provided

5    on an extended basis within an unincorporated area in the county; or

6        [(2)  Such] (b)  such services are requested in a petition for the initiation of proceedings

7    for the formation of a county service area or for the furnishing of additional types of service within

8    an unincorporated area in the county.

9        (2) (a)  A county legislative body may, as provided in this Subsection (2), create a county

10    service area to provide the service of erecting or burying utility lines.

11        (b)  Before a county legislative body may create a county service area under this

12    Subsection (2), the creation of the county service area shall be approved by voters of the area of

13    the proposed county service area at an election held for that purpose.

14        (c)  If a majority of those voting at an election under Subsection (2)(b) vote in favor of

15    creating a county service area, the county legislative body may adopt an ordinance under

16    Subsection 17A-2-410(1) creating the county service area.

17        (d)  Subsection (1), Subsections 17A-2-405(2) and (3) and 17A-2-410(3), (4), and (5) , and

18    Sections 17A-2-406, 17A-2-407, 17A-2-408, and 17A-2-409 do not apply to the creation of a

19    county service area under this Subsection (2).
