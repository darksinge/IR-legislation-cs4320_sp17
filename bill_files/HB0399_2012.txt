 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends the Utah Medical Practice Act and the Utah Osteopathic Medical
            
11

    
Practice Act relating to licensing requirements.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    prohibits the Division of Occupational Licensing from requiring board or specialty
            
15

    
certification as a requirement for licensure; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
