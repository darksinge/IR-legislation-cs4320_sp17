H.J.R. 9





            
1

    
 JOINT RESOLUTION ON UTAH EPILEPSY PUBLIC

            
2

    
 EDUCATION, OUTREACH, AND AWARENESS



            
3

    
2014 GENERAL SESSION

            
4

    
STATE OF UTAH

            
5

    


 Chief Sponsor:  Marie H. Poulson



            
6

    



Senate Sponsor: 






  Patricia W. Jones





            
7

    

            
8

    
 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This joint resolution of the Legislature urges the Utah Department of Health and the
            
11

    
Utah Department of Human Services to collaborate with other state agencies to deliver
            
12

    
public epilepsy education, outreach, and awareness campaign materials and messages to
            
13

    
individuals and organizations having regular interaction with individuals subject to
            
14

    
epilepsy and seizure incidents.
            
15

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
16

    
    This resolution:
            
17

    
    .    urges that, in implementing the existing federal public education, outreach, and
            
18

    
awareness campaign under 42 U.S.C. Chapter 6A, the Public Health Service Act,
            
19

    
Sec. 330E-1, the Utah Department of Health and the Utah Department of Human
            
20

    
Services collaborate with the Utah Department of Veterans' and Military Affairs, the
            
21

    
Utah State Office of Education, the Utah Labor Commission, the attorney general's
            
22

    
office, and others to deliver the campaign's educational materials and messages to
            
23

    
individuals and organizations, including:
            
24

    
        .    teachers, administrators, and other education personnel;
            
25

    
        .    state, county, and local law enforcement personnel, and first responders;
            
26

    
        .    employers and labor organizations; and
            
27

    
        .    other individuals and organizations the Department of Health and the

            
28


    
Department of Human Services consider to have regular interaction with individuals who are
            
29

    
subject to epilepsy and seizure incidents.
            
30

    
 Special Clauses:
            
31

    
    None
            
32

    

            
33

    
Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
34

    
    WHEREAS, epilepsy is the third most common neurological disorder, after stroke and
            
35

    
Alzheimer's and related dementias;
            
36

    
    WHEREAS, epilepsy affects an estimated 100,000 people in Utah;
            
37

    
    WHEREAS, one in every 10 people in Utah will experience a seizure during the
            
38

    
person's lifetime;
            
39

    
    WHEREAS, traumatic brain injury (TBI) is one of the most common injuries of war;
            
40

    
    WHEREAS, experts believe that up to 50% of military personnel affected by TBI will
            
41

    
develop epilepsy; and
            
42

    
    WHEREAS, injury, death, and other negative health, economic, or life outcomes can be
            
43

    
avoided through seizure training and awareness in school, the workplace, and the community:
            
44

    
    NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah
            
45

    
urges that, in implementing the existing federal public education, outreach, and awareness
            
46

    
campaign under 42 U.S.C. Chapter 6A, the Public Health Service Act, Sec. 330E-1, the Utah
            
47

    
Department of Health and the Utah Department of Human Services should, to the greatest
            
48

    
extent practical, collaborate with the Utah Department of Veterans' and Military Affairs, the
            
49

    
Utah State Office of Education, the Utah Labor Commission, the attorney general's office, and
            
50

    
others to deliver the campaign's educational materials and messages to individuals and
            
51

    
organizations, including:
            
52

    
    * teachers, administrators, and other school or education personnel;
            
53

    
    * state, county, and local law enforcement personnel, and first responders;
            
54

    
    * employers and labor organizations; and
            
55

    
    * other individuals and organizations the Department of Health and the Department of
            
56

    
Human Services, in consultation with the individuals and representatives described in Section
            
57

    
330E-1(c)(2) of the Public Health Service Act, consider to have regular interaction with
            
58

    
individuals who are subject to epilepsy and seizure incidents.

            
59


    
    BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the United
            
60

    
States Secretary of Health and Human Services, the Utah Department of Health, the Utah
            
61

    
Department of Human Services, and the members of Utah's congressional delegation.
