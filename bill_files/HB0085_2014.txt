 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends provisions related to the electronic filing of a misdemeanor or
            
11

    
infraction citation.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    exempts a county of the fourth, fifth, or sixth class from electronic filing
            
15

    
requirements for a misdemeanor or infraction citation.
            
16

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
17

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None

 Utah Code Sections Affected:

, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2013, Chapter 65
            
23

    

            
24

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
25

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
77-7-20

 is amended to read:
            
26

    
     77-7-20.  Service of citation on defendant -- Filing in court -- Electronic filing --
            
27

    
 Contents of citations.

            
28


    
    (1)  A peace officer or public official who issues a citation pursuant to Section 

77-7-18



            
29

    
shall give the citation to the person cited and shall within five days electronically file the data
            
30

    
from Subsections (2)(a) through (2)(g) with the court specified in the citation. The data
            
31

    
transmission shall use the court's electronic filing interface.  A nonconforming filing is not
            
32

    
effective.
            
33

    
    (2)  The citation issued under authority of this chapter shall contain the following data:
            
34

    
    (a)  the name of the court before which the person is to appear;
            
35

    
    (b)  the name of the person cited;
            
36

    
    (c)  a brief description of the offense charged;
            
37

    
    (d)  the date, time, and place at which the offense is alleged to have occurred;
            
38

    
    (e)  the date on which the citation was issued;
            
39

    
    (f)  the name of the peace officer or public official who issued the citation, and the
            
40

    
name of the arresting person if an arrest was made by a private party and the citation was
            
41

    
issued in lieu of taking the arrested person before a magistrate;
            
42

    
    (g)  the time and date on or before and after which the person is to appear or a statement
            
43

    
that the court will notify the person of the time to appear;
            
44

    
    (h)  the address of the court in which the person is to appear; and
            
45

    
    (i)  a notice containing substantially the following language:
            
46

    
READ CAREFULLY

            
47

    
    This citation is not an information and will not be used as an information without your
            
48

    
consent.  If an information is filed you will be provided a copy by the court.  You MUST
            
49

    
appear in court on or before the time set in this citation or as directed by the court.  IF YOU
            
50

    
FAIL TO APPEAR, THE COURT MAY ISSUE A WARRANT FOR YOUR ARREST.
            
51

    
    (3)  By electronically filing the data with the court, the peace officer or public official
            
52

    
certifies to the court that:
            
53

    
    (a)  the citation or information, including the summons and complaint, was served upon
            
54

    
the defendant in accordance with the law;
            
55

    
    (b)  the defendant committed the offense set forth in the served documents; and
            
56

    
    (c)  the court to which the defendant was directed to appear is the proper court pursuant
            
57

    
to Section 

77-7-21


.
            
58

    
    (4) The provisions of this section requiring electronic filing do not apply to counties of

            
59


    
the fourth, fifth, or sixth class.
