Section  .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Constitutional debt limitation.                                     The commission may not issue bonds under this chapter in an amount that violates the                           limitation described in Utah Constitution Article XIV, Section  .                                     For purposes of applying the debt limitation contained in Utah Constitution Article                           XIV, Section  , the value of the taxable property in Utah is considered to be      of the fair                           market value of the taxable property of the state, as computed from the last assessment for state                           purposes previous to the issuance of the bonds.                                Section  .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Tax levy    Abatement of tax.                                     Each year after issuance of the bonds and until all outstanding bonds are retired, there                           is levied a direct annual tax on all real and personal property within the state subject to state                           taxation, sufficient to pay                                  a   applicable bond redemption premiums, if any                                  b   interest on the bonds as it becomes due  and                                 c   principal of the bonds as it becomes due.                                     a   The State Tax Commission shall fix the rate of the direct annual tax levy each year.                                 b   The tax shall be collected and the proceeds applied as provided in this chapter.                                     The direct annual tax imposed under this section is abated to the extent money is                           available from sources, other than ad valorem taxes in the sinking fund, for the payment of bond                           interest, principal, and redemption premiums.                                Section   .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Creation of sinking fund.                                        There is created a sinking fund, to be administered by the state treasurer, entitled the                                 Highway General Obligation Bonds Sinking Fund.                                      All monies deposited in the sinking fund, from whatever source, shall be used to pay                           debt service on the bonds.                                     The proceeds of all taxes levied under this chapter are appropriated to this fund.                                     The state treasurer may create separate accounts within the sinking fund for each series                           of bonds issued.                                Section   .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Payment of interest, principal, and redemption premiums.                                     The Division of Finance shall draw warrants on the state treasury before any interest,                           principal, or redemption premiums become due on the bonds.                                     After receipt of the warrants, the state treasurer shall                                  a   promptly pay the warrants from funds within the sinking fund  and                                 b   immediately transmit the amount paid to the paying agent for the bonds.                                Section   .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Investment of sinking fund money.                                     The state treasurer may, by following the procedures and requirements of Title   ,                           Chapter  , State Money Management Act, invest any money contained in the sinking fund until                           it is needed for the purposes for which the fund is created.                                     Unless otherwise provided in the resolution of the commission authorizing the issuance                           of bonds under this chapter, the treasurer shall retain all income from the investment of any money                           contained in the sinking fund in the sinking fund and use it for the payment of debt service on the                           bonds. Section   .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Certification of satisfaction of conditions precedent    Conclusiveness.                                     The commission may not issue any bond under this chapter until it finds and certifies                           that all conditions precedent to issuance of the bonds have been satisfied.                                     A recital on any bond of this finding and certification conclusively establishes the                           completion and satisfaction of all such conditions.                                Section   .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                   B      .  Tax exemption.                                The bonds issued under this chapter, any interest paid on the bonds, and any income from                           the bonds are not taxable in this state for any purpose, except for the corporate franchise tax.
