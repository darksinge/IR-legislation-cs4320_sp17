 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies Title 58, Occupations and Professions, by creating a state
            
10

    
certification designation for music therapists.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    defines terms, including "state certification," which means a designation granted by
            
14

    
the division on behalf of the state to an individual who has met the requirements for
            
15

    
state certification related to an occupation or profession;
            
16

    
    .    requires the Division of Occupational and Professional Licensing to grant state
            
17

    
certification to a person who qualifies under this chapter to engage in the practice of
            
18

    
music therapy as a state certified music therapist;
            
19

    
    .    provides the qualifications and renewal requirements for being a state certified
            
20

    
music therapist, which include the applicant providing satisfactory documentation
            
21

    
of being board certified in good standing with the Certification Board for Music
            
22

    
Therapists or an equivalent board as determined by division rule;
            
23

    
    .    provides that this act may not be construed to prevent a person from lawfully
            
24

    
engaging in the practice of music therapy without state certification; and

26

    
therapy, including that it is unlawful for a person who is not a state certified music
            
27

    
therapist to use the title state certified music therapist, or represent that the person is

a state certified music therapist, in connection with the person's name or business.
