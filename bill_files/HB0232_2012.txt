 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the definition of neglect and eliminates a responsibility of the
            
10

    
Division of Child and Family Services.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    adds willful, wanton, and reckless negligence to the definition of neglect; and
            
14

    
    .    eliminates the division's responsibility to conduct court-ordered home evaluations in
            
15

    
custody proceedings.
