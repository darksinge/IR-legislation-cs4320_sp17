    This bill amends the Social Worker Licensing Act by providing that an applicant for
            
11

    
licensure must satisfactorily complete a program accredited by, or in candidacy for
            
12

    
accreditation by, the Council on Social Work Education.

17

    
accreditation, on the day on which the applicant for licensure satisfactorily
            
18

    
completed the program; and

    .    makes technical changes.
