    APPROPRIATION FOR STATEWIDE


     INTEGRATION OF ENGINEERING EDUCATION




    1997 GENERAL SESSION


    STATE OF UTAH


     Sponsor: Craig A.  Peterson


    AN ACT RELATING TO HIGHER EDUCATION; PROVIDING A $500,000

    APPROPRIATION TO STRENGTHEN THE INTEGRATION OF ENGINEERING AND

    PREENGINEERING PROGRAMS AT THE STATE'S NINE INSTITUTIONS OF

    HIGHER EDUCATION; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

    This act enacts uncodified material.

    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

        Section 1.   Appropriation.

        (1)  There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year 1997-98, $500,000 to the

    State Board of Regents for a one-time appropriation to strengthen the statewide integration of

    engineering and preengineering programs at the state's nine institutions of higher education.

        (2)  The board shall distribute the money as follows:

        (a)  $140,000 to the University of Utah;

        (b)  $95,000 to Utah State University;

        (c)  $65,000 to Weber State University;

        (d)  $50, 000 to Salt Lake Community College;

        (e)  $50, 000 to Utah Valley State College;

        (f)  $25,000 to College of Eastern Utah;

        (g)  $25,000 to Dixie College;

        (h)  $25,000 to Snow College; and

        (i)  $25,000 to Southern Utah University.

        Section 2.   Effective date.

        This act takes effect on July 1, 1997.
