DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE                             DEMOGRAPHIC DATA TRACKING                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Mark A. Wheatley                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires the Division of Corporations and Commercial Code to create forms                           that allow a person to provide information about the gender and race of certain business                           owners.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the division to include on a form created by the division a way to indicate                           the gender and race of one or more owners of the entity for which the form is filed                                .    provides that information concerning gender or race is not required to be provided                            and                               .    makes technical changes.
