7    AN ACT RELATING TO LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES; CLARIFYING HOW

8    CERTAIN CHANGES IN MANAGERS OR MEMBERS ARE REPORTED; AND MAKING

9    TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.

10    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

11    AMENDS:

12         48-2b-120, as last amended by Chapter 176, Laws of Utah 1996

13         48-2b-121, as last amended by Chapter 176, Laws of Utah 1996

14    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

15        Section 1.  Section  48-2b-120 is amended to read:

16         48-2b-120.  Annual report.

17        (1)  Each limited liability company and each foreign limited liability company authorized

18    to transact business in this state shall file with the division, during the month of its anniversary

19    date of formation, in the case of domestic limited liability companies, or during the month of the

20    anniversary date of being granted authority to transact business in this state, in the case of foreign

21    limited liability companies authorized to transact business in this state, an annual report setting

22    forth:

23        (a)  the name of the limited liability company and the state or country under the laws of

24    which it is formed;

25        (b)  the street address of the registered office and the name of the agent for service of

26    process at that address, as required to be maintained under Section 48-2b-123;


1        (c)  if there is a change of the registered agent required to be maintained by Section

2    48-2b-123;

3        (d)  if the street address or legal name of any manager or member with management

4    authority named in the articles of organization[, as amended,] of a domestic limited liability

5    company, or named in the application for the registration of a foreign limited liability company,

6    has changed, the new street address or legal name of the member or manager; and

7        (e)  any change in the persons constituting the managers or members with management

8    authority, of a foreign limited liability company.

9        (2)  A change in the person constituting the managers, or members with management

10    authority, of a domestic limited liability company shall be reflected in amended articles of

11    organization, as provided in Section 48-2b-121.

12        (3)  The annual report shall be made on forms prescribed and furnished by the division, and

13    the information contained on the annual report shall be given as of the date of execution of the

14    report.  The annual report forms shall include a statement notifying the limited liability company

15    that failure to file the annual report will result in the suspension and eventual cancellation of its

16    certificate of organization, in the case of a domestic limited liability company, or of its registration,

17    in the case of a foreign limited liability company authorized to transact business in this state.

18        (4)  The annual report shall be signed by any manager or member with management

19    authority.  If the registered agent has changed since the last annual report, the annual report shall

20    also be signed by the new registered agent.

21        (5)  If the report conforms to the requirements of this chapter, the division shall file the

22    report.  If the report does not conform, the division shall mail the report first class postage prepaid

23    to the limited liability company at the street address set forth for its agent for service of process

24    in the certificate of organization or most recent report, for any necessary corrections.  If a report

25    is returned, the penalties for failure to file the report within the time prescribed in this section do

26    not apply, as long as the report is corrected and returned to the division within 30 days from the

27    date the nonconforming report was mailed to the limited liability company.

28        Section 2.  Section  48-2b-121 is amended to read:

29         48-2b-121.  When amendments required.

30        (1)  The articles of organization of a limited liability company shall be amended when:

31        (a)  there is a change in the name of the limited liability company;

- 2 -


1        (b)  there is a change in the character of the business of the limited liability company

2    specified in the articles of organization;

3        (c)  there is a false or erroneous statement in the articles of organization;

4        (d)  there is a change in the time, as stated in the articles of organization, for the dissolution

5    of the limited liability company;

6        (e)  there is a change in who is a manager of the limited liability company or, if the limited

7    liability company is managed by its members, a change in who is a member of the limited liability

8    company;

9        (f)  the members determine to fix a time, not previously specified in the articles of

10    organization, for the dissolution of the limited liability company; or

11        (g)  the members desire to make a change in any other statement in the articles of

12    organization in order for the articles to accurately represent the agreement among them.

13        (2)  Each limited liability company shall file with the division a copy of any amendment

14    to the articles within 60 days after the adoption of the amendment.

15        (3)  A limited liability company is not required to amend its articles of organization to

16    report a change in:

17        (a)  the street or mailing address of a manager or member with management authority; or

18        (b)  the legal name of a manager or member with management authority.
