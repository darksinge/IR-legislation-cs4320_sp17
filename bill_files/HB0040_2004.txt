    This bill modifies provisions of the Election Code that govern the process for filling
            
10

    
midterm vacancies in the Legislature.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    makes technical corrections to the process for filling vacancies by replacing the
            
14

    
term "central committee" with the term "party liaison";

16
