 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Insurance Code to address inducements.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    addresses when a de minimis gift or meal may be given;
            
13

    
    .    addresses what constitutes fair market value of certain inducements; and
            
14

    
    .    makes technical changes.
