This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                        , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  . There is established the Utah National Parks Highway comprising the existing highway          from Route   at the entrance of Zion National Park near Springdale easterly on Route   to Route             near La Verkin then northerly on Route    to Interstate Highway    then northerly on Interstate          Highway   , the Veterans  Memorial Highway, to Route    near Cedar City then southeasterly          on Route    to Route     near Cedar Breaks National Monument then northerly on Route     to          Route     near the north end of Cedar Breaks National Monument then northeasterly on Route              to Route    near Panguitch then southerly on Route    to Route    near Red Canyon then          northeasterly on Route   , the Clem Church Memorial Highway, to Route    near Torrey then          easterly on Route    to Route    near Hanksville then southeasterly on Route   , the Bicentennial          Highway, to Route     near Blanding then northerly on Route     to the junction with Interstate          Highway    near Crescent Junction.
