S.B. 105 Enrolled





            

    
NEW JUDICIAL POSITIONS



            

    
1999 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    
 Sponsor:  Lyle W. Hillyard

            

    
Mike  Dmitrich



            

    
AN ACT RELATING TO THE JUDICIARY; INCREASING THE NUMBER OF JUDGES IN
            

    
THE FIRST AND SEVENTH JUDICIAL DISTRICTS FROM ONE TO TWO; AND
            

    
PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
            

    
This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:
            

    
AMENDS:
            

    
     78-1-2.3, as last amended by Chapter 179, Laws of Utah 1998
            

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
78-1-2.3

 is amended to read:
            

    
     78-1-2.3.  Number of juvenile judges and jurisdictions.
            

    
    The number of juvenile court judges shall be:
            

    
    (1)  [one] two juvenile [judge] judges in the First Juvenile District;
            

    
    (2) five juvenile judges in the Second Juvenile District;
            

    
    (3)  eight juvenile judges in the Third Juvenile District;
            

    
    (4)  four juvenile judges in the Fourth Juvenile District;
            

    
    (5)  two juvenile judges in the Fifth Juvenile District;
            

    
    (6)  one juvenile judge in the Sixth Juvenile District;
            

    
    (7)  [one] two juvenile [judge] judges in the Seventh Juvenile District; and
            

    
    (8)  one juvenile judge in the Eighth Juvenile District.
            

    
    Section 2.   Effective date.
            

    
    This act takes effect on July 1, 1999.
