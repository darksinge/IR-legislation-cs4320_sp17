H.B. 292





1     UTAH NATIONAL GUARD TUITION


2     ASSISTANCE




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor:  Bradley T. Johnson


6    AN ACT RELATING TO MILITIAS AND ARMORIES; PROVIDING FOR TUITION

7    ASSISTANCE FOR ACTIVE NATIONAL GUARD MEMBERS ATTENDING

8    POSTSECONDARY INSTITUTIONS, INCLUDING APPLIED TECHNOLOGY CENTERS;

9    APPROPRIATING $200,000; MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS; AND PROVIDING

10    AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

11    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

12    AMENDS:

13         39-1-63, as last amended by Chapter 144, Laws of Utah 1979

14    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

15        Section 1.  Section  39-1-63 is amended to read:

16         39-1-63.  Tuition assistance for National Guard members -- Use and allocation --

17     Appropriation.  

18        (1)  [There shall be available to individual members of the] The Utah National Guard

19    [scholarships] may provide tuition assistance to members for [undergraduate] study at

20    postsecondary institutions of learning, including Applied Technology Centers, subject to the

21    following requirements:

22        (a)  The individual must be, at the time he or she receives the [benefits] assistance, an

23    active member of the Utah National Guard.

24        (b)  The [benefit hereunder shall be] assistance is for tuition only and shall be [equal to]

25    not more than the resident tuition for the actual course of postsecondary study engaged in by the

26    individual.

27        [(c)  The scholarship shall be payable only to individuals not eligible to receive scholastic


1    financial assistance from the federal government by reason of having been a member of the armed

2    forces of the United States.]

3        (2)  Tuition assistance shall be awarded for each academic year.  Students may apply for

4    assistance for each year during which he or she is an active member of the Utah National Guard.

5        [(2)] (3)  [Scholarships provided hereunder are] Tuition assistance is to be paid by the

6    adjutant general of the state [of Utah] directly to the institutions involved from the [fund] funds

7    appropriated [hereunder].

8        [(3)] (4)  The adjutant general of the state [of Utah] shall [have the power to] establish

9    [such] regulations, procedures, forms, and reports [as are] necessary to administer the allocation

10    of [scholarships] assistance and [payments] payment of funds under this [act] section.

11        [(4)  There is appropriated annually to the adjutant general out of the General Fund the sum

12    of not to exceed $25,000 as specified in the annual appropriation bill. These funds shall be

13    expended for the granting of scholarships in accordance with this act.]

14        (5)  The adjutant general shall include a request each year for funds for this program in the

15    annual budget for the National Guard.

16        Section 2.   Appropriation.

17        There is appropriated from the Uniform School Fund for fiscal year 1998-99 $200,000 to

18    the Utah National Guard to be used for post-secondary education tuition assistance for active

19    members of the National Guard.

20        Section 3.   Effective date.

21        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
