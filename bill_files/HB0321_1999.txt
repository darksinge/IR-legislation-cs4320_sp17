PRISON EDUCATION APPROPRIATION                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Loraine T. Pace                           AN ACT RELATING TO EDUCATION  PROVIDING A     ,    APPROPRIATION TO THE                          STATE BOARD OF REGENTS TO INITIATE UNIVERSITY EXTENSION PROGRAMS AT                          COUNTY PRISONS THROUGHOUT THE STATE  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE                          DATE.                          This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Appropriation.                                    Except as provided in H.B.  , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated                          from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,     ,    to the State Board of Regents to                          initiate university extension programs at county prisons throughout the state.                                   The board shall use the appropriation to establish higher education extension programs                          at the Beaver, Duchesne, San Juan, and Washington County prisons pursuant to its education                          responsibilities under Section       A      .     .                                   The board shall use                                a      ,    of the appropriation as one time monies for downlinks and media equipment                          at the prison sites  and                               b       ,    of the appropriation for ongoing costs to operate, support, and maintain the                          extension programs.                                   The board shall closely monitor the extension programs and report its findings, together                          with recommendations regarding ongoing funding, to the Legislative Education Interim Committee                          prior to November   ,     .                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .        Legislative Review Note      as of                AM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
