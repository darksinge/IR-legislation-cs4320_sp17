     63-38f-608.  Science education program.

            
30


    
    (1) (a)  There is established an informal science and technology education program
            
31

    
within the Governor's Office of Economic Development.
            
32

    
    (b)  The state science advisor shall act as the executive director of the program.
            
33

    
    (c)  The State Advisory Council on Science and Technology shall advise the program,
            
34

    
including:
            
35

    
    (i)  approving all monies expended by the science and technology education program;
            
36

    
    (ii)  approving all operations of the program; and
            
37

    
    (iii)  making policies and procedures to govern the program.
            
38

    
    (2)  The program may:
            
39

    
    (a)  provide informal science and technology-based education to elementary and
            
40

    
secondary students;
            
41

    
    (b)  expose public education students to college level science and technology
            
42

    
disciplines;
            
43

    
    (c)  administer a science and technology camp program; and
            
44

    
    (d)  provide other informal promotion of science and technology education in this state,
            
45

    
including the direct sponsorship of science fairs and science olympiads.
            
46

    
    (3)  The science and technology camp program described under Subsection (2)(c) shall
            
47

    
be:
