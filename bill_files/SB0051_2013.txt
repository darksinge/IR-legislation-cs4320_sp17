 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions related to tuition waivers at institutions of higher
            
10

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    removes the cap on the number of waivers allotted to institutions of higher
            
14

    
education for nonresident students who meet certain academic and admission
            
15

    
requirements;

17

    
grandchildren of alumni; and
            
18

    
    .    requires the State Board of Regents to set certain surcharges related to a tuition
            
19

    
waiver.
