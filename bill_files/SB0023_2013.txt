 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends provisions in Title 20A, Election Code, related to candidates for the
            
10

    
office of lieutenant governor.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    allows a person who has filed a declaration of candidacy for another office to
            
14

    
withdraw as a candidate for that office and file a declaration of candidacy for the
            
15

    
office of lieutenant governor; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
17

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
18

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:
            
20

    
    None

 Utah Code Sections Affected:

     20A-9-201, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2011, Third Special Session, Chapter 2

Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

    Section 1.  
Section  
20A-9-201

 is amended to read:

 more than one political party prohibited with exceptions -- General filing and form

 requirements -- Affidavit of impecuniosity.

    (a)  be a United States citizen; and

    (b)  meet the legal requirements of that office.

35

    
Utah during any election year; or

    (ii)  appear on the ballot as the candidate of more than one political party.

38

    
or Vice President of the United States and another office, if the person resigns the person's
            
39

    
candidacy for the other office after the person is officially nominated for President or Vice

President of the United States.

one justice court judge office.

(6)

73

resident of the prosecution district within 30 days after receiving appointment to the office.

county clerk shall ensure that the person filing the declaration of candidacy:

    (A)  as of the date of filing:

    (I)  is a United States citizen;

    (II)  is a registered voter in the county in which the person seeks office;

20A-11-1603

.
