 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Criminal Code regarding offenses that constitute racketeering by
            
10

    
a pattern of unlawful activity.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    adds the offenses of human trafficking, human smuggling, and aggravated human
            
14

    
trafficking to the list of offenses that, when conducted as a pattern of unlawful
            
15

    
activity, would constitute the offense of racketeering.
            
16

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
17

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None

     76-10-1602.  Definitions.
