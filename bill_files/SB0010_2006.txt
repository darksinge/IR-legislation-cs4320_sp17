 General Description:

    This bill amends the provisional ballot requirements under the Election Code.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    provides that a voter whose identity or right to vote is challenged shall be issued a
            
13

    
provisional ballot and be permitted to vote;

    .    removes the procedure requiring an affidavit to be signed in order to obtain a ballot

    .    provides a criminal penalty for knowingly providing false information when voting
            
18

    
using a provisional ballot; and
            
19

    
    .    makes technical changes.
