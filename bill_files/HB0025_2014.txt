    This bill:

    .    amends condemnation notice requirements for a municipality and project entity;

    .    amends provisions relating to an arbitration or mediation facilitated by the Office of

    .    amends the public uses for which the right of eminent domain may be exercised;

    .    requires a political subdivision or other person exercising the right of eminent
            
21

    
domain to provide a written statement of certain disclosures to a private property
            
22

    
owner; and

    .    makes technical corrections.
