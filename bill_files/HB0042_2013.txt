 LONG TITLE

 Committee Note:
            
9

    
    The Business and Labor Interim Committee recommended this bill.
            
10

    
 General Description:
            
11

    
    This bill modifies provisions related to the Legislature to repeal a health insurance
            
12

    
mandate review.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    repeals the health insurance mandate review by the Business and Labor Interim
            
16

    
Committee and the Health and Human Services Interim Committee; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
18

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
19

    
    None
            
20

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
21

    
    None
            
22

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
23

    
AMENDS:
            
24

    
     36-12-5, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2008, Chapter 142
            
25

    

            
26

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
27

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
36-12-5

 is amended to read:

            
28


    
     36-12-5.  Duties of interim committees.
            
29

    
    (1)  Except as otherwise provided by law, each interim committee shall:
            
30

    
    (a)  receive study assignments by resolution from the Legislature;
            
31

    
    (b)  receive study assignments from the Legislative Management Committee, created
            
32

    
under Section 

36-12-6

;
            
33

    
    (c)  place matters on its study agenda after requesting approval of the study from the
            
34

    
Legislative Management Committee, which request, if not disapproved by the Legislative
            
35

    
Management Committee within 30 days of receipt of the request, the interim committee shall
            
36

    
consider it approved and may proceed with the requested study;
            
37

    
    (d)  request research reports from the professional legislative staff pertaining to the
            
38

    
committee's agenda of study;
            
39

    
    (e)  investigate and study possibilities for improvement in government services within
            
40

    
its subject area;
            
41

    
    (f)  accept reports from the professional legislative staff and make recommendations for
            
42

    
legislative action with respect to such reports; and
            
43

    
    (g)  prepare and recommend to the Legislature a legislative program in response to the
            
44

    
committee's study agenda.
            
45

    
    [(2) (a)  As used in this Subsection (2):]
            
46

    
    [(i)  "Health insurance" is as defined in Section 

31A-1-301

.]
            
47

    
    [(ii)  "Health insurance mandate" means a mandatory obligation with respect to a
            
48

    
coverage, benefit, or provider that, but for Title 31A, Insurance Code, would not be required
            
49

    
for a policy of health insurance.]
            
50

    
    [(iii)  "Review committee" means:]
            
51

    
    [(A)  the Business and Labor Interim Committee; and]
            
52

    
    [(B)  the Health and Human Services Interim Committee.]
            
53

    
    [(b)  In addition to the duties established pursuant to Subsection (1), annually each
            
54

    
review committee shall:]
            
55

    
    [(i)  identify the one or more health insurance mandates listed under Subsection (2)(d)
            
56

    
that:]
            
57

    
    [(A)  are in effect for five or more years as of May 1; and]
            
58

    
    [(B)  have not been reviewed during the previous 10 years as of May 1;]

            
59


    
    [(ii)  select which of the one or more health insurance mandates identified under
            
60

    
Subsection (2)(b)(i) that the review committee elects to review, subject to the direction of the
            
61

    
Legislative Management Committee; and]
            
62

    
    [(iii)  review a health insurance mandate selected under Subsection (2)(b)(ii) to
            
63

    
determine whether the health insurance mandate should be continued, modified, or repealed.]
            
64

    
    [(c)  The review under this Subsection (2) shall include:]
            
65

    
    [(i)  the estimated fiscal impact of the health insurance mandate on state and private
            
66

    
health insurance; and]
            
67

    
    [(ii)  the purpose and effectiveness of the health insurance mandate.]
            
68

    
    [(d)  The Insurance Department shall:]
            
69

    
    [(i)  provide a list of the health insurance mandates in this state in its annual report; and]
            
70

    
    [(ii)  assist in a review if requested by a review committee.]
            
71

    
    [(3)] (2)  Except as otherwise provided by law, reports and recommendations of the
            
72

    
interim committees shall be completed and made public prior to any legislative session at
            
73

    
which the reports and recommendations are submitted.  A copy of the reports and
            
74

    
recommendations shall be mailed to each member or member-elect of the Legislature, to each
            
75

    
elective state officer, and to the state library.
