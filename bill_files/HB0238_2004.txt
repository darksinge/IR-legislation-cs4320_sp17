 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends the Workers' Compensation Laws to clarify that the Labor
            
10

    
Commission has rulemaking authority regarding a physician's disclosure of medical
            
11

    
records relevant to an employee's industrial accident or occupational disease claim.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    requires an injured employee and a physician to comply with commission rules
            
15

    
regarding disclosure of medical records relevant to the employee's industrial
            
16

    
accident or occupational disease; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
18

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
19

    
    None
            
20

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
21

    
    None

 Utah Code Sections Affected:

the following may provide notification of the injury to the employee's employer:

    (i)  the employee's next-of-kin; or

    (ii)  the employee's attorney.

medical records of the employee relevant to the industrial or occupational disease claim.

employee fails to notify within the time period described in Subsection (3)(b):

    (i)  the employee's employer in accordance with Subsection (2); or

    (ii)  the division.

    (i)  180 days of the day on which the injury occurs; or

    (ii)  in the case of an occupational hearing loss, the time period specified in Section

34A-2-506

(9); or
