PENALTY FOR STEALING MOTOR FUEL                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Carl R. Saunders                             This act modifies provisions of the Motor Vehicle Code and the Criminal Code.  The act                          creates an offense of theft of fuel for a motor vehicle.  The act provides a license suspension                          penalty for a second or subsequent conviction of theft of fuel for a motor vehicle. Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                         . .  Theft of fuel for a motor vehicle.                                     A person commits theft of fuel if the person knowingly fails to remit payment for fuel                           dispensed at an establishment where fuel for a motor vehicle is offered for retail sale.                                     In addition to any penalties imposed under this part, a court may order the driver                           license of a person convicted of a second or subsequent violation of Subsection     suspended for                           a period of    days in accordance with Section                 .                                     If a court orders suspension of a person s driver license under Subsection    , the court                           shall notify and provide a record of the conviction to the Driver License Division.
