 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends a provision in the Wildlife Resources Code regarding the requirement
            
11

    
to wear hunter orange.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    exempts a person from the requirement to wear hunter orange while using certain
            
15

    
types of hunting permits.
            
16

    Section 1.  
Section  
23-20-31

 is amended to read:
            
26

    
     23-20-31.  Requirement to wear hunter orange.
            
27

    
    (1)  As used in this section:

            
28


    
    (a) (i)  "Centerfire rifle hunt" means a hunt for which a hunter may use a centerfire rifle,
            
29

    
except as provided in Subsection (1)(a)(ii).
            
30

    
    (ii)  "Centerfire rifle hunt" does not include:
            
31

    
    (A)  a bighorn sheep hunt;
            
32

    
    (B)  a mountain goat hunt;
            
33

    
    (C)  a hunt requiring the hunter to possess a statewide conservation permit; or
            
34

    
    (D)  a hunt requiring the hunter to possess a statewide sportsman permit.
            
35

    
    (b)  "Statewide conservation permit" means a permit:
            
36

    
    (i)  issued by the division;
            
37

    
    (ii)  distributed through a nonprofit organization founded for the purpose of promoting
            
38

    
wildlife conservation; and
            
39

    
    (iii)  valid:
            
40

    
    (A)  on open hunting units statewide; and
            
41

    
    (B)  for the species of big game and time period designated by the Wildlife Board.
            
42

    
    (c)  "Statewide sportsman permit" means a permit:
            
43

    
    (i)  issued by the division through a public draw; and
            
44

    
    (ii)  valid:
            
45

    
    (A)  on open hunting units statewide; and
            
46

    
    (B)  for the species of big game and time period designated by the Wildlife Board.
            
47

    
    (2) (a)  A person shall wear a minimum of 400 square inches of hunter orange material
            
48

    
while hunting any species of big game, except as provided in Subsection (3).
            
49

    
    (b)  Hunter orange material must be worn on the head, chest, and back.
            
50

    
    (c)  A camouflage pattern in hunter orange does not meet the requirements of
            
51

    
Subsection (2)(a).
            
52

    
    (3)  A person is not required to wear the hunter orange material described in Subsection
            
53

    
(2):
            
54

    
    (a)  during an archery, muzzle-loader, mountain goat, or bighorn sheep hunt, unless a
            
55

    
centerfire rifle hunt is in progress in the same area; and
            
56

    
    (b)  while using:
            
57

    
    (i)  a premium limited entry permit; or
            
58

    
    (ii)  a limited entry permit.
