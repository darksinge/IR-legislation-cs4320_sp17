 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Transportation Code by amending provisions relating to the
            
10

    
Department of Transportation.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides that the governor shall appoint an executive director to be the chief
            
14

    
executive officer of the department, after consultation with, rather than
            
15

    
recommendations from, the Transportation Commission.
            
16

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
17

    
    None
            
18

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
19

    
    None
            
20

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
21

    
AMENDS:
            
22

    
     72-1-202, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2002, Chapter 176
            
23

    

            
24

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
25

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
72-1-202

 is amended to read:
            
26

    
     72-1-202.  Executive director of department -- Appointment -- Qualifications --
            
27

    
 Term -- Responsibility -- Power to bring suits -- Salary.
            
28

    
    (1) (a)  The governor, [with recommendations from] after consultation with the
            
29

    
commission and with the consent of the Senate, shall appoint an executive director to be the

            
30


    
chief executive officer of the department.
            
31

    
    (b)  The executive director shall be a qualified executive with technical and
            
32

    
administrative experience and training appropriate for the position.
            
33

    
    (c)  The executive director shall remain in office until a successor is appointed.
            
34

    
    (d)  The executive director may be removed by the governor.
            
35

    
    (2)  In addition to the other functions, powers, duties, rights, and responsibilities
            
36

    
prescribed in this chapter, the executive director shall:
            
37

    
    (a)  have responsibility for the administrative supervision of the state transportation
            
38

    
systems and the various operations of the department;
            
39

    
    (b)  have the responsibility for the implementation of rules, priorities, and policies
            
40

    
established by the department and the commission;
            
41

    
    (c)  have full power to bring suit in courts of competent jurisdiction in the name of the
            
42

    
department as the executive director considers reasonable and necessary for the proper
            
43

    
attainment of the goals of this chapter;
            
44

    
    (d)  receive a salary, to be established by the governor within the salary range fixed by
            
45

    
the Legislature in Title 67, Chapter 22, State Officer Compensation, together with actual
            
46

    
traveling expenses while away from the executive director's office on official business; and
            
47

    
    (e)  purchase all necessary equipment and supplies for the department.
