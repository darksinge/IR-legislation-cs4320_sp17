H.B. 435





1     REPORTS AND NOTICES AMENDMENTS




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor:  Gene  Davis


5    AN ACT RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS IN GENERAL; MODIFYING CERTAIN

6    RESTRICTIONS ON REPORTS AND NOTICES PREPARED BY GOVERNMENT

7    ENTITIES; AND MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.

8    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

9    AMENDS:

10         63-93-201, as enacted by Chapter 256, Laws of Utah 1997

11         63-93-202, as enacted by Chapter 256, Laws of Utah 1997

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.  Section  63-93-201 is amended to read:

14         63-93-201.  Prohibition -- Penalty.

15        (1)  Except as provided in [Subsections] Subsection (2) [and (3)], a government entity may

16    not include, as part of any report, the photograph[,] or likeness[, name, or signature] of any elected

17    official.

18        [(2)  Notwithstanding Subsection (1), a report may contain the name or signature of an

19    elected official if the name or signature is required for attribution.]

20        [(3)  Notwithstanding Subsection (1), a]

21        (2)  A report may contain the photograph[,] or likeness[, name, or signature] of the chief

22    executive officer of a government entity if the report is promotional literature.

23        Section 2.  Section  63-93-202 is amended to read:

24         63-93-202.  Penalties.

25        If an elected official's photograph[,] or likeness[, name, or signature] appears on any report

26    in violation of this section that was prepared under the authority or at the direction of the elected

27    official, that elected official is personally liable for the cost of preparing and distributing the report.
