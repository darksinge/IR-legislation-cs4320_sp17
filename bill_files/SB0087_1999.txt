S.B. 87 Enrolled





            

    
RESTRICTION ON CREATION OF LOCAL DISTRICTS



            

    
1999 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    
 Sponsor:  R. Mont Evans

            

    
AN ACT RELATING TO LIMITED PURPOSE LOCAL GOVERNMENT ENTITIES;
            

    
EXTENDING THE DATE BEFORE WHICH THE PROCESS TO CREATE A LOCAL
            

    
DISTRICT MAY NOT BE INITIATED.
            

    
This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:
            

    
AMENDS:
            

    
     17B-2-217, as enacted by Chapter 368, Laws of Utah 1998
            

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
17B-2-217

 is amended to read:
            

    
     17B-2-217.  Limitation on initiating process to create local district.
            

    
    Notwithstanding any other provision of this part and except as provided in Section
            

    

17A-2-101

, the process to create a local district under this part may not be initiated before May [3]
            

    
2, [1999] 2000.
