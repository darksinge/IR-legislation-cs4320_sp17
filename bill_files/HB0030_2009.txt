 Committee Note:
            
9

    
    The Transportation Interim Committee recommended this bill.
            
10

    
 General Description:
            
11

    
    This bill modifies the Transportation Code by amending provisions relating to
            
12

    
commercial motor vehicles.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    provides definitions;
            
16

    
    .    requires a motor carrier to have lettered on both sides of a vehicle the location of
            
17

    
domicile by city and state only if the vehicle is an intrastate commercial vehicle; 
            
18

    
    .    provides that a vehicle is exempt from the motor carrier lettering and identification
            
19

    
number display requirements only if the vehicle is an intrastate commercial motor
            
20

    
vehicle used by a farmer for the production of agricultural products;
            
21

    
    .    provides that an operator of a farm vehicle or combination of farm vehicles is
            
22

    
exempt from certain requirements for physical qualifications, medical examinations,
            
23

    
and medical certification only if the farm vehicle or combination of farm vehicles
            
24

    
being operated are:
            
25

    
        .    under 26,001 pounds gross vehicle weight rating; 
            
26

    
        .    controlled and driven by a farmer to transport agricultural products, farm
            
27

    
machinery, or farm supplies to a farm within 150 miles of the farmer's farm and

            
28


    
not as a motor carrier for hire; and
            
29

    
        .    not operated as an interstate commercial vehicle; 
            
30

    
    .    grants the Utah Highway Patrol and other law enforcement agencies certified by the
            
31

    
department authority to conduct inspections as needed to enforce state and federal
            
32

    
laws related to the operation of a motor carrier; and
            
33

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
34

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
35

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:
