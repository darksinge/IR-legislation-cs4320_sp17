Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                               CHAPTER   .  ANESTHESIOLOGIST ASSISTANT LICENSING ACT                            Part  .  General Provisions                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Anesthesiologist Assistant Licensing Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               In addition to the definitions in Section                 , as used in this chapter                                      Anesthesiologist assistant  means a person licensed under this chapter to engage in                          the practice of anesthesiologist assistant.                                     Board  means the Physician Licensing Board created in Section                  .                                     Practice of anesthesiologist assistant  means                                 a   the provisions of anesthesiology services as defined by the division by                          administrative rule                                 b   under the supervision of a physician                                 i   licensed under Chapter   , Utah Medical Practice Act  and                                ii   specializing in anesthesia  and                                c   when the physician described under Subsection     b  is                                 i   actively engaged in clinical practice  and                                ii   immediately available on site to provide supervision of the anesthesiologist                          assistant.                                     Unlawful conduct  is as defined in Sections                  and                  .                                     Unprofessional conduct  is as defined in Sections                  and                   and as                          may be further defined by administrative rule adopted by the division in accordance with Title                            G, Chapter  , Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            Part  .  Board                                         .  Board.                                    Anesthesiologist assistants shall be regulated by the division under the supervision                          of the Physician Licensing Board created in Section                  .                                    The board may designate one of its members on a permanent or rotating basis to                                 a   assist the division in reviewing complaints concerning the unlawful or                             unprofessional conduct of a licensee  and                                b   advise the division in its investigation of these complaints.                                    A board member who has, under Subsection    , reviewed a complaint or advised                          in its investigation may be disqualified from participating with the board when the board                          member serves as a presiding officer of an administrative proceeding concerning the complaint.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            Part  .  Licensing                                         .  Licensure required    Issuance of licenses    Effect on insurers.                                    Beginning January  ,     , and except as provided in Sections                  and                                          , a license is required to engage in the practice of anesthesiologist assistant.                                     The division shall issue to any person who qualifies under this chapter a license to                           practice as an anesthesiologist assistant.                                     Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to require payment from insurers for                           anesthesiologist assistant services.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Qualifications for licensure    Temporary license.                                     Except as provided in Subsection    , each applicant for licensure as an                           anesthesiologist assistant under this chapter shall                                  a   submit an application in a form prescribed by the division                                  b   pay a fee determined by the department under Section            .                                       c   be of good moral character                                  d   provide satisfactory documentation of having earned certification from the National                           Commission of Certified Anesthesiologist Assistants  NCCAA  or its successor organization                                  e   within    months of completing the training under Subsection     d , pass the                           certification exam offered by the NCCAA  and                                 f   complete other requirements as specified by the division by administrative rule.                                     The division may issue a temporary license, in accordance with Section                                           and any other conditions established by rule, to an applicant who meets all of the requirements                           for licensure except the examination requirement of Subsection     e .                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Term of license    Expiration    Renewal.                                        The division shall issue each license under this chapter in accordance with a                           two year renewal cycle established by rule.  The division may by rule extend or shorten a                           renewal cycle by as much as one year to stagger the renewal cycles it administers.                                     Each licensee shall, at the time of applying for renewal, demonstrate compliance                           with continuing education requirements established by rule by the division in collaboration                           with the board.                                     Each license automatically expires on the expiration date shown on the license                           unless the licensee renews it in accordance with Section                 .                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Exemptions from licensure.                                In addition to the exemptions from licensure set forth in Section                 , the                           following persons may engage in the practice of anesthesiologist assistant subject to the stated                           circumstances and limitations without being licensed under this chapter                                      an individual licensed as a physician and surgeon or osteopathic physician and                           surgeon under Chapter   , Utah Medical Practice Act, and Chapter   , Utah Osteopathic                           Medical Practice Act  and                                     a commissioned physician or surgeon serving in the armed forces of the United                           States or other federal agency.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                             Part  .  License Denial and Discipline                                          .  Grounds for denial of license    Disciplinary proceedings.                                Grounds for refusing to issue a license to an applicant, for refusing to renew the license                           of a licensee, for revoking, suspending, restricting, or placing on probation the license of a                           licensee, for issuing a public or private reprimand to a licensee, and for issuing a cease and                           desist order shall be in accordance with Section                 .                                Section   .    Section                  is enacted to read                             Part  .  Unlawful and Unprofessional Conduct                                          .  Unlawful conduct.                                 Unlawful conduct  includes, in addition to the definition in Section                 , using                           the title  anesthesiologist assistant  or any other title or designation tending to indicate that the                           person is an anesthesiologist assistant unless that person has a current license as an                              anesthesiologist assistant issued under this chapter.                                Section   .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Unprofessional conduct.                                 Unprofessional conduct  includes, in addition to the definition in Section                  and                           as may be further defined by rule                                      engaging in any act or practice in a professional capacity which the licensee is not                           competent to perform through training or experience                                      failing to refer a client to other competent professionals when the licensee is unable                           or unwilling to adequately support or serve the client                                      failing to maintain the confidentiality of any information received from a client,                           unless released by the client or otherwise authorized or required by law  and                                     exploiting a client for personal advantage, profit, or interest.
