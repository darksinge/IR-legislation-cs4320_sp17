ILLEGAL USE OF OFF HIGHWAY VEHICLES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Michael E. Noel                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending provisions relating to                           off highway vehicle use.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that a person may not tear down, mutilate, deface, or destroy                                    .    a sign, signboard, or other notice that regulates off highway vehicle use  or                                    .    a fence, gate, or other enclosure                                .    provides that a person may not operate or give another person permission to operate                           an off highway vehicle on public land unless the location is posted by sign or                           designated by map or description as open to off highway vehicle use                                .    provides penalties and sentencing provisions for violating the prohibition on                           operating an off highway vehicle on public land                                .    provides that a person is guilty of aggravated unlawful use of an off highway                           vehicle on public or private land if                                    .    the person violates certain restrictions on the use of an off highway vehicle on                           public or private land  and                                   .    has previously been convicted of the restrictions on use of an off highway                           vehicle on public or private land or knowingly, intentionally, or recklessly                           causes certain damage or harasses wildlife or livestock                                   .    provides penalties and sentencing provisions for an aggravated unlawful use of an                           off highway vehicle on public or private land conviction                                .    provides certain exceptions to the off highway vehicle use restrictions  and                               .    makes technical changes.
