 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions in the Health Code related to the duties of the state medical
            
10

    
examiner's office when the death appears to be the result of a highway accident.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    requires the state medical examiner to assume custody of a body when it appears
            
14

    
that the death is the result of a highway accident.
            
15

17

    
    .    $350,000 from the General Fund, Ongoing, for fiscal year 2012-13, to the
            
18

    
Department of Health - Medical Examiner's Office.
