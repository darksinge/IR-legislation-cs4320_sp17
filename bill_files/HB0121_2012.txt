 General Description:

    This bill modifies the Utah State Personnel Management Act by amending provisions

    This bill:

    .    defines terms;

    .    provides that a law enforcement officer or state correctional officer shall be given a
            
16

17

    
retires or reaches the retirement age of 62 years if the officer sustains an injury:

        .    while in the lawful discharge of the officer's duty; and

21

    
causing the accident;

23

    
offset by any benefit received under workers' compensation, long-term disability,
            
24

    
and certain other payments; and
