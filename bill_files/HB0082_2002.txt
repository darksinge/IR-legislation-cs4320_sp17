STORAGE OF CONCEALED FIREARMS ON                           FACILITIES WITH SECURE AREAS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   John E. Swallow                             This act modifies provisions relating to concealed firearms.  The act requires an entity                          operating a facility with a secure area to provide a firearms storage area for persons with                          permits to carry concealed firearms.  The act provides for an immediate effective date.
