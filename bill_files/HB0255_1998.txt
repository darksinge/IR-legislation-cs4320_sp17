H.B. 255









1     RETIREES DEFINED BENEFIT


2     ENHANCEMENT




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor: Orville D. Carnahan


6    AN ACT RELATING TO PENSIONS; AUTHORIZING PAYMENT OF AN ADDITIONAL

7    RETIREMENT BENEFIT TO CERTAIN RETIREES; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE

8    DATE.

9    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

10    ENACTS:

11         49-1-617, Utah Code Annotated 1953

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.  Section  49-1-617 is enacted to read:

14         49-1-617.  Payment of additional retirement benefit -- Rules for implementation.

15        (1) (a)  The board shall provide an additional benefit to retirees under Title 49, Chapters

16    2 and 3, whose retirement allowance, when calculated, amounts to less than the allowance

17    provided by using a 2% per year of service factor for all years of service.

18        (b)  The benefit provided in Subsection (1)(a) shall be calculated by the board using a

19    factor that is as close as reasonably possible to 2% per year of service for each retiree.

20        (2)  Upon receipt of the legislative appropriation and after consultation with representatives

21    of those retirees under Title 49, Chapters 2 and 3, the board shall adopt standards and rules

22    governing the distribution of the money.

23        Section 2.   Effective date.

24        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
