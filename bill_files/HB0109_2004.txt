 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies the State Affairs in General title, the Uniform Driver License Act,
            
9

    
and the Utah Highway Patrol Act to amend legal presence provisions.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    defines "governmental entity";
            
13

    
    .    prohibits a governmental entity from accepting a matricula card issued by the
            
14

    
Mexican government as proof of personal identification;
            
15

    
    .    repeals, as a substitute for a Social Security number, the use of a temporary
            
16

    
identification number (ITIN) issued by the Internal Revenue Service for purposes of
            
17

    
obtaining a driver license or identification card issued by the Driver License
            
18

    
Division; and
            
19

    
    .    requires the Utah Highway Patrol to establish and implement procedures for:
            
20

    
        .    recognizing, identifying, and reporting of detained persons who are not legally
            
21

    
present in the United States;
            
22

    
        .    cooperating with the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services for the
            
23

    
enforcement of legal presence laws; and
            
24

    
        .    training troopers on the enforcement of legal presence laws.
            
25

    None
