 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Insurance Code to address sunset dates for e-commerce fees.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    deletes the time frame limiting when e-commerce fees are treated as dedicated
            
14

    
credits;
            
15

    
    .    deletes the sunset date for the e-commerce fees; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes and conforming amendments.
            
17

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
18

    
    None
            
19

    
 Other Special Clauses:

    None

    (i)  issuing and renewing licenses and certificates of authority;

Section

.

44

    (i)  reasonable and fair; and
