CREDIT ALLOWED IN TEACHER TRANSFER                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Mike  Thompson                             This act modifies provisions relating to the State System of Public Education by requiring                          a local school board to include the total teaching experience in Utah public schools when                          negotiating a contract to teach.  The act permits but does not require applicants with                          teaching experience outside the state who are negotiating an employment contract to receive                          credit for     of those years.  The act takes effect July  ,     .
