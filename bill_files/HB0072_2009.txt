 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill changes the appointing authority for some members of the Advisory Board on
            
11

    
Children's Justice, adds a health professional, and removes the chairs of local advisory
            
12

    
boards from the statewide Advisory Board.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    changes the appointing authority from the governor to the attorney general for six
            
16

    
members of the Advisory Board on Children's Justice;
            
17

    
    .    adds a licensed health professional to the board; and
            
18

    
    .    removes the chairs of local Children's Justice Center boards from the board.
            
19

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
20

    
    None
