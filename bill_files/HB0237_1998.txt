8    AMENDS:

10    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12         63-55-263.  Repeal dates, Titles 63, 63A, and 63C.

17    July 1, 2007.

18        (3)  The Resource Development Coordinating Committee, created in Section 63-28a-2, is
