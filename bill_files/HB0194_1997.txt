EDUCATION ASSESSMENT PROGRAM                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Richard M.  Siddoway          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  PROVIDING FOR A STATEWIDE         STUDENT ASSESSMENT PROGRAM IN THE TENTH GRADE TO FACILITATE         STUDENT EDUCATION AND CAREER PLANNING  AND PROVIDING FOR AN         EVALUATION OF THE PROGRAM.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                  A      , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    A       is enacted to read                  A      .  Education and career planning assessment.                     The State Board of Education shall                a   require an assessment program in the tenth grade in each school district to facilitate          student education and career planning                b   require the State Office of Education to submit and recommend a student education          and career planning assessment device to the board for approval, adoption, and distribution to each          school district.                   School districts shall use the assessment device required under Subsection     to                a   provide a statewide profile of student abilities and career and educational interests                b   provide critical information to students, parents, counselors, and district and state level          program managers in implementing guidance services for students  and               c   assist each tenth grader to plan for the last two years of high school as well as job          preparation and post secondary education or both.                   a   School districts shall implement the assessment program during the         school          year.              b   Each district shall provide for distribution of the information obtained from its         assessment program to students, parents, teachers, and the state board.                  a   The state board shall submit to the Legislature, annually, a budget to implement the         assessment program authorized in this section.              b   As part of its budget recommendation, the state board shall include an evaluation of         the program and proposed modifications, if appropriate.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
