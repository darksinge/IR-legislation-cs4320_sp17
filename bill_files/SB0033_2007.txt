 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by creating a special group license plate for
            
11

    
the parents, spouse, or siblings of a person who is killed while serving in the United
            
12

    
States armed forces.

14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    creates a special group license plate for the parents, spouse, or siblings of a person
            
16

    
who is killed while serving in the United States armed forces; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
18

19

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:
