 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Criminal Code and the Code of Criminal Procedure regarding
            
11

    
procedures for the release of jail inmates for work or education.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    clarifies that the court may order whether the inmate is allowed to be released;
            
15

    
    .    specifies that the custodial authority of the jail determines the terms and conditions
            
16

    
of an inmate's release; and
            
17

    
    .    provides the procedure for an inmate to appeal the denial of release time by the
            
18

    
custodial authority if the denial does not comply with the facility's policy in
            
19

    
determining release for the inmate.
            
20

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
21

    
    None
            
22

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
23

    
    None
