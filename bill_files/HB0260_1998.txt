H.B. 260









1     PUBLIC EDUCATION SERVICES


2     APPROPRIATION




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor: James R. Gowans


6    AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION; PROVIDING FOR A $300,000

7    APPROPRIATION TO EXPAND THE TECHNOLOGY CAPABILITIES OF CERTAIN

8    PUBLIC SCHOOLS IN ORDER TO HELP MAINTAIN A POSITIVE RELATIONSHIP

9    WITH THE STATE OF NEVADA AND IMPROVE THE QUALITY OF EDUCATIONAL

10    SERVICES AT THE SCHOOLS; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

11    This act enacts uncodified material.

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.   Appropriation.

14        (1)  There is appropriated from the Uniform School Fund for fiscal year 1998-99, $300,000

15    to the State Board of Education for a one-time appropriation to expand the technology capabilities

16    of public schools in the state's northwest desert area.

17        (2)  The purpose of the appropriation is to help maintain a positive relationship with the

18    state of Nevada as related to the quality of public education services available at Utah schools

19    close to the borders of both states.

20        Section 2.   Effective date.

21        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
