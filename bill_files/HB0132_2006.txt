 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions relating to local government land use and impact fees.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    prohibits counties and municipalities from withholding a building permit because of
            
13

    
an applicant's failure to comply with a requirement that is not expressed in
            
14

    
ordinance; and
            
15

    
    .    prohibits counties and municipalities from imposing a requirement on a holder of a
            
16

    
building permit if that requirement is not expressed in the building permit.
