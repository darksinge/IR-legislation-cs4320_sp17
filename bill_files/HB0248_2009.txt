REGULATING THE USE OF A WIRELESS                           COMMUNICATION DEVICE WHILE                           OPERATING A MOTOR VEHICLE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Carol Spackman Moss                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                           General Description                                This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending provisions relating to using a                           wireless communication device while operating a motor vehicle.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides definitions                                .    prohibits a person from using a wireless communication device while operating a                           motor vehicle unless the wireless communication device is designed and configured                           to allow for hands free talking and listening and is used in that manner                                .    provides exceptions to the wireless communication device prohibition                                .    provides that it is a class C misdemeanor for violating the wireless communication                           device prohibition  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                               ENACTS                                    a     , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section        a          is enacted to read                                    a     .  Prohibition on using a wireless communication device while operating                           a motor vehicle    Exceptions    Penalty.                                    As used in this section                                 a    Text messaging  has the same meaning as defined in Section                 .                                b   i    Wireless communication device  means a device used for the transfer of                          information without the use of electrical conductors or wires.                                ii    Wireless communication device  includes a                                 A   wireless telephone                                 B   personal digital assistant                                 C   pager  or                                D   text messaging device.                                    a   Except as provided in Subsection    , a person may not use a wireless                          communication device while operating a motor vehicle upon a highway of this state unless the                          wireless communication device                                 i   is specifically designed and configured to allow hands free listening and talking                           and                                ii   is used in the manner described in Subsection     a  i .                                b   The use of a wireless communication device under this Subsection     includes the                          use of a wireless communication device for text messaging or electronic mail communication.                                    Subsection     does not prohibit a person from using a wireless communication                          device while operating a motor vehicle                                 a   during an emergency                                 b   when reporting a safety hazard or requesting assistance relating to a safety hazard                           or                                c   when reporting criminal activity or requesting assistance relating to a criminal                          activity.                                       A person who violates this section is guilty of a class C misdemeanor.
