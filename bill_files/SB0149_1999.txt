S.B. 149 Enrolled





            

    
PRISON INMATE STATUS



            

    
1999 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    
 Sponsor:  Lyle W. Hillyard

            

    
AN ACT RELATING TO CORRECTIONS AND STATE INSTITUTIONS; CREATING A
            

    
TRUSTEE STATUS FOR PRISON INMATES.
            

    
This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:
            

    
ENACTS:
            

    
     64-13-14.6, Utah Code Annotated 1953
            

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
64-13-14.6

 is enacted to read:
            

    
     64-13-14.6.  Inmate trustee status.
            

    
    (1)  The department may grant an offender trustee status.
            

    
    (2)  Trustee status may be granted to an inmate who has:
            

    
    (a)  been granted a release date by the Board of Pardons and Parole; and
            

    
    (b)  agreed to the terms and conditions established by the department to participate in
            

    
compensatory service or work projects prior to his parole date.
            

    
    (3)  The department shall develop rules governing inmates granted trustee status.
