 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill makes changes to low-income housing reporting procedures and related
            
10

    
penalties for purposes of property taxation.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    requires the owner of property subject to a low-income housing covenant to
            
14

    
annually provide certain information to a county assessor;
            
15

    
    .    provides for assessment when the required information is not provided;
            
16

    
    .    provides for a penalty for failure to provide certain information to an assessor; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical and conforming changes.
            
18

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
19

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:
