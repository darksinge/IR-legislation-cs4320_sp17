6    AN ACT RELATING TO HEALTH; PERMITTING AN INSURED TO CHOOSE A HEALTH

7    CARE PROVIDER HEALTH CARE FACILITY, OR MEDICAL EQUIPMENT SUPPLIER;

8    REQUIRING THE INSURER TO REIMBURSE THE INSURED'S PROVIDER, FACILITY,
