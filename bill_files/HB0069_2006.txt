 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends the Utah Dairy Act to allow the sale of raw milk under certain
            
10

    
conditions.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    defines a cow-share program;
            
14

    
    .    prohibits cow-share programs; and
            
15

    
    .    allows a producer to sell raw milk at a self-owned retail store if certain requirements
            
16

    
are met.
            
17

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
18

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None
