    The Judiciary, Law Enforcement, and Criminal Justice Interim Committee

 General Description:
            
12

    
    This bill modifies the Criminal Code by enhancing the penalty for multiple theft
            
13

    
offenses.
            
14

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
15

    
    This bill:
            
16

    
    .    enhances the penalty for an individual who returns and commits retail theft again at
            
17

    
a property where the individual has previously been prohibited from returning;
            
18

    
    .    amends the penalty for theft offenses if the defendant has committed two prior theft
            
19

    
offenses within the prior 10 years;
            
20

    
    .    authorizes a merchant to prohibit an individual who has committed retail theft from
            
21

    
reentering the property on which the individual committed retail theft; and
            
22

    
    .    specifies how a merchant may give written notice prohibiting an individual who has
            
23

    
previously committed retail theft from reentering the property.
