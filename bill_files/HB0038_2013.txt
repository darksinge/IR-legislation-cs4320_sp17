    This bill:

    .    amends definitions;

    .    prohibits an anonymous cash contribution over $100;

    .    requires a candidate to disburse an anonymous cash contribution or public service

assistance over $100 to:

501(c)(3), Internal Revenue Code;

    .    prohibits a filing entity, other than a candidate, from using an anonymous cash

    .    increases the amount at which certain filing entities must report the recipient of an
