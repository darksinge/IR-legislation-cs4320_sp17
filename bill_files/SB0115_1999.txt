APPROPRIATION FOR UTAH STATE FAIRPARK



            

    
1999 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    
 Sponsor:  Pete  Suazo

            

    
AN ACT RELATING TO COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND
            

    
APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $100,000 FROM THE GENERAL FUND FOR
            

    
FISCAL YEAR 1999-2000 TO THE UTAH STATE FAIR PARK; AND PROVIDING AN
            

    
EFFECTIVE DATE.
            

    
This act enacts uncodified material.
            

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            

    
    Section 1.   Appropriation.
            

    
    There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year 1999-2000, $100,000 to the
            

    
Utah State Fair Park for building maintenance and equipment replacement.
            

    
    Section 2.   Effective date.
            

    
    This act takes effect on July 1, 1999.
