SCHOOLS FOR THE DEAF AND BLIND                           SALARY ADJUSTMENT                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor   Karen W. Morgan                            Senate Sponsor         Karen Hale                                                       LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies provisions pertaining to salary adjustments for staff of the Utah                           Schools for the Deaf and the Blind.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    directs the State Board of Education to include in its annual budget request for the                           Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind an amount of money sufficient to                           implement salary adjustments, as required by law, including money to fund step and                           lane changes.
