 General Description:

    This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by establishing vehicle operator turn off
            
11

    
requirements and responsibilities on certain highways.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    defines slow moving vehicle;
            
15

    
    .    requires an operator of a slow-moving vehicle causing a line of five or more
            
16

    
vehicles behind the operator's vehicle on a highway with one lane in each direction
            
17

    
to:
            
18

    
        .    turn off the roadway where a safe turn-out exists; and

21

    
to turn off the roadway and permit the vehicles following behind the operator's
            
22

    
vehicle to pass; and

    .    makes technical changes.
