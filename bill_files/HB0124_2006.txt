 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill provides requirements for parenting plans when domestic violence is involved.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    requires that a court not allow parent-time unless the parent who is the perpetrator
            
13

    
of domestic violence completes an appropriate treatment plan;
            
14

    
    .    requires that if a protective order or civil stalking injunction is in place, a third party
            
15

    
must be utilized to conduct parent-time; and
            
16

    
    .    allows for changes to the parenting plan as needed.
            
17

18

    
    None
