H.B. 284









1     APPROPRIATION FOR BRIDGERLAND ICE ARENA




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor:  Craig W. Buttars


5    AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $2,000,000 FROM THE

6    GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 1998-99 TO THE DEPARTMENT OF

7    COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FOR USE IN CONSTRUCTION OF

8    THE BRIDGERLAND COMMUNITY ICE ARENA; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE

9    DATE.

10    This act enacts uncodified material.

11    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12        Section 1.   Appropriation.

13        There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year 1998-99 $2,000,000 to the

14    Department of Community and Economic Development for distribution to the Bridgerland

15    Community Ice Arena, Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization,  for construction of the

16    Bridgerland Community Ice Arena in partnership with Hyde Park City, to help fulfill the statewide

17    public purpose of providing sports and recreation facilities for the people of the state and of

18    providing a potential practice facility for the 2002 Olympic Winter Games.

19        Section 2.   Effective date.

20        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
