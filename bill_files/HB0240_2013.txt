 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Alcoholic Beverage Control Act to address when a restaurant
            
10

    
may sell, offer for sale, or furnish an alcoholic product.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    modifies the language related to a restaurant licensee confirming that a patron has
            
14

    
the intent to order food as a condition of receiving an alcoholic product; and
            
15

    
    .    makes technical changes.
