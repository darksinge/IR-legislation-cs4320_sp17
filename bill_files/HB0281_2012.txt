 General Description:

    This bill amends Title 62A, Chapter 2, Licensure of Programs and Facilities, by

permitting a renewal license that lasts 24 months.

 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    authorizes the Office of Licensing to renew a human services license for 24 months
            
14

    
if:

        .    the licensee has already been licensed by the office for at least 24 months; and

    .    clarifies that criminal background checks on licensees will continue to be conducted
            
18

    
annually; and
            
19

    
    .    makes technical changes.
