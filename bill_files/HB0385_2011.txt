 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill provides a standard for when jails are to provide medical care to prisoners and
            
10

    
amends procedures for collecting medical expenses owed by an inmate.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides standards regarding when a sheriff is required to provide medical care to
            
14

    
persons being held in the county jail;
            
15

    
    .    allows a county to seek reimbursement from an inmate for medical care in addition
            
16

    
to any other outstanding costs the inmate owes to the county jail at the time of
            
17

    
release;
            
18

    
    .    includes inmate medical care in the costs incurred by a county that the court may
            
19

    
order be paid by the inmate to the county as restitution; and
            
20

    
    .    extends the time period when a county jail may make a claim against a current
            
21

    
inmate for costs incurred during a prior incarceration from three years to 10 years.
            
22

23

    
    None
            
24

    
 Other Special Clauses:
