 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions related to a county constable and deputy constable.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    prohibits a county from contracting with a constable for a period that exceeds four
            
13

    
years;
            
14

    
    .    requires a constable to present certain identification;
            
15

    
    .    requires a constable or deputy constable to notify the agency of jurisdiction before
            
16

    
serving a bench warrant;

    .    limits a constable's appointed term to four years;
            
18

    
    .    prohibits a constable from operating in a county unless the constable registers with
            
19

    
the county; and

    .    makes technical corrections.
