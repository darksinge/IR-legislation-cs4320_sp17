 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies Title 63G, Chapter 7, Governmental Immunity Act of Utah.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides that governmental immunity is not waived for injury that arises out of, in
            
14

    
connection with, or results from the activity of a wild animal; and
            
15

    
    .    makes technical changes.
