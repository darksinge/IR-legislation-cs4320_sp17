 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Insurance Code to address continuing education requirements.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    provides different continuing education requirements for title insurance producers
            
13

    
who have been licensed 20 or more consecutive years;
            
14

    
    .    provides that an attorney meeting requirements for the Utah State Bar meets the
            
15

    
continuing education requirements for certain title insurance licenses;
            
16

    
    .    requires continuing education documentation to be retained for two years; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
18

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:

    None

 Other Special Clauses:
