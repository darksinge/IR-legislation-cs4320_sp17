H.B. 266









1     MOTOR VEHICLE INSURANCE PREMIUMS




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor:  Nora B. Stephens


5    AN ACT RELATING TO INSURANCE CODE; PROHIBITING CERTAIN INSURANCE

6    RATE INCREASES FOR VEHICLES UNDER CERTAIN CIRCUMSTANCES.

7    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

8    ENACTS:

9         31A-19-211, Utah Code Annotated 1953

10    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

11        Section 1.  Section  31A-19-211 is enacted to read:

12         31A-19-211.  Premium increases prohibited when not liable.

13        (1)  Each rate, rating schedule, and rating manual filed with the commissioner for any

14    insurance covering a vehicle or the operation of a vehicle may not permit a premium increase due

15    to a claim resulting from an incident in which the person named in the policy or any other person

16    using the insured motor vehicle with the express or implied permission of the named insured is not

17    at fault.

18        (2)  This section is an exception to the provisions of Section 31A-19-201.
