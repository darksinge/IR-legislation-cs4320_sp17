 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions of Title 76, Chapter 10, Part 5, Weapons, related to conduct
            
10

    
involving the carrying of a concealed dangerous weapon.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides an exemption for a person, who is 21 years of age or older and who may
            
14

    
lawfully possess a dangerous weapon, from certain criminal provisions related to
            
15

    
the carrying of a concealed dangerous weapon.
