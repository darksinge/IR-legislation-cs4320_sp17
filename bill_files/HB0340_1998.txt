H.B. 340





1     REVISING TERM LIMITS




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor: John B. Arrington


5    AN ACT RELATING TO TERM LIMITS; REPEALING UTAH'S TERM LIMITS LAW.

6    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

7    REPEALS:

8         20A-10-101, as enacted by Chapter 264, Laws of Utah 1994

9         20A-10-102, as enacted by Chapter 264, Laws of Utah 1994

10         20A-10-201, as enacted by Chapter 264, Laws of Utah 1994

11         20A-10-301, as enacted by Chapter 264, Laws of Utah 1994

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.  Repealer.

14        This act repeals:

15        Section  20A-10-101,  Title.

16        Section  20A-10-102,  Definitions.

17        Section  20A-10-201,  Term limits -- State officers.

18        Section  20A-10-301,  Term limits -- Federal officers.




 Legislative Review Note
    as of   1-27-98  11:43 AM


A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.

 Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel
