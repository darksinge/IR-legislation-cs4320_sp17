COURT SECURITY RESTRICTED ACCOUNT                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Eric K. Hutchings                                Senate Sponsor             Jon J. Greiner                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Court Security Account.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    increases the security surcharge in courts of record for criminal convictions and                           juvenile delinquency judgments                                .    increases the security surcharge on all convictions for moving violations and                           allocates the increase to the Court Security Account created in Section       A                                      and                               .    allows the Administrative Office of the Courts to use the security surcharge for                           perimeter security at all court locations. In addition to any fine, penalty, forfeiture, or other surcharge, a security surcharge                           of           shall be assessed in all courts of record on all criminal convictions and juvenile                           delinquency judgments.
