 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions in the Minimum School Program Act and the Property Tax
            
12

    
Act relating to certain property tax levies and the funding of public school programs.

 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    repeals the authority of school districts to levy certain property taxes;
            
16

    
    .    increases the statewide minimum basic tax rate;
            
17

    
    .    creates a local school district discretionary levy;
            
18

    
    .    sets the tax rate for the local school district discretionary levy for the first taxable
            
19

    
year;
            
20

    
    .    provides procedures for setting the tax rate for the local school discretionary levy
            
21

    
after the first taxable year;
            
22

    
    .    adjusts a school district's certified tax rate due to the repeal or amendment of the
            
23

    
property taxing authority of the school district;
            
24

    
    .    eliminates the capital outlay foundation program;
            
25

    
    .    amends the provisions relating to the requirement that a school district in a county
            
26

    
of the first class levy a property tax of at least .0006 per dollar of taxable value;
            
27

    
    .    amends the provisions relating to the requirement that a school district in a divided

            
28


    
school district levy a property tax of at least .0006 per dollar of taxable value;
            
29

    
    .    defines terms; and
            
30

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
31
