Section    A       is amended to read                  A      .  Compensation for services    Additional per diem    Insurance              Certified statements of expenses.                   Each member of the State Board of Education shall receive    ,       ,    per year,          payable monthly, as compensation for services, in addition to necessary travel expenses.                   Each member may receive additional per diem compensation as established by the          director of the Division of Finance for attendance at meetings or activities related to any business          of the board, not to exceed    per year.
