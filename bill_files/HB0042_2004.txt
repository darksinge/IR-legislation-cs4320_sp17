 INTEREST RATES ON JUDGMENTS

 LONG TITLE
            
7

    
 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies provisions of the Judicial Code related to interest on judgments.
            
9

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
10

    
    This bill:
            
11

    
    .    changes the interest rate added to special damages actually incurred in personal
            
12

    
injury and tort actions from 10% to the federal postjudgment interest rate plus 2%;
            
13

    
and
            
14

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
15

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
16

    
    None
            
17

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
18

    
    None

 Utah Code Sections Affected:

AMENDS:

     78-27-44, as last amended by Chapter 123, Laws of Utah 1991
            
22

    

            
23

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
24

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
78-27-44

 is amended to read:
            
25

    
     78-27-44.  Personal injury judgments -- Interest authorized.
            
26

    
    (1)  In all actions brought to recover damages for personal injuries sustained by any
            
27

    
person, resulting from or occasioned by the tort of any other person, corporation, association,


            
28


    
or partnership, whether by negligence or willful intent of that other person, corporation,
            
29

    
association, or partnership, and whether that injury shall have resulted fatally or otherwise, the
            
30

    
plaintiff in the complaint  may claim interest on the special damages actually incurred from the
            
31

    
date of the occurrence of the act giving rise to the cause of action.
            
32

    
    (2) (a)  It is the duty of the court, in entering judgment for plaintiff in [that] an action
            
33

    
described in Subsection (1), to add to the amount of special damages actually incurred that are
            
34

    
assessed by the verdict of the jury, or found by the court, interest on that amount calculated [at
            
35

    
the legal rate, as defined] in [Section 

15-1-1

] accordance with Subsection (2)(b), from the date
            
36

    
of the occurrence of the act giving rise to the cause of action to the date of entering the
            
37

    
judgment, and to include it in that judgment.
            
38

    
    (b)  For purposes of Subsection (2)(a), interest shall be calculated at the federal
            
39

    
postjudgment interest rate as defined in Section 

15-1-4

, as of January 1 of each year, plus 2%.
            
40

41
