    APPROPRIATION FOR HILL AIR FORCE BASE HERITAGE


     MUSEUM AND FORT DOUGLAS MILITARY MUSEUM




    1997 GENERAL SESSION


    STATE OF UTAH


     Sponsor:  Robert F. Montgomery


    AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $200,000 TO RESTORE A

    HISTORIC WORLD WAR II B-24 BOMBER AIRCRAFT TO MUSEUM QUALITY;

    ALLOCATING A PORTION OF THE APPROPRIATION TO THE FORT DOUGLAS

    MILITARY MUSEUM; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

    This act enacts uncodified material.

    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

        Section 1.   Appropriation.

        (1)  There is appropriated $200,000 from the General Fund to the Department of

    Community and Economic Development for fiscal year 1997-98 to restore a World War II B-24

    bomber aircraft to museum quality.

        (2)  Fifteen percent of this appropriation is allocated towards the renovation and expansion

    of displays at the Fort Douglas Military Museum.

        (3)  These funds are nonlapsing.

        Section 2.   Effective date.

        This act takes effect on July 1, 1997.
