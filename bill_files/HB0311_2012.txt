    This bill amends provisions in Title 52, Chapter 4, Open and Public Meetings Act,
            
10

    
related to electronic meetings held by charter school boards.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    defines terms;

    .    authorizes a charter school board to conduct and convene an electronic meeting in
            
15
