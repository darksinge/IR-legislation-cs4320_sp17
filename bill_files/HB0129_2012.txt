 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill clarifies the statute regarding civil liability for defending against criminal
            
10

    
activity.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    clarifies that a person is justified in defending their home and family against
            
14

    
criminal activity and may not be liable for civil damages for injury or damage
            
15

    
occurring while doing so.
