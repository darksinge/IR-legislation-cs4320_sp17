Section         is amended to read                      .  Definitions.              As used in this chapter                     Abuse  means attempting to cause, or intentionally or knowingly causing to an adult          or minor physical harm or intentionally placing another in fear of imminent physical harm.                    Cohabitant  means an emancipated person pursuant to Section        or a person who          is    years of age or older who                a   is or was a spouse of the other party                b   is or was living as if a spouse of the other party                c   is related by blood or marriage to the other party                d   has one or more children in common with the other party  or               e   resides or has resided in the same residence as the other party.                    Notwithstanding Subsection    ,  cohabitant  does not include the relationship of         natural parent, adoptive parent, or step parent to a minor.                   Court clerk  means a district court clerk or juvenile court clerk.                   Department  means the Department of Human Services.                   Domestic violence  means the same as that term is defined in Section        .                   Ex parte protective order  means an order issued without notice to the defendant in         accordance with this chapter.                   Foreign protective order  means a protective order issued by another state, territory,         or possession of the United States, tribal lands of the United States, the Commonwealth of Puerto          Rico, or the District of Columbia shall be given full faith and credit in Utah, if the protective order          is similar to a protective order issued in compliance with Title   , Chapter  , Cohabitant Abuse          Act, or Title   , Chapter   , Cohabitant Abuse Procedures Act, and includes the following          requirements                a   the requirements of due process were met by the issuing court, including subject matter          and personal jurisdiction                b   the respondent received reasonable notice  and               c   the respondent had an opportunity for a hearing regarding the protective order.                    Law enforcement unit  or  law enforcement agency  means any public agency having          general police power and charged with making arrests in connection with enforcement of the          criminal statutes and ordinances of this state or any political subdivision.                     Orders for protection  includes protective orders, ex parte protective orders, and          foreign protective orders.                            Peace officer  means those persons specified in Section     a  .                            Protective order  means a restraining order issued pursuant to this chapter          subsequent to a hearing on the petition, of which the petitioner has given notice in accordance with          this chapter.
