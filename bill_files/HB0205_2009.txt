WATER SOURCE PROTECTION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Michael E. Noel                                Senate Sponsor             Stephen H. Urquhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends a section relating to a water source protection ordinance.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    limits the requirement to adopt a water source protection ordinance to counties of                           the first and second class  and                               .    limits the authorization of a municipality to adopt a water source protection                           ordinance to municipalities located within a county of the first or second class.
