Brown                           AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  CREATING A SCHOOL LAND TRUST                          PROGRAM FOR EACH PUBLIC SCHOOL  PROVIDING THAT THE INTEREST ON THE                          STATE SCHOOL FUND SHALL BE USED TO FUND THE PROGRAM  PROVIDING THAT                          THE FUNDS SHALL GO TO EACH PUBLIC SCHOOL TO ENHANCE ACADEMIC                           PERFORMANCE AND IMPROVE EDUCATIONAL EXCELLENCE  PROVIDING A                           DISTRIBUTION FORMULA  REQUIRING SCHOOL COMMITTEES AND SCHOOL PLANS                           FOR INCREASED OR ENHANCED ACADEMIC PERFORMANCE  REQUIRING                           ACCOUNTABILITY AND REPORTING TO LOCAL SCHOOL BOARDS  AND PROVIDING                           AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
