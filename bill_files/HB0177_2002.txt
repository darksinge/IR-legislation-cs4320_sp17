ANNUAL REPORT ON STATE SCHOOL FUND                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   James A. Ferrin                             This act modifies the State Money Management Act by requiring the state treasurer to                          prepare an annual report on the State School Fund.  This act takes effect on July  ,     .                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           ENACTS                                     . , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section          .      is enacted to read                                      . .  State School Fund report.                                    The state treasurer shall prepare a report at the end of each fiscal year on the State                          School Fund created in Utah Constitution Article X, Section  .                                    The report shall include the following information                                 a   the name of each asset within the fund and the fair market value of each asset as of                          June                                    b   the amount and date of each contribution to the fund during the fiscal year                                 c   a balance sheet for the most recently completed fiscal year and the previous fiscal year                                 d   the fund s internal rate of return for the previous year, three years, five years, and ten                          years                                 e   a summary of the asset allocation policy for the fund                                 f   a description of the fund income, including amounts distributed and amounts retained                           and                                g   expenses in dollars and as a percent of fund assets.                                    The state treasurer shall submit the report to the Education Interim Committee by                          October   of each year.                                  Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
