Bourdeaux                                Senate Sponsor             Karen Hale                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires the school performance report to contain disaggregated totals for                           advanced placement and concurrent enrollment data and requires the State Board of                           Education and school districts to promote accelerated learning programs.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the school performance report                                .    requires advanced placement and concurrent enrollment data to be disaggregated                           with respect to race, ethnicity, gender, limited English proficiency, and those                           students who qualify for free or reduced price school lunch                                 .    requires the State Board of Education and school districts  to promote accelerated                           learning programs  and                               .    makes technical corrections. Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                                   A       .  Promotion of accelerated learning programs.                                     As used in this section,  accelerated learning programs  includes                                  a   programs for the gifted and talented                                  b   concurrent enrollment  and                                 c   advanced placement.                                        The State Board of Education and school districts shall                                  a   generally promote accelerated learning programs to students, parents, and                           guardians                                  b   provide information showing the benefits of accelerated learning programs,                           including cost comparisons with comparable higher education courses                                  c   provide specific notice of accelerated learning program requirements, including                           registration deadlines and program prerequisites to students, parents, and guardians  and                                 d   encourage participation in accelerated learning programs by at risk and other                           classes of students who have shown low participation in the programs.
