 General Description:

    This bill amends the Lobbyist Disclosure and Regulation Act to amend definitions.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    amends the definition of "approved meeting or activity" to include certain meetings
            
14

    
or activities approved by the governor or lieutenant governor for a public official
            
15

    
who holds a position in the executive branch;

    .    amends the definition of "expenditure" to include admission to or attendance at
            
17

    
certain events or travel to certain events; and
            
18

    
    .    makes technical and conforming changes.
