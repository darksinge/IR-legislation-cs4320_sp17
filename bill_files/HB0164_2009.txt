MIGRATORY BIRD PRODUCTION AREAS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Curtis Oda                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts provisions relating to a migratory bird production area.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    authorizes the creation of a migratory bird production area                                .    authorizes a landowner to add or remove land from a migratory bird production                           area                                .    requires a municipality to have the written permission of all landowners within a                           migratory bird production area to annex land within the production area                                .    clarifies the effect the creation of a migratory bird production area would have on                           receiving the benefits of the Farmland Assessment Act                                .    prohibits a county from enacting certain ordinances regarding a migratory bird                           production area                                .    requires a county to exclude activities associated with a migratory bird production                           area from being a public nuisance                                .    establishes a defense for a migratory bird production area in civil or criminal                           nuisance actions  and                               .    prohibits the use of eminent domain on land within a migratory bird production                           area. Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                             CHAPTER   .  MIGRATORY BIRD PRODUCTION AREA                             Part  .  General Provisions                                          .  Title.                                This chapter is known as the  Migratory Bird Production Area.                                 Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Definitions.                                As used in this chapter,  migratory bird  is as defined in    U.S.C. Sec.    j.                                Section  . Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Adding or removing property from a migratory bird production area.                                     A landowner may file a revision notice with the county recorder of the county in                           which the migratory bird production area is located to                                  a   remove land from a migratory bird production area, even if removal of the land                           would result in a migratory bird production area of less than     contiguous acres  or                                 b   add land to an existing migratory bird production area if the land is contiguous with                           the migratory bird production area.                                     The revision notice shall contain                                  a   a legal description of the land to be added or removed from the migratory bird                           production area                                  b   the name of the owner or owners of the land to be added or removed from the                           migratory bird production area  and                                 c   an affidavit signed by the owner or owners                                     i   indicating that the land is removed from the migratory bird production area  or                                 ii   indicating that the land                                  A   is added to the migratory bird production area  and                                 B   meets the description provided in Subsection                      b  iii .                                     A person who files a revision notice under this section shall give a copy of the                           revision notice within ten days of its filing to the legislative body of the county in which the                           migratory bird production area is located.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                             Part  .  Protections                                          .  Farmland Assessment Act.                                     Creation of a migratory bird production area does not impair the ability of land                           within the migratory bird production area to qualify for the benefits of Title   , Chapter  , Part                            , Farmland Assessment Act.                                     The eligibility of land for the benefits of Title   , Chapter  , Part  , Farmland                           Assessment Act, is determined exclusively by the provisions of that act, notwithstanding the                           land s location within a migratory bird production area.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Limitations on local regulations.                                     A county within which a migratory bird production area is located shall                                  a   encourage the continuity, development, and viability of the land within the                           migratory bird production area for the benefit of migratory bird habitat and hunting  and                                 b   allow the following structures or improvements as permitted uses under the                           county s zoning law, ordinance, or regulation                                  i   a structure used for                                  A   lodging                                  B   maintenance                                  C   management                                  D   administration  or                                 E   storage  and                                 ii   a structure actually or customarily used in conjunction with an activity for which                           the land within the migratory bird production area is used.                                        A county within which a migratory bird production area is located may not                                  a   enact a law, ordinance, or regulation that unreasonably restricts an activity carried                           out in the normal course of management or operation of land within the migratory bird                           production area for migratory bird habitat or hunting                                  b   change the zoning designation of or a zoning regulation applying to land within a                           migratory bird production area unless the county receives written approval for the change from                           all the landowners within the migratory bird production area  or                                 c   enact a law, ordinance, or regulation concerning the use, operation, or discharge of                           a firearm that is more restrictive than state law, notwithstanding Subsection                    b .                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Nuisances.                                     a   A county shall exclude the activities described in Subsection     b  from the                           definition of public nuisance in a county law or ordinance regulating a public nuisance.                                 b   An activity or occurrence normally associated with the management and operation                           of land within a migratory bird production area for migratory bird hunting or migratory bird                           habitat is not a nuisance, including                                  i   hunting                                  ii   discharging a firearm                                  iii   improving habitat                                  iv   trapping                                  v   eradicating weeds                                  vi   discing                                  vii   planting                                  viii   impounding water                                  ix   raising a bird or other domestic animal                                  x   grazing                                  xi   an activity conducted in the normal course of an agricultural operation as defined                           in Section       B             and                                 xii   an odor.                                     In a civil action for nuisance or a criminal action for public nuisance under Section                                           , it is a complete defense if the action involves migratory bird hunting or activities                              used for the management or operation of the land within a migratory bird production area if                           those activities were                                  a   conducted within a migratory bird production area  and                                 b   not in violation of any federal or state law.                                     An owner of a new development located in whole or in part within  ,    feet of a                           migratory bird production area shall provide the following notice on any plat filed with the                           county recorder                                  Migratory Bird Production Area                                This property is located in the vicinity of an established migratory bird production area                           in which hunting and activities related to the management and operation of land for the benefit                           of migratory birds have been afforded the highest priority use status.  It can be anticipated that                           these uses and activities may now or in the future be conducted on land within the migratory                           bird production area.  The use and enjoyment of this property is expressly conditioned on                           acceptance of any annoyance or inconvenience that may result from migratory bird hunting,                           migratory bird habitat management, and related activities.                                 Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Eminent domain restrictions.                                Notwithstanding Section       B          , the state, a state agency, or political subdivision                           may not exercise eminent domain power to condemn any real property within a migratory bird                           production area for any purpose.                                Section   .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Annexation restrictions.                                A municipality may annex real property within a migratory bird production area as                           provided by Title   , Chapter  , Part  , Annexation.
