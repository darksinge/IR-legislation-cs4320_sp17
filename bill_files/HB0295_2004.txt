 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions related to the application for and issuance of a concealed
            
10

    
firearm permit.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    modifies the type and amount of information an applicant is required to provide in
            
14

    
order to receive a permit to carry a concealed firearm; and
            
15

    
    .    makes certain technical changes.
            
16

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
17

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None

[: (a)  for two years; or (b)] for five years [for permits issued or renewed on or

    (a)  has not been convicted of a felony;

    (b)  has not been convicted of [any] a crime of violence;

    (c)  has not been convicted of [any offenses] an offense involving the use of alcohol;

    (d)  has not been convicted of [any] an offense involving the unlawful use of narcotics

Section

 may

in:

 or federal law; or

Subsection (16).
