7    entity.

9        (a)  holding the position of trustee, director, officer, or other similar position with a

10    business entity; or

18         63-95-103.  Benefits to interested parties of a quasi-governmental entity.

19        (1)  If a quasi-governmental entity is privatized, the following may not receive any benefit
