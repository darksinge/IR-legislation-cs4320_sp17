REVENUE BOND, CAPITAL FACILITY, AND                           PROPERTY ACQUISITION AUTHORIZATIONS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Kevin S. Garn                                Senate Sponsor             Scott K. Jenkins                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill authorizes certain state agencies and higher education institutions to issue                           revenue bonds, build capital facilities using agency or institutional funds, transfer                           capital facilities monies between funds, or acquire or exchange property.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    authorizes the issuance of revenue bonds by the State Building Ownership Authority                           and the State Board of Regents                                .    authorizes other capital facility construction to be funded from agency or                           institutional funds                                .    authorizes the transfer of certain higher education capital facilities monies from one                           fund to another  and                               .    authorizes the acquisition or exchange of certain state property.
