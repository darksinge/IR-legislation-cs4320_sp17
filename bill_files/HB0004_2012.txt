12

    .    provides appropriations for the use and support of higher education agencies and
            
16

    
institutions;

    .    provides appropriations for the use and support of the Utah Education Network;
            
18

    
    .    provides appropriations for the use and support of the Utah Medical Education Council;
            
19

    
    .    provides appropriations for other purposes as described.

    .    $252,167,500 from the Education Fund;
