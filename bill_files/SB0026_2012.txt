 General Description:

10

    
disposition of a portion of realigned highways.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    provides that when a state highway is realigned, the department may dispose of the
            
14

    
former portion of the highway by complying with certain disposal of real property
            
15

    
provisions if the former highway was purchased in fee; and
