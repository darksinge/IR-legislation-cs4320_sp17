 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends provisions relating to filling a midterm vacancy in an elected office in
            
10

    
a municipality or on a local school board.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    requires a municipality and local school board to interview potential appointees in a
            
14

    
public meeting;
            
15

    
    .    prohibits the discussion of filling a midterm vacancy in a closed meeting; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
17

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
18

    
    None
            
19

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
20

    
    None
