 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Off-Highway Vehicles Code by amending provisions relating to
            
11

    
off-highway vehicle equipment requirements.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    provides that a safety flag may be attached to the protective headgear of a person
            
15

    
operating a motorcycle rather than attaching the safety flag to the motorcycle as
            
16

    
required when operating on certain sand dunes.
            
17

 Other Special Clauses:

Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
26

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
41-22-10.7

 is amended to read:
            
27

    
     41-22-10.7.  Vehicle equipment requirements -- Rulemaking -- Exceptions.
            
28

    
    (1)  Except as provided under Subsection (3), an off-highway vehicle shall be equipped
            
29

    
with:

            
30


    
    (a)  brakes adequate to control the movement of and to stop and hold the vehicle under
            
31

    
normal operating conditions;
            
32

    
    (b)  headlights and taillights when operated between sunset and sunrise;
            
33

    
    (c)  a noise control device and except for a snowmobile, a spark arrestor device; and
            
34

    
    (d)  when operated on sand dunes designated by the board, a safety flag[,] that is:
            
35

    
    (i)  red or orange in color [and];
            
36

    
    (ii)  a minimum of six by 12 inches[,]; and
            
37

    
    (iii)  attached to:
            
38

    
    (A)  the off-highway vehicle so that the safety flag is at least eight feet above the
            
39

    
surface of level ground[, when operated on sand dunes designated by the board]; or
            
40

    
    (B)  the protective headgear of a person operating a motorcycle so that the safety flag is
            
41

    
at least 18 inches above the top of the person's head.
            
42

    
    (2)  In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act,
            
43

44

    
dunes where safety flags are required under Subsection (1).
            
45

    
    (3)  An off-highway implement of husbandry used only in agricultural operations and
            
46

    
not operated on a highway, is exempt from the provisions of this section.
