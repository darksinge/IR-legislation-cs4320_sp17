 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Water and Irrigation code.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    modifies the definition of "water conveyance facility";
            
13

    
    .    requires the state engineer, by July 1, 2017, to inventory and maintain a list of all
            
14

    
open, human-made water conveyance systems in the state;

    .    requires the state engineer to contract with the Utah Association of Conservation
            
16

    
Districts to provide technical support for a canal owner who is adopting a
            
17

    
management plan; and

    .    makes technical changes.

    None

.

under Section
