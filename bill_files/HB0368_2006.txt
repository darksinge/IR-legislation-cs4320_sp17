Killpack                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions related to Labor in General to enact the Commission                           Payment Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    enacts the Commission Payment Act                                .    defines terms                                .    addresses jurisdiction                                .    addresses void agreements                                .    requires written agreements                                .    provides for payment requirements including upon termination and settlement                                .    addresses revocable commission offers  and                               .    addresses remedies. Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   . COMMISSION PAYMENT ACT                            Part  .  General Provisions                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Commission Payment Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Business relationship  means any agreement entered into or renewed on or after                          May  ,     , that governs the relationship of principal and sales representative.                                     Commission  means                                 a   compensation                                 i   that accrues to a sales representative                                 ii   for payment by a principal  and                                iii   at a rate expressed as a percentage of the dollar amount of sales, orders, or profits                           or                                b   any other method of compensation agreed to between a sales representative and a                          principal including                                 i   fees for services  and                                ii   a retainer.                                     Principal  means a person who                                    a   engages in any of the following activities with regard to a product or service                                 i   manufactures                                 ii   produces                                 iii   imports                                 iv   sells  or                                v   distributes                                 b   establishes a business relationship with a sales representative to solicit orders for a                          product or a service described in Subsection     a   and                                c   agrees to compensate a sales representative, in whole or in part, by commission.                                    a   Except as provided in Subsection     b ,  sales representative  means a person                          who enters into a business relationship with a principal                                 i   to solicit orders for a product or a service  and                                ii   under which the person is compensated, in whole or in part, by commission.                                b    Sales representative  does not include an employee of a principal.                                     Terminates  or  termination  means the end of a business relationship between a                          sales representative and a principal, whether by                                 a   agreement                                 b   expiration of a time period  or                                c   exercise of a right of termination by either the principal or the sales representative.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Jurisdiction in courts.                               An action under this chapter may be brought against a principal in a court of this state if                          the principal                                     enters into a business relationship in this state with a sales representative to solicit                          orders for a product or a service  or                                    a   a product of the principal is                                 i   manufactured, distributed, sold, or received in this state  or                                ii   imported to or from this state  or                                b   a service of the principal is provided in this state.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Void provisions.                                  Any of the following provisions in an agreement between a sales representative and a                          principal is void                                     an express waiver of any right under this chapter                                     for a writing required by Section                   that is entered into in this state, a                          provision that makes the sales representative subject to the laws of another state  or                                    a requirement that the sales representative pursue a claim under this chapter in a                          court not located in the state.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            Part  .  Requirements and Prohibitions                                         .  Written business relationship.                                     The business relationship between a sales representative and a principal shall be in                           a writing signed by both the principal and the sales representative.                                     The writing required by Subsection     shall set forth the method by which the sales                           representative s commission is                                  a   computed  and                                 b   paid.                                     The principal shall provide the sales representative with a copy of the signed                           writing required by Subsection    .                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Payment of commission    Payment on termination    Settlement.                                     The principal shall pay a sales representative all commissions due to the sales                           representative during the time the business relationship between the principal and sales                           representative is in effect in accordance with the writing required by Section                  .                                     If a business relationship between a principal and sales representative terminates,                           the principal shall pay to the sales representative                                  a   within    days after the effective date of the termination, all commissions due at the                           effective date of the termination  and                                 b   within    days after the day on which a commission becomes due if the                           commission is due after the effective date of the termination.                                     a   Unless payment is made pursuant to a binding and final written settlement                           agreement and release, the acceptance by a sales representative of a partial commission                              payment from the principal does not constitute a release as to the balance of any commission                           claimed due.                                 b   A full release of all commission claims required by a principal as a condition to a                           partial commission payment is null and void.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Revocable offer of commission.                                If a principal makes a revocable offer of a commission to a sales representative, the                           sales representative is entitled to the commission agreed upon if                                      the principal revokes the offer of commission                                      the sales representative establishes that the revocation is for a purpose of avoiding                           payment of the commission                                      the revocation occurs after the principal obtains an order for the principal s product                           or service through the efforts of the sales representative  and                                     the principal s product or service that is the subject of the order is provided to and                           paid for by a customer.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                             Part  .  Remedies                                          .  Failure to pay commission.                                     A sales representative may bring a civil action in a court of competent jurisdiction                           against a principal for failure by the principal to comply with                                  a   any provision of an agreement relating to the payment of commission  or                                 b   Subsection                      or    .                                     If a principal is found liable under Subsection    , the principal is liable to the sales                           representative for                                  a    three times the amount calculated by                                  i   determining the sum of unpaid commission owed to the sales representative  and                                 ii   subtracting from the amount determined under Subsection     a  i  monies the sales                           representative owes the principal                                  b   reasonable attorney s fees  and                                 c   court costs.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                             .  Other remedies.                                This chapter does not                                      invalidate or restrict any alternative or additional right or remedy available to a                           sales representative  or                                     preclude a sales representative from seeking to recover in an action on all claims                           against a principal.
