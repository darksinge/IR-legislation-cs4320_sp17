MANUAL ON UNIFORM TRAFFIC CONTROL                           DEVICES FOR STREETS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Kory M. Holdaway                             This act modifies the Motor Vehicle Code to require the Department of Transportation to                          adopt the most recent edition of the  Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices for Streets                          and Highways.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                       , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Manual and specifications for uniform system of traffic control devices and                           school crossing guards.                                    a   In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act,                           the Department of Transportation shall make rules consistent with this chapter adopting a manual                           and establishing specifications for a uniform system of traffic control devices for use upon                           highways within this state.                                b   The manual shall correlate with, and where possible conform to, the system set forth                           in the        most recent edition of the  Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices for Streets                           and Highways  and other standards issued or endorsed by the federal highway administrator.                                    In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                           Department of Transportation shall make rules adopting a manual and establishing specifications                           for a uniform system of traffic control devices and school crossing guards for school crossing                           zones, which shall supplement the manual adopted under Subsection    .        Legislative Review Note      as of                AM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
