 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Utah Code of Criminal Procedure and the Utah Criminal Code
            
11

    
regarding sexual offenses.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    provides that when any employee of or volunteer at a public or private elementary
            
15

    
or secondary school commits or attempts to commit against an enrolled student of
            
16

    
the school any sexual offense that is not currently a first degree felony or a second
            
17

    
degree felony, the offense is a second degree felony;

    .    imposes the same second degree felony penalty if the offense against a student
            
19

    
occurs within five years after the offender was most recently employed by or had
            
20

    
volunteered at a school; and
            
21

    
    .    includes these convicted persons on the sex offender registry.
