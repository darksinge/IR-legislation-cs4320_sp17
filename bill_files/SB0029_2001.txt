S.B. 29 Enrolled





            

    
 FAMILIES, AGENCIES, AND COMMUNITIES TOGETHER

            

    
 FOR CHILDREN AND YOUTH AT RISK AMENDMENTS



            

    
2001 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    


 Sponsor:  Ron  Allen



            

    
David H. Steele
            

    
Howard A. Stephenson
Dan R. Eastman
Karen  Hale 


            

    
This act modifies the Families, Agencies, and Communities Together for Children and Youth
            

    
 at Risk Act by changing the sunset date from July 1, 2001, to July 1, 2006.
            

    
This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:
            

    
AMENDS:
            

    
     63-55-263, as last amended by Chapters 1, 78, and 281, Laws of Utah 2000
            

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
63-55-263

 is amended to read:
            

    
     63-55-263.  Repeal dates, Titles 63, 63A, and 63C.
            

    
    (1) (a)  Title 63, Chapter 25a, Part 1, Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice, is repealed
            

    
July 1, 2002.
            

    
    (b)  Title 63, Chapter 25a, Part 3, Sentencing Commission, is repealed January 1, 2002.
            

    
    (2)  The Crime Victims' Reparations Board, created in Section 

63-25a-404

, is repealed July 1,
            

    
2007.
            

    
    (3)  The Resource Development Coordinating Committee, created in Section 

63-28a-2

, is
            

    
repealed July 1, 2004.
            

    
    (4)  Title 63, Chapter 38c, State Appropriations and Tax Limitation Act, is repealed July 1,
            

    
2005.
            

    
    (5)  Title 63, Chapter 75, Families, Agencies, and Communities Together for Children and Youth
            

    
At Risk Act, is repealed July 1, [2001] 2006.
            

    
    (6)  Title 63, Chapter 88, Navajo Trust Fund, is repealed July 1, 2005.
            

    
    (7)  Sections 

63A-4-204

 and 

63A-4-205

, authorizing the Risk Management Fund to provide

            

    
coverage to nonstate entities, are repealed July 1, 2001.
            

    
    (8)  Title 63A, Chapter 7, Utah Sports Authority Act, is repealed July 1, 2003.
            

    
    (9)  Title 63A, Chapter 10, State Olympic Coordination Act, is repealed July 1, 2003.
