Johnny  Anderson

 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Uniform Driver License Act by amending provisions relating to
            
10

    
the ignition interlock program.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides that the Driver License Division may only clear the suspension for an
            
14

    
ignition interlock violation if the division electronically verifies that the person does
            
15

    
not have a vehicle registered in the person's name in the state of Utah, rather than a
            
16

    
vehicle registered in any state; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
18

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
19

    
    None
            
20

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
21

    
    None
            
22

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
23

    
AMENDS:
            
24

    
     53-3-1007, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2011, Chapter 421
            
25

    

            
26

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
27

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
53-3-1007

 is amended to read:
            
28

    
     53-3-1007.  Ignition interlock system provider -- Notification to the division upon
            
29

    
 installation or removal of an ignition interlock system -- License suspension or revocation

            
30


    
 for failure to install or remove.
            
31

    
    (1)  An ignition interlock system provider who installs an ignition interlock system on a
            
32

    
person's vehicle shall:
            
33

    
    (a)  provide proof of installation to the person; and
            
34

    
    (b)  electronically notify the division of installation of an ignition interlock system on
            
35

    
the person's vehicle.
            
36

    
    (2)  [A] An ignition interlock system provider shall electronically notify the division if a
            
37

    
person has removed an ignition interlock system from the person's vehicle.
            
38

    
    (3)  If an individual is an interlock restricted driver, the division shall:
            
39

    
    (a)  suspend the person's driving privilege for the duration of the restriction period as
            
40

    
defined in Section 

41-6a-518.2

;
            
41

    
    (b)  notify the person of the suspension period in place and the requirements for
            
42

    
reinstatement of the driving privilege with respect to the ignition interlock restriction
            
43

    
suspension; and
            
44

    
    (c)  clear the suspension upon:
            
45

    
    (i)  receipt of payment of the fee or fees specified in Section 

53-3-105

; and
            
46

    
    (ii) (A)  receipt of electronic notification from an ignition interlock system provider
            
47

    
showing proof of the installation of an ignition interlock system on the person's vehicle; or
            
48

    
    (B)  electronically verifying that the person does not have a vehicle registered in the
            
49

    
person's name in the state of Utah.
            
50

    
    (4)  By following the emergency procedures in Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative
            
51

    
Procedures Act, the division shall suspend the license of any person without hearing and
            
52

    
without receiving a record of the person's conviction of crime seven days after receiving
            
53

    
electronic notification from [a] an ignition interlock system provider that a person has removed
            
54

    
an ignition interlock system from the person's vehicle if the person is an interlock restricted
            
55

    
driver until:
            
56

    
    (a)  the division:
            
57

    
    (i)  receives payment of the fee or fees specified in Section 

53-3-105

; and

            
58


    
    (ii) (A)  receives electronic notification from an ignition interlock system provider
            
59

    
showing new proof of the installation of an ignition interlock system; or
            
60

    
    (B)  electronically verifies that the person does not have a vehicle registered in the
            
61

    
person's name in the state of Utah; or
            
62

    
    (b)  the person's interlock restricted period has expired.
            
63

    
    (5)  In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the
            
64

    
division shall make rules establishing:
            
65

    
    (a)  procedures for certification and regulation of ignition interlock system providers;
            
66

    
    (b)  acceptable documentation for proof of the installation of an ignition interlock
            
67

    
device;
            
68

    
    (c)  procedures for an ignition interlock system provider to electronically notify the
            
69

    
division; and
            
70

    
    (d)  policies and procedures for the administration of the ignition interlock system
            
71

    
program created under this section.
