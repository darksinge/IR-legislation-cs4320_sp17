POWERS OF MAYORS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Peter C. Knudson          AN ACT RELATING TO CITIES AND TOWNS  PROVIDING FOR MANAGERS TO BE         APPOINTED BY MAYORS WITH THE ADVICE AND CONSENT OF THE MUNICIPAL         LEGISLATIVE BODY  CLARIFYING THE EMPLOYMENT STATUS OF MANAGERS          AND MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                        , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                            , as enacted by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               REPEALS                        , as enacted by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Powers of mayors in cities of third class and towns.                     The mayor in cities of the third class and towns        shall  be  is the chief executive          officer to whom all employees of the municipality shall report   .                   The mayor shall                a   keep the peace and enforce the laws of the city or town                     shall   b    remit fines and forfeitures  and may release any person imprisoned for          violation of any municipal ordinance                      shall   c    report  such remittance or release  those remittances to the council at its          next regular session                     shall   d    perform all duties prescribed by law, resolution, or ordinance                     shall   e    ensure that all the laws and ordinances and resolutions are faithfully           executed and observed                    may at any reasonable time examine and inspect the books, papers, records or         documents of the city or town or of any officer, employee or agent of the city or town                     shall   f    report to the council the condition of the city or town and recommend for         council consideration any measures  as deemed  that the mayor considers to be in the best interests         of the city or town                    shall,   g    when necessary, call on the residents of the city or town over the age of            years to assist in enforcing the laws of the state and ordinances of the municipality  and                    shall   h    appoint, with the advice and consent of the council, persons to fill          municipal offices or vacancies on commissions or committees of the municipality.  This          subsection shall not apply to managers appointed under Section         .                    a   The mayor may                i   at any reasonable time, examine and inspect the official books, papers, records, or          documents of the city or town or any officer, employee, or agent of the city or town  and               ii   release any person imprisoned for violation of any municipal ordinance.               b   The mayor shall report any releases granted to the council at its next regular session.              Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Appointment of manager.                     a   The  governing  legislative body of any city or town may                i   by ordinance, establish a manager form of government  and               ii   except as provided in Subsection     b , appoint  any  a person to  be known  act as           the  city manager.               b   A manager appointed by the legislative body of a city or town under Subsection              serves at the pleasure of the legislative body.                   a   If a third class city or town has not adopted an optional form of municipal          government, the mayor may appoint a person to act as city manager with the advice and consent          of the municipal legislative body.               b   A manager appointed by the mayor and confirmed by the municipal legislative body          under Subsection     a  serves at the pleasure of the mayor.              Section  .   Repealer.              This act repeals                      Section          ,   Term of office.
