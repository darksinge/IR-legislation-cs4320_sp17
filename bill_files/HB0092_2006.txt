 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends the Utah Medical Practices Act and the Utah Osteopathic Medical
            
11

    
Practice Act to establish limitations on the use of noncompetition clauses in physician
            
12

    
contracts.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    beginning with contracts signed after July 1, 2006, invalidates contracts to the
            
16

    
extent that the contract:
            
17

    
        .    imposes a restriction on the right of a physician to practice medicine within a
            
18

    
particular geographic area, unless the contract is for certain specific
            
19

    
circumstances; or
            
20

    
        .    imposes financial penalties on a physician for terminating a professional
            
21

    
relationship;
            
22

    
    .    creates limited exceptions to the prohibition against financial penalties; and
            
23

    
    .    permits a contract to limit a departing physician's ability to recruit patients from an
            
24

    
existing practice.
            
25
