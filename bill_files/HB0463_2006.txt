WORKERS  COMPENSATION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   J. Stuart Adams                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions related to the Workers  Compensation Fund.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    addresses the legal nature of the Workers  Compensation Fund and its assets  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  A       , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A            is amended to read                                   A       .  Legal nature of Workers  Compensation Fund.                                    The Workers  Compensation Fund is                                 a   a nonprofit, self supporting, quasi public corporation serving a public purpose                             and                                    b   subject to statutes governing the Workers  Compensation Fund that may be enacted                          or modified by the Legislature including                                 i   modifying the structure and management of the Workers  Compensation Fund                                 ii   adjusting the level of the state s influence on the Workers  Compensation Fund  and                                iii   modifying the state s involvement in administering workers  compensation                          insurance  and                                 b    c   a legal entity, that may sue and be sued in its own name.                                    All of the business and affairs of the corporation shall be conducted in the name of                           the Workers  Compensation Fund or if conducted through a subsidiary, such other corporate                           names that comply with state law.                                    The Workers  Compensation Fund assets, including the Injury Fund, are not owned                          by the state, other than as a policyholder.      Legislative Review Note      as of                  AM   Based on a limited legal review, this legislation has not been determined to have a high  probability of being held unconstitutional.
