 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill enacts language related to euthanasia of an animal by an animal shelter.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    authorizes an animal shelter to euthanize an animal by means of carbon monoxide
            
13

    
gas only in certain circumstances;
            
14

    
    .    authorizes an animal shelter to use other certain methods to euthanize an animal;
            
15

    
    .    requires an animal shelter to adopt a euthanasia policy and training program; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical and conforming corrections.
