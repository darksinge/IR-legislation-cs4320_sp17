 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Utah Division of Indian Affairs Act to address meetings with
            
11

    
tribal leaders and agency contacts.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    decreases the number of meetings the division is to coordinate and attend with tribal
            
15

    
leaders;
            
16

    
    .    clarifies references to tribes and tribal governments;
            
17

    
    .    expands the agencies encouraged to participate in the meetings;
            
18

    
    .    modifies the agencies required to provide the division contact information; and
            
19

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
20

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
21

    
    None
            
22

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
23

    
    None
            
24

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
25

    (b)  individuals designated by elected officials of the tribes described in Subsection
            
35

    
(1)(a).

9-9-104.6

, at least [six] five times each year, the division shall

Subsection (2)(b) for the purpose of coordinating the efforts of state and tribal governments in
            
39

    
meeting the needs of the Native Americans residing in the state.

shall be selected as follows:

Ouray Reservation selected by the [Ute] Uintah and Ouray Tribal Business Committee;

elected official selected under Subsection (2)(b)(i) to represent the Indian tribe at a meeting

67

    
held on the same day as a meeting held in accordance with Subsection (2)(a) if:

    (A)  the division does not coordinate the meeting described in this Subsection (2)(c)(ii);
            
69

    
    (B)  no state agency participates in the meeting described in this Subsection (2)(c)(ii);

71

72

    
expenses the representative receives under Subsection (2)(d) for attending a meeting described
            
73

    
in Subsection (2)(a); and

    (D)  the meeting described in this Subsection (2)(c)(ii) is not held:

    (I)  after a meeting described in Subsection (2)(a) begins; and
            
76

    
    (II)  before the meeting described in Subsection (2)(c)(ii)(D)(I) adjourns.
            
77

 and

.
