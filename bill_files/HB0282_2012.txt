 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Criminal Code regarding the offenses of lewdness and sexual
            
10

    
battery.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    revises the Criminal Code so that the offenses of lewdness, sexual battery, and
            
14

    
public urination are each in a separate code section; and
            
15

    
    .    provides that a plea of guilty or no contest that is held in abeyance regarding a
            
16

    
lewdness offense is the equivalent of a conviction.
            
17
