 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions regarding the recording of documents of real estate.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    eliminates the requirement that in order for a full reconveyance of a trust deed and
            
13

    
full release of mortgage to be recorded in the office of any county recorder a
            
14

    
document must contain a legal description of the real property affected; and
            
15

    
    .    requires a full reconveyance of trust deed and full release of mortgage to be
            
16

    
recorded if it contains a reference to the book and page where the description of the
            
17

    
real property is recorded.
            
18

19

    
    None
