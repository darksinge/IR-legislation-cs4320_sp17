 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends provisions governing the automatic withdrawal of an area from certain
            
10

    
local districts.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    requires the legislative body of a newly incorporated municipality to adopt a
            
14

    
resolution no later than 180 days after the effective date of incorporation to approve
            
15

    
the withdrawal of an area from certain local districts.
            
16

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
17

    
    None
            
18

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
19

    
    None
            
20

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
21

    
AMENDS:
            
22

    
     17B-1-502, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2011, Chapter 68
            
23

    

            
24

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
25

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
17B-1-502

 is amended to read:
            
26

    
     17B-1-502.  Withdrawal of area from local district -- Automatic withdrawal in
            
27

    
 certain circumstances -- Definitions.

            
28


    
    (1) (a)  An area within the boundaries of a local district may be withdrawn from the
            
29

    
local district only as provided in this part.
            
30

    
    (b)  Except as provided in Subsections (2) and (3), the inclusion of an area of a local
            
31

    
district within a municipality because of a municipal incorporation under Title 10, Chapter 2,
            
32

    
Part 1, Incorporation, or a municipal annexation or boundary adjustment under Title 10,
            
33

    
Chapter 2, Part 4, Annexation, does not affect the requirements under this part for the process
            
34

    
of withdrawing that area from the local district.
            
35

    
    (2) (a)  An area within the boundaries of a local district is automatically withdrawn
            
36

    
from the local district by the annexation of the area to a municipality or the adding of the area
            
37

    
to a municipality by boundary adjustment under Title 10, Chapter 2, Part 4, Annexation, if:
            
38

    
    (i)  the local district provides:
            
39

    
    (A)  fire protection, paramedic, and emergency services; or
            
40

    
    (B)  law enforcement service;
            
41

    
    (ii)  an election for the creation of the local district was not required because of
            
42

    
Subsection 

17B-1-214

(3)(d); and
            
43

    
    (iii)  before annexation or boundary adjustment, the boundaries of the local district do
            
44

    
not include any of the annexing municipality.
            
45

    
    (b)  The effective date of a withdrawal under this Subsection (2) is governed by
            
46

    
Subsection 

17B-1-512

(2)(b).
            
47

    
    (3) (a)  An area within the boundaries of a local district located in a county of the first
            
48

    
class is automatically withdrawn from the local district by the incorporation of a municipality
            
49

    
whose boundaries include the area if:
            
50

    
    (i)  the local district provides:
            
51

    
    (A)  fire protection, paramedic, and emergency services; or
            
52

    
    (B)  law enforcement service;
            
53

    
    (ii)  an election for the creation of the local district was not required because of
            
54

    
Subsection 

17B-1-214

(3)(d); and
            
55

    
    (iii)  the legislative body of the newly incorporated municipality:
            
56

    
    (A)  adopts a resolution no later than 180 days after the effective date of incorporation
            
57

    
approving the withdrawal that includes the legal description of the area to be withdrawn; and
            
58

    
    (B)  delivers a copy of the resolution to the board of trustees of the local district.

            
59


    
    (b)  The effective date of a withdrawal under this Subsection (3) is governed by
            
60

    
Subsection 

17B-1-512

(2)(a).
