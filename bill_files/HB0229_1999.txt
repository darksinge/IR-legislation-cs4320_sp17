There is created the Olympic Coordination Committee consisting of the following                              members                                  a   five members of the Senate, appointed by the president of the Senate, not more than                           three of whom may be of the same political party  and                                 b   seven members of the House of Representatives, appointed by the speaker of the House                           of Representatives, not more than five of whom may be of the same political party.                                     The cochairs of the Sports Advisory Committee, created pursuant to Section                                A          , shall serve as cochairs of the Olympic Coordination Committee.                                        The committee shall determine committee quorum and voting requirements in                           accordance with Legislative Rules.                                     Salaries and expenses of the members of the committee shall be paid in accordance                           with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                     The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support to                           the committee and shall consult with the Office of Legislative Fiscal Analyst on fiscal issues                           studied by the committee.                                     The committee may review and make recommendations to the Legislature and the                           governor on any issue that relates to                                  a   the state s involvement in hosting the Olympics                                  b   the state s role as a creditor and a secured party in relationship to the Olympics and the                           Organizing Committee                                  c   the Olympics                                  d   the Organizing Committee  or                                 e   the impact of the Olympics on the state after the Olympic games have concluded.
