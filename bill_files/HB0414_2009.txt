Frank                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill provides that litigation expenses shall be awarded in condemnation actions                           where the final award exceeds the written offer by   .                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines  litigation expenses                                 .    provides that litigation expenses shall be awarded to the property owner if the final                           award amount exceeds the written offer by     and                               .    specifies who determines the amount of litigation expenses.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  B      , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                  B      .  Costs and fees.                                       As used in this section,  litigation expenses  means the sum of the costs,                          disbursements, and expenses, including reasonable attorney, appraisal, and engineering fees                          necessary to prepare for or participate in actual or anticipated condemnation proceedings or                          dispute resolution.                                    The property owner shall be awarded reasonable litigation expenses if the                          compensation determined by the court or through dispute resolution exceeds by    or more the                          final written offer tendered by the condemnor before the complaint is filed or dispute resolution                          commenced.                                    Reasonable litigation expenses shall be determined by                                 a   the arbitrator, if the amount of compensation was determined through arbitration  or                                b   the court, if the amount of compensation was determined by                                 i   litigation  or                                ii   mediation, and the parties cannot agree on an amount.
