 Committee Note:
            
10

    
    The Government Operations Interim Committee recommended this bill.
            
11

    
 General Description:
            
12

    
    This bill amends the signature requirements for a petition to change the form of a
            
13

    
county government.
            
14

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
15

    
    This bill:
            
16

    
    .    requires that a petition to change the form of a county government be signed by
            
17

    
registered voters residing in the county equal in number to at least 10% of the total
            
18

    
number of votes cast in the county at the most recent election for president of the
            
19

    
United States; and
            
20

    
    .    makes technical corrections.
            
21

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:

10% of the total number of votes cast in the county at the most recent election for [governor]

    (c)  be filed in the office of the clerk of the county in which the petition signers reside.

52
