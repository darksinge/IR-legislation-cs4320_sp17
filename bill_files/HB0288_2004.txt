 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill establishes processes for submitting a nonbinding opinion question to the
            
10

    
voters of Utah.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    establishes procedures for submitting a nonbinding opinion question to the voters of
            
14

    
Utah;
            
15

    
    .    outlines duties of the lieutenant governor and election officials in submitting an
            
16

    
opinion question to the voters;
            
17

    
    .    establishes procedures for ballot form, manner of voting, and the canvass of returns
            
18

    
of a nonbinding opinion question; and
            
19

    
    .    sunsets Sections 36-16a-101 through 36-16a-108 on January 1, 2005.
