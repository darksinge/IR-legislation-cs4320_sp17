    APPROPRIATION FOR TUACAHN FOUNDATION




    1997 GENERAL SESSION


    STATE OF UTAH


     Sponsor: Lorin V.  Jones


    AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $25,000 FROM THE

    GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 1997-98 TO THE DEPARTMENT OF

    COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FOR THE TUACAHN

    FOUNDATION; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

    This act enacts uncodified material.

    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

        Section 1.   Appropriation.

        There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year 1997-98 $25,000 to the

    Department of Community and Economic Development for the Tuacahn Foundation to further the

    state's interest in promoting cultural arts, travel, and tourism.

        Section 2.   Effective date.

        This act takes effect on July 1, 1997.
