UTAH DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION           AUTHORITY TO CONSTRUCT TOLL ROADS AND LANES                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Brian R. Allen          AN ACT RELATING TO HIGHWAYS  PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT,         OPERATION, AND APPROVAL OF TOLLWAYS  PROVIDING DEFINITIONS          ESTABLISHING RULEMAKING  AND CREATING THE TOLLWAY RESTRICTED         ACCOUNT WITHIN THE TRANSPORTATION FUND.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                         . , Utah Code Annotated                             . , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           .  is enacted to read                         . .  Definitions    Establish and operate tollways    Imposition and collection           of tolls    Amount of tolls    Rulemaking    Tollways.                   As used in this section                a    Toll  means any tax, fee, or charge assessed for the specific use of a tollway.               b    Tollway  means a highway, highway lane, bridge, path, tunnel, or right of way          designed and used as a transportation route that is constructed, operated, or maintained through          the use of toll revenues.                   Subject to the provisions of Subsection    , the department may                a   establish and operate tollways and related facilities for the purpose of funding in whole          or in part the acquisition right of way and the design, construction, reconstruction, operation, and          maintenance of a transportation route for use by the public                b   enter into contracts, agreements, licenses, franchises, or other arrangements to          implement this section  and              c   impose and collect tolls on any tollway established under this section.                  a   The department may not establish or operate a tollway except as approved by the         commission.              b   In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the         commission shall set the amount of any toll imposed or collected on a tollway.                  In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the         department shall make rules necessary to establish and operate tollways.  The rules shall include         minimum criteria for having a tollway.              Section  .  Section           .  is enacted to read                         . .  Tollway Restricted Account    revenue    nonlapsing.                   There is created within the Transportation Fund a restricted account known as the           Tollway Restricted Account.                    The account shall be funded from the following sources                a   tolls collected under Section          .                 b   appropriations made to the account by the Legislature                c   contributions from other public and private sources for deposit into the account                d   interest earnings on cash balances  and               e   all monies collected for repayments and interest on account monies.                   All monies appropriated to the account are nonlapsing.                   a   Monies shall be appropriated by the Legislature from the restricted account to the          commission for tollway purposes.               b   The commission may authorize the monies under Subsection     a  to be spent by the          department to establish and operate tollways and related facilities, including design, construction,          reconstruction, operation, maintenance, and the acquisition of right of way.              Legislative Review Note      as of                  AM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
