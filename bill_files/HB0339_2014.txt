 General Description:

    This bill enacts language related to a county providing monetary assistance to a

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    defines terms;

    .    authorizes a county to appropriate money to or provide nonmonetary assistance to a

nonprofit entity in certain circumstances;

    .    authorizes a county to appropriate money in aid of a private enterprise project in

private enterprise project; and

    .    allows a person to appeal a county's decision to appropriate money to a private

enterprise project in district court.
