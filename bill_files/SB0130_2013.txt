 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Security Personnel Licensing Act.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    modifies the Security Personnel Licensing Act by:
            
13

    
        .    adding an additional board member; and
            
14

    
        .    changing the requirements for an applicant to be licensed as an armored car
            
15

    
company or a contract security company; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
