 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Criminal Code to repeal the unconstitutional criminal libel
            
11

    
provision and to repeal related sections.  This bill also repeals criminal slander statutes.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    repeals the criminal libel statute, which was found unconstitutional by the Utah
            
15

    
Supreme Court;
            
16

    
    .    repeals the definition and presumptions relating to the criminal libel offense; and
            
17

    
    .    repeals the Criminal Code offense of slander regarding female chastity.
            
18

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
19

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:
