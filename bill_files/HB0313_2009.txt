Section      B            is enacted to read                                   B       .  Severability.                                If a provision of this part or its application to a person or circumstance is held invalid,                           the invalidity does not affect the other provisions or applications of this part.  The provisions of                           this part are severable.                                Section  .    Section      B            is enacted to read                                   B       .  Responsibility for an underage drinking gathering.                                A responsible person is liable under this part regardless of whether the responsible                           person is present at an underage drinking gathering.                                Section  .    Section      B            is enacted to read                                   B       .  Citation    Civil penalty.                                     A law enforcement officer may issue a written citation to a responsible person who                           knowingly conducts, aids, allows, permits, or condones an underage drinking gathering on                           private property.                                     A person issued a citation under this section is subject to a civil penalty equal to the                           sum of                                  a   i   a fine of      for a first citation  or                                    ii   double the fine imposed for an immediately preceding citation for each subsequent                           citation  and                                 b   the response costs of the underage drinking gathering, not to exceed   ,   .                                     One or more responsible persons for an underage drinking gathering are jointly and                           severally liable under this section for a civil penalty attributable to the underage drinking                           gathering. Section  .    Section      B            is enacted to read                                   B       .  Reservation of legal options.                                     a   This part may not be construed as a waiver by a local entity of a right to seek                           reimbursement for actual costs of response services through another legal remedy or procedure.                                 b   The procedure provided for in this part is in addition to any other civil or criminal                           statute.                                 c   This part does not limit the authority of a law enforcement officer or private citizen                           to make an arrest for a criminal offense arising out of conduct regulated by this part.                                     A local entity may impose by ordinance a stricter provision related to the conduct                           of an underage drinking gathering, including the imposition of a different civil penalty amount,                           except that the ordinance shall provide that a civil penalty for an underage drinking gathering                           may only be imposed by a local entity for which an emergency response provider provides                           services at the underage drinking gathering.                                Section  .    Section      B            is enacted to read                                   B       .  Appeals.                                A person upon whom is imposed a civil penalty under this part may appeal the                           imposition of the civil penalty pursuant to the procedures used by the local entity for appealing                           a traffic citation or a violation of an ordinance.
