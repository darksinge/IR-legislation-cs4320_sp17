H.B. 414





1     APPROPRIATION TO SUPPORT LITIGATION


2     AGAINST CREATION OF ESCALANTE


3     MONUMENT




4    1998 GENERAL SESSION


5    STATE OF UTAH


6     Sponsor: Glenn Way


7    AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $250,000 TO THE OFFICE

8    OF PLANNING AND BUDGET FROM THE GENERAL FUND IN FISCAL YEAR 1998-99

9    FOR LEGAL COSTS INCURRED BY THE UTAH ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES TO

10    OPPOSE THE GRAND STAIRCASE - ESCALANTE NATIONAL MONUMENT

11    DESIGNATION; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

12    This act enacts uncodified material.

13    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

14        Section 1.   Appropriation.

15        (1)  Because the land and resources contained within the Grand Staircase - Escalante

16    National Monument are valuable to the people of the state, there is appropriated $250,000 to the

17    Office of Planning and Budget from the General Fund in fiscal year 1998-99, to be disbursed to

18    the Utah Association of Counties to be used to pay legal costs incurred by the Utah Association

19    of Counties to oppose the Grand Staircase - Escalante National Monument designation.

20        (2)  The money appropriated in Subsection (1) is nonlapsing.

21        Section 2.   Effective date.

22        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
