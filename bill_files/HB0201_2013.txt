 is amended to read:
            
27

    
     53A-1-201.  Election and appointment of officers -- Removal from office.

            
28


    
    (1)  The State Board of Education shall elect from its members a chair, and at least one
            
29

    
vice chair, but no more than three vice chairs, each year at a meeting held any time between
            
30

    
November 15 and January 15.
            
31

    
    (2) (a)  If the election of officers is held subsequent to the election of a new member of
            
32

    
the board, but prior to the time that the new member takes office, the new member shall
            
33

    
assume the position of the outgoing member for purposes of the election of officers.
            
34

    
    (b)  In all other matters the outgoing member shall retain the full authority of the office
            
35

    
until replaced as provided by law.
            
36

    
    (3)  The duties of these officers shall be determined by the board.
            
37

    
    (4)  The board shall appoint a secretary who serves at the pleasure of the board.
            
38

    
    (5)  An officer appointed or elected by the board under this section may be removed
            
39

    
from office for cause by a vote of two-thirds of the board.
