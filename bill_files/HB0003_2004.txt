General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and operation
            
9

    
of state government for the fiscal years beginning July 1, 2004 and ending June 30, 2005 and
            
10

    
beginning July 1, 2003 and ending June 30, 2004.

Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides budget increases and decreases for the use and support of certain state agencies;

    .    provides funds for the bills with fiscal impact passed in the 2004 General Session;
            
15

    
    .    provides budget increases and decreases for other purposes as described;
            
16

    
    .    provides intent language;
            
17

    
    .    amends previously approved internal service fund employment levels and capital
            
18

    
acquisition amounts.
