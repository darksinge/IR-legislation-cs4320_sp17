There is created an expendable trust fund known as the Water Commissioner Fund.                                    The fund consists of assessments paid to the state engineer by water users pursuant to                          Subsection                  .                                    a   The fund shall earn interest.                                b   Interest earned on fund monies shall be deposited into the fund.                                    The state engineer shall use fund monies to pay for salary and expenses of water                          commissioners and other expenses related to the distribution of water specified in Subsection                                          .
