Nelson                           AN ACT RELATING TO POLITICAL PARTIES  MODIFYING DEFINITIONS  EXPANDING                          CONSTITUTION AND BYLAW REQUIREMENTS FOR REGISTERED POLITICAL                          PARTIES  REQUIRING COUNTY AFFILIATES OF REGISTERED POLITICAL PARTIES                          TO PROVIDE CERTAIN INFORMATION  AND MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS. Section      A           is amended to read                                   A      .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Continuing political party  means an organization of voters that participated in the last                           regular general election and polled a total vote for any of its candidates for any office equal to                              or more of the total votes cast for all candidates for the United States House of Representatives.                                     County political party  means all of the persons within a single county who belong                          to a particular registered political party.                                           Newly registered political party  means  an  a statewide organization of voters                           that has complied with the petition and organizing procedures of this chapter to become a                           registered political party.                                           Registered political party  means an organization of voters that                                 a   participated in the last regular general election and polled a total vote for any of its                              candidates for any office equal to    or more of the total votes cast for all candidates for the                           United States House of Representatives  or                                b   has complied with the petition and organizing procedures of this chapter.                                     State political party  means all of the persons in Utah who belong to a particular                          registered political party.                               Section  .
