Redd6     Senate Sponsor:  ____________7      8     LONG TITLE9     General Description:10          This joint resolution of the Legislature requests that Utah's congressional delegation11     submit federal legislation amending federal block grant maintenance-of-effort12     requirements.13     Highlighted Provisions:14          This resolution:15                recognizes that federal maintenance-of-effort requirements can severely limit state16     budget flexibility; and 17                requests that Utah's congressional delegation submit federal legislation adding the18     following language to each maintenance-of-effort statutory requirement: "In nowise19     shall the state amount obligated as a maintenance-of-effort calculation exceed the20     amount of federal funding provided through the grant." 21     Special Clauses:22          None23      24     Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:25          WHEREAS, block grants offered by the federal government often include26     maintenance-of-effort provisions specifying required state spending levels necessary to receive27     a grant;28          WHEREAS, if a state does not meet its maintenance-of-effort requirements in any29     fiscal year, the federal government will often reduce dollar-for-dollar the state's federal block30     grant in the following year;31          WHEREAS, maintenance-of-effort requirements can be several times larger than the32     amount of the federal block grant;33          WHEREAS, large maintenance-of-effort requirements can severely limit state34     legislative budget flexibility, especially during downturns;35          WHEREAS, in fiscal year 2015, Utah received a $3.2 million Mental Health Block36     Grant that required a maintenance-of-effort obligation of $29.3 million;37          WHEREAS, in fiscal year 2015, Utah received a $5 million Baby Watch Early38     Intervention Grant that required a maintenance-of-effort obligation of $12 million;39          WHEREAS, in fiscal year 2015, Utah received a $16.5 million Substance Abuse40     Prevention and Treatment Block Grant that required a maintenance-of-effort obligation of41     $19.2 million; and42          WHEREAS, the state of Utah obligates hundreds of millions of dollars in43     maintenance-of-effort requirements:44          NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah45     requests that Utah's congressional delegation submit federal legislation amending federal block46     grant maintenance-of-effort requirements by adding the following language to each47     maintenance-of-effort statutory requirement: "In nowise shall the state amount obligated as a48     maintenance-of-effort calculation exceed the amount of federal funding provided through the49     grant."50          BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the President of51     the United States, the Majority Leader of the United States Senate, the Speaker of the United52     States House of Representatives, and the members of Utah's congressional delegation.Legislative Review NoteOffice of Legislative Research and General Counsel
