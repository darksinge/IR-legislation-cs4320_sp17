VEHICLE SAFETY INSPECTION                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Wayne A. Harper                             This act modifies the Motor Vehicle and Public Safety Code by amending the frequency                          required for safety inspections and emissions testing for motor vehicles.  This act amends the                          maximum fee an inspection station may charge for motor vehicle safety inspections.  The act                          increases the decal fee the state charges for safety inspections.  This act requires the                           establishment of a safety inspection apprenticeship program.  This act takes effect on                           January  ,     .
