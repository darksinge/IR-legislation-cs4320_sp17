Valentine                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Alcoholic Beverage Control Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies definitions                                .    addresses the nature of an adjudicative proceeding as a civil action including the                           burden of proof and the general applicability of mens rea requirements                                .    makes procedural clarifications                                .    clarifies the application of criminal procedures, principles, and penalties  and                               .    makes technical changes.
