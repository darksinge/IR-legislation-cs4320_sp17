 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies the State System of Public Education Code by amending educator
            
9

    
licensing and license renewal provisions. 
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    provides that completion of the  Passport to Teaching certification from the
            
13

    
American Board for Certification of Teacher Excellence satisfies the requirements
            
14

    
for a level 1 license;
            
15

    
    .    provides that completion of the Master Teacher certification from the American
            
16

    
Board for Certification of Teaching Excellence satisfies the requirements for a level
            
17

    
3 license; and 
            
18

    
    .    provides that completion of either the Passport to Teaching certification or the 
            
19

    
Master Teacher certification satisfies all license points or other professional
            
20

    
activities required for renewal of a level 1, 2, or 3 license.
            
21
