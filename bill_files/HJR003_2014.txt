H.J.R. 3





            
1

    
 JOINT RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING SISTER CITY

            
2

    
 RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN MAGNA, UTAH, AND YUZAWA,

            
3

    
 NIIGATA, JAPAN



            
4

    
2014 GENERAL SESSION

            
5

    
STATE OF UTAH

            
6

    


 Chief Sponsor:  Susan Duckworth



            
7

    



Senate Sponsor: 






  Daniel W. Thatcher





            
8

    

            
9

    
 LONG TITLE
            
10

    
 General Description:
            
11

    
    This joint resolution of the Legislature expresses support for the Magna-Yuzawa Sister
            
12

    
City Program on the 10th anniversary of the educational and cultural exchanges between
            
13

    
Magna, Utah, and Yuzawa, Niigata, Japan.
            
14

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
15

    
    This resolution:
            
16

    
    .    recognizes the 10th anniversary of the student exchange between Magna, Utah, and
            
17

    
Yuzawa, Niigata, Japan, which created a framework for the relationship between the
            
18

    
two communities; and
            
19

    
    .    recognizes the benefits of the sister city relationship between Magna and Yuzawa.
            
20

    
 Special Clauses:
            
21

    
    None
            
22

    

            
23

    
Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
24

    
    WHEREAS, in early August 2003, the Magna Township, assisted by Salt Lake County,
            
25

    
hosted a sister city search committee from Yuzawa, Niigata, Japan, which came to the United
            
26

    
States to find an American community with which to begin a short-stay educational and
            
27

    
cultural exchange;

            
28


    
    WHEREAS, during its time in the United States, the Yuzawa Committee also visited
            
29

    
cities in Washington and California as a part of its sister city search;
            
30

    
    WHEREAS, after returning to Japan, the Yuzawa Committee reported its findings and
            
31

    
recommendation to local government officials;
            
32

    
    WHEREAS, on September 25, 2003, based on the Yuzawa Committee's report and
            
33

    
recommendation, the Yuzawa Mayor and Town Council chose Magna, Utah, to be the
            
34

    
community with which to begin the journey of conducting an annual educational and cultural
            
35

    
exchange;
            
36

    
    WHEREAS, in July 2004, Yuzawa's Mayor, Takayuki Murayama, came to Magna with
            
37

    
the first group of students and their adult escorts to see Magna, observe the programs and
            
38

    
activities Magna had prepared for the exchange, and formally invite Magna to annually send
            
39

    
students to Yuzawa;
            
40

    
    WHEREAS, in July 2005, Magna sent its first group of students to Yuzawa to learn
            
41

    
about Japanese culture and life;
            
42

    
    WHEREAS, over the years, Magna has sent 60 students to visit and learn more about
            
43

    
Yuzawa and Japan;
            
44

    
    WHEREAS, these students were housed in Yuzawa by volunteer host families;
            
45

    
    WHEREAS, the 96 students Yuzawa sent to Magna since 2005 were housed by
            
46

    
volunteer host families while learning about Magna, the state of Utah, and the United States;
            
47

    
    WHEREAS, in July 2010, Yuzawa sent a delegation to Magna requesting that it be
            
48

    
allowed to take part in helping Magna celebrate its 100-year anniversary during its 4th of July
            
49

    
parade and 4th of July celebration;
            
50

    
    WHEREAS, on July 7, 2012, Yuzawa and Magna formalized their sister city
            
51

    
relationship by resolution in a ceremony in Yuzawa, Japan;
            
52

    
    WHEREAS, on August 9, 2013, in commemoration of the one-year anniversary of the
            
53

    
signing of the Magna-Yuzawa Sister City Resolution and the 100-year anniversary of the
            
54

    
Japanese "Gift of Trees" to Washington, D.C., Yuzawa gave 30 Akebono flowering cherry
            
55

    
trees to Magna to solemnize the friendship between the two communities;
            
56

    
    WHEREAS, in 2011, Brigham Young University (BYU) was considering expanding its
            
57

    
International Study Program in Japan to the Niigata Prefecture;
            
58

    
    WHEREAS, due to the relationship and friendship developed between Magna and

            
59


    
Yuzawa, BYU found a community receptive to working with the university to bring its
            
60

    
educational, cultural, and community service program to Yuzawa and Niigata; and
            
61

    
    WHEREAS, in the spring of 2014, BYU will begin working with Yuzawa to develop
            
62

    
the infrastructure for the kickoff of its Study Abroad relationship, which is scheduled to begin
            
63

    
in 2015:
            
64

    
    NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah
            
65

    
recognizes the 10th anniversary of the commencement of educational and cultural exchanges
            
66

    
between the sister cities of Magna and Yuzawa.
            
67

    
    BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Legislature recognizes the benefits of the sister
            
68

    
city relationship to both Magna and Yuzawa.
            
69

    
    BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the city of
            
70

    
Magna and the city of Yuzawa, Niigata, Japan.
