 General Description:

    This bill modifies the disaster loan program by changing financial terms of certain
            
10

    
outstanding promissory notes.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    requires that new promissory notes be issued to refinance any promissory notes
            
14

    
issued by the program that are outstanding as of April 30, 2006; and
            
15

    
    .    requires that the new promissory notes be issued:
            
16

    
        .    for the principal balance of the outstanding promissory notes;
            
17

    
        .    with a due date of May 15, 2007; and
            
18

    None

39
