APPROPRIATION FOR CHILDREN S ART           FACILITY EXPANSION                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  J. Brent Haymond          AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS  PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION OF           ,   ,    FROM THE GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR      TO THE         DEPARTMENT OF ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT TO BE ALLOCATED TO THE         SPRINGVILLE MUSEUM  OF ART ON A MATCHING BASIS FOR A CHILDREN S ART          FACILITY EXPANSION  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act enacts uncodified material.          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .   Appropriation.                   There is appropriated   ,   ,    from the General Fund for fiscal year         to the          Department of Community and Economic Development for the Springville Museum of Art.                   The funds appropriated under Subsection     shall be                a   distributed on a matching basis with every state dollar being matched with two dollars          raised by the Springville Museum of Art from any other source  and               b   used to benefit Utah students, teachers, and families in furtherance of the state s interest          in preserving and displaying Utah s rich cultural heritage of fine arts, expanding creative and          educational horizons, promoting travel and tourism, and sustaining the educational and cultural          mission of the Springville Museum of Art through                i   a   ,    square foot expansion of the museum for                A   a gallery dedicated to the annual Utah All State High School Exhibition and other          western regional exhibitions of children s art                B   a gallery dedicated to children s art by prominent Utah artists                C   an instructional gallery to teach Utah students about art                D   a center for the statewide partnership of Utah art educators to promote Utah school         district collections and the evening for educators program  and              E   increased exhibition space for Utah art and improved preservation of Utah         masterpieces  and              ii   upgrading the structure of the museum to meet               A   the American Association of Museums Accreditation Standards               B   the Americans  with Disabilities Act,    U.S.C.  Sec.        et seq.  and              C   applicable building codes.             Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    This bill raises the following constitutional or statutory concerns          Article XIII, Section   of the Utah Constitution prohibits the Legislature from imposing a tax  for the purpose of any county, city, town or other municipal corporation.   The bill does not  violate Article XIII, Section   to the extent that the Springville Museum of Art can be  considered as operating for a statewide purpose instead of a local purpose.
