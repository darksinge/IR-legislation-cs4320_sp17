 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Teacher Salary Supplement Program.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    replaces the Department of Human Resource Management with the State Board of
            
13

    
Education as the administrator of the Teacher Salary Supplement Program; and
            
14

    
    .    makes technical changes.

(1)(b)(ii) and (iii).
