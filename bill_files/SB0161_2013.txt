 General Description:

    This bill amends the Individual Income Tax Act, the Utah Code of Criminal Procedure,
            
10

    
and provisions related to the Division of Finance to assist in, and increase, the
            
11

    
collection of restitution for crime victims.

    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    defines terms;
            
15

    
    .    provides for a restitution order or judgment to be paid from income tax
            
16

    
overpayment funds;
            
17

    
    .    requires the State Office of Debt Collection to collect restitution for victims ordered
            
18

    
in a court if the account receivable is transferred to the office by a court;
            
19

    
    .    requires the Judicial Council to create a financial declaration form to be completed
            
20

    
by a defendant in a case where restitution is likely to be ordered;
            
21

    
    .    makes it a class B misdemeanor to make a false statement in a financial declaration
            
22

    
form; and
            
23

    
    .    makes technical changes.
