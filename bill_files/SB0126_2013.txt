 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill creates a veterans' services coordinator position and requires certain
            
10

    
government entities to provide data to the Utah Department of Veterans' Affairs.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    creates a veterans' coordinator position and qualifications;
            
14

    
    .    requires government entities to designate a veterans' services specialist within each
            
15

    
entity;
            
16

    
    .    requires contractors receiving government entity funds to participate;
            
17

    
    .    provides for training of agency veterans' services specialists; and
            
18

    
    .    requires an annual report to the Government Operations Interim Committee.
            
19
