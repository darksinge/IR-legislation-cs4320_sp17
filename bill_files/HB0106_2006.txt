 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill adds informational requirements for when a misdemeanor citation is issued.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    adds the “address” as a required piece of information on a citation;
            
13

    
    .    requires that if the citation is for a traffic violation that it also contain the person's
            
14

    
driver license number and the vehicle identification number of the vehicle; and
            
15

    
    .    makes technical cross-reference corrections.
            
16
