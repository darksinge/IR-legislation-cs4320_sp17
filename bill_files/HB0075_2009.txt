 General Description:

    This bill makes modifications to the executive committee for the Utah Communications
            
11

    
Agency Network.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    increases the number of members on the executive committee from 15 members to
            
15

    
17 members;
            
16

    
    .    increases the number of executive committee members that are elected by the board
            
17

    
at its annual meeting from ten members to 12 members;
            
18

    
    .    requires that:
            
19

    
        .    one of the new committee positions has an initial term of two years, with four
            
20

    
year terms thereafter; and
            
21

    
        .    the other new committee position has a four year term from its inception;
            
22

    
    .    raises the attendance requirement for a quorum from nine persons to ten persons;
            
23

    
and
            
24

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
25
