 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Designation of State Highways Act by amending the process for
            
10

    
the addition to or deletion of highways to or from the state highway system.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    adds additional criteria that may be considered by the Transportation Commission
            
14

    
when considering changes to the state highway system;
            
15

    
    .    requires the department to report any proposed additions or deletions to the state
            
16

    
highway system to the Transportation Interim Committee;
            
17

    
    .    requires the commission to report annually to the Transportation Interim Committee
            
18

    
regarding any proposed or recommended additions to or deletions from the state
            
19

    
highway system that were considered by the commission during the preceding year;
            
20

    
    .    requires the Transportation Commission to make rules governing the process that
            
21

    
will be followed when it is considering additions to or deletions from the state
            
22

    
highway system;
            
23

    
    .    outlines items that must be included in the rules including:
            
24

    
        .    notification to highway authorities of the department's intent to collect proposed
            
25

    
changes to the state highway system and report them to the Transportation
            
26

    
Interim Committee;
            
27

    
        .    a public hearing of proposed changes;
            
28

    
        .    notification to affected highway authorities; and
            
29

    
        .    the ability of any highway authority to initiate the process; and

            
30


    
     .    makes technical changes.
