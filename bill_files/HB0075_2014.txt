 General Description:

    This bill exempts nonviolent felons from the categories of restricted persons who are
            
11

    
prohibited from possessing a dangerous weapon.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    exempts persons convicted of certain nonviolent felonies from the categories of
            
15

    
restricted persons prohibited from possessing a dangerous weapon.

76-3-203.5

76-3-203.5


; or
            
35

    
    (v)  is an alien who is illegally or unlawfully in the United States.
            
36

37

    
    (i)  has been convicted of any felony;

    (ii)  within the last seven years has been adjudicated delinquent for an offense which if

    (iii)  is an unlawful user of a controlled substance as defined in Section

    (viii)  has been dishonorably discharged from the armed forces; or
