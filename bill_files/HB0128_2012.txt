 General Description:

    This bill modifies requirements for the operation of school community councils.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    modifies deadlines for providing notice of open school community council positions
            
13

    
and holding a school community council election;
            
14

    
    .    requires a school community council to conduct deliberations and take action
            
15

    
openly;
            
16

    
    .    establishes requirements for the notice, agenda, and minutes of a school community
            
17

    
council meeting;
            
18

    
     .    exempts a school community council from the Open and Public Meetings Act; and
            
19

    
    .    makes technical changes.
