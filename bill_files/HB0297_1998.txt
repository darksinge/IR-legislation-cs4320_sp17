4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor: Gary F. Cox


6    AN ACT RELATING TO COUNTIES; AND ALLOWING COUNTY EMPLOYEES RUNNING

7    FOR COUNTY OFFICE TO TAKE AN UNPAID LEAVE DURING CAMPAIGN PERIOD.

8    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

9    ENACTS:

10         17-15-26, Utah Code Annotated 1953

11    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12        Section 1.  Section  17-15-26 is enacted to read:

13         17-15-26.  Leave of absence for county employee seeking election to county office.

14        (1)  A county employee who has filed a declaration of candidacy under Section 20A-9-202

15    for a county office may, at the employee's discretion, take an unpaid leave of absence for some or

16    all of the period from the filing of the declaration of candidacy until the earliest of:

17        (a)  the employee's defeat at a primary election;

18        (b)  the employee's withdrawal as a candidate for the county office; or

19        (c)  the day after the regular general election for which the employee is a candidate.

20        (2)  A leave of absence under Subsection (1) may not be used as the basis for any adverse

21    employment action against the employee, including discipline or termination.
