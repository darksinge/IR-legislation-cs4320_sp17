 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends the rights of a property owner in disposing of abandoned personal
            
11

    
property left by a former tenant or occupant.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    requires a property owner to give notice to a former tenant of abandoned personal
            
15

    
property before it is sold or donated;
            
16

    
    .    allows a tenant to recover abandoned personal property under certain requirements;
            
17

    
    .    provides that a property owner is not required to store certain hazardous or
            
18

    
dangerous abandoned personal property; and
            
19

    
    .    makes technical changes.
