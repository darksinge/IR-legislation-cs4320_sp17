 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Utah Labor Code to address the safety division.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    clarifies regulatory authority of the safety division;
            
14

    
    .    changes the name of the safety division; and
            
15

    
    .    makes technical changes.
