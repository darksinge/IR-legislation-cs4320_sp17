H.B. 240









1     UTAH FOUNDATION FOR EDUCATIONAL


2     EXCELLENCE REPEALER




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor:  Tammy J. Rowan


6    AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION; REPEALING THE UTAH FOUNDATION

7    FOR EDUCATIONAL EXCELLENCE ACT.

8    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

9    REPEALS:

10         53A-4-101, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

11         53A-4-102, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

12         53A-4-103, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

13         53A-4-104, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

14         53A-4-105, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

15         53A-4-106, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

16         53A-4-107, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

17         53A-4-108, as enacted by Chapter 2, Laws of Utah 1988

18    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

19        Section 1.  Repealer.

20        This act repeals:

21        Section  53A-4-101, Short title.

22        Section  53A-4-102, Legislative findings and declarations.

23        Section  53A-4-103, Definitions.

24        Section  53A-4-104, Foundation created -- Independence from public entities -- Powers.

25        Section  53A-4-105, Board of directors -- Composition -- Term -- General powers.

26        Section  53A-4-106, Restrictions on powers of foundation.

27        Section  53A-4-107, Tax exemption of foundation.


1        Section  53A-4-108, Construction of chapter.
