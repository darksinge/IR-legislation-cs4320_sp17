 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends provisions relating to a legislative subpoena.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    describes the nature and purpose of a legislative subpoena;
            
13

    
    .    makes it a class A misdemeanor for a person to disobey or fail to comply with a
            
14

    
legislative subpoena or to fail to testify in response to a legislative subpoena in
            
15

    
relation to a matter upon which the person may be lawfully interrogated;
            
16

    
    .    describes methods of enforcing a legislative subpoena;
            
17

    
    .    describes the duties of a court in relation to enforcing a legislative subpoena;
            
18

    
    .    establishes a presumption that a legislative subpoena is valid and enforceable;
            
19

    
    .    describes the burden of proof and grounds required for a challenge to a legislative
            
20

    
subpoena; and
            
21

    
    .    provides for the appeal of a district court decision in relation to a legislative
            
22

    
subpoena.

36

37

    
state law, to enable the Legislature to fulfill its constitutional and statutory duties and exercise

39

    
Utah; and

43

    
lawfully interrogated, that person is in contempt of the Legislature.
            
44

    
    (b)  Contempt of the Legislature is a class A misdemeanor.
            
45

46

    
pursuant to a legislative subpoena and refuses to testify to a matter upon which the person may
            
47

has a business presence; or

55

57

    
legislative subpoena that are not challenged by the motion or other legal action; and
