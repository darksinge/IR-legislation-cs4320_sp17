Johnson                           AN ACT RELATING TO RIGHTS OF WAY ACROSS FEDERAL LANDS  DIRECTING THE                          COLLEGE OF NATURAL RESOURCES AT UTAH STATE UNIVERSITY TO INVENTORY                          AND MAP R.S.      RIGHTS OF WAY USING GLOBAL POSITIONING SYSTEM                           TECHNOLOGY AND TO PHOTOGRAPH ROADS, EVIDENCE OF VALID EXISTING                           RIGHTS, AND OTHER STRUCTURES ON FEDERAL LANDS  APPROPRIATING     ,                              FROM THE GENERAL FUND IN FISCAL YEAR           FOR THE WORK  AND                           PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            ENACTS                                        . , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                        . .  Inventory and mapping of R.S.       rights of way    Photographing of                           roads, structures, and evidence of valid and existing rights on federal lands.                                    The College of Natural Resources at Utah State University, in coordination with the                          Cooperative Extension Service and Utah Association of Counties, shall                                 a   inventory and map R.S.       rights of way using Global Positioning System  GPS                           technology  and                                b   photograph                                 i   roads and other evidence of construction of R.S.       rights of way                                 ii   structures or natural features that may indicate the purpose of an R.S.                                  right of way, such as mines, agricultural facilities, recreational facilities, or scenic overlooks  and                                iii   evidence of valid and existing rights on federal lands, such as mines and agricultural                          facilities.                                    In selecting lands on which to perform the work described in Subsection    , priority                          shall be given to federal lands                                 a   that a federal land management agency proposes for special management, such as lands                          to be managed as an area of critical environmental concern or primitive area  or                                b   proposed to receive a special designation by Congress, such as lands to be designated                          as wilderness or a national conservation area.                                    Utah State University and each county in which the work described in Subsection                              is performed shall enter into a memorandum of agreement which shall specify                                 a   the scope of work                                 b   a project budget  and                                c   any monetary or in kind contributions of the county.                                    To avoid duplication of work and minimize expenses, Utah State University is                          encouraged to obtain maps and other relevant data on file with state agencies.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                                    Except as provided in H.B.   , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated                              ,    from the General Fund in fiscal year          , to the College of Natural Resources at                          Utah State University to inventory and map R.S.      rights of way and photograph roads and                          other structures as provided by Section             .     .                                   The money appropriated in Subsection     is nonlapsing.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
