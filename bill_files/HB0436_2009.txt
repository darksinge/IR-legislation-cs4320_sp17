STATE PROHIBITION OF SUDAN RELATED                           CONTRACTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   David Litvack                                Senate Sponsor             Luz Robles                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Utah Procurement Code by requiring the State Procurement                           Policy Board to make certain rules related to contracting with companies doing                           business in Sudan.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the State Procurement Policy Board to make rules that are consistent with                           provisions of the federal Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of                                forbidding a state agency from contracting with a person who does business in                           Sudan                                .    requires the board to make an annual report to the Legislature concerning the                           establishment, implementation, and enforcement of rules  and                               .    makes technical changes.
