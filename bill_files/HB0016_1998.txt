H.B. 16









1     FUNDING CLASSROOM SUPPLIES




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor: Ron Bigelow


5    AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION; PROVIDING A $3,000,000

6    APPROPRIATION FOR TEACHER CLASSROOM SUPPLIES AND MATERIALS; AND

7    PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

8    This act enacts uncodified material.

9    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

10        Section 1.   Appropriation.

11        (1)  There is appropriated from the Uniform School Fund for fiscal year 1998-99,

12    $3,000,000 to the State Board of Education for distribution to public school teachers for classroom

13    supplies and materials.

14        (2)  The board shall distribute the appropriation directly to classroom teachers through the

15    state's 40 school districts on the basis of the number of classroom teachers in each public school

16    as compared to the total number of classroom teachers in the 40 school districts.

17        (3)  Each teacher shall receive up to $150 and may spend the money for school supplies,

18    materials, or field trips under rules adopted by the State Board of Education.

19        (4)  As used in this section:

20        (a)  "Public schools" and "school districts" include the Schools for the Deaf and the Blind

21    and Applied Technology Centers.

22        (b)  "Teachers" mean permanent teacher positions filled by one teacher or two or more

23    job-sharing teachers who are certificated personnel, paid on the teacher's salary schedule, and

24    whose primary function is to provide instructional or a combination of instructional and counseling

25    services to students in the public schools.  They must be hired by the district for an entire contract

26    period.

27        Section 2.   Effective date.


1        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
