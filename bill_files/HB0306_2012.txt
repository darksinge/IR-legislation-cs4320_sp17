 General Description:

    This bill amends the Pharmacy Practice Act to require the pharmacy board to adopt
            
10

    
rules regarding the disposal of unused prescription drugs in accordance with federal
            
11

    
laws and regulations.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

15

    
prescription drugs if the federal rules permit a pharmacy take back program; and
            
16

    
    .    requires the pharmacy board to enact administrative rules regarding unused drug
            
17

    
take back programs once federal rules related to drug take back programs are
            
18
