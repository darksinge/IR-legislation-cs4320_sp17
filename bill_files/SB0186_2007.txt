 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Motor Vehicle Business Regulation Act by amending provisions
            
10

    
relating to motor vehicle business licenses.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides that a dealer supplemental license for an additional place of business may
            
14

    
only be issued if the dealer is licensed and bonded and is in compliance with
            
15

    
existing administrative rules promulgated by the administrator of the Motor Vehicle
            
16

    
Enforcement Division;
            
17

    
    .    provides that a dealer supplemental license for an additional place of business for a
            
18

    
new motor vehicle dealer may not be issued for an additional place of business that
            
19

    
is beyond the geographic specifications of a new motor vehicle dealer's area of
            
20

    
responsibility as determined in the dealer's franchise agreement;
            
21

    
    .    requires a new motor vehicle dealer to submit a copy of the portion of the dealer's
            
22

    
franchise agreement to the administrator before being issued a supplemental license
            
23

    
for an additional place of business; and
            
24

    
    .    provides an exception to the restriction:
            
25

    
        .    if the license is being issued for the sale of used motor vehicles; or
            
26

    
        .    for trade shows or exhibitions.
