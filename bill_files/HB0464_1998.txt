1     PRIVATIZATION OF FLEET OPERATIONS




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor: Glenn Way


5    AN ACT RELATING TO THE DIVISION OF FLEET OPERATIONS; REQUIRING

6    PREPARATION OF A REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS TO PRIVATIZE THE DIVISION OF

7    FLEET OPERATIONS; REQUIRING REVIEW OF THAT REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS; 

8    ELIMINATING THE DIVISION OF FLEET OPERATIONS EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 1999;

9    AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

10    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

11    ENACTS:

12         63A-1-114, Utah Code Annotated 1953

13    REPEALS:

14         63A-9-101, as enacted by Chapter 334, Laws of Utah 1996

17         63A-9-203, as enacted by Chapter 334, Laws of Utah 1996

18         63A-9-301, as last amended by Chapters 85 and 252, Laws of Utah 1997

25    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

26        Section 1.  Section  63A-1-114 is enacted to read:

27         63A-1-114.  Executive director to prepare Request for Proposals.


1        (1)  The executive director shall, by following the procedures and requirements of Section

2    63-56-21, prepare a request for proposals soliciting a private entity to perform some or all of the

3    duties and responsibilities of the Division of Fleet Operations relating to a central state motor

4    vehicle fleet.

5        (2)  Before issuing the request for proposals, and no later than September 1, 1998, the

6    executive director shall submit the completed request for proposals to the Office of Legislative

7    Fiscal Analyst and Interim Executive Appropriations Committee of the Utah Legislature for its

8    review.

9        Section 2.  Repealer.

10        This act repeals:

11        Section  63A-9-101,  Definitions.

12        Section  63A-9-201,  Creation.

13        Section  63A-9-202,  Director of division -- Appointment.

14        Section  63A-9-203,  Director -- Duties.

15        Section  63A-9-301,  Motor Vehicle Review Committee -- Composition.

16        Section  63A-9-302,  Committee duties.

17        Section  63A-9-401,  Division -- Duties.

18        Section  63A-9-402,  State-owned vehicle report -- Contents.

19        Section  63A-9-501,  Complaints about misuse or illegal operation of state vehicles --

20     Disposition.

21        Section  63A-9-601,  Marking of vehicles.

22        Section  63A-9-701,  Subscription to motor pool by certain local government entities.

23        Section 3.   Effective date.

24        (1)  Section 63A-1-114 is effective May 4, 1998.

25        (2)  Section 2, Repealer, is effective July 1, 1999.
