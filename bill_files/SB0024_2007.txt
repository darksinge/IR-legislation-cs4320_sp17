 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill increases the total amount of an estate which may be settled by affidavit from
            
10

    
$25,000 to $100,000.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides that an estate of less than $100,000 may be settled by affidavit.
            
14

    None

 Other Special Clauses:
