 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill creates a restricted account to be used to fund litigation expenses for highway
            
11

    
projects.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    creates a restricted account;
            
15

    
    .    establishes the account's revenue sources and use;
            
16

    
    .    authorizes the Division of Finance to:
            
17

    
        .    create subaccounts in the restricted account; and
            
18

    
        .    apportion interest and donations to the subaccounts;
            
19

    
    .    provides intent language for the appropriation;
            
20

    
    .    provides that monies not spent from the subaccounts be appropriated by the
            
21

    
Legislature to the LeRay McAllister Critical Land Conservation Fund; and
            
22

    
    .    creates an exception to the statutory cap on the LeRay McAllister Critical Land
            
23

    
Conservation Fund so that all of those appropriations can be deposited in the fund.
            
24

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:

    This bill appropriates: 
            
26

    
    .    $5,000,000 one-time to the Litigation Fund for Highway Projects Account.

 Other Special Clauses:
