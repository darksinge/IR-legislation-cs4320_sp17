H.B. 187









1     PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION REPORTING


2     AMENDMENTS




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor: Beverly Ann Evans


6    AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES; PROVIDING THAT THE PUBLIC SERVICE

7    COMMISSION SUBMIT A REPORT ON TELECOMMUNICATIONS BIANNUALLY TO

8    THE PUBLIC UTILITIES AND TECHNOLOGY INTERIM COMMITTEE.

9    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

10    AMENDS:

11         54-8b-2.5, as enacted by Chapter 269, Laws of Utah 1995

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.  Section  54-8b-2.5 is amended to read:

14         54-8b-2.5.  Report to governor and Legislature.

15        Beginning October 15, 1998, and biannually thereafter, the commission shall submit a

16    report to the governor, Legislature, the Public Utilities and Technology Interim Committee, and

17    Information Technology Commission on the state of the telecommunications industry and make

18    recommendations for any regulatory changes necessary to achieve the policy of the state as set

19    forth in Section 54-8b-1.1.  The commission shall determine criteria to be used to evaluate the

20    performance of price regulation and the information necessary to conduct the evaluation.




 Legislative Review Note
    as of  11-19-97  2:48 PM


A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.

 Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel


 Committee Note


The Public Utilities and Technology Interim Committee recommended this bill.
