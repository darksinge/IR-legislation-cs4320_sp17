 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill makes changes and additions to the Utah Exemptions Act for bankruptcy.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    updates dollar amounts for a debtor's primary personal residence;
            
13

    
    .    adds an exemption for firearms and ammunition;
            
14

    
    .    provides an exemption for wages;
            
15

    
    .    exempts a motor vehicle used by an individual in the individual's business, trade, or
            
16

    
profession; and
            
17

    
    .    exempts nonresidents from using the state exemptions.
            
18

    None
