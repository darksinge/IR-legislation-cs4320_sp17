This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           ENACTS                                    a    , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section        a         is enacted to read                                    a    .  Supplemental benefit established    Deferred compensation plan                              Contribution by employer and employee    Immediate vesting of contributions.                                    There is established a supplemental deferred compensation benefit for members of this                          system.                                a   i   For public safety employees of the state and state institutions of higher education,                          the employer shall contribute on behalf of each of its employees  .   of the employee s salary to                          the deferred compensation plan qualified under Section     k  of the Internal Revenue Code which                          is sponsored by the board.                                ii   The employee may also make elective contributions to the qualified     k  plan which                          receives the  .   employer contribution described in Subsection     a  i , but only up to an                          amount permitted by federal law.                                b   i   For all other members of this system not described under Subsection     a , the                          participating employer may contribute on behalf of each of its employees any amount to the                          deferred compensation plan qualified under Section     k  of the Internal Revenue Code which                          is sponsored by the board.                                   ii   The employee may also make elective contributions to the qualified     k  plan which                          receives the employer contribution described in Subsection     b  i , but only up to an amount                          permitted by federal law.                                c   The employee may not make elective contributions to any other qualified     k  plan                          sponsored by a state or local government.                                    The total amount contributed by the employer under Subsection     a  or  b  vests to                          the employee s benefit immediately and is nonforfeitable.
