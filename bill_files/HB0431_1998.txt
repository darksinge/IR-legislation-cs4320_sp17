H.B. 431





1     GENDER PRICE EQUALITY




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor:  Steve  Barth


5    AN ACT RELATING TO COMMERCE AND TRADE; AND DEFINING DISCRIMINATORY

6    BEHAVIOR BASED ON GENDER.

7    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

8    ENACTS:

9         13-7-5, Utah Code Annotated 1953

10    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

11        Section 1.  Section  13-7-5 is enacted to read:

12         13-7-5.  Price discrimination based on gender prohibited.

13        (1)  For purposes of this section:

14         (a)  "Discriminate" means charging a person a higher price than another person for the

15    same or similar service based on a factor prohibited by law.

16        (b)  "Services" include:

17        (i)  dry cleaning;

18        (ii)  clothing alterations; and

19        (iii)  services that fall within the practice of cosmetology/barbering as defined in Section

20    58-11a-102.

21        (2)  A business establishment may not discriminate, with respect to the price charged for

22    services, against a person because of the person's gender.

23        (3)  Notwithstanding Subsection (2), a business may base price differences specifically on:

24        (a)  the amount of time;

25        (b)  difficulty; or

26        (c)  cost of providing the service.

27        (4)  A business establishment that provides services shall post in a prominent place in its


1    establishment any gender-related differences in price charged for services in accordance with

2    Subsection (3).

3        (5)  A business establishment that discriminates with respect to the price charged for

4    services against a person because of the person's gender violates Section 13-7-3.
