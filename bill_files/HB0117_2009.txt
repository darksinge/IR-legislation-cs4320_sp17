MECHANICAL CONTRACTOR LICENSING                           PROVISIONS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Larry B. Wiley                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies Title   , Chapter   , Utah Construction Trades Licensing Act, to                           license heating, venting, and air conditioning  HVAC  mechanical technicians.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies construction trade licensing provisions to include HVAC mechanical                           technicians                                .    sets the HVAC mechanical technician to apprentice ratio                                .    creates the HVAC Mechanical Technicians Licensing Board                                .    modifies the membership of the Construction Services Commission                                 .    authorizes the Division of Occupational and Professional Licensing to issue licenses                           to various HVAC mechanical technicians                                .    repeals provisions excepting HVAC mechanical technicians from licensure                                .    removes a repeal date                                .    requires that a licensed HVAC mechanical technician complete fuel and electricity                           efficiency education  and                               .    makes technical corrections.
