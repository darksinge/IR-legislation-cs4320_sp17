    This bill modifies the requirements for issuing and renewing a concealed weapon

permit for a United States active duty service member and spouse.

    This bill:

    .    modifies the requirements for a concealed weapon permit for a United States

military active duty service member and spouse who are not residents of the state;

duty service members, and national guard active duty service members from the five

and nonresident active duty service members and waives the renewal fee for the

active duty service members' spouses; and

    .    defines active duty service member and spouse of an active duty service member.
