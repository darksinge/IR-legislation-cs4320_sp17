The executive director, with the advice of an advisory board consisting of the                              chairman of the Department of Pathology at the University of Utah medical school and the                           dean of the law school at the University of Utah, shall appoint a chief medical examiner who                           shall be licensed to practice medicine in the state and shall meet the qualifications of a forensic                           pathologist, certified by the American Board of Pathologists.                                    a   The medical examiner shall serve at the will of the executive director.
