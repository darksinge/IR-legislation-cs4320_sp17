 Committee Note:
            
9

    
    The Business and Labor Interim Committee recommended this bill.
            
10

    
 General Description:
            
11

    
    This bill amends the Utah Optometry Practice Act to permit certain people in limited
            
12

    
circumstances to sell eyeglasses without being licensed under the chapter.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    allows a person in certain circumstances to sell, fit, adjust, and dispense eyeglasses
            
16

    
or spectacles without being licensed under the chapter;
            
17

    
    .    requires a prescription for eyeglasses; and
            
18

    
    .    specifies the activities that the unlicensed person may engage in regarding the sale
            
19

    
or dispensing of eyeglasses or spectacles.
            
20

    None
