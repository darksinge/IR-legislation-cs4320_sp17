 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends the Health Care Facility Licensing and Inspection Act by providing
            
11

    
measures to discourage identity theft and health insurance fraud, and to protect the
            
12

    
accuracy of medical records.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    defines the term, "medical facility";
            
16

    
    .    requires a medical facility to, prior to providing services to a patient, request
            
17

    
identification for:
            
18

    
        .    the patient; and
            
19

    
        .    an individual who consents to the provision of services to the patient, if the
            
20

    
patient lacks the capacity to consent;
            
21

    
    .    permits a medical facility to use methods to document or confirm a patient's
            
22

    
identity;
            
23

    
     .    prohibits a medical facility that is subject to the federal Emergency Medical
            
24

    
Treatment and Active Labor Act (EMTALA) from denying services to an individual
            
25

    
on the basis that the individual does not provide identification when requested;
            
26

    
    .    prohibits a medical facility that is not subject to EMTALA from denying services to
            
27

    
an individual on the sole basis that the individual does not provide identification

            
28


    
when requested;
            
29

    
    .    provides that a medical facility is not subject to a private right of action for failing
            
30

    
to ask for identification; and
            
31

    
    .    prohibits the imposition of penalties if a medical facility does not request
            
32

    
documentation.
            
33

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
34

    
    None
            
35

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
36

    
    None

 Utah Code Sections Affected:

     26-21-25.  Patient identity protection.
            
44

    
    (1)  As used in this section:
            
45

    
    (a)  "EMTALA" means the federal Emergency Medical Treatment and Active Labor
            
46

    
Act.
            
47

    
    (b)  "Medical facility" means:
            
48

    
    (i)  a general acute hospital;
            
49

    
    (ii)  a specialty hospital;
            
50

    
    (iii)  a home health agency;
            
51

    
    (iv)  a hospice;
            
52

    
    (v)  a nursing care facility;
            
53

    
    (vi)  a residential-assisted living facility;
            
54

    
    (vii)  a birthing center;
            
55

    
    (viii)  an ambulatory surgical facility;
            
56

    
    (ix)  a small health care facility;
            
57

    
    (x)  an abortion clinic;
            
58

    
    (xi)  a facility owned or operated by a health maintenance organization;

            
59


    
    (xii)  an end stage renal disease facility;
            
60

    
    (xiii)  a health care clinic;
            
61

    
    (xiv)  a physician's office;
            
62

    
    (xv)  a dental office; or
            
63

    
    (xvi)  any other health care facility that the committee designates by rule.
            
64

    
    (2) (a)  In order to discourage identity theft and health insurance fraud, and to reduce
            
65

    
the risk of medical errors caused by incorrect medical records, a medical facility shall request
            
66

    
identification from an individual prior to providing in-patient or out-patient services to the
            
67

    
individual.
            
68

    
    (b)  If the individual who will receive services from the medical facility lacks the legal
            
69

    
capacity to consent to treatment, the medical facility shall request identification:
            
70

    
    (i)  for the individual who lacks the legal capacity to consent to treatment; and
            
71

    
    (ii)  from the individual who consents to treatment on behalf of the individual described
            
72

    
in Subsection (2)(b)(i).
            
73

individual on the sole basis that the individual did not provide identification when requested;

    (d)  is not subject to a private right of action for failing to ask for identification;

    (iii)  palm scan; or
