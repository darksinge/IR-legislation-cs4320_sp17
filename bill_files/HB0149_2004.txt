 General Description:

    This bill modifies the New Automobile Franchise Act to amend provisions relating to
            
9

    
the issuance or relocation of a franchise.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    requires a franchisor to provide certain documents with a notice that the franchisor
            
13

    
intends to enter into a franchise or relocate a franchise within the relevant market
            
14

    
area to the Utah Motor Vehicle Franchise Advisory Board and existing franchisees
            
15

    
within the relevant market area;
            
16

    
    .    provides that if a document required to be provided with the notice is not provided,
            
17

    
it may not be used in a hearing as evidence to establish good cause; and
            
18

    
    .    makes technical changes.
