 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Utah State Retirement and Insurance Benefit Act by amending
            
11

    
postretirement provisions for certain nonbenefitted reemployed retirees.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    clarifies that a participating employer is only required to make the amortization rate
            
15

    
contribution for a reemployed retiree who:
            
16

    
        .    has completed the one-year separation after retirement; and
            
17

    
        .    elects to continue to receive a retirement allowance; and
            
18

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
19
