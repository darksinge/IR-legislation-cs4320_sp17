 LONG TITLE
            
7

    
 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies provisions dealing with licensed private investigators and their
            
9

    
accessing private records.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    limits access to protected motor vehicle and driver license records by a licensed
            
13

    
private investigator by requiring the investigator to hold a valid agency or registrant
            
14

    
license;
            
15

    
    .    provides that an apprentice private investigator may not directly obtain protected
            
16

    
information from the Motor Vehicle Division or Driver License Division within the
            
17

    
Department of Public Safety; and
            
18

    
    .    makes certain technical changes.
            
19

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
20

    
    None
            
21

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
22

    
    None
