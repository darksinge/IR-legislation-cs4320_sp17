Acquisition of alcohol or drug enforcement equipment    Rulemaking              Legislative findings.                   From monies appropriated by the Legislature and any other funds made available for          the purposes described under this section, the department shall assist the law enforcement agencies          of the state and its political subdivisions in the acquisition, improvement, and replacement of          equipment used in the enforcement of alcohol or drug related offenses to measure alcohol or drug          content in a person s body.                   In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the          commissioner shall make rules for granting monies and providing equipment to law enforcement          agencies under this section based on criteria established in the rules.                   The Legislature finds that these monies are for a general and statewide public purpose.              Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .
