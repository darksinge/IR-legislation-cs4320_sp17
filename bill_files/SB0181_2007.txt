    This bill:

    .    provides definitions;

    .    authorizes the Motor Vehicle Division to impose a fee on certain motor vehicle,

costs of electronic payments;

        .    need not be separately identified; and

payments;

    .    authorizes the State Tax Commission to charge convenience fees to cover the costs
            
26

    
of electronic payments of taxes and fees administered by the commission and
            
27

    
provides that the fees shall be used as dedicated credits by the commission; and

    .    makes technical changes.
