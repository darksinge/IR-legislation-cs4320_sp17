UTAH STRATEGIC HIGHWAY PROJECTS                           FUND                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   David Ure                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the State Appropriations and Tax Limitation Act and the                           Transportation Finances Act by creating the Utah Strategic Highway Projects Fund.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Utah Strategic Highway Projects Fund to pay the costs of maintenance,                           construction, reconstruction, or renovation to state and federal highways                                .    exempts the Utah Strategic Highway Projects Fund from spending limit provisions                            and                               .    makes technical changes. Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  Utah Strategic Highway Projects Fund.                                    There is created a special revenue fund entitled the Utah Strategic Highway                          Projects Fund.                                    The fund consists of monies generated from the following revenue sources                                 a   any voluntary contributions received for the construction, major reconstruction, or                          major renovation of state or federal highways  and                                b   appropriations made to the fund by the Legislature.                                     a   The fund shall earn interest.                                 b   All interest earned on fund monies shall be deposited into the fund.                                     a   The executive director may use fund monies, as prioritized by the                           Transportation Commission, only to pay the costs of construction, major reconstruction, or                           major renovation to state and federal highways.                                 b   Notwithstanding the provisions of Section                 , the fund monies may be used                           to pay the costs of construction, major reconstruction, or major renovation to state and federal                           highways that are not prioritized through the written project prioritization process under                           Section                 .
