 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance Program to add
            
11

    
radiology technicians and medical laboratory technicians to the list of health care
            
12

    
professionals who may participate in the program.

14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    amends the definition of health care professional;
            
16

    
    .    amends the duties of the Department of Health with regard to the Utah Health Care
            
17

    
Workforce Financial Assistance Program; and
            
18

    
    .    amends the membership of the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance
            
19

    
Program Advisory Committee.
            
20

23

    
General Fund for fiscal year 2006-07 to the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial
            
24

    
Assistance Program in the Department of Health.
