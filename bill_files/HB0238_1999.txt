APPROPRIATIONS FOR LAND USE PLANS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Dennis H. Iverson                           AN ACT RELATING TO COUNTIES AND OTHER LOCAL GOVERNMENTS                           APPROPRIATING     ,    TO THE OFFICE OF PLANNING AND BUDGET TO                          DISTRIBUTE TO COUNTIES AND OTHER LOCAL GOVERNMENTS AS MATCHING                          FUNDS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF LAND USE PLANS AND                          TO SOUTHERN UTAH UNIVERSITY TO CONDUCT WORKSHOPS ON LAND PLANNING.                           This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Intent.                               The Legislature finds that the value of property throughout the state is enhanced by                          appropriate land use planning by the counties and other local governments and that the quality of                          land use planning improves with training and education.  It is the intent of the Legislature in                          appropriating these funds to help meet the statewide need for training and education of county,                          local, and state officials involved in land use planning activities to improve the quality of land use                          planning.
