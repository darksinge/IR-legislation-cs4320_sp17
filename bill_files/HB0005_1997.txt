AUTOMATED TELLER MACHINE AMENDMENTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Fred R. Hunsaker          AN ACT RELATING TO FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS  AMENDING DEFINITIONS  AND         MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          AMENDS                     , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section         is amended to read                      .  Definitions.              As used in this chapter                     Automated teller machine  means an  unmanned, free standing  electronic          information processing device , located separate and apart from a financial institution s principal          office, branch, or detached facility, which uses either the direct transmission of electronic impulses          to a financial institution or the recording of electronic impulses or other indicia of a transaction for          delayed transmission to a financial institution in order to perform financial transactions  that                a   is readily accessible to the general public  and               b   on behalf of an issuer                i   dispenses currency or coin  or               ii   accepts deposits or payments.                         a    Consumer funds transfer facility  means  either                 i   an automated teller machine ,   or               ii   a point of sale terminal, including any supporting equipment, structures, or systems.                b   A point of sale terminal owned or operated by and on the premises of a person          primarily engaged in the business of selling or leasing goods or non financial services and capable           of performing the functions of a consumer funds transfer facility, is not  considered to be  a         consumer funds transfer facility unless connected on line or off line to a financial institution for         the purpose of performing financial transactions.                         Control  means ownership, directly or indirectly, of a majority of the outstanding         shares or more than     of the shares voted for the election of directors at the preceding election.                   Device for access  means a card, code, or other means of access to a customer s         account, or any combination of these, that is used to deposit or withdraw cash through an         automated teller machine.   Device for access  includes an access device as defined in Federal         Reserve Board Regulation E,    C.F.R. Part    ,     , adopted under the Electronic Fund          Transfer Act,    U.S.C.      et seq.                          Financial transaction  means                a   cash withdrawals ,                 b   deposits ,                 c   account transfers ,                 d   payments from deposit, loan, or thrift accounts ,                 e   disbursements under a preauthorized credit agreement , or                 f   loan payments  and   or               g   other similar transactions initiated by an account holder.                    Issuer  means                a   a depository institution authorized to do business in this state that issues a device for          access  or               b   a state or federal government agency that issues a device for access that allows a person          to receive benefits from or through the state or federal governmental agency.                          Merchant  means a person primarily engaged in the retail sale or lease of goods          or non financial services.                         a    Point of sale terminal  means  a manned  an electronic information          processing device , other than a telephone, located at the point of sale and separate and apart from          a financial institution s principal office, branch, or detached facility, which uses either the direct          transmission of electronic impulses to a financial institution or the recording of electronic impulses          or other indicia of a transaction for delayed transmission to a financial institution in order to          perform financial transactions.  However, point of sale terminals includes electronic information                 processing devices which interface with the telephone transmission system and which, either         through the direct transmission of electronic impulses or the recording and delayed transmission         of electronic impulses to a financial institution, perform financial transactions.  Nothing in this         definition prevents a device which constitutes a point of sale terminal from being used to perform,         for its operator, any internal business functions that are not financial transactions.  located at the         point of sale that is controlled by or used by the merchant or other provider of goods or services         that               a   authorizes a debit or credit in payment for goods or services to a customer s account         at                i   a depository institution  or               ii   a state or federal governmental agency  or               b   authorizes the merchant or other provider of goods or services to dispense currency or          coin to a customer who uses a device for access issued by a state or federal governmental agency.
