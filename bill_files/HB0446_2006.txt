SURPLUS LANDS AMENDMENTS                                GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor   Mark W. Walker                            Senate Sponsor                                                                           LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts provisions relating to the sale and purchase of state and local surplus                           lands.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    enacts provisions allowing certain counties and municipalities to purchase lands                           declared to be surplus by the state or a political subdivision of the state                                .    establishes a process for the purchase of those surplus lands                                .    requires that surplus lands purchased as provided in this bill shall be used for a                           public purpose  and                               .    authorizes the original seller of surplus land to reacquire the land if the acquiring                           entity later declares the land to be surplus property.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                         , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                                , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                           , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  SURPLUS LANDS ACT                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Surplus Lands Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Eligible entity  means                                 a   a city or town with a population density of  ,    or more people per square mile  or                                b   a county whose unincorporated area includes a qualifying township.                                     Purchase price  means the greater of                                 a       of the appraised value of the surplus property, based on the predominant zone                          in the surrounding area  and                                b   the amount the surplusing entity paid to acquire the surplus property.                                     Qualifying township  means a township under Section          a         that has a                          population density of  ,    or more people per square mile within the boundaries of the                          township.                                     Surplusing entity  means the state or political subdivision of the state that owns                          the surplus property.                                     Surplus property  means land that                                 a   was purchased with taxpayer money                                 b   is located within a city or town that is an eligible entity or within a qualifying                          township                                 c   consists of one contiguous tract at least three acres in size                                 d   is owned by the state or a political subdivision of the state  and                                e   has been declared by the owner to be surplus.                                  Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Purchase of surplus property.                                    An eligible entity may purchase, and each surplusing entity shall sell, surplus                          property as provided in this section.                                    a   Upon declaring land to be surplus property, each surplusing entity shall give                          written notice to each eligible entity in which the surplus property is located.                                b   Each notice under Subsection     a  shall                                 i   state that the surplusing entity has declared the land to be surplus property  and                                ii   describe the surplus property.                                    Subject to Subsection    , an eligible entity may purchase the surplus property by                          paying the surplusing entity the purchase price.                                    a   The legislative body of each eligible entity desiring to purchase surplus property                          under this section shall                                 i   within    days after the eligible entity receives notice under Subsection    , adopt a                          resolution declaring the intent to purchase the surplus property and deliver a copy of the                          resolution to the surplusing entity  and                                ii   within    days after delivering a copy of the resolution under Subsection     a  i                           to the surplusing entity, deliver to the surplusing entity an earnest money offer to purchase the                          surplus property at the purchase price.                                b   If an eligible entity fails to comply with either of the requirements under Subsection                              a  within the applicable time period, the eligible entity forfeits the right to purchase the                          surplus property.                                    Surplus property acquired by an eligible entity shall be used only for a public                          purpose.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Resale of surplus property.                                    If an eligible entity that has acquired surplus property under Section                                           afterwards declares that property to be surplus, the surplusing entity from which the eligible                          entity acquired the property may purchase, and the eligible entity shall sell, the property as                          provided in Section                  , except that the price at which the surplusing entity may                          reacquire the property shall be the price that the eligible entity paid for the property, plus the                             cost of any existing improvements that the eligible entity made to the property after it                          purchased the property.                                    If the surplusing entity does not reacquire the surplus property under Subsection                              and the eligible entity sells the surplus property to another buyer, the eligible entity and the                          surplusing entity shall equally share any proceeds of that sale that exceed the amount the                          eligible entity paid for the property plus the cost of any existing improvements the eligible                          entity made to the property after it purchased the property.                               Section  .    Section                is enacted to read                            ARTICLE   .  SURPLUS STATE LAND                                       .  Compliance with Surplus Lands Act required.                                If a department, division, or other agency of the state declares land owned by the state                           to be surplus, the department, division, or other agency shall comply with the requirements of                           Title   , Chapter   , Surplus Lands Act.
