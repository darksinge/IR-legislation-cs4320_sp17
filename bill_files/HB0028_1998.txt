H.B. 28









1     APPROPRIATION FOR DAVIS COUNTY


2     CONFERENCE AND COMMUNITY CENTER




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor:  Blake D. Chard


6    AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $2,000,000 FROM THE

7    GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 1998-99 TO THE DEPARTMENT OF

8    COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FOR THE DAVIS COUNTY

9    CONFERENCE AND COMMUNITY CENTER; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

10    This act enacts uncodified material.

11    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12        Section 1.   Appropriation.

13        There is appropriated $2,000,000 from the General Fund for fiscal year 1998-99 to the

14    Department of Community and Economic Development to be disbursed for the construction of the

15    Davis County Conference and Community Center to help fulfill the statewide public purposes of

16    contributing to the state's economic growth and providing conference and recreation facilities for

17    the people of the state.

18        Section 2.   Effective date.

19        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
