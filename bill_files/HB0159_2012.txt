 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Criminal Code regarding the offense of unlawful detention.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    creates the offense of unlawful detention of a minor when a person 18 years of age
            
13

    
or older causes a minor who is 14 or 15 years of age to stay with the person without
            
14

    
the consent of the minor's parent or guardian, and in a situation that is not the
            
15

    
offense of kidnapping.
            
16

17

    
    None
