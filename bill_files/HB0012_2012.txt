 Committee Note:

    The Education Interim Committee recommended this bill.

 General Description:
            
11

    
    This bill modifies provisions addressing the education of persons in the custody of the
            
12

    
Utah Department of Corrections.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    removes the State Board of Regents' responsibility for the education of persons in
            
16

    
the custody of the Utah Department of Corrections;
            
17

    
    .    provides that the State Office of Education and Utah Department of Corrections
            
18

    
shall collaborate with the State Board of Regents and other entities in preparing and
            
19

    
implementing a recidivism reduction plan;
            
20

    
    .    requires the Utah Department of Corrections to report on the impact of corrections
            
21

    
education programs on recidivism; and
            
22

    
    .    makes technical amendments.
            
23

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
24

    
    None
