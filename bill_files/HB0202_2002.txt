RECONVEYANCE OF TRUST DEED OR                           RELEASE OF MORTGAGE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Thomas V. Hatch                             This act amends Title   , Real Estate.  The act adds a definition of  deliver.   The act                          eliminates the requirement that a title insurer or title agent wait    days after payment in                          full of an obligation secured by a trust deed or mortgage to give to the beneficiary,                          mortgagee, or loan servicer a notice of intent to release or reconvey.  This act makes                           technical corrections.
