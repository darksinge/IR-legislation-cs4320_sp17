Bramble                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Registration and Protection of Trademarks and Service Marks Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    prohibits the use of a registrant s mark to deliver or display an advertisement in                           Utah                                    .    that is in response to a request submitted to an interactive information service                            and                                   .    that is a bad faith attempt to divert a consumer from the registrant s goods or                           services                                .    allows a registrant to file an action to enjoin delivery or display in the state of the                           advertisement  and                               .    makes technical corrections.
