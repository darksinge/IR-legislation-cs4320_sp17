S.B. 6 Enrolled




    
 RETIREMENT AND INDEPENDENT ENTITIES BASE BUDGET


    2013 GENERAL SESSION

    STATE OF UTAH


     Chief Sponsor: Todd Weiler

    House Sponsor: Kraig Powell


 .....................................................................
LONG TITLE
 General Description:
    This bill appropriates funds for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal
year beginning July 1, 2013 and ending June 30, 2014.
 Highlighted Provisions:
    This bill:
    .    provides appropriations for the use and support of certain state agencies;
    .    provides appropriations for other purposes as described; and
    .    approves employment levels for internal service funds.
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
    This bill appropriates $39,161,500 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2014,
including:
    .    $3,408,500 from the General Fund;
    .    $17,235,500 from the Education Fund;
    .    $18,517,500 from various sources as detailed in this bill.
    This bill appropriates $11,494,200 in business-like activities for fiscal year 2014.
 Other Special Clauses:
    This bill takes effect on July 1, 2013.
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
    ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL
