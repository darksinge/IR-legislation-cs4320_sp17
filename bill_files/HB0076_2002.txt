PROCESS FOR CREATION OF NEW SCHOOL                           DISTRICTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   David N. Cox                             This act modifies provisions related to public schools. The act provides a process for creating                          new school districts. It also provides for the transfer of property to and the assumption of                          indebtedness by the new school district and for the election of new school board members.                           The act takes effect July  ,     .
