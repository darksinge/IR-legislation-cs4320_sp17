 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies requirements governing voter registration.
            
9

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
10

    
    This bill:
            
11

    
    .    modifies the voter registration form and the driver license voter registration form to
            
12

    
include a citizen affidavit and a notice of penalties for illegal registration;
            
13

    
    .    requires the county clerk to review the submitted voter registration forms and refer
            
14

    
them to the county attorney if the clerk believes the applicant is seeking to register
            
15

    
to vote illegally; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical amendments.
            
17

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
18

    
    None
            
19

    
 Other Special Clauses:
