 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 Committee Note:
            
9

    
    The Veterans Reintegration Task Force recommended this bill.
            
10

    
        Membership:    5 legislators    15 non-legislators
            
11

    
        Legislative Vote:    5 voting for    0 voting against    0 absent
            
12

    
 General Description:
            
13

    
    This bill requires a college or university within the state system of higher education to
            
14

    
notify veterans applying for admission that in order to receive credit for military
            
15

    
training and experience, the veteran is required to meet with a college counselor.
            
16

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
17

    
    This bill:
            
18

    
    .    requires institutions of higher education to provide a written notification process for
            
19

    
advising veterans of certain requirements necessary to receive college credit for
            
20

    
military training and experience.
            
21

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
22

    
    None
            
23

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
24

    
    None
            
25

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
26

    
AMENDS:
            
27

    
     
53B-16-107
, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2013, Chapter 126

            
28


    

            
29

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
30

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
53B-16-107

 is amended to read:
            
31

    
     53B-16-107.  Credit for military service and training -- Notification --
            
32

    
 Transferability -- Reporting.
            
33

    
    (1)  As used in this section, "credit" includes proof of equivalent noncredit course
            
34

    
completion awarded by the Utah College of Applied Technology.
            
35

    
    (2)  An institution of higher education listed in Section 

53B-2-101


 shall [award]
            
36

    
provide written notification to each student applying for admission that the student is required
            
37

    
to meet with a college counselor in order to receive credit for military service and training as
            
38

    
recommended by a postsecondary accreditation agency or association designated by the State
            
39

    
Board of Regents or the Utah College of Applied Technology Board of Trustees if:
            
40

    
    (a)  credit for military service and training is requested by the student; and
            
41

    
    (b)  the student has met with an advisor at an institution of higher education listed in
            
42

    
Section 

53B-2-101


 at which the student intends to enroll to discuss applicability of credit to
            
43

    
program requirements, possible financial aid implications, and other factors that may impact
            
44

    
attainment of the student's educational goals.
            
45

    
    (3)  Upon transfer within the state system of higher education, a student may present a
            
46

    
transcript to the receiving institution for evaluation and to determine the applicability of credit
            
47

    
to the student's program of study, and the receiving institution shall evaluate the credit to be
            
48

    
transferred pursuant to Subsection (2).
            
49

    
    (4)  The State Board of Regents and the Utah College of Applied Technology Board of
            
50

    
Trustees shall annually report the number of credits awarded under this section by each
            
51

    
institution of higher education to the Education Interim Committee and the Utah Department of
            
52

    
Veterans' Affairs.
