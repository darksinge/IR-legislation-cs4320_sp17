APPROPRIATION FOR PIONEER DISPLAY                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Brian R. Allen          AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS  APPROPRIATING    ,    TO THE DIVISION         OF FACILITIES CONSTRUCTION AND MANAGEMENT FOR A STATE CAPITOL         EXHIBIT  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.         This act enacts uncodified material.         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .   Appropriation.              There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year            ,    to the          Division of Facilities Construction and Management to be distributed to the Pioneers of Progress          Awards Committee to construct an exhibit in the state capitol honoring present and past Utah          citizens who have made significant scientific and technological contributions to the history of Utah          or who, over the course of their lifetime, made significant contributions to their community.              Section  .   Effective date.              If approved by two thirds of all the members elected to each house, this act takes effect          upon approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah          Constitution Article VII, Section  , without the governor s signature, or in the case of a veto, the          date of veto override.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
