    This bill modifies nonhazardous solid waste disposal fees and reduces the amount of

    .    reduces the amount of waste disposal fees that are deposited into the General Fund;

    .    modifies provisions relating to the payment of fees on wastes containing
            
16

    
polychlorinated biphenyls;
