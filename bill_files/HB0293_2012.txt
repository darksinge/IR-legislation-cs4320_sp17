 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Transportation Code by amending provisions relating to vehicle
            
11

    
oversize and overweight permits.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    provides an exception to the restriction that the draw-bar or other connection
            
15

    
between any two vehicles, one of which is towing or drawing the other on a
            
16

    
highway, may not exceed 15 feet in length from one vehicle to the other when
            
17

    
operating under an oversize or overweight permit;
            
18

    
    .    authorizes the Department of Transportation to issue an oversize permit for a
            
19

    
vehicle or combination of vehicles that exceeds the draw-bar length restriction; and
            
20

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
21

    None
