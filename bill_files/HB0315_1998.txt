6    AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC OFFICERS; PROVIDING RESTRICTIONS ON

7    SEVERANCE PAY TO OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES OF GOVERNMENTAL ENTITIES.

8    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

9    ENACTS:

10         52-5-101, Utah Code Annotated 1953

11         52-5-102, Utah Code Annotated 1953

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.  Section  52-5-101 is enacted to read:

14     CHAPTER 5.  COMPENSATION


15         52-5-101.  Definitions.

16        As used in this chapter:

17        (1)  "Employee" includes a governmental entity's officers and employees.

18        (2)  "Governmental entity" means:

19        (a)  the entities included in the definition of "governmental entity" under Subsection

20    36-21-1(1); and

21        (b)  the Legislature.

22        (3) (a)  "Severance pay" means money or other form of remuneration paid or given to an

23    employee at the time of termination of employment with a governmental entity.

24        (b)  "Severance pay" does not include accrued:

25        (i)  vacation pay;

26        (ii)  compensatory pay; or

27        (iii)  sick pay.


1        Section 2.  Section  52-5-102 is enacted to read:

2         52-5-102.  Limitation on severance pay.

3        A governmental entity may not grant severance pay to an employee that exceeds the

4    amount of the regular salary or wage received by the employee during the employee's last six

5    months of employment.
