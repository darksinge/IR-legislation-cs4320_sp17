 General Description:

 Highlighted Provisions:

14

     76-5-102.6.  Propelling substance or object at a correctional or peace officer --
            
26

    
 Penalties.
            
27

    
    (1)  Any prisoner or person detained pursuant to Section 

77-7-15

 who throws or
            
28

    
otherwise propels any substance or object at a peace or correctional officer is guilty of a class A
            
29

    
misdemeanor, except as provided under Subsection (2).

            
30

    (a)  the object or substance is:
