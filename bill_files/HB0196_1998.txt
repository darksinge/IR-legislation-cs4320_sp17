14        [(a)] (1)  a maximum of two years of disability benefits calculated under this chapter;

15        [(b)] (2)  during the period of monthly disability benefits not to exceed two years, payment

16    of a maximum of $10,000 for psychiatric expenses, including rehabilitation expenses approved by

17    the board's consultants; and

18        [(c)] (3)  if the employee is institutionalized, payment of disability benefits according to

19    contractual provisions for a period not to exceed five years.

20        Section 11.   Effective date.

21        If approved by two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, this act takes effect

22    upon approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah

23    Constitution Article VII, Section 8, without the governor's signature, or in the case of a veto, the

24    date of veto override.
