SEISMIC UPGRADE REQUIREMENTS FOR                           CONDOMINIUM CONVERSION PROJECTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Larry B. Wiley                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires a seismic evaluation for a structure that is converted to a                           condominium.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a seismic evaluation for a structure that is converted to a condominium                                .    requires the seismic evaluation to be performed                                    .    by a licensed professional structural engineer  and                                   .    under a standard recognized by the International Building Code for the seismic                           evaluation of an existing building or the seismic rehabilitation of an existing                           building  and                               .    provides certain other requirements for the structure owner if the seismic evaluation                           identifies an item that is not in compliance with the applicable standard.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                          , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is enacted to read                                       .  Condominium conversion    Seismic evaluation    Other requirements.                                    If a structure is converted to a condominium in accordance with the provisions                          under this chapter, the structure owner shall cause a seismic evaluation to be performed by a                          person licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Professional Engineers and Professional Land                          Surveyors Licensing Act, as a professional structural engineer.                                    The seismic evaluation described under Subsection     shall be performed under a                          standard recognized by the International Building Code for the seismic evaluation of an                          existing building or the seismic rehabilitation of an existing building.                                    If the seismic evaluation described under Subsection     identifies an item that is                          not in compliance with the applicable standard, the structure owner shall, prior to occupancy of                          the converted condominium                                 a   review the item of noncompliance with the appropriate county or municipality                                 b   correct the item of noncompliance  and                                c   comply with applicable county or municipality requirements, including                                 i   submitting the converted condominium to an inspection  and                                ii   obtaining an approval or a permit.
