CORPORATE FRANCHISE AND INCOME TAX                           CREDITS FOR BIODIESEL FUEL                           PRODUCTION                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Michael E. Noel                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                           General Description                                This bill modifies the Revenue and Taxation Code by creating two corporate franchise                           and income tax credits for the production of biodiesel fuel.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides definitions                                .    creates a nonrefundable corporate franchise and income tax credit for                                    .    the machinery, equipment, or materials used to construct or remodel a biodiesel                           production facility  and                                   .    the production of biodiesel that is distributed for use in Utah                                .    provides that the tax credits may be carried forward for a three taxable year period                           under certain circumstances  and                               .    requires the State Tax Commission to make rules for                                    .    qualification for the tax credit  and                                   .    verification of amounts of the tax credit claimed.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   This bill takes effect for taxable years beginning on or after January  ,     .
