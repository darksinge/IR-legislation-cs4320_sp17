TOBACCO AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Carl R. Saunders                             This act modifies provision related to tobacco.  The act removes the limitation on money                          spent enforcing the prohibition of tobacco sales to minors.  The act makes technical                          amendments.
