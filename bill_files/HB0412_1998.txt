H.B. 412





1     RETIREMENT AMENDMENTS




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor:  Blake D. Chard


5    AN ACT RELATING TO RETIREMENT; PROVIDING FOR CERTAIN RETIREMENT

6    SERVICE CREDIT TRANSFERS BETWEEN RETIREMENT SYSTEMS FOR YOUTH

7    CORRECTIONS EMPLOYEES.

8    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

9    ENACTS:

10         49-4a-207, Utah Code Annotated 1953

11    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12        Section 1.  Section  49-4a-207 is enacted to read:

13         49-4a-207.  Service credit conversion -- Youth corrections employees.

14        (1)  A youth corrections employee with current employment in that position as of January

15    1, 1998 may elect to transfer the employee's retirement service credits from any other system under

16    this title to the system under this chapter if:

17        (a)  the service credits were earned between January, 1, 1985 and June 30, 1998 while the

18    employee was a youth corrections employee;

19        (b)  the employee applies to the retirement office, in writing, by June 30, 1998, for the

20    transfer of the service credits; and

21        (c)  the employee is certified by the Peace Officer Standards and Training Council by

22    September 30, 1998.

23        (2) (a)  The actual costs of the transfers described in this section shall be presented by the

24    board to the Legislature prior to its 1999 General Session.

25        (b)  The transfers described in this section shall be effective on July 1, 1999.
