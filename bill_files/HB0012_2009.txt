 General Description:
            
12

    
    This bill modifies provisions in Title 17, Counties, relating to the qualifications of a
            
13

    
county sheriff.
            
14

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
15

    
    This bill:
            
16

    
    .    provides new certification requirements for county sheriffs elected after the 2008
            
17

    
regular general election;
            
18

    
    .    requires a person filing a declaration of candidacy for county sheriff to submit a
            
19

    
certificate issued by the Peace Officer Standards and Training Division stating that
            
20

    
the candidate:
            
21

    
        .    has successfully met the standards and training requirements established for law
            
22

    
enforcement officers in Title 53, Chapter 6, Part 2, Peace Officer Training and
            
23

    
Certification Act; and
            
24

    
        .    has qualified to be certified as a law enforcement officer, as defined in Section
            
25

    

53-13-103

; 
            
26

    
    .    requires an elected county sheriff to be certified at the time of taking office as:
            
27

    
        .    a correctional officer, as defined in Section 

53-13-104

; or

            
28


    
        .    a correctional facility manager by having completed a correctional facility
            
29

    
management course approved by the POST Council; and
            
30

    
    .    requires a county sheriff to remain certified as a law enforcement officer and as a
            
31

    
correctional officer or correctional facility manager during the sheriff's term of
            
32

    
office.
