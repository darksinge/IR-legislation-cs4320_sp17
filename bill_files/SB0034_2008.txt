 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Impaired Persons Licensing Act by amending provisions relating to
            
11

    
notifying the Driver License Division of an impairment.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    provides that a person may notify the Driver License Division if the person is aware
            
15

    
of a physical, mental, or emotional impairment of another person that is an imminent
            
16

    
threat to driver safety;
            
17

    
    .    provides that the Driver License Division may require a person that is the subject of
            
18

    
a notification to submit to certain medical reports or certain tests;
            
19

    
    .    provides that, if requested by the person making a notification, the notification is a
            
20

    
protected record and may not be disclosed by the division;
            
21

    
    .    prohibits the Driver License Division from accepting an anonymous notification;
            
22

    
    .    grants the Driver License Division rulemaking authority to establish a procedure to
            
23

    
make a protected notification; and
            
24

    
    .    provides that it is a class C misdemeanor to make a notification with the intent to
            
25

    
annoy, intimidate, or harass a person.
            
26
