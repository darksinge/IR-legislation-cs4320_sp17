STATE AND LOCAL AGENCIES  CRIME                           REDUCTION PLANS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   John E. Swallow                           AN ACT RELATING TO CRIMINAL LAW AND STATE AFFAIRS  REQUIRING STATE                          AND LOCAL CRIMINAL JUSTICE AGENCIES TO CREATE PLANS FOR THE REDUCTION                          OF CRIME  PROVIDING STRATEGIC PLANNING CONFERENCES TO AID LOCAL                          CRIMINAL JUSTICE AGENCIES WITH THIS PLANNING  PROVIDING INCENTIVE                           FUNDING TO IMPLEMENT THE PLANS  AND APPROPRIATING     ,    FOR FISCAL                           YEAR           TO THE COMMISSION ON CRIMINAL AND JUVENILE JUSTICE TO                           FUND THESE ACTIVITIES.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                     a    , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                ENACTS                                     a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section         a         is amended to read                                      a    .  Executive director    Qualifications    Compensation    Appointment                              Functions.                                    The governor, with the advice and consent of the Senate, shall appoint a person                           experienced in the field of criminal justice and in administration as the executive director of the                              Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice.  The governor shall establish the executive                           director s salary within the salary range fixed by the Legislature in Title   , Chapter   , State                           Officer Compensation.                                    a   The executive director, under the direction of the commission, shall administer the                           duties of the commission, including those outlined in Sections          a         and          a        , and                           act as the governor s advisor on state, regional, metropolitan, and local government planning as it                           relates to criminal justice.                                b  This chapter does not derogate the planning authority conferred on state, regional,                           metropolitan, and local governments by existing law.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                            Part  .  State and Local Agencies  Crime Reduction Plans                                     a    .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Commission  means the Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice.                                     Executive director  means the executive director of the Commission on Criminal and                          Juvenile Justice.                                     Local criminal justice agency  means each sheriff s and police chief s law enforcement                          agency.                                     State criminal justice agency  means the Department of Public Safety, Department                          of Corrections, the Division of Youth Corrections, and the Department of Human Services.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  State criminal justice agencies    Reporting Requirements.                               A state criminal justice agency shall submit a crime reduction plan each year on or before                          September   to the executive director.  The plan shall contain information about how the agency                          plans to reduce crime victimization in its jurisdiction by     each year and     over the next five                          years.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Notification of requirements and grant funds.                                    The executive director shall send a letter on or before July  ,     , notifying                                 a   the director of each state criminal justice agency of the statutory requirement to create                          a crime reduction plan each year  and                                   b   the chief law enforcement official of each local criminal justice agency of the                          availability of and requirements to apply for grant money to implement a crime reduction plan.                                    The executive director may offer assistance to a state or local criminal justice agency                          in creating and formatting a crime reduction plan.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Strategic planning conferences for crime reduction.                                    a   The Utah Law Enforcement Strategic Planning Committee shall sponsor two                          one day strategic planning conferences regarding reducing crime victimization.                                b   The first conference shall be held before September  ,     , and the second conference                          shall be held within six months after the first conference.                                    The sponsors of the conference                                 a   shall provide three meals during each conference                                 b   may not require any fee of representatives of local criminal justice agencies, or                          representatives of a local political subdivision within which a local criminal justice agency is                          located, to attend either conference  and                                c   may not reimburse any participant for travel, lodging, or meals.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Application for grant funds    Balance nonlapsing.                               A local criminal justice agency may apply for a one time   ,    grant from the commission                          to implement its plan after                                     a local government representative and local criminal justice agency representative                          official from the jurisdiction of the local criminal justice agency have both attended two strategic                          planning conferences under Section          a          and                                    submitting to the commission a plan to reduce crime victimization in its jurisdiction                          by     each year and     over the next five years.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                                    Except as provided in H.B.   , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated                          for fiscal year           from the General Fund to the Commission on Criminal and Juvenile                          Justice                                a       ,    to be used as grant money for local criminal justice agencies to create a crime                          reduction plan  and                                  b      ,    to cover the costs of the two conferences to be sponsored by the Utah Law                          Enforcement Strategic Planning Committee under Section          a        .                                   Any unexpended balance at the end of the fiscal year is nonlapsing.
