 DEATH PENALTY PROVISIONS

 Sponsor: Sheryl L. Allen

6

    
 LONG TITLE
            
7

    
 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill repeals use of a firing squad as a means of carrying out the death penalty.
            
9

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
10

    
    This bill:
            
11

    
    .    repeals references to the use of a firing squad, and makes the repeal retroactive; and
            
12

    
    .    makes provision for the possibility that the retroactive provision is ruled
            
13

    
unconstitutional, in which case the defendant who has selected the firing squad may
            
14

    
be so executed.
            
15

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
16

    
    None
            
17

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
18

    
    This bill provides an immediate effective date.
            
19

    
    This bill provides revisor instructions.
            
20

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
21
