 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill provides that only the manufacturer of a product may be liable for that product.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    provides that a manufacturer may not be liable for a product unless the claimant
            
13

    
proves by a preponderance of the evidence that the defendant was the actual
            
14

    
manufacturer of the actual product that caused the harm.
