Supervisors shall be elected to four year terms of office  provided, that at the initial         election of supervisors under this part two of the supervisors elected shall be elected to only two         year terms of office.  The two candidates at such initial election who receive the fourth and fifth          largest number of votes are deemed elected to the two year terms of office.
