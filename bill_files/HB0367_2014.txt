 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill adds dry needling to a physical therapist's scope of practice.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    amends the definition of "therapeutic intervention" in the Physical Therapy Practice
            
13

    
Act to include dry needling; and
            
14

    
    .    makes technical and conforming amendments.
            
15

    None
