UTAH LAKE AUTHORITY LEGISLATIVE                           TASK FORCE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Glenn L. Way                             This act creates the Utah Lake Authority Legislative Task Force.  The act provides for                          membership of the task force and compensation of members.  The act outlines the                          responsibilities and duties of the task force.  The act provides for reports to various                          legislative committees.  The act appropriates    ,    from the General Fund for fiscal year                                  .  The act provides for staffing of the task force.  The act is repealed November   ,                               .                           This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Utah Lake Authority Legislative Task Force    Creation    Membership                              Interim rules followed    Compensation    Staff.                                    There is created the Utah Lake Authority Legislative Task Force consisting of the                          following    members                                 a   five members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          three of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   seven members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the House                          of Representatives, no more than four of whom may be from the same political party.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed under                          Subsection     a  as a cochair of the committee.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the committee.                                    In conducting its business, the committee shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees.                                       Salaries and expenses of the members of the committee shall be paid in accordance                          with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support to                          the committee.                               Section  .   Duties    Interim report.                                    The committee shall review and make recommendations on the following issues                                 a   the feasibility of creating a lake authority for the management and development of Utah                          Lake                                 b   possible funding source for a Utah Lake Authority other than general tax revenues,                          including fees on property that would be enhanced by the development of the lake and fees from                          additional water capacity in the lake                                 c   the feasibility and effects of dredging portions of the lake and creating islands in the                          lake                                 d   the construction of a causeway across the lake and potential funding sources for the                          causeway, including the collection of tolls                                 e   the potential economic development benefits of improvements to the lake  and                                f   the potential for increased water storage and conservation in the lake.                                    A final report, including any proposed legislation shall be presented before November                            ,     , to the                                 a   Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee                                 b   Revenue and Taxation Interim Committee                                 c   Workforce Services and Economic Development Interim Committee  and                                d   Legislative Management Committee.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        , a one time                          appropriation of                                      ,    to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the                          committee                                      ,    to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses of                          representatives on the committee  and                                      ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for staffing                             the committee.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This act is repealed November   ,     .       Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
