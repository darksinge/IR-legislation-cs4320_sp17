 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies provisions of the Utah Construction Trades Licensing Act related to
            
11

    
the installation, repair, maintenance, cleaning, or replacement of a residential or
            
12

    
commercial gas appliance or combustion system.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    modifies the definition of a combustion system to specifically include air ducts; and
            
16

    
    .    modifies provisions related to the work and scope of practice in the installation,
            
17

    
repair, maintenance, cleaning, or replacement of a residential or commercial
            
18

    
combustion system by requiring certification for maintenance and cleaning in
            
19

    
addition to the current certification required for installation, repair, or replacement
            
20

    
of a combustion system.
            
21
