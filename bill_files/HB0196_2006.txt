Section                 is enacted to read                            Part  .  Notice of Presence of Military Installation                                        .  Recording notice of presence of military installation.                                    A person may record notice of the presence of a military installation on the record                             for a parcel of real property that abuts the military installation in the office of the county                          recorder for the county in which the property is located.                                    A notice of the presence of a military installation recorded under Subsection                              may contain a description of the type of activities occurring on the military installation and any                          possible nuisances the activities may cause.
