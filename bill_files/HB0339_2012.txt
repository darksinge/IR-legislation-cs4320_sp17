    This bill modifies provisions in Title 20A, Election Code, related to election ballots.

    This bill:

    .    provides a definition;

    .    requires an election officer to include a business reply mail envelope with an

    .    requires an elections officer to ensure that an absentee or provisional ballot is not

accessible until the ballot is counted; and

    .    makes technical changes.
