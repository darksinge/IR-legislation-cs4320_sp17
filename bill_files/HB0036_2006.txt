 General Description:

    This bill requires notice for certain transfers of money out of utility funds.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    requires towns, cities, counties, and special districts, to provide notice, under certain
            
14

    
circumstances, before transferring money out of a utility fund;

    .    describes the scope of notice required; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
