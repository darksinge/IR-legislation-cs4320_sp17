 General Description:

    This bill establishes an employment first priority policy for a person with a disability, a
            
10

    
person with a mental illness, and a person seeking treatment for a substance abuse
            
11

    
problem within the Employment Support Act and the Substance Abuse and Mental
            
12

    
Health Act.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    defines terms;
            
16

    
    .    requires the Department of Workforce Services and Division of Substance Abuse
            
17

    
and Mental Health to, when providing services to a person with a disability, a
            
18

    
person with a mental illness, or a person seeking treatment for a substance abuse
            
19

    
problem, give priority to providing services that assist the person in obtaining and
            
20

    
retaining meaningful and gainful employment;
            
21

    
    .    requires the entities described in the preceding paragraph to:

paragraph; and

25

    
determine whether the goals for the previous year have been met, and modify
            
26

    
the plan as needed; and
