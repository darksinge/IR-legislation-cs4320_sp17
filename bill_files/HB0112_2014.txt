 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends the Utah Health Code and the Utah Criminal Code to modify and add
            
10

    
provisions related to electronic cigarettes.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    prohibits a person from selling or manufacturing electronic cigarettes or electronic
            
14

    
cigarette substances without a license from the Department of Health;
            
15

    
    .    prohibits a person from selling or manufacturing electronic cigarettes or electronic
            
16

    
cigarette substances that contain nicotine that is not pharmaceutical-grade nicotine;
            
17

    
    .    prohibits a person from advertising that electronic cigarettes or electronic cigarette
            
18

    
substances help an individual stop using tobacco or a product containing tobacco;
            
19

    
    .    directs the Department of Health to issue licenses to manufacture or sell electronic
            
20

    
cigarettes under certain conditions;
            
21

    
    .    provides that the Department of Health can revoke a person's license to sell
            
22

    
electronic cigarettes if the person sells an electronic cigarette or an electronic
            
23

    
cigarette substance to an individual who is less than 19 years of age;
            
24

    
    .    prohibits a business owner from allowing a minor to use an electronic cigarette in
            
25

    
the business;
            
26

    
    .    prohibits an individual who is less than 19 years of age from buying, attempting to
            
27

    
buy, or possessing an electronic cigarette product; and

            
28


    
    .    makes technical and conforming amendments.
            
29
