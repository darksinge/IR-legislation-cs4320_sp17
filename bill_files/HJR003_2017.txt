and29          WHEREAS, it is the solemn duty of the states to protect the liberty of our people -30     particularly for the generations to come - by proposing amendments to the Constitution of the31     United States through a convention of the states under Article V for the purpose of restraining32     these and related abuses of power:33          NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah34     hereby applies to Congress, under the provisions of Article V of the Constitution of the United35     States, for the calling of a convention of the states limited to proposing amendments to the36     Constitution of the United States that impose fiscal restraints on the federal government, limit37     the power and jurisdiction of the federal government, and limit the terms of office for its38     officials and for members of Congress.39          BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the President of40     the United States Senate, Speaker of the United States House of Representatives, members of41     Utah's congressional delegation, and the state legislators of the other 50 states, requesting their42     cooperation.43          BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this joint resolution constitutes a continuing44     application in accordance with Article V of the Constitution of the United States until the45     legislatures of at least two-thirds of the several states have made applications on the same46     subject.Legislative Review NoteOffice of Legislative Research and General Counsel
