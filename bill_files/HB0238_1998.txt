H.B. 238









1     TRANSIT DISTRICT AUTHORITY




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor: Ron Bigelow


5    AN ACT RELATING TO SPECIAL DISTRICTS; AUTHORIZING CERTAIN PUBLIC

6    TRANSIT DISTRICT SERVICES OUTSIDE OF A DISTRICT'S AREA.

7    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

8    AMENDS:

9         17A-2-1004, as last amended by Chapter 79, Laws of Utah 1996

10    ENACTS:

11         17A-2-1061, Utah Code Annotated 1953

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.  Section  17A-2-1004 is amended to read:

14         17A-2-1004.  Definitions.

15        As used in this part:

16        (1)  "District" means a public transit district organized under this part.

17        [(4)] (2)  "Municipality" means any incorporated city or town.

18        (3)  "Public agency" includes the state of Utah and any county, city, town, district, or other

19    public agency or entity created under the laws of this state, the federal government, and any agency

20    thereof.

21        [(2)] (4)  "Public transit" means the transportation of passengers only, and their incidental

22    baggage by means other than chartered bus, sightseeing bus, taxi, or other vehicle not on an

23    individual passenger fare paying basis.  [Nothing in this section shall be construed to prohibit the

24    district from leasing its buses to private certified public carriers, or operating transit services

25    requested by a recreational, tourist, or convention bureau provided for under Section 17-31-2 by

26    a governmental entity when the recreational, tourist, or convention bureau certifies that privately

27    owned carriers furnishing like services or operating like equipment within the area served by the


1    bureau have declined to provide the service or do not have the equipment necessary to provide the

2    service.  Nothing in this section shall be construed to prohibit the district from providing school

3    bus services for transportation of pupils and supervisory personnel between homes and school and

4    other related school activities within the area served by the district, or to prohibit the transportation

5    of passengers covered by an elderly or handicapped program within the district where all or part

6    of the transportation services are paid for by public funds.]

7        Section 2.  Section  17A-2-1061 is enacted to read:

8         17A-2-1061.  Transit district special services.

9        (1)  As used in this section, "bureau" means a recreational, tourist, or convention bureau

10    established under Section 17-31-2.

11        (2) (a)  A district may lease its buses to private certified public carriers or operate transit

12    services requested by a governmental entity when a bureau certifies that privately-owned carriers

13    furnishing like services or operating like equipment within the area served by the bureau have

14    declined to provide the service or do not have the equipment necessary to provide the service.

15        (b)  A district may lease its buses or operate services as authorized under Subsection (2)(a)

16    outside of the area served by the district.

17        (3)  A district may provide school bus services for transportation of pupils and supervisory

18    personnel between homes and school and other related school activities within the area served by

19    the district, or may provide the transportation of passengers covered by an elderly or disabled

20    persons program within the district where all or part of the transportation services are paid for by

21    public funds.
