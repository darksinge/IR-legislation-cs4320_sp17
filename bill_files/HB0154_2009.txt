Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the definition of final completion of an original contract and project                                .    modifies the subcontractor preliminary notice requirements                                .    modifies the notice of commencement filing requirements                                .    modifies the DOPL standardized building permit numbering system                                .    prohibits a compliance agency from deviating from the DOPL standardized building                           permit numbering system  and                                .    makes technical corrections.
