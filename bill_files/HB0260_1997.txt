LICENSURE OF ESTHETICIANS AND NAIL           TECHNICIANS WITHIN THE           COSMETOLOGIST BARBERING LICENSING ACT                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Orville D. Carnahan          AN ACT RELATING TO OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS  REQUIRING         ESTHETICIANS AND NAIL TECHNICIANS TO BE LICENSED  SETTING FORTH         REQUIREMENTS FOR LICENSURE AS AN ESTHETICIAN OR NAIL TECHNICIAN           BRINGING ESTHETICIANS AND NAIL TECHNICIANS WITHIN THE JURISDICTION          OF THE COSMETOLOGY BARBERING BOARD  AMENDING THE COMPOSITION OF          THE COSMETOLOGY BARBERING BOARD TO INCLUDE AN ESTHETICIAN AND A          NAIL TECHNICIAN  MAKING TECHNICAL AND CONFORMING AMENDMENTS  AND          PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE. Section       a     is amended to read                      a    .  Definitions.                   Approved cosmetologist barber apprenticeship  means an apprenticeship that meets         the requirements of Section      a     and the requirements established by rule.                   Approved esthetician apprenticeship  means an apprenticeship that meets the         requirements of Section      a     and the requirements established by rule.                   Approved nail technician apprenticeship  means an apprenticeship that meets the         requirements of Section      a     and the requirements established by rule.                         Board  means the Cosmetology Barbering Licensing Board created in Section              a    .                          Cosmetologist barber  means a person who is licensed under this chapter to          engage in the practice of cosmetology barbering.                          Cosmetologist barber apprentice  means a person who is licensed under this          chapter to engage in the practice of cosmetology barbering in an approved cosmetologist barber          apprenticeship.                          Cosmetologist barber instructor  means a cosmetologist barber who is licensed          under this chapter to teach cosmetology barbering at a licensed cosmetology barber school.                          Direct supervision  means that the supervisor of the cosmetology barber          apprentice or instructor of a cosmetology barber student is immediately available for consultation,          advice, instruction, and evaluation.                          Electrologist  means a person who is licensed under this chapter to engage in          the practice of electrology.                     Esthetician  means an individual who is licensed under this chapter to engage in          practice as an esthetician.                     Esthetician apprentice  means an individual who is licensed under this chapter to          engage in practice as an esthetician in an approved esthetician apprenticeship.                     Esthetician instructor  means an esthetician licensed under this chapter to teach the          practice of estheticians in a licensed esthetician school.                     Esthetician school  means an esthetician school licensed under this chapter.                           Licensed cosmetology barber school  means a cosmetology barber school          licensed under this chapter.                     Nail technician  means an individual who is licensed under this chapter to engage                 in practice as a nail technician.                    Nail technician apprentice  means an individual who is licensed under this chapter         to engage in practice as a nail technician in an approved nail technician apprenticeship.                    Nail technician instructor  means a nail technician licensed under this chapter to         teach the practice of nail technicians in a licensed nail technician school.                    Nail technician school  means a nail technician school licensed under this chapter.                           Practice of cosmetology barbering  or  practice as a cosmetologist barber          means               a   styling, arranging, dressing, curling, waving, permanent waving, cleansing, singeing,          bleaching, dyeing, tinting, coloring, or similarly treating the hair of the head of a person                b   cutting, clipping, or trimming the hair by the use of scissors, shears, clippers, or other          appliances                c   arching eyebrows, or tinting eyebrows or eyelashes, or both                d   removing hair from the face, neck, shoulders, arms, or legs of a person by the use of          depilatories or shaving equipment                e   cutting, curling, styling, fitting, measuring, or forming caps for wigs or hairpieces or          both on the human head  or               f   practicing hair weaving or hair fusing or servicing previously medically implanted hair.                            Practice of cosmetology barbering instruction  means instructing          cosmetology barbering in a licensed cosmetology barber school.                            Practice of electrology  means the removal of superfluous hair from the body          of a person by the use of electricity.                    a    Practice as an esthetician  or  practice of estheticians  means to enhance the          appearance of the face, neck, arms, legs, and shoulders of an individual by topically massaging,          cleaning, or stimulating those areas with or without the use of compounds or procedures including          makeup, eyelashes, depilatories, tonics, lotions, waxes, sanding, and tweezing, whether performed          by manual, mechanical, chemical, or electrical means and instruments.               b    Practice as an esthetician  does not mean the practice of electrology as defined this          section.                     Practice as a nail technician  or  practice of nail technicians  means to cut, clean,          manicure, shape, massage, or enhance the appearance of the nails of the hands or feet of an                 individual including the application and removal of sculptured or artificial nails.                           Recognized cosmetology barber school  means a cosmetology barber school         located in another state, whose students, upon graduation, are recognized as having completed the         educational requirements for licensure in that state.                           Recognized electrology school  means an electrology school               a   located in this state whose curriculum consists of     hours of instruction approved         by rule  or              b   located in another state               i   whose curriculum consists of     hours of instruction  and               ii   whose students, upon graduation, are recognized as having completed the educational          requirements for licensure in that state.                     Recognized esthetician school  means an esthetician school                a   located in this state whose curriculum consists of     hours of instruction approved          by rule  or               b   located in another state                i   whose curriculum consists of     hours of instruction  and               ii   whose students, upon graduation, are recognized as having completed the educational          requirements for licensure in that state.                     Recognized nail technician school  means a nail technician school                a   located in this state whose curriculum consists of     hours of instruction approved          by rule  or               b   located in another state                i   whose curriculum consists of     hours of instruction  and               ii   whose students, upon graduation, are recognized as having completed the educational          requirements for licensure in that state.                            Cosmetology barber shop  Shop  means a place, shop, or establishment in          which  cosmetology, barbering, or both, are practiced  a licensee under this chapter is engaged in          the practice of cosmetology barbering, electrology, estheticians, or nail technicians.                            Unlawful conduct  is as defined in Section         .                            Unprofessional conduct  is as defined in Sections          and      a              and as may be further defined by rule.                     Section  . There is created the Cosmetology Barbering Licensing Board consisting of               a   three cosmetologists barbers               b   one cosmetologist barber instructor               c   one representative of a cosmetology barber school               d   one electrologist   and               e   one esthetician               f   one nail technician  and                e    g   one member from the general public.                   The board shall be appointed and serve in accordance with Section         .                   The duties and responsibilities of the board are in accordance with Sections                   and         .  In addition, the board shall designate one of its members on a permanent or rotating          basis to                a   assist the division in reviewing complaints concerning the unlawful or unprofessional          conduct of a licensee  and               b   advise the division in its investigation of these complaints.                   A board member who has, under Subsection    , reviewed a complaint or advised in          its investigation may be disqualified from participating with the board when the board serves as          a presiding officer in an adjudicative proceeding concerning the complaint.
