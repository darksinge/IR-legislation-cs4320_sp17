ALLOCATION OF STATE HOSPITAL BEDS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Jack A. Seitz                             This act amends the Human Services Code.  The act amends the number of psychiatric and                          geriatric beds at the state hospital allocated to local mental health authorities.  The act                          sunsets the reduced number on June   ,      and requires the department to increase the                          number of beds allocated during fiscal year      as funding permits.  The act has an                          immediate effective date.
