Section   .    Section         a    .      is enacted to read                                      a    . .  Violations    Civil proceedings and penalties    Orders.                                     A person who violates any provision of this chapter or any order, permit, plan approval,                           or rule issued or adopted under this chapter is subject to a civil penalty of not more than    ,                                 per day for each day of violation as determined in a civil hearing under Title   , Chapter   b,                           Administrative Procedures Act, except                                  a   any violation of Subsection          a    .         or    , regarding landfills, is subject to the                           penalty under Subsection          a    .         rather than the penalties under this section  and                                 b   any violation of Subsection          a           ,    , or     regarding payment of the                           recycling fee by the tire retailer is subject to penalties as provided in Subsection          a                                      rather than the penalties under this section.                                     The board may bring an action in the name of the state to restrain a person from                           continuing a violation of this chapter and to require the person to perform necessary remediation                           regarding a violation of this chapter.                                     When the executive secretary finds a situation exists in violation of this chapter that                           presents an immediate threat to the public health or welfare, the executive secretary may issue an                           emergency order under Title   , Chapter   b, Administrative Procedures Act.                                     The executive secretary may revoke the registration of a waste tire recycler or                           transporter who violates any provision of this chapter or any order, plan approval, permit, or rule                           issued or adopted under this chapter.                                     The executive secretary may revoke the tire storage permit for a storage facility that                           is in violation of any provision of this chapter or any order, plan approval, permit, or rule issued                           or adopted under this chapter.                                     If a person has been convicted of violating a provision of this chapter prior to a finding                           by the executive secretary of a violation of the same provision in an administrative hearing, the                           executive secretary may not assess a civil monetary penalty under this section for the same offense                           for which the conviction was obtained.                                     All penalties collected under this section shall be deposited in the trust fund.                                Section   .
