APPROPRIATION FOR RURAL GROWTH                           MANAGEMENT                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Keele  Johnson                           AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS  APPROPRIATING     ,    TO THE OFFICE                          OF PLANNING AND BUDGET TO SUPPORT THE GOVERNOR S   ST CENTURY                          COMMUNITIES INITIATIVE AND TO PROVIDE REGIONAL CIRCUIT RIDER PLANNERS                          TO ASSIST RURAL COUNTIES AND COMMUNITIES WITH PLANNING NEEDS                            PROVIDING THAT THE FUND BE NONLAPSING  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE                           DATE.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Legislative intent.                               The Legislature supports the objectives of the Governor s   st Century Communities                          Initiative of preparing rural areas of the state for population and visitor growth, creating new jobs,                          reducing unemployment, diversifying rural economies, and protecting quality of life.  It is the                          intent of the Legislature in appropriating these funds to help meet the statewide public purpose of                          assisting local government leaders to deal with the economic development, community and land                          planning, technology, and growth concerns of local government through the Governor s   st                          Century Communities Initiative.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                                    Except as provided in H.B.   , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated                          from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,     ,    to the Office of Planning and Budget                          for purposes stated in Subsection    .                                   a   The appropriated funds shall be used to                                i   help meet the objectives of the Governor s   st Century Communities Initiative                                ii   provide local government officials with training and certification opportunities and                             materials                                iii   provide professional circuit rider planners                                iv   provide tools and expertise to assist with the planning, economic development, land                          use, technology, and growth management concerns of local government                                v   provide information exchange, research, data, and linkages between various                          government entities, including local, state, and federal agencies, as well as between private and                          nonprofit entities involved in providing services and information to local communities  and                               vi   provide for administration expenses, recognition and awards to communities, meeting                          expenses of the Governor s Rural Partnership Board, and other administrative expenses relating                          to meeting the objectives of the   st Century Communities Initiative.                               b   The Office of Planning and Budget shall determine, in conjunction with the Governor s                          Rural Partnership Board, the allocation of appropriated funds to best meet the purposes stated in                          Subsection     a .                                   The money appropriated in Subsection     is nonlapsing.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
