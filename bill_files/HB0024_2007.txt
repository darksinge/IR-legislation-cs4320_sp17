Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    deletes outdated provisions in the definitions section of the Employment Support                           Act regarding  passenger vehicle  and  average monthly number of families   and                               .    deletes a reporting provision to an entity that no longer exists, the Utah Tomorrow                           Strategic Planning Committee.
