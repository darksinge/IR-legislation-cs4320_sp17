    This bill modifies the Governmental Immunity Act of Utah by amending provisions
            
11

    
related to limitations of judgments.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    amends judgment limitation amounts to reflect amounts currently in effect as
            
15

    
adjusted by the state risk manager based on changes in the Consumer Price Index;

17

    
periodically adjusted; and
