 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions of the Utah Vital Statistics Act relating to certificates of

death.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    defines terms;

    .    replaces the term "funeral director" with the term "funeral service director";

    .    removes the authority of a "person acting as the funeral director" to engage in

     .    describes the persons who have the authority and responsibility to fulfill certain

duties relating to the signing and filing of a certificate of death, and the gathering,
            
19

    
recording, and provision of information;

    .    provides that it is a class B misdemeanor for a person who is not a funeral service
            
21

    
director employed by a licensed funeral establishment to intentionally sign the
            
22

    
portion of a certificate of death that is required to be signed by a funeral service
            
23

    
director; and

    .    makes technical changes.
