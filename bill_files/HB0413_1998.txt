H.B. 413





1     EMERGENCY SERVICES TRAFFIC CONTROL


2     SYSTEMS




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor:  Susan J. Koehn


6    AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING $800,000 FROM THE

7    GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 1998-99 TO THE DEPARTMENT OF

8    TRANSPORTATION FOR TRAFFIC CONTROL SYSTEMS; AND PROVIDING AN

9    EFFECTIVE DATE.

10    This act enacts uncodified material.

11    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12        Section 1.   Appropriation.

13        (1)  There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year 1998-99, $800,000 to the

14    Department of Transportation.

15        (2)  It is the intent of the Legislature that this appropriation shall be used for the purpose

16    of developing, preparing to install, and installing a traffic signal emergency priority control system

17    within the automated traffic management system (A.T.M.S.) component of the Interstate 15

18    reconstruction project.

19        (3)  The money appropriated in Subsection (1) is nonlapsing.

20        Section 2.   Effective date.

21        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
