, Utah Code

26

32

33

34

    
under the "Level 5" scale.
            
35

    
    Employees who are working their 10th to 14th annual general session shall be paid
            
36

    
under the "Level 6" scale.
            
37

    
    Employees who are working their 15th to 19th annual general session shall be paid
            
38

    
under the "Level 7" scale.
            
39

    
    Employees who are working their 20th or more annual general session shall be paid
            
40

    
under the "Level 8" scale.
            
41

    
    Senate employees are designated with an "S."  House of Representatives employees are
            
42

    
designated with an "H."
