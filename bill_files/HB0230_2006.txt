HIGH SCHOOL CURRICULUM                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Bradley M. Daw                                Senate Sponsor             Curtis S. Bramble                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill establishes additional high school curriculum provisions.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    establishes a rigorous postsecondary preparation curriculum for high school                           students                                .    provides a method for parents and students to choose the postsecondary preparation                           curriculum  and                               .    encourages a meeting to select which curriculum the student will follow.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  A      . , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A      .      is enacted to read                                  A      . .  High school curriculum.                                        In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                          State Board of Education shall make rules to                                 a   establish a rigorous postsecondary education curriculum, in addition to the core                          curriculum required by Sections       A           and       A      .       and                                b   require a school district to provide the postsecondary preparation curriculum                          options to all high school students, and allow a student and the student s parent or guardian to                          indicate in writing a preference for the student to pursue a specific postsecondary curriculum.                                    The State Board of Education shall encourage a school district to convene a                          meeting prior to or during a student s ninth grade year to discuss the best curriculum path based                          on the student s needs, involving                                 a   the student                                 b   the student s parent or guardian                                 c   the student s teacher  and                                d   any other appropriate school officials, including the principal and counselors.      Legislative Review Note      as of                  AM   Based on a limited legal review, this legislation has not been determined to have a high  probability of being held unconstitutional.
