 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill requires that a separate information sheet be filed when filing a judgment lien.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    requires the filing of a separate information sheet with a judgment lien.
