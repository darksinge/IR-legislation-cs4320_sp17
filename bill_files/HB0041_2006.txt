 LONG TITLE
            
12

    
 General Description:
            
13

    
    This bill modifies provisions of the State Library Division within the Department of
            
14

    
Community and Culture related to its duties and responsibilities for providing access to
            
15

    
and preserving state publications.
            
16

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
17

    
    This bill:
            
18

    
    .    requires the State Library Division to establish, operate, and maintain a digital
            
19

    
library of state publications;
            
20

    
    .    requires state agencies and political subdivisions to deposit digital copies of their
            
21

    
publications with the division;
            
22

    
     .    provides that state agencies may not remove state publications from public access
            
23

    
unless digital copies of the publications have been deposited with the division; and
            
24

    
    .    eliminates distinctions between complete and selective depository libraries.
            
25

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
26

    
    None
            
27

    
 Other Special Clauses:

    None
