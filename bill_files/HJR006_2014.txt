3

    
 A NEW LOCATION

2014 GENERAL SESSION

            
5

    
STATE OF UTAH

 Chief Sponsor:  Gregory H. Hughes

Senate Sponsor: 






  Jerry W. Stevenson

 LONG TITLE
            
10

    
 General Description:
            
11

    
    This joint resolution of the Legislature grants approval for the construction and
            
12

    
operation of a commercial nonhazardous solid waste disposal facility to receive
            
13

    
nonhazardous solid waste at a new location.
            
14

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
15

    
    This resolution:
            
16

    
    .    addresses the proposed Stericycle Medical Waste Commercial Nonhazardous Solid
            
17

    
Waste Disposal Facility, owned by Stericycle, Inc., to be constructed at a new
            
18

    
location, in unincorporated Tooele County; and
            
19

    
    .    grants statutorily required legislative approval of the facility, subject to the approval
            
20

    
process in Utah Code Section 

19-6-108


, to construct and operate a commercial
            
21

    
nonhazardous solid waste disposal facility and receive nonhazardous medical waste
            
22

    
and other nonhazardous solid waste as approved by the Department of
            
23

    
Environmental Quality.
            
24

    
 Special Clauses:
            
25

    
    None
            
26

    

            
27

    
Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

            
28


    
    WHEREAS, Stericycle, Inc. desires to construct and operate a commercial
            
29

    
nonhazardous solid waste disposal facility to receive nonhazardous medical waste and other
            
30

    
nonhazardous solid waste as approved by the Department of Environmental Quality;
            
31

    
    WHEREAS, the facility is to be located on an approximately 40-acre parcel in
            
32

    
unincorporated Tooele County, Utah, within or near Section 3 of Township 1 North, Range 8
            
33

    
West, Salt Lake Base and Meridian, located approximately 45 miles west of Salt Lake City;
            
34

    
and
            
35

    
    WHEREAS, an applicant for authority to construct a commercial nonhazardous solid
            
36

    
waste disposal facility shall receive approval in accordance with Utah Code Section 

19-6-108


:
            
37

    
    NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah
            
38

    
grants approval to Stericycle, Inc. to construct and operate a commercial nonhazardous solid
            
39

    
waste disposal facility at a new location, in unincorporated Tooele County, Utah, but only
            
40

    
subject to, and in compliance with, the approval process outlined in Utah Code Section
            
41

    

19-6-108
