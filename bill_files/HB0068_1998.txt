H.B. 68









1     APPROPRIATION FOR USU PASTURE AND


2     FORAGE INITIATIVE




3    1998 GENERAL SESSION


4    STATE OF UTAH


5     Sponsor:  Eli H. Anderson


6    AN ACT RELATING TO HIGHER EDUCATION; PROVIDING A $250,000

7    APPROPRIATION TO CONTINUE THE UTAH PASTURE AND FORAGE INITIATIVE,

8    A RESEARCH AND EDUCATION PROJECT DESIGNED TO IMPROVE OPEN SPACES

9    AND TO ASSIST LIVESTOCK PRODUCERS TO IMPROVE IRRIGATED PASTURE

10    PRODUCTION; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

11    This act enacts uncodified material.

12    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

13        Section 1.   Appropriation.

14        (1)  There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year 1998-99, $250,000 to Utah

15    State University to be used by the Utah Agricultural Experiment Station as matching funds for a

16    federal grant to continue the Utah Pasture and Forage Initiative, a research and education project

17    designed to improve open spaces and to assist livestock producers to improve irrigated pasture

18    production.

19        (2)  The university shall use the appropriation under the initiative for concurrent research

20    and education projects for livestock production on irrigated pastures appropriate to the needs and

21    conditions faced by Utah agricultural producers.

22        (3) (a)  For fiscal year 1999-2000 and for each fiscal year thereafter, the State Board of

23    Regents shall include a requested line item appropriation to fund the Utah Pasture and Forage

24    Initiative.

25        (b)  The appropriation requests shall continue for only as long as matching funds are made

26    available by the federal government.

27        Section 2.   Effective date.


1        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
