Cosgrove                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires certain state and local governmental entities to recycle certain                           materials.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms  and                               .    requires a first or second class county and a state or municipal agency located in a                           first or second class county to recycle certain materials.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                       , Utah Code Annotated                                            . , Utah Code Annotated                                      G      , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                   Section  .    Section                is enacted to read                                       .  Participation in recycling.                                    As used in this section                                 a   i    Municipal facility  means a building, structure, or other improvement that is                          constructed on property                                 A   owned by the municipality  and                                B   located in a first or second class county.                                ii    Municipal facility  does not mean a privately owned structure that is located on                          property owned by the municipality.                                b   i    Recyclable material  means an item that may be reused from the following                          types of material                                 A   newspaper                                 B   paper                                 C   cardboard                                 D   plastic                                 E    steel  or                                F   aluminum.                                ii    Recyclable material  does not include material                                 A   containing confidential information that requires shredding  or                                B   that will be burned to generate electricity.                                    A municipality shall                                 a   divert recyclable material from the solid waste stream generated at a municipal                          facility  and                                b   contract for the collection of recyclable material in accordance with                                 i   Title   G, Chapter  , Utah Procurement Code  or                                ii   a municipal procurement ordinance.                                    a   A municipality is responsible for the cost of the contract required by Subsection                              b .                                b   A municipality shall retain any profits or savings received as a result of the contract                          required by Subsection     b .
