 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends disease testing procedures for a prisoner who comes in contact with an
            
11

    
emergency services provider or volunteer.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    defines terms;
            
15

    
    .    permits blood testing under certain circumstances with a court order;
            
16

    
    .    allows for the use of reasonable force to draw blood from a prisoner if ordered by
            
17

    
the court; and
            
18

    
    .    makes technical changes.
