 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
10

    
    This joint rules resolution of the Legislature modifies provisions relating to legislator
            
11

    
pay and the reimbursement of transportation, lodging, and meal expenses.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This resolution:
            
14

    
    .    modifies definitions;
            
15

    
    .    requires the Legislative Expenses Oversight Committee to ensure that procedures
            
16

    
are established for the purpose of making payments or reimbursements;
            
17

    
    .    provides that lodging expenses may be paid to a legislator only as a reimbursement
            
18

    
of expenses that are necessarily incurred by the legislator on authorized legislative
            
19

    
days;
            
20

    
    .    limits lodging expenses to nightly lodging rates set by state administrative rule;
            
21

    
    .    provides procedures and requirements for reimbursement for lodging expenses;
            
22

    
    .    provides that a legislator may be reimbursed for actual meal expenses incurred by
            
23

    
the legislator on authorized legislative days;
            
24

    
    .    limits meal expenses to the per diem meal rates for in-state travel set by state
            
25

    
administrative rule;
            
26

    
    .    provides procedures and requirements for reimbursement for meal expenses;
            
27

    
    .    provides that a legislator may be reimbursed for actual expenses incurred by the

            
28


    
legislator to travel to and from legislative meetings on authorized legislative days;
            
29

    
    .    requires that transportation cost reimbursements be subject to certain type and cost
            
30

    
requirements similar to state administrative rules;
            
31

    
    .    requires each legislator to submit supporting documentation with each
            
32

    
reimbursement request;
            
33

    
    .    permits a legislator to decline to request or receive reimbursement for costs or
            
34

    
expenses;
            
35

    
    .    permits a legislator to request and receive a partial or reduced reimbursement
            
36

    
amount;
            
37

    
    .    provides that legislators are paid a daily compensation for each authorized
            
38

    
legislative day;
            
39

    
    .    repeals provisions relating to expense policies that will be discontinued with
            
40

    
adoption of the new salary and reimbursement rules; and
            
41

    
    .    makes technical and conforming changes.
