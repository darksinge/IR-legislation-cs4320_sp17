 General Description:

    This bill modifies provisions relating to forfeiture and nonuse applications.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    modifies provisions relating to forfeiture and nonuse applications;

    .    clarifies a provision relating to protection from forfeiture for a water right subject to
            
14

    
an approved nonuse application; and

    .    makes technical changes.
