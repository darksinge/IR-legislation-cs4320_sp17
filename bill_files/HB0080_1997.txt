REAL PROPERTY   PRESUMPTION OF TITLE                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  John E. Swallow          AN ACT RELATING TO REAL ESTATE, CONVEYANCES  CREATING THE         PRESUMPTION OF JOINT TENANCY AND ELIMINATING THE PRESUMPTION OF         TENANCY IN COMMON  PROVIDING DECLARATORY TERMS AND CONDITIONS          AND MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                      , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section         is amended to read                      .  Creation of joint tenancy presumed    Tenancy in common.                   a   Every interest in real estate granted to two or more married persons in their own          right  shall  is presumed to be a joint tenancy  in common , unless severed, converted, or expressly          declared in the grant to be otherwise.               i   Use of words  joint tenancy  or  with full rights of survivorship  or  and to the survivor          of them  or words of similar import  shall declare  means a joint tenancy.               ii   Use of words  tenancy in common  or  with no rights of survivorship  or  undivided              interest  or words of similar import shall declare a tenancy in common.               b   A sole owner of real property shall create a joint tenancy in himself and another or          others                i   by making a transfer to himself and  such other  another or others as joint tenants by          use of  such  the words  as herein  provided in this section  or               ii   by conveying to another person or persons an interest in land in which an interest is          retained by the grantor and by declaring the creation of a joint tenancy by use of  such  the words            as herein  provided in this section.              c   In all cases, the interest of joint tenants must be equal and undivided.                  A  joint tenancy  is converted into a  tenancy in common  by a joint tenant by making         a bona fide conveyance of the joint tenant s interest in the property to himself and a third party         which terminates the joint tenancy.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
