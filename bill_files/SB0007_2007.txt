 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill amends the Utah Human Services Code to provide that a court may, under
            
11

    
certain circumstances, award court costs and reasonable attorney fees to a prevailing
            
12

    
party in an action for exploitation of a vulnerable adult.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    provides that, if the plaintiff prevails in an action for exploitation of a vulnerable
            
16

    
adult, the court may order the defendant to pay the court costs and reasonable
            
17

    
attorney fees of the plaintiff; and
            
18

    
    .    provides that, if the defendant prevails in an action for exploitation of a vulnerable
            
19

    
adult, the court may order the plaintiff to pay the court costs and reasonable attorney
            
20

    
fees of the defendant, if the action was frivolous, unreasonable, or taken in bad
            
21

    
faith.
