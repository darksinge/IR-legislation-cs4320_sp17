 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Indigent Defense Act.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    defines "defense service provider", "legal defense", and "regional legal defense";
            
13

    
    .    allows a person charged with a serious offense to file a claim of indigency with the
            
14

    
court;
            
15

    
    .    requires a defense service provider to provide all legal defense services as a
            
16

    
package;
            
17

    
    .    provides procedures for the court to follow when a defendant hires private counsel;
            
18

    
and
            
19

    
    .     extends subsequent terms of county commissioners and county attorneys who serve
            
20

    
on the Indigent Defense Fund Board to be four years rather than two years.
            
21
