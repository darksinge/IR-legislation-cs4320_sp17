S.B. 20 Enrolled





            

    
 REPORTING REQUIREMENTS FOR SCIENCE

            

    
 CENTER AUTHORITY



            

    
2001 GENERAL SESSION

            

    
STATE OF UTAH

            

    


 Sponsor:  Parley  Hellewell



            

    
 This act modifies the Community and Economic Development Code to amend the reporting
            

    
 requirements of the Science Center Authority and to make technical changes.
            

    
This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:
            

    
AMENDS:
            

    
     9-3-410, as last amended by Chapter 13, Laws of Utah 1998
            

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
9-3-410

 is amended to read:
            

    
     9-3-410.  Relation to certain acts.
            

    
    (1)  The authority is exempt from:
            

    
    (a)  Title 51, Chapter 5, Funds Consolidation Act;
            

    
    (b)  Title 63, Chapter 38, Budgetary Procedures Act;
            

    
    (c)  Title 63, Chapter 56, Utah Procurement Code;
            

    
    (d)  Title 63A, Chapter 1, Utah Administrative Services Code; and
            

    
    (e)  Title 67, Chapter 19, Utah State Personnel Management Act.
            

    
    (2)  The authority shall be subject to audit by:
            

    
    (a)  the state auditor pursuant to Title 67, Chapter 3, Auditor; and [by]
            

    
    (b)  the legislative auditor general pursuant to Section 

36-12-15

.
            

    
    (3)  The authority shall annually report to the [Business, Labor, and Economic
            

    
Development Interim] Legislative Quasi-Governmental Entities Committee concerning the
            

    
authority's implementation of this part.
