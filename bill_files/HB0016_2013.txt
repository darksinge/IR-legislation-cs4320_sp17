 Committee Note:

 General Description:
            
11

    
    This bill exempts certain property acquired by the Department of Transportation from
            
12

    
the state surplus property program.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    provides that the Department of Transportation is exempt from using the state
            
16

    
surplus property program when disposing of surplus personal property that was
            
17

    
acquired as part of a transaction or legal action by the department acquiring real
            
18

    
property for a state transportation purpose;
            
19

    
    .    provides that proceeds from the sale, exchange, or other disposition of certain
            
20

    
surplus personal property shall be deposited with the state treasurer and credited to
            
21

    
the Transportation Fund if sold, exchanged, or disposed of by the Department of
            
22

    
Transportation; and
            
23

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
24
