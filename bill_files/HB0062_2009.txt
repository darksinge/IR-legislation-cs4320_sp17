 Committee Note:

 General Description:
            
14

    
    This bill amends a provision related to membership criteria for the State Water
            
15

    
Development Commission.

 Highlighted Provisions:
            
17

    
    This bill:
            
18

    
    .    allows two senators from the same river district to serve on the State Water
            
19

    
Development Commission.

    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None

 Utah Code Sections Affected:

AMENDS:

     73-27-102, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2000, Chapter 124
            
27

    


            
28


    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
29

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
73-27-102

 is amended to read:
            
30

    
     73-27-102.  State Water Development Commission created.
            
31

    
    (1)  The Joint Gubernatorial and Legislative State Water Development Commission is
            
32

    
created to determine the state's role in the protection, conservation, and development of the
            
33

    
state's water resources.
            
34

    
    (2)  The commission membership shall include:
            
35

    
    (a)  four members of the Senate, of whom no more than [one] two shall represent the
            
36

    
same river district and no more than two shall be from the same party;
            
37

    
    (b)  six members of the House of Representatives, of whom no more than two shall
            
38

    
represent the same river district and no more than four shall be from the same party;
            
39

    
    (c)  a representative of the Office of the Governor;
            
40

    
    (d)  a representative of the Green River District;
            
41

    
    (e)  a representative of the Upper Colorado River District;
            
42

    
    (f)  a representative of the Lower Colorado River District;
            
43

    
    (g)  a representative of the Lower Sevier River District;
            
44

    
    (h)  a representative of the Upper Sevier River District;
            
45

    
    (i)  a representative of the Provo River District;
            
46

    
    (j)  a representative of the Salt Lake District;
            
47

    
    (k)  a representative of the Weber River District;
            
48

    
    (l)  a representative of the Bear River District;
            
49

    
    (m)  the executive director of the Department of Natural Resources;
            
50

    
    (n)  the executive director of the Department of Environmental Quality;
            
51

    
    (o)  the commissioner of agriculture and food;
            
52

    
    (p)  a member of the Board of Water Resources;
            
53

    
    (q)  a representative of an organized environmental group; and
            
54

    
    (r)  a representative of agricultural production.
            
55

    
    (3)  The president of the Senate and the speaker of the House shall appoint the
            
56

    
commission members from the Senate and the House, respectively.  The governor shall appoint
            
57

    
the other commission members.
            
58

    
    (4)  The president of the Senate and the speaker of the House shall designate one

            
59


    
senator and one representative, respectively, as cochair.
            
60

    
    (5)  Attendance of a majority of the members at a meeting constitutes a quorum for the
            
61

    
transaction of the business of the commission.
