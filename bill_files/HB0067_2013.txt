 General Description:

    This bill addresses the property taxation of personal property.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    applies the primary residential exemption to household furnishings, furniture, and
            
13

    
equipment used exclusively within a rental primary residential property;

    .    increases a property tax exemption for personal property;

    .    repeals a consumer price index adjustment for purposes of the property tax
            
16

    
exemption for personal property;
            
17

    
    .    provides that a signed statement submitted to a county assessor may only contain
            
18

    
certain information under certain circumstances;
            
19

    
    .    addresses property tax exemption, abatement, and deferral provisions relative to the
            
20

    
primary residential exemption; and
            
21

    
    .    makes technical and conforming changes.
