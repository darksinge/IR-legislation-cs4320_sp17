 CURRENT FISCAL YEAR SUPPLEMENTAL APPROPRIATIONS ACT

     Chief Sponsor: Lyle W. Hillyard

    House Sponsor: Ron Bigelow


 ...................................................................
..
 LONG TITLE
General Description:
    This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and operation
of state government for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2007 and ending June 30, 2008.
 Highlighted Provisions:
    This bill:
    .    provides budget increases and decreases for the use and support of certain state agencies;
    .    provides budget increases and decreases for the use and support of certain institutions of
higher education;
    .    provides budget increases and decreases for other purposes as described;
    .    provides intent language.
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
    This bill appropriates for fiscal year 2008:
    .    $56,541,700 from the General Fund;
    .    $2,401,500 from the Uniform School Fund;
    .    $2,408,500 from the Education Fund;
    .    $46,541,300 from various sources as detailed in this bill.
 Other Special Clauses:
    This bill takes effect immediately.
