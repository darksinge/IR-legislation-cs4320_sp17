AGGRESSIVE DRIVING LAW                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor  Don E. Bush                           AN ACT RELATING TO MOTOR VEHICLES  AMENDING CERTAIN RECKLESS DRIVING                          PENALTIES  REQUIRING COMPLETION OF A DEFENSIVE DRIVING COURSE THAT                          INCLUDES EDUCATION ON THE EFFECTS OF AGGRESSIVE AND RECKLESS                          DRIVING IN CERTAIN CIRCUMSTANCES.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                       , as last amended by Chapters     and    , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Reckless driving    Penalties    Defensive driving course    Costs.                                      A person who operates any vehicle in willful or wanton disregard for the safety of                           persons or property is guilty of reckless driving.                                    a   A person convicted of reckless driving is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.                                b   i   Upon a first conviction, the penalty is a minimum term of imprisonment of not                           fewer than five days, or a minimum fine of not less than           .                                ii   In addition to the penalty imposed under Subsection     b  i , the court may order the                          person to complete a defensive driving course approved by the commissioner of the Department                          of Public Safety that includes education on the effects of aggressive and reckless driving.                                c   i   On a second or subsequent conviction, the penalty is a minimum term of                           imprisonment of not fewer than ten days, or a minimum fine of not less than           .                                ii   In addition to any other penalty imposed under Subsection     c  i , the court shall                          order the person to complete a defensive driving course approved by the commissioner of the                          Department of Public Safety that includes education on the effects of aggressive and reckless                             driving.                                    a   The court may allow the assessment, education, or treatment imposed under Section                                         to substitute for the defensive driving course under this section, if the imposed assessment,                          education, or treatment is for the same incident.                                b   A person ordered to complete a defensive driving course under this section shall pay                          the cost of the course unless the person is found by the court to be impecunious.
