 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions related to employment by a municipality.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    clarifies provisions related to the classified civil service;

    .    amends provisions related to duration and termination of a municipal employee;

15

    
municipal employee and related procedures; and

    .    makes technical corrections.
