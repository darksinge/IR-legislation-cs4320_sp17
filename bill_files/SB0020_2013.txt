servers.

    This bill:

    .    beginning July 1, 2013, requires a health care provider who participates in the state

law;

patients;

information officer to:

Department of Technology Services or executive branch agency budgets;

        .    inform the speaker of the House of Representatives and the president of the

branch agency security standards at least once every two years;
