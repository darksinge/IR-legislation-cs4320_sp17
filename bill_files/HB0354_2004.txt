 SALVAGE VEHICLE AMENDMENTS



            
2

    
2004 GENERAL SESSION

            
3

    
STATE OF UTAH

            
4

    


 Sponsor: Greg J. Curtis



            
5

    

            
6

    
 LONG TITLE
            
7

    
 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code to amend provisions relating to salvage
            
9

    
vehicle buyer licenses.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    provides that a salvage vehicle buyer license may be issued to a motor vehicle
            
13

    
dealer, dismantler, or body shop who is licensed in another country.
            
14

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
15

    
    None
            
16

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
17

    
    None
            
18

    
 Utah Code Sections Affected:
            
19

    
AMENDS:
            
20

    
     41-3-202, as last amended by Chapter 157, Laws of Utah 2003
            
21

    

            
22

    
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
23

    
    Section 1.  
Section  
41-3-202

 is amended to read:
            
24

    
     41-3-202.  Licenses -- Classes and scope.
            
25

    
    (1)  A new motor vehicle dealer's license permits the licensee to:
            
26

    
    (a)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange new motor vehicles if the licensee possesses a
            
27

    
franchise from the manufacturer of the motor vehicle offered for sale, sold, or exchanged by the

            
28


    
licensee;
            
29

    
    (b)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange used motor vehicles;
            
30

    
    (c)  operate as a body shop; and
            
31

    
    (d)  dismantle motor vehicles.
            
32

    
    (2)  A used motor vehicle dealer's license permits the licensee to:
            
33

    
    (a)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange used motor vehicles;
            
34

    
    (b)  operate as a body shop; and
            
35

    
    (c)  dismantle motor vehicles.
            
36

    
    (3)  A new motorcycle, off-highway vehicle, and small trailer  dealer's license permits
            
37

    
the licensee to:
            
38

    
    (a)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange new motorcycles, off-highway vehicles, or small
            
39

    
trailers if the licensee possesses a franchise from the manufacturer of the motorcycle,
            
40

    
off-highway vehicle, or small trailer offered for sale, sold, or exchanged by the licensee;
            
41

    
    (b)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange used motorcycles, off-highway vehicles, or small
            
42

    
trailers; and
            
43

    
    (c)  dismantle motorcycles, off-highway vehicles, or small trailers.
            
44

    
    (4)  A used motorcycle, off-highway vehicle, and small trailer  dealer's license permits
            
45

    
the licensee to:
            
46

    
    (a)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange used motorcycles, off-highway vehicles, and small
            
47

    
trailers; and
            
48

    
    (b)  dismantle motorcycles, off-highway vehicles, or small trailers.
            
49

    
    (5)  A salesperson's license permits the licensee to act as a motor vehicle salesperson
            
50

    
and is valid for employment with only one dealer at a time.
            
51

    
    (6) (a)  A manufacturer's license permits the licensee to construct or assemble motor
            
52

    
vehicles subject to registration under Title 41, Chapter 1a, Motor Vehicle Act, at an established
            
53

    
place of business and to remanufacture motor vehicles.
            
54

    
    (b)  Under rules made by the administrator, the licensee may issue and install vehicle
            
55

    
identification numbers on manufactured motor vehicles.
            
56

    
    (c)  The licensee may franchise and appoint dealers to sell manufactured motor vehicles
            
57

    
by notifying the division of the franchise or appointment.
            
58

    
    (7)  A transporter's license permits the licensee to transport or deliver motor vehicles

            
59


    
subject to registration under Title 41, Chapter 1a, Motor Vehicle Act, from a manufacturing,
            
60

    
assembling, or distributing point or from a dealer, to dealers, distributors, or sales agents of a
            
61

    
manufacturer or remanufacturer, to or from detail or repair shops, and to financial institutions
            
62

    
or places of storage from points of repossession.
            
63

    
    (8)  A dismantler's license permits the licensee to dismantle motor vehicles subject to
            
64

    
registration under Title 41, Chapter 1a, Motor Vehicle Act, for the  purpose of reselling parts or
            
65

    
for salvage, or selling dismantled or salvage vehicles to a crusher or other dismantler.
            
66

    
    (9)  A distributor or factory branch and distributor branch's license permits the licensee
            
67

    
to sell and distribute new motor vehicles, parts, and accessories to their franchised dealers.
            
68

    
    (10)  A representative's license, for factory representatives or distributor representatives
            
69

    
permits the licensee to contact his authorized dealers for the purpose of making or promoting
            
70

    
the sale of motor vehicles, parts, and accessories.
            
71

    
    (11) (a) (i)  A remanufacturer's license permits the licensee to construct, reconstruct,
            
72

    
assemble, or reassemble motor vehicles subject to registration under Title 41, Chapter 1a,
            
73

    
Motor Vehicle Act, from used or new motor vehicles or parts.
            
74

    
    (ii)  Evidence of ownership of parts and motor vehicles used in remanufacture shall be
            
75

    
available to the division upon demand.
            
76

    
    (b)  Under rules made by the administrator, the licensee may issue and install vehicle
            
77

    
identification numbers on remanufactured motor vehicles.
            
78

    
    (12)  A crusher's license permits the licensee to engage in the business of crushing or
            
79

    
shredding motor vehicles subject to registration under Title 41, Chapter 1a, Motor Vehicle Act,
            
80

    
for the purpose of reducing the useable materials and metals to a more compact size for
            
81

    
recycling.
            
82

    
    (13)  A body shop's license permits the licensee to rebuild, restore, repair, or paint
            
83

    
primarily the  body of motor vehicles damaged by collision or natural disaster, and to dismantle
            
84

    
motor vehicles.
            
85

    
    (14)  A special equipment dealer's license permits the licensee to:
            
86

    
    (a)  buy incomplete new motor vehicles with a gross vehicle weight of 12,000 or more
            
87

    
pounds from a new motor vehicle dealer and sell the new vehicle with the special equipment
            
88

    
installed without a franchise from the manufacturer;
            
89

    
    (b)  offer for sale, sell, or exchange used motor vehicles;

            
90


    
    (c)  operate as a body shop; and
            
91

    
    (d)  dismantle motor vehicles.
            
92

    
    (15) (a)  A salvage vehicle buyer license permits the licensee to bid on or purchase a
            
93

    
vehicle with a salvage certificate as defined in Section 

41-1a-1001

 at any motor vehicle
            
94

    
auction.
            
95

    
    (b)  A salvage vehicle buyer license may only be issued to a motor vehicle dealer,
            
96

    
dismantler, or body shop who qualifies under rules made by the division and is licensed in any
            
97

    
state or country as a motor vehicle dealer, dismantler, or body shop.
            
98

    
    (c)  The division may not issue more than two salvage vehicle buyer licenses to any one
            
99

    
dealer, dismantler, or body shop.
            
100

    
    (d)  In accordance with Title 63, Chapter 46a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the
            
101

    
administrator shall make rules establishing qualifications of an applicant for a salvage vehicle
            
102

    
buyer license.  The criteria shall include:
            
103

    
    (i)  business history;
            
104

    
    (ii)  salvage vehicle qualifications;
            
105

    
    (iii)  ability to properly handle and dispose of environmental hazardous materials
            
106

    
associated with salvage vehicles; and
            
107

    
    (iv)  record in demonstrating compliance with the provisions of this chapter.
