 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill moves the Office of Inspector General of Medicaid Services from the
            
10

    
Governor's Office of Planning and Budget into the State Auditor's Office.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    places the Office of Inspector General of Medicaid Services in the State Auditor's
            
14

    
Office;
            
15

    
    .    permits the state auditor to appoint the inspector general of Medicaid services; and
            
16

    
    .    requires the inspector general of Medicaid services to make recommendations to the
            
17

    
Legislature and the governor.
            
18

    
 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
            
19

    
    None
            
20

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
21

    
    This bill provides an immediate effective date.
