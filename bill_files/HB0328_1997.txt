DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS   RECORD REQUESTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Robert H. M. Killpack          AN ACT RELATING TO STATE INSTITUTIONS  DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS           STATE PRISON  PROVIDING DEFINITIONS  EXEMPTING THE DEPARTMENT FROM         GOVERNMENT RECORD ACCESS MANAGEMENT ACT PROVISIONS  PROVIDING         GROUNDS FOR DENIAL  PROVIDING FOR OFFENDER S APPEAL OF DENIAL OF         ACCESS  REQUIRING RULEMAKING AND PROCEDURAL GUIDELINES  AND          MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                        , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Definitions    Records    Access    Grounds for denial    Right to appeal              Rulemaking.              For purposes of this section                     Legitimate penological interests  means consideration                a   of whether or not there is a valid, rational connection between the department          regulation and the legitimate and neutral governmental interest put forward to justify the          regulation                b   of whether or not there are alternative means of exercising the right that remain open          to the offender                c   of the impact the accommodation of the asserted constitutional right will have on          department guards and other offenders, and on the allocation of department resources generally           and               d   whether or not there is an absence of ready alternatives.                   Offender  means any person who is incarcerated, treated, on probation, or on parole         under the supervision of the department or held pursuant to a contract or compact agreement.                        a   The Law Enforcement and Technical Services Division of the Department of         Public Safety, county attorneys  offices, and state and local law enforcement agencies shall furnish         to the department upon request a copy of records of any person arrested in this state.              b   The department shall maintain centralized files on  all offenders  any offender under         the jurisdiction of the department and make the files available for review by other criminal justice         agencies upon request in cases where offenders are the subject of active investigations.                         All records maintained by programs under contract to the department providing          services to  public  offenders are the property of the department.                   Notwithstanding the provisions of Title   , Chapter  , Government Records Access          and Management Act, the department is not required to provide offenders reasonable access and          inspection of any department record requested by an offender if the access and inspection is          contrary to legitimate penological interests.                   If an offender s request to access and inspect department records is denied, the offender          may appeal the denial only as provided by department rule promulgated pursuant to Title   ,          Chapter   b, Utah Administrative Procedures Act.                   The department shall promulgate rules on policy and procedure governing the          offender s access and inspection of department records in accordance with Title   , Chapter   a,          Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, and Title   , Chapter   b, Administrative Procedures Act.
