Section         c         is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   c.  STATE REIMBURSEMENT TO COUNTY                            CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES                                        c    .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                     a    Core inmate incarceration costs  means the county correctional facility s direct                          costs of incarcerating an inmate, including housing, feeding, and clothing.  These costs also                          include the costs of programs the facility provides for inmates, but these costs do not include                          programs provided only for inmates housed at the facility under this chapter.                                b    Core inmate incarceration costs  do not include costs of inmate transportation services                          or medical care.                                     Department  means the Department of Corrections.                                     Fund  means the Inmate Costs Reimbursement Trust Fund created in Section                                  c        .                                     Inmate costs  includes core inmate incarceration costs, and also inmate transportation                          services and inmate medical care.                               Section  .    Section         c         is enacted to read                                     c    .  County housing of state prisoners.                                    a   When a person convicted of a felony is committed to serve time in a county                          correctional facility as a condition of probation under Subsection                   , a county shall accept                          and house the prisoner in a county correctional facility, subject to available resources.  If a county                          is unable to accept a person due to lack of resources, the county may negotiate with another county                          to accept and house the person.                                b   The department may contract with a county or other entity to house inmates, other than                          those committed under Subsection                    as a term of probation, in a county or other                          correctional facility.                                c   The department shall use all available county correctional facility bed spaces for which                          it has contracted under Subsection     b  before it may contract with an entity for correctional                          facility bed space.                                    Compensation to a county for inmates incarcerated under this chapter shall be made                          by the department based on Section          c        .                               Section  .    Section         c         is enacted to read                                     c    .  Expendable trust fund created    Funding    Purposes.                                    a   There is created the expendable trust fund known as the Inmate Costs                             Reimbursement Trust Fund.                                b   Monies in the fund shall consist of appropriations from the Legislature.                                    The director of the Department of Corrections shall use the fund monies for the sole                          purpose of reimbursing counties and other entities for costs incurred by housing inmates pursuant                          to this chapter.                                    The fund monies may not be used for the costs of administering the inmate cost                          reimbursement program under this chapter.  Those costs shall be covered by legislative                          appropriation.                               Section  .    Section         c         is enacted to read                                     c    .  Procedures for setting county reimbursement for core inmate                           incarceration costs, and medical and transportation costs.                                    a   In order for counties to receive reimbursement under this chapter, the Utah Sheriffs                          Association and the department shall annually before July   negotiate for the fiscal year beginning                          on July   a single reimbursement rate, applicable to all counties, for daily core inmate incarceration                          costs.                                b   Additional entities may participate in the negotiations if their participation is agreed                          upon by both the Utah Sheriff s Association and the department.                                c   The rates established under this Subsection     apply also to reimbursement for any                          entity accepting inmates for incarceration under this chapter.                                    Each county shall negotiate directly with the department to establish reimbursement                          rates for providing transportation services and medical care for inmates housed under Section                                  c        .                               Section  .    Section         c         is enacted to read                                     c    .  Payment of reimbursement    Any shortfall.                                    a   The director of the department shall reimburse counties and other entities for daily                          core inmate costs according to the amounts established under Section          c        , and to the extent                          monies are available in the fund.                                b   The department shall by rule establish procedures for the distribution of reimbursement                          from the fund.                                    If it becomes apparent that monies in the fund will be insufficient to pay all                          reimbursement due under this chapter in a timely manner, the department and the counties and                             entities affected shall report the shortfall at the next meeting of the interim or standing legislative                          Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Committee as is appropriate for that time of year.                                    Counties and entities that receive reimbursement from the department under this                          chapter shall annually on or before June    submit a report to the department that includes                                 a   the costs to the county or entity of housing inmates under Section          c         and a                          comparison of these costs to the reimbursement rate established under Section          c                                         b   the number of inmates the county or entity housed under this chapter as                                 i   a condition of a sentence of probation  and                                ii   by contract with the department  and                                c   the total number of inmate days of incarceration provided.
