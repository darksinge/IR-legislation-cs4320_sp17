    This bill amends the definition of the term "Mental Health Therapist" under the Mental
            
11

    
Health Professional Practice Act and the Psychologist Licensing Act and clarifies the

13

    
counselor.

    This bill:

    .    defines terms;

    .    amends the definition of "Mental Health Therapist":

        .    in the Mental Health Professional Practice Act and the Psychologist Licensing

specializing in psychiatric mental health nursing"; and
