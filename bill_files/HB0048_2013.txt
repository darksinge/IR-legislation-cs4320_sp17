 Committee Note:

    The Health and Human Services Interim Committee recommended this bill.
            
10

    
 General Description:
            
11

    
    This bill amends the Local Health Authorities code and the Occupational and
            
12

    
Professional Licensing code related to Medical Reserve Corps.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    authorizes the local health departments to train health care professionals who
            
16

    
volunteer as medical reserve corps during public health emergencies and disasters;
            
17

    
and
            
18

    
    .    amends the Healthcare Providers Immunity from Liability Act to provide liability
            
19

    
protections to the medical reserve corps who are training to respond to public health
            
20

    
emergencies or disasters.
            
21

22

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None
