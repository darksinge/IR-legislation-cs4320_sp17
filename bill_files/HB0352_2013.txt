     17-16a-4 (Effective 05/01/13).  Prohibited use of official position -- Exception.
            
26

    
    (1)  Except as provided in Subsection (3), it is an offense for an elected or appointed
            
27

    
officer, under circumstances not amounting to a violation of Section 

63G-6a-2304

 or 

76-8-105

,

            
28


    
to:
            
29

    
    (a)  disclose confidential information acquired by reason of the officer's official position
            
30

31

32

33

34

35

    
discharge of the officer's official duties.
            
36

    
    (2)  This section is inapplicable to:

    (b)  an award publicly presented;

    (c)  any bona fide loan made in the ordinary course of business; or
            
40
