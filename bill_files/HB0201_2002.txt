Harper                             This act amends the Property Tax Act to increase the amount of a taxing entity s share of a                          judgment or order that is required to impose a judgment levy.  The act provides that refunds                          for which a judgment levy is imposed that are less than   ,    are required to be paid by a                          taxing entity to a taxpayer within    days after the date the refunds are ordered to be paid,                          and makes technical changes.
