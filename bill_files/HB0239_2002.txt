SALVAGE VEHICLE AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   David L. Hogue                             This act modifies the Motor Vehicle Code by prohibiting the registration of and revoking the                          certificate of title of a  vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor that is issued a junk permit.  This                          act allows for the collection of fees and for rulemaking.  This act takes effect on October  ,                              .
