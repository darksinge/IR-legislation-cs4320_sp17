    This bill establishes a pilot program to develop students' behaviors and skills that

    .    requires the State Board of Education to administer a grant program for the purpose
            
14

    
of developing students' behaviors and skills that enhance a school's learning
            
15

    
environment and are vital to success in a career;
            
16

    
    .    allows an elementary school to participate in the pilot program in accordance with
            
17
