 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the provisions of the Public Safety Code regarding the collection of
            
10

    
DNA from offenders.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    provides that law enforcement agencies may collect DNA samples at the time of
            
14

    
booking for any person arrested for any felony offense beginning May 13, 2014
            
15

    
through December 31, 2014; and
            
16

    
    .    on and after January 1, 2015, requires law enforcement agencies to collect DNA
            
17

    
samples at the time of booking for any person arrested for any felony offense.
