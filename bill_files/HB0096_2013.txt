 General Description:

    This bill amends corporate and individual income tax credits for cleaner burning fuels.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    modifies eligibility requirements to claim tax credits for cleaner burning fuels;

14

    .    makes technical and conforming changes.
