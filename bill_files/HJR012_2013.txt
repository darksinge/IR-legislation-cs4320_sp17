 JOINT RESOLUTION DISSOLVING CLARKSTON JUSTICE

 COURT



            
3

    
2013 GENERAL SESSION

            
4

    
STATE OF UTAH

 Chief Sponsor:  Jack R. Draxler

  Lyle W. Hillyard

 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This joint resolution approves the dissolution of the Clarkston Town Justice Court.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This resolution:
            
13

    
    .    approves the dissolution of the Clarkston Town Justice Court.
            
14

    
 Special Clauses:
            
15

    
    This resolution provides an effective date.
            
16

    

            
17

    
Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:
            
18

    
    WHEREAS, the town of Clarkston has had a justice court for many years;
            
19

    
    WHEREAS, the town of Clarkston has determined that it is no longer feasible for the
            
20

    
town to operate a justice court;
            
21

    
    WHEREAS, with the dissolution of the Clarkston Town Justice Court, the caseload
            
22

    
from the court will fall upon the First District Court in Cache County;
            
23

    
    WHEREAS, the Clarkston Town Council has given notice to the Utah Judicial Council
            
24

    
of its intent to dissolve the Clarkston Town Justice Court, effective not later than June 30,
            
25

    
2013; and
            
26

    
    WHEREAS, Section 

78A-7-123

 requires the Legislature to approve by joint resolution
            
27

    
the dissolution of a justice court:

            
28


    
    NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah
            
29

    
approves the closure of the Clarkston Town Justice Court.
            
30

    
    BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the Utah
            
31

    
Judicial Council and the town of Clarkston.
            
32

    
    BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this resolution takes effect upon approval by a
            
33

    
constitutional majority vote of all members of the House of Representatives and Senate.
