 LONG TITLE
            
9

    
 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies provisions of the Acupuncture Licensing Act.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    modifies the definition of the practice of acupuncture and changes the designation
            
14

    
of "acupuncturist" to "licensed acupuncturist";
            
15

    
    .    modifies provisions regarding the licensing and the renewal of a license of an
            
16

    
acupuncturist;
            
17

    
    .    makes conforming changes in the manner in which the title "licensed acupuncturist"
            
18

    
may be used with an individual's name and practice;
            
19

    
    .    modifies a provision dealing with presumptions related to third party insurance
            
20

    
payments to a person practicing acupuncture; and
            
21

    
    .    makes certain technical changes.
            
22

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
23

    
    None
            
24

    
 Other Special Clauses:
            
25

    
    None
