In cases not amounting to a violation of Section        , a person may not operate or          be in actual physical control of a motor vehicle within this state if the person has any measurable          controlled substance or metabolite of a controlled substance in the person s body.                   It is an affirmative defense to prosecution under this section that the controlled          substance was involuntarily ingested by the accused or prescribed by a practitioner for use by the          accused.                   A person convicted of a violation of Subsection     is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.
