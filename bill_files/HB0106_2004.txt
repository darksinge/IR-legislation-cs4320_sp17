    .    changes the grace period for nonpayment of premium to 15 days;

    .    clarifies coverage during a grace period;

    .    provides that if the Comprehensive Health Insurance Pool is dissolved or

discontinued, or if enrollment is capped or suspended, a covered carrier:

HIPAA eligible individuals;

    .    repeals the provision that links individual premium rates to the rates established by
            
23

    
the Comprehensive Health Insurance Pool;

    .    amends preexisting conditions waiver provisions for the Comprehensive Health

    .    makes technical amendments.
