TOWING OF MOTOR VEHICLES BY           DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC SAFETY                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Carl R. Saunders          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC SAFETY  ESTABLISHING A COMPULSORY TOW         TRUCK SERVICE ROTATION PROGRAM  PROVIDING FOR EXCEPTIONS AND         PENALTIES  AND PROVIDING FOR RULEMAKING.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                        , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is enacted to read                        .  Compulsory tow truck service rotation program    Definitions              Applicability    Provisions    Exceptions    Penalties    Rulemaking.                   As used in this section                a    Compulsory tow truck service  means towing vehicles that are transported in response          to                i   a peace officer dispatch call                ii   a call initiated by a government entity to enforce a provision of Title   , Chapter a,          Motor Vehicle Act  and               iii   any other call where the owner of the vehicle has not consented to removal of the          owner s vehicle.               b    Motor carrier  has the same meaning as defined in Section          .               c    Program  means a compulsory tow truck service rotation program establish under this          section.               d    Tow truck  has the same meaning as defined in Section          .                   a   The division shall establish a compulsory tow truck service rotation program.              b   The program shall be implemented by               i   the highway patrol, all state dispatch centers, and all other state and local law         enforcement agencies within the state that use dispatch services provided by the department  and              ii   any law enforcement agency while executing a State Tax Commission vehicle         impound under Title   , Chapter  a, Motor Vehicle Act.              c   An agency required to implement the program may enter into contracts or agreements         with other agencies, organizations, or parties to assist in implementing the program.                  The program shall provide that                a   any eligible tow truck motor carrier may participate in the compulsory tow truck          service rotation program and shall be placed on a list to be called when needed for compulsory tow          truck services                b   tow truck motor carriers that are not available and decline when called, lose that          rotation turn and the next tow truck motor carrier is called                c   if a tow truck motor carrier is not available to respond immediately to the scene when          called, the tow truck motor carrier                i   shall decline the call  and               ii   may not send a replacement                d   only a tow truck motor carrier who accepts a compulsory tow truck service call and          responds to the scene within the time established by the agencies implementing the program may          perform a compulsory tow truck service                e   the rates charged by a tow truck motor carrier to perform a compulsory tow truck          service shall be in accordance with the rates established under Section            and               f   a tow truck operator who performs a compulsory tow truck service is responsible for          cleaning up debris within the traveled portion of the highway that is caused by the vehicles          involved in the incident.                   The compulsory tow truck service rotation program and its rotation list is not affected          when                a   the on scene commander determines that                i   the closest available tow truck is needed due to traffic or safety conditions near the          scene of the incident  or                      ii   a special type of tow truck is needed               b   the vehicle owner, operator, or other person responsible for the vehicle requests a         specific tow truck motor carrier  or              c   a tow truck motor carrier accepts a call and does not perform a compulsory tow truck         service because the on scene commander called off the tow truck.                  A tow truck motor carrier that fails to perform in accordance with this section or rules         made under it or that is not in compliance with Title   , Chapter   , Part  , Tow Truck Provisions,         is not eligible to participate in the program for a minimum of three months for each violation.                  In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the          division shall make rules governing the                a   reasonable response time for a tow truck motor carrier who accepts a compulsory tow          truck service call under this section  and               b   hearing and sanction procedures in which a tow truck motor carrier may be suspended          from and reinstated in the program for violations of this section.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 AM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
