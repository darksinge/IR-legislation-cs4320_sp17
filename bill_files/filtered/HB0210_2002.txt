RECREATIONAL VEHICLE   LENGTH AND                           WIDTH AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Don E. Bush                             This act modifies the Transportation Code to amend length and width requirements for                          vehicles. The act provides that attached shade awnings are excluded when measuring length                          and width of recreational vehicles.
