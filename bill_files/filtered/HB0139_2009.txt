LEGISLATOR GIFT REPORTING ACT                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Stephen D. Clark                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts the Legislator Gift Reporting Act to require certain reporting of gifts                           accepted by legislators.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides definitions                                .    requires legislators to file a gift report with the lieutenant governor disclosing gifts                           received that are worth more than                                    .    provides that the gift report must be filed within    days of the end of the annual                           general session of the Legislature                                .    provides that the gift report include                                    .    a list of gifts, the giver, and the estimated value of the gift, and total of the gifts                                    .    the total net campaign balance listed in the year end campaign finance                           statement  and                                   .    the signature of the legislator certifying the accuracy of the gift report                                .    requires the lieutenant governor to                                    .    give ten days notice to legislators that a gift report is due                                    .    make each gift report available for public inspection including available on the                           Internet  and                                   .    send a copy of each legislator s gift report to each registered voter household                              within the district of the legislator  and                               .    provides that failure to file a gift report is a class B misdemeanor. Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  LEGISLATOR GIFT REPORTING ACT                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Legislator Gift Reporting Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                     a    Gift means a transfer of any good or service or combination of any goods or                          services                                 i   for less than fair and adequate consideration                                 ii   with a value of more than     in a day  and                                iii   given by                                 A   a lobbyist as defined under Section                                                   B   a principal as defined under Section                                                   C   an interested person as defined under Section                    or                                D   any other person that will receive a direct benefit from a legislative action currently                             being considered.                                b   If qualified under Subsection     a ,  gift  includes admission price or cost for                          events, meals, food, beverage, recreation, outings, or functions.                                c   Notwithstanding the provisions of Subsections     a  and  b ,  gift  does not                          include a good or service given to the entire Legislature, the entire Senate, or the entire House                          of Representatives, or to all members of a committee, subcommittee, or task force.                                     Gift report  means the report required under Section                  .                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Reporting of gifts    Gift report.                                    Within    days after the end of the annual general session, a legislator shall file a                          gift report with the lieutenant governor in accordance with the provisions of Section                  .                                    A gift report shall include                                 a   a list of all gifts received by the legislator during the    month period beginning                          with April   of the previous calendar year and ending on March    of the current calendar year,                          including                                 i   a description of the gift                                 ii   a good faith estimate of the value of the gift                                 iii   the name of the person who gave the gift                                 iv   the name of the entity that the person who gave the gift represents, if any  and                                v   a single figure equal to the total of the estimated value of all the gifts being reported                          in the gift report                                 b   a single figure equal to the net balance for the year as reported in the campaign                          financial statement year summary report that was due on January    of the current calendar                          year under Subsection       A               a  viii  for legislative candidates or Subsection                               A               a  viii  for officeholders  and                                c   a certification to be signed by the legislator that certifies that the information                          provided in the gift report is true, accurate, and complete to the best of the legislator s                          knowledge and belief.                                    If a legislator does not receive any gifts during the reporting period, the legislator                          shall file a gift report listing  none.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                            .  Notice of pending gift report    Form of submission    Public                           availability.                                    Ten days before a gift report is due under this chapter, the lieutenant governor shall                          inform each legislator by postal mail or, if requested by the legislator, by electronic mail                                 a   of the deadline for the gift report  and                                b   that the legislator may be guilty of a class B misdemeanor for failing to file the gift                          report.                                    A legislator submitting a gift report required by this chapter to the lieutenant                          governor shall submit the gift report on a form provided by the lieutenant governor, which may                          allow for facsimile, electronic mail, or Internet submission.                                     A gift report is considered timely filed if                                  a   it is received in the lieutenant governor s office no later than   p.m. on the date that                           it is due                                  b   it is received in the lieutenant governor s office with a postmark three days or more                           before the date that it was due  or                                 c   the legislator has proof that it was mailed, with appropriate postage and addressing,                           three days before it was due.                                     a   Notwithstanding any provision of Title   G, Chapter  , Government Records                           Access and Management Act, the lieutenant governor shall                                  i   make each gift report filed by a legislator available for public inspection and                           copying no later than one business day after the gift report is filed  and                                 ii   post an electronic copy of the contents of each gift report on a website established                           by the lieutenant governor no later than seven business days after the date the gift report is due.                                 b   The website established by the lieutenant governor under Subsection     a  shall                                  i   feature a searchable Internet database of the contents of each gift report  and                                 ii   allow summary capabilities.                                     a   No later than May   of each year the lieutenant governor shall send a copy of                           each legislator s gift report, by any available mail service, to each registered voter household                           within the district that the legislator represents.                                 b   If a gift report has not been received, the lieutenant governor shall send a notice of                           that fact in lieu of the gift report in accordance with Subsection     a .                                   Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Penalties.                                     A person who fails to file a gift report in accordance with this chapter is guilty of a                           class B misdemeanor.                                     The lieutenant governor shall report a violation of this chapter to the attorney                           general.
