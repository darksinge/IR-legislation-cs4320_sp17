Enrolled                ELECTRONIC LAW LIBRARY                GENERAL SESSION         STATE OF UTAH          Sponsor  Lyle W. Hillyard         AN ACT RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS  PROVIDING A PURPOSE STATEMENT         APPROPRIATING    ,    FROM THE GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR                FOR THE PURCHASE OF AN ELECTRONIC RESEARCH LAW LIBRARY        FOR THE OFFICE OF THE ATTORNEY GENERAL, THE JUDICIAL BRANCH, THE        OFFICE OF LEGISLATIVE RESEARCH AND GENERAL COUNSEL, AND        MEMBERS OF THE LEGISLATURE  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.        This act enacts uncodified material.        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah             Section  .   Purpose statement.            It is the intent of the Legislature to establish a statewide contract for the purchase of        electronic legal research services, available year round to the Office of Attorney General, the        Judicial Branch, the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel, and available during the        annual legislative session to members of the Legislature.            Section  .   Appropriation.            There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        ,    ,    to be        allocated as follows                     ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel                     ,    to the Office of the Attorney General  and                    ,    to the Office of the State Court Administrator.            Section  .   Effective date.            This act takes effect on July  ,     .
