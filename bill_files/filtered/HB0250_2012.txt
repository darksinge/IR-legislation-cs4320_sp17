TAX CREDIT FOR DEPENDENT WITH A DISABILITY                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   John Dougall                                Senate Sponsor             John L. Valentine                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Nonrefundable Tax Credit Act to allow a tax credit for a                           dependent adult with a disability or dependent child with a disability as part of the                           taxpayer tax credit.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    allows a tax credit for a dependent adult with a disability or dependent child with a                           disability as part of the taxpayer tax credit  and                               .    makes technical and conforming changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill has retrospective operation for a taxable year beginning on or after January  ,                               .
