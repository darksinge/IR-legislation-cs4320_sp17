Enrolled                           RETAILER REQUIREMENTS IN PRINTING                       FINANCIAL TRANSACTION CARD RECEIPT                              GENERAL SESSION                      STATE OF UTAH                         Sponsor   Carlene M. Walker                         This act modifies the Commerce and Trade Code by enacting provisions prohibiting a                      person from printing more than the last five digits of a financial transaction card account                      number or the expiration date on a financial transaction card receipt.  The act provides a                      cause of action when a person violates these provisions.  This act takes effect on January                       ,     .
