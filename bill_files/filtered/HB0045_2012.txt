General Description                                This bill modifies the State Fire Code Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    enacts a definition of  premixed  with respect to an antifreeze and water solution                                .    deletes and replaces a provision relating to hazardous environmental conditions  and                               .    modifies provisions relating to antifreeze used in automatic sprinkler systems.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
