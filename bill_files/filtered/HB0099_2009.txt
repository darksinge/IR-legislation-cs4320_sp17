PROFESSIONAL LICENSURE EXEMPTIONS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Larry B. Wiley                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions of Title   , Occupations and Professions, regarding                           exemptions from licensure as a licensed architect, professional engineer, or professional                           structural engineer.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    removes the exemption from licensure which allows a licensed professional                           engineer to practice architecture                                .    removes the exemption from licensure which allows a licensed architect to practice                           engineering or structural engineering  and                               .    makes certain technical changes.
