CONCEALED WEAPONS FEES AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor  Curtis Oda                                Senate Sponsor           Allen M. Christensen                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions of the Concealed Weapons Act by creating a dedicated                           credit as a revenue stream to cover the costs incurred by the Criminal Investigations and                           Technical Services Division of the Department of Public Safety in issuing concealed                           weapons permits.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows the Criminal Investigations and Technical Services Division to use fees                           collected for the issuance, renewal, and replacement of concealed weapons permits                           as a dedicated credit to cover the costs of issuing, renewing, and replacing those                           permits  and                               .    makes certain technical changes.
