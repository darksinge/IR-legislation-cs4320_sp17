Killpack          AN ACT RELATING TO CITIES AND TOWNS  MODIFYING THE PROVISIONS         RELATING TO THE FILLING OF A POSITION IN THE CLASSIFIED CIVIL SERVICE          AND MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                         , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section            is amended to read                         .  Certification of applicants for position    Number    Eligible lists,           removal.               Whenever       If a position in the classified civil service is to be filled, the civil service          commission shall as soon as possible certify to the appointing power the names of  five persons          to fill such position from those persons having                 a   ten persons, if the position to be filled is entry level  or               b   five persons, if the position to be filled is other than entry level.                   Persons certified under Subsection     shall have the highest standing in the eligible          list, but a lesser number may be certified  when there is not  if the required number is not on the          eligible list.                   If more than one position is available in the same department, the civil service          commission shall also certify to the appointing power one additional name for each additional          position to be filled.                   a   All persons not appointed shall be restored to their relative positions on the eligible           list.              b   All persons who have been on the eligible list for two years without appointment shall         be removed  therefrom  from the list and  can only  may be returned  thereto  to it only upon         regular examination.
