COUNTY HEALTH DEPARTMENTS                             OPERATIONAL STRUCTURE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Kevin S. Garn                             This act modifies provisions related to Local Health Departments.  The act removes the                          requirement that municipalities join with counties to create and maintain a local health                          department.  The act requires county governments to create health departments to serve                          incorporated and unincorporated areas.  The act permits contiguous counties to form a                           health department through an interlocal agreement.  The act amends provisions related to                           the board and powers and duties of the local health department.  The act amends provisions                           related to the costs of local health departments and the use of local health department funds.                            The act makes technical and clarifying amendments.  The act requires local health                           departments to conform to new requirements by December   ,     .
