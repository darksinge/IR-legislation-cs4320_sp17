Lockhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions relating to redevelopment agencies.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    adds a definition of  inactive industrial site                                 .    modifies requirements applicable to a project area plan                                .    modifies a provision imposing a size restriction on project areas to make an                           exception for areas that include an inactive industrial site                                .    excludes inactive industrial sites from the calculation of combined incremental                           value for purposes of a provision prohibiting the adoption of a project area plan if a                           limit on combined incremental value is exceeded                                .    makes the presence of an inactive industrial site in a project area an alternative to                           finding blight for purposes of a redevelopment project  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill coordinates with S.B.    , Revisions to Redevelopment Agency Provisions,                           by merging the amendments.
