Section           is amended to read                       .   Centennial Highway Trust Fund.                  There is created an expendable trust fund entitled the Centennial Highway Trust Fund.                  The fund consists of monies generated from the following revenue sources                a   any voluntary contributions received for the construction, major reconstruction, or          major renovation of state or federal highways                b   appropriations made to the fund by the Legislature                c   registration fees designated under Subsection     a          and               d   the sales and use tax amounts provided for in Subsection                  .                   a   The fund shall earn interest.               b   All interest earned on fund monies shall be deposited into the fund.                   The executive director may use fund monies, as prioritized by the Transportation          Commission, only to pay the costs of construction, major reconstruction, or major renovation to          state and federal highways.
