         UTAH EDUCATIONAL SAVINGS PLAN AMENDMENTS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Stewart Barlow      Senate Sponsor  Stephen H. Urquhart             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill amends provisions relating to the Utah Uniform Probate Code.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   amends the Utah Uniform Probate Code so that custodial property is created and a       transfer is made when contributions are made into a custodial account at the Utah       Educational Savings Plan  and                  makes technical changes.       Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             This bill provides a special effective date.
