DIVORCE ORIENTATION                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Lorie D. Fowlke                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates a divorce orientation course as a pilot program in the Third and Fourth                           Judicial Districts.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates a pilot program in the Third and Fourth Judicial Districts requiring a divorce                           orientation course before a complaint can be filed                                .    requires that the course be taught by certain persons certified by the Administrative                           Office of the Courts                                .    specifies the curriculum and length for the course                                .    allows the Administrative Office of the Courts to combine divorce orientation with                           the mandatory divorce education course                                .    provides for a provider fee of not more than    , and makes provision for those                           who cannot pay                                .    requires that information about the course be provided to both petitioner and                           respondent and encourages both to attend  and                               .    prohibits the continuance of the divorce action until the petitioner has taken the                           course.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                              Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                      . , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section          .      is enacted to read                                      . .  Divorce orientation    Pilot program in Third and Fourth District                           Courts.                                    Prior to the filing of a complaint for divorce, a petitioner in the Third or Fourth                          Judicial District shall attend a divorce orientation course.  A certificate of completion, dated no                          more than six months prior, shall be filed with the petition.                                    The clerk of the court shall provide to a petitioner who attempts to file a complaint                          for divorce without the certificate of completion, a list of providers certified to provide divorce                          orientation and a copy of this section.                                    A divorce orientation course shall be at least one hour in duration and include                                 a   options available as alternatives to divorce                                 b   resources available from courts and administrative agencies for resolving custody                          and support issues without filing for divorce                                 c   resources available to improve the marriage and to address the issues that are                          causing the marriage to break down, including but not limited to marriage counseling and                          marriage education                                 d   a discussion of the consequences of divorce                                 e   a discussion of the process of divorce  and                                f   options available for proceeding with a divorce, such as                                 i   mediation                                 ii   collaboration  and                                iii   litigation.                                    The following persons may provide divorce orientation courses after certification                          by the Administrative Office of the Courts                                    a   licensed attorneys                                 b   mediators on the roster of mediators maintained by the Administrative Office of the                          Courts                                 c   marriage and family therapists licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Part  , Social                          Worker Licensing Act, Part  , Marriage and Family Therapist Licensing Act, Part  ,                          Professional Counselor Licensing Act, or Title   , Chapter   , Psychologist Licensing Act                                 d   any member of the clergy                                 e   marriage education providers  or                                f   any other provider approved by the court.                                    The course may be provided in conjunction with the mandatory course for                          divorcing parents required by Section            .     .                                    The Administrative Office of the Courts shall administer the course pursuant to                          Title   , Chapter   , Utah Procurement Code, through private or public contracts and organize                          the program as a pilot program in the Third and Fourth Judicial Districts.                                    Each participant shall pay the costs of the course, which may not exceed    , to the                          course provider.  A participant who is unable to pay the costs of the course may attend without                          payment and request an Affidavit of Impecuniosity from the provider to be filed with the                          complaint.  The provider shall be reimbursed for its costs by the Administrative Office of the                          Courts.  A petitioner who is later determined not to meet the qualifications for impecuniosity                          may be ordered to pay the costs of the course.                                    Appropriations from the General Fund to the Administrative Office of the Courts                          for the Divorce Orientation Course shall be used to pay the costs of an indigent petitioner who                          is determined to be impecunious as provided in Subsection    .                                    The Online Court Assistance Program shall include instructions with the forms for                          divorce that inform the petitioner of the requirement of this section.                                     Both parties are encouraged to attend a divorce orientation course.  Information                          regarding the course shall be included with the complaint when served on the respondent.                                     It shall be an affirmative defense in all divorce actions that the prefiling                          requirements were not complied with, and the action may not continue until the petitioner has                          complied.
