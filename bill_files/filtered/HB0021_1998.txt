SELECTIVE SERVICE FILING PREREQUISITE TO           RECEIVING POSTSECONDARY FINANCIAL AID                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Bryan D. Holladay          AN ACT RELATING TO POSTSECONDARY EDUCATION  REQUIRING MALE         STUDENTS TO CERTIFY THEIR SELECTIVE SERVICE STATUS AS A PREREQUISITE         TO RECEIVING FINANCIAL AID TO ATTEND A STATE INSTITUTION OF HIGHER         EDUCATION.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                  B       , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    B        is enacted to read                  B       .  Eligibility for student financial aid    Filing of selective service status.                   A male born after December   ,     , may not receive any state supported loan, grant,          or scholarship for attendance at an institution within the state system of higher education unless          he has filed a statement of selective service status with the institution.                   The statement shall certify one of the following                a   that the male has registered with the selective service system in accordance with the          Military Selective Service Act,    U.S.C.  App.     , as amended                b   that the male is not required to register with the selective service system because he          is                i   under    or over    years of age                ii   on active duty with the armed forces of the United States other than for training in a          reserve or national guard unit                iii   a nonimmigrant alien lawfully in the United States in accordance with the          Immigration and Nationality Act,   U.S.C. Sec.      a       or              iv   not a citizen of the United States and is a permanent resident of the Trust Territory of         the Pacific Islands or the Northern Mariana Islands.                   a   The board of regents, through the commissioner of higher education, shall specify         the form of statement to be filed under Subsection    .              b   Each statement shall contain a section               i   certifying registration with the selective service system and a space for the student to         record his selective service number  and              ii   for the certification of nonregistration and for an explanation of the reason for          exemption.               c   The board may require documentation for the certifications under Subsection     b .                   Institutions within the state system of higher education may not make or guarantee any          loan, grant, scholarship, or other state supported financial assistance to a male student unless the          student has filed the statement required under Subsection    .                   a   If an institution within the state system of higher education has received a statement          certifying that the individual is registered under Subsection     a  or is exempt from registration          for a reason other than he is under    years of age, the individual is not required to file any further          statement with the institution under this section.               b   If the institution receives a statement of exemption because the individual is under             years of age, it shall require the filing of a new statement each time the individual seeks to apply          for financial assistance for educational expenses, until it receives a statement certifying that the          individual has registered with the selective service system or is exempt from registration for a          reason other than being under    years of age.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
