Walker                                House Sponsor            David  Clark                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Identity Fraud Act within the Criminal Code regarding use of                           another person s identifying information.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that if a person uses another party s identifying information with fraudulent                           intent and to obtain anything of value, it is not a defense that the person did not                           know that the identifying information belonged to another person.
