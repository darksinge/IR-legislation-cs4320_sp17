Stuart Adams                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Motor Vehicles Code and Revenue and Taxation Code by creating                           an optional six month motor vehicle registration for certain motor vehicles.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides definitions                                .    exempts a six month motor vehicle registration from selected twelve month motor                           vehicle registration provisions                                .    creates an optional six month motor vehicle registration for certain motor vehicles                                .    addresses six month motor vehicle registration renewals                                .    makes a six month motor vehicle registration subject to the Motor Vehicles Act                                .    creates a uniform fee schedule for motor vehicles registered under the six month                           registration period  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on January  ,     .
