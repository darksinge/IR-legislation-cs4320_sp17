STATE DISASTER CONTINGENCY FUNDING                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Curtis Oda                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates a State Disaster Contingency Fund to be managed by the Division of                           Emergency Services and Homeland Security.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that all principal and interest paid by local governments that have received                           a loan under the state loan program for disasters be paid into the State Disaster                           Contingency Fund                                .    removes provisions permitting additional loans under the state loan program for                           disasters                                .    establishes the State Disaster Contingency Fund and provides that the fund shall be                           administered by the Division of Emergency Services and Homeland Security                                .    provides that the fund may be used for                                    .    state emergencies and disasters  and                                   .    loans to counties and municipalities for local emergencies and disasters  and                               .    provides the division with rulemaking power to set the terms, conditions, and                           interest rates for providing loans from the fund to counties and municipalities. Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                         . .  Establishing the State Disaster Contingency Fund    Providing for                            deposits and expenditures from the fund.                                     There is created a restricted special revenue fund to be known as the State Disaster                           Contingency Fund.                                     The fund shall be administered by the Division of Emergency Services and                           Homeland Security.                                     The purpose of the fund is to provide                                  a   monies to respond to state of emergencies and disasters  and                                 b   loans, in relation to local emergencies and local disasters, to a county or                           municipality under the conditions, terms, and interest rates established under this part.                                     The fund shall contain                                  a   all monies appropriated to it by the Legislature  and                                 b   all interest and principal paid in association with repayment of a loan made under                           this section or Section             .     .                                     All interest generated by the State Disaster Contingency Fund shall be deposited                           into the fund.                                     Any unallocated balance in the fund at the end of a fiscal year shall be nonlapsing.                                     In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                           Division of Emergency Services and Homeland Security shall make rules for the conditions,                              terms, and interest rates for loans made from the State Disaster Contingency Fund created                           under this part.
