EDUCATOR LICENSING AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  David N. Cox                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the State System of Public Education Code by amending educator                          licensing and license renewal provisions.                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that completion of the  Passport to Teaching certification from the                           American Board for Certification of Teacher Excellence satisfies the requirements                           for a level   license                                .    provides that completion of the Master Teacher certification from the American                           Board for Certification of Teaching Excellence satisfies the requirements for a level                             license  and                                .    provides that completion of either the Passport to Teaching certification or the                            Master Teacher certification satisfies all license points or other professional                           activities required for renewal of a level  ,  , or   license.
