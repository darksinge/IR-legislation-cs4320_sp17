Stephens          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES  ENACTING PROVISIONS FOR THE         ENFORCEMENT OF INTERCONNECTION SERVICE QUALITY STANDARDS AND         INTERCONNECTION AGREEMENTS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                    b   , Utah Code Annotated                        b   , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section      b    is enacted to read                    b   .  Public Service Commission authority to enforce interconnection service           quality standards and interconnection agreements.                   For purposes of this section,  interconnection service quality standards  means          specific, measurable criteria that shall be applied to an incumbent telephone corporation s          provision of                a   interconnection services                b   services for resale                c   unbundled network elements  and               d   the operations support systems that support those services and elements, including          standards for the forecasting of the volume of telecommunications traffic, provisioning intervals,          and network capacity.                   To serve the public interest and to enable the development and growth of competition          within the telecommunications market in the state the commission shall, by order when considered          necessary by the commission, enforce                a   rules regarding interconnection service quality standards adopted by the commission         under authority of this chapter  and              b   commission approved interconnection arrangements entered into pursuant to Sections             and     of the Federal Telecommunications Act, whether by               i   means of a commission approved interconnection agreement               ii   a statement of generally available terms under Section     f  of the Federal         Telecommunications Act  or              iii   an interconnection tariff.                  Whenever a party to an approved interconnection agreement or a person aggrieved by          a violation of the commission s interconnection service quality rules, a statement of generally          available terms, or an interconnection tariff files a complaint with the commission, under          Subsection     b  .     e , the commission shall have the power to enforce                a   the terms of the interconnection agreement                b   the commission s interconnection service quality rules                c   the statement of generally available terms  or               d   the interconnection tariff. Copies of the answer          or responsive pleading shall be served on the complainant and the Division of Public Utilities.               c   A prehearing conference shall be held not later than
