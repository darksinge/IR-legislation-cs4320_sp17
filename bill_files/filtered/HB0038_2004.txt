SALES AND USE TAX   REPEAL OF CABLE                           AND SATELLITE SERVICE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Greg J. Curtis                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Sales and Use Tax Act to modify the transactions that are subject                           to sales and use tax.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    deletes certain definitions                                .    removes certain amounts paid or charged for multi channel video or audio service                           from the sales and use tax base  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an effective date.
