Holmgren         AN ACT RELATING TO THE BEAR RIVER DEVELOPMENT ACT  CHANGING THE TIME        PERIOD FOR REPAYMENT OF CONSTRUCTION AND ENVIRONMENTAL        MITIGATION COSTS.        This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows         AMENDS                       , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                           , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah             Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah             Section  .  Section            is amended to read                       .  Period for repayment of construction and environmental mitigation costs.                 The division and the contracting entity shall, by contractual agreement, establish when        and in what amount water developed by the project will be delivered to the contracting entity.                       If a contract was made before completion of the project, the contracting entity        shall repay the construction and environmental mitigation costs  within a period not to exceed           years from the date the project was completed.  as follows              a   any developed water taken by the contracting entity during the first ten years after the        project is completed shall be repaid within    years from the date the developed water is delivered        to the contracting entity  and             b   any developed water taken by the contracting entity after the tenth anniversary date of        the project s completion shall be repaid within    years from the date the project was completed.                       If a contract was made after the project was completed, the contracting entity shall        repay the construction and environmental mitigation costs within a period not to exceed    years        from the date the contract was made.             Section  .  Section            is amended to read                       .  Water charges.             The division shall set prices for the sale or lease of developed water sufficient to                  recover the reimbursable construction and environmental mitigation costs within the time        period specified in Section                       and pay for the interest on those costs                  pay for operation and maintenance costs  and                 accumulate an adequate reserve for repair and replacement.
