LAW ENFORCEMENT   REPORTING                           HAZARDOUS MATERIALS RELATING TO                           CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor  Eric K. Hutchings                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Clandestine Drug Lab Act and the Local Health Departments                           chapter to require law enforcement agencies to report to local health departments any                           sites where the agency has reason to believe there is contamination due to illegal drug                           labs.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires law enforcement agencies to report to the local health department those                           sites of arrests involving clandestine drug laboratories where there is reasonable                           cause to believe there is contamination                                .    requires local health departments to maintain searchable records of law enforcement                           agency reports of clandestine lab activity sites                                .    requires the local health departments to make these records available to the public                           for a reasonable fee and notify the public that the reports are advisory only  and                               .    requires the local health departments to provide upon request a list of local                           businesses that provide testing and cleanup services.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                    This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
