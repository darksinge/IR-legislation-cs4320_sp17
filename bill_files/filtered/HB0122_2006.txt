UTAH SMALL BUSINESS HEALTH PLAN                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Steven R. Chris Buttars                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the Utah Small Business Health Plan that allows small businesses to                           obtain health coverage under the Public Employees  Benefit and Insurance Program,                           beginning on July  ,     .                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Utah Small Business Health Plan and establishes its eligibility, benefits,                           funding and reserves, and partner networks                                .    provides definitions                                .    creates a Utah Small Business Health Plan Advisory Council  and                               .    makes technical corrections.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an effective date. Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Plan  means the Utah Small Business Health Plan created in this part.                                    a    Small business  means an employer that has                                 i   between   and    employees and their immediate families  and                                ii   filed at least one business tax return showing normally accepted business activity.                                b    Small business  does not include                                 i   aggregate employers or majority ownership employers with greater than    total                          employees  and                                ii   a business created for the purpose of obtaining insurance under this part. Eligibility.                                     a   To be eligible to enroll in the plan, an employer shall meet the requirements                           under this part and under rules made in accordance with this part.                                 b   The rules for plan participation eligibility shall include                                  i   an employer shall meet the definition of a small business under Section                                                    ii   an employer shall have filed at least one business tax return showing normally                           accepted business activity                                  iii   an employer has not had other health insurance coverage for a designated time                           period that shall not exceed    months                                  iv   an annual cap of    ,    on medical care available to an employee and the                           employee s immediate family  and                                 v   other necessary participation and plan design details.                                     An employer may not be determined to be ineligible to enroll in the plan based on a                           diagnosis or preexisting condition.                                     a   The program shall determine eligibility and send notification of the decision                           within    days after receiving the application for coverage.                                 b   If the program cannot reach a decision because the applicant fails to take a required                           action or there is an administrative or other emergency beyond the program s control, the                           program shall                                  i   document the reason for the delay in the applicant s case record  and                                 ii   inform the applicant of the status of the application and time frame for completion. The plan shall be self funded by employer premiums and cost sharing requirements                           for enrollees, including deductibles, copayments, and coinsurance requirements.                                     Plan reserves shall be funded from                                  a   plan monies                                  b   requiring employers to make a deposit equal to a final month of premiums  and                                 c   appropriations or loans made for this purpose by the Legislature.                                     a   All insurance risk associated with the Utah Small Business Health Plan shall be                           the responsibility of the plan and not the program, the office, or any of its other programs or                           plans.                                 b   The program shall indemnify the benefit plans or purchase commercial reinsurance                           as considered appropriate by the program.
