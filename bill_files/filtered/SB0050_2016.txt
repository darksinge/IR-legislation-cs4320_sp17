Section           is amended to read                      . Autism Treatment Account    Medical loss ratio calculation    Use of       account.                 There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the Autism       Treatment Account.                 The account shall consist of              a   gifts, grants, devises, donations, and bequests of real property, personal property, or       services, from any source, or any other conveyance that may be made to the account from       private sources              b   interest and other earnings derived from the account money  and             c   any additional amounts as appropriated by the Legislature.                 If an insurer contributes to the account, for purposes of calculating an insurer s       medical loss ratio under the PPACA, as defined in Section   A      , the insurance       commissioner shall consider the contribution to the account to be a claims payment by the       insurer.                 Except as provided in Subsection    , the executive director of the department shall       be responsible for administering the account.                 The committee shall, consistent with the requirements of this title              a   prioritize spending of account funds, as permitted under Subsection                  b   determine which treatment providers qualify for disbursements from the account       for services rendered  and             c   authorize all other distributions from the account, except that disbursements for       expenses authorized under Subsections     b  and  c  shall also require the approval of the       executive director.                 Account money may be used to              a   evaluate and treat a qualified child by utilizing applied behavior analysis or other       proven effective treatments as determined by the committee under Subsection              b               b   pay all actual and necessary operating expenses for the committee and staff  and             c   pay administrative or other expenses of the Department of Health related to the       program, except where those expenses are greater than    of the total account funds.                 All interest and other earnings derived from the account money shall be deposited       into the account.                 The state treasurer shall invest the money in the account under Title   , Chapter  ,       State Money Management Act.
