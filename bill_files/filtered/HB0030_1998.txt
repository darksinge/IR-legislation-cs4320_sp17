PUBLIC SCHOOL HAZARDOUS BUS ROUTES           APPROPRIATION                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Wayne A. Harper          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  PROVIDING A     ,    APPROPRIATION         TO THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION FOR DISTRIBUTION TO SCHOOL         DISTRICTS TO FUND BUS ROUTES REQUIRED BECAUSE OF HAZARDOUS         WALKING CONDITIONS  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act enacts uncodified material.          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .   Appropriation.                   a   There is appropriated from the Uniform School Fund for fiscal year        ,              ,    to the State Board of Education for distribution to school districts to fund or help fund          bus routes required because of hazardous walking conditions.               b   As used in this section,  hazardous walking conditions  means those conditions caused          by major road construction or repair or the rerouting of vehicular traffic due to major road          construction or repair that threaten the safety of students traveling to and from school.                   This appropriation is in addition to the appropriation made in Section   A   a     for          state supported transportation of public school students and the other revenue sources authorized          for student transportation purposes under Section   A   a    .                   The board shall allocate the appropriation to school districts on the basis of competitive          grant applications and compliance with qualifying criteria established by the board pursuant to its          rulemaking authority.              Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
