WILD TURKEY MANAGEMENT                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ronda Rudd Menlove                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends provisions relating to the management of turkeys.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows a person to take a turkey if the turkey is on the person s private property                                .    requires the Division of Wildlife Resources to investigate and mitigate damage by a                           turkey                                  .    requires the Division of Wildlife Resources to establish two general season turkey                           hunts per year  and                                     .    makes technical changes. Damage by turkeys.                                    a   If a turkey damages a person s private property the person may                                 i   notify the division of the damage  and                                ii   request that the division take action to alleviate the damage.                                b   The person shall allow division staff reasonable access to the damaged property to                          verify and alleviate the damage.
