General Description                                This bill amends provisions related to the dates on which a statewide or local special                           election may be scheduled.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends the dates on which the governor, Legislature, or the legislative body of a                           local political subdivision may schedule, respectively, a statewide or local special                           election  and                               .    makes technical changes.
