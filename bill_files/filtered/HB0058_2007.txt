NEW SCHOOL DISTRICT LEEWAY                           RETENTION                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Bradley M. Daw                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill allows certain newly created school districts to continue certain property tax                           levies existing in a predecessor district.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows certain newly created school districts to continue certain property tax levies                           approved by a vote of the predecessor district electorate                                .    places limitations on the levies  and                               .    exempts the levies from truth in taxation advertisement requirements for a certain                           period of time.
