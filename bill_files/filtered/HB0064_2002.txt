CONSUMER PROTECTION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Afton B. Bradshaw                             This act amends the Commerce and Trade Code and the Utah Criminal Code.  The act                          amends the definition of  unsolicited telephone call.   The act adjusts the bonding                          requirements for those making telephone solicitations.  The act amends the right of recision                          of a person making a purchase from a telephone soliciting business.  The act amends the                          provision for investigations of identity theft violations.  The act makes technical changes.
