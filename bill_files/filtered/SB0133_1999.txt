This act enacts uncodified material.                     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                           Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,     ,    to                     the State Board of Regents for adult apprenticeship related instruction programs at the state s nine                     institutions of higher education.                              The board shall use the appropriation to                           a   coordinate essential training programs designed to provide highly skilled workers                     throughout the state which are needed by the crafts and trades industry and consumers                           b   coordinate the development and distribution of statewide articulated curricula                           c   coordinate the development and distribution of the articulated curriculum needed in                     rural locations throughout the state using distance learning delivery systems                           d   implement statewide articulated curricula including essential operating costs  and                          e   enhance the uniformity of apprenticeship related distance learning management                     services statewide.                              The board shall distribute the appropriation on the basis of a formula driven by                     annualized apprenticeship full time enrollments.                          Section  .   Effective date.                          This act takes effect on July  ,     .
