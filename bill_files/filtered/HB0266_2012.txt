Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that the Driver License Division may not license a person who is    years                           of age or older unless the person                                    .    has completed an approved course in driver training  or                                   .    certifies that the person, under the authority of a learner permit issued by the                           Driver License Division, has completed at least    hours of driving a motor                           vehicle, of which at least    hours were completed during night hours after                           sunset  and                               .    makes technical changes.
