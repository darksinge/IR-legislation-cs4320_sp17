CRIMINAL PENALTIES FOR SEXUAL CONTACT WITH A                           STUDENT                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   LaVar Christensen                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Utah Code of Criminal Procedure and the Utah Criminal Code                           regarding sexual offenses.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that when any employee of or volunteer at a public or private elementary                           or secondary school commits or attempts to commit against an enrolled student of                           the school any sexual offense that is not currently a first degree felony or a second                           degree felony, the offense is a second degree felony                                .    imposes the same second degree felony penalty if the offense against a student                           occurs within five years after the offender was most recently employed by or had                           volunteered at a school  and                               .    includes these convicted persons on the sex offender registry.
