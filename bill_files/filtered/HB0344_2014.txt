INCORPORATION ELECTION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jon Cox                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends provisions related to an election held to determine the incorporation of                           a city or town.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    authorizes a county to hold a local special election on the proposed incorporation of                           a city or town                                .    amends the definitions of  incorporation election  and  incorporation petition   and                               .    makes technical corrections.
