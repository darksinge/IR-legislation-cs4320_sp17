PROHIBITING RESIDENTIAL LOTS FROM                           CROSSING COUNTY LINES                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   David L. Hogue                             This act modifies the Utah Municipal Code and the county code to prohibit residential lots                          under one acre in size from crossing county boundary lines.  The act also prohibits the                          division of a residence by a county line.  The act provides an effective date.
