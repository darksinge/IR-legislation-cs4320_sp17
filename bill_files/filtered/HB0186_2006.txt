SUICIDE PREVENTION PLAN                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   David L. Hogue                                Senate Sponsor             Curtis S. Bramble                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts uncodified material requiring the Division of Substance Abuse and                           Mental Health within the Department of Human Services to develop a suicide                           prevention plan.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health to develop a suicide                           prevention plan prior to November  ,                                     .    requires the division to develop the plan with the input of specified groups  and                               .    requires the division to submit the plan to the Health and Human Services Interim                           Committee at or before the committee s November      interim meeting.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed on December   ,     .                           Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .  Suicide prevention plan.                                       Prior to November  ,     , the Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health                          within the Department of Human Services shall develop a suicide prevention plan.  The plan                          shall                                 a   include specific, measurable actions that may be taken by various governmental                          agencies, nongovernmental organizations, families, and individuals to prevent and respond to                          suicide, particularly among adolescent and elderly Utahns                                 b   identify those actions under Subsection     a  which have already been                          implemented                                 c   identify those actions under Subsection     a  which have not already been                          implemented but could be implemented within existing state budgets                                 d   identify those actions under Subsection     a  which will require new funding to be                          implemented or continued  and                                e   specify the amount of funding required for each action under Subsection     d  and                          potential sources of those funds.                                    The Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health shall develop the plan under                          Subsection     with the input of                                 a   the Division of Aging and Adult Services and the Division of Juvenile Justice                          Services within the Department of Human Services                                 b   the Department of Health                                 c   the Administrative Office of the Courts                                 d   local substance abuse and mental health authorities                                 e   local health departments                                 f   the State Office of Education                                 g   the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention                                 h   local and national suicide prevention organizations  and                                i   persons who have been affected by suicide.                                    The Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health shall submit the suicide                          prevention plan developed under Subsection     to the Health and Human Services Interim                          Committee at or before the committee s November      interim meeting.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This bill is repealed on December   ,     .
