Section      C            is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  SNAKE VALLEY AQUIFER ADVISORY COUNCIL                                  C       .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Snake Valley Aquifer Advisory Council.                                Section  .    Section      C            is enacted to read                                  C       .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Council  means the Snake Valley Aquifer Advisory Council created in Section                               C           .                                     Research team  means the Snake Valley Research Team created in Section                               C           .                               Section  .    Section      C            is enacted to read                                  C       .  Council creation    Members    Terms.                                    There is created a state advisory council known as the  Snake Valley Aquifer                          Advisory Council.                                     The advisory council is composed of the following six members                                 a   one member from the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives                                 b   one member from the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate                                 c   the governor or the governor s designee  and                                d   three members appointed by the governor as follows                                 i   one county commissioner, from individuals recommended by an organization that                          represents counties in the state, who is a resident of                                    A   Tooele County                                 B   Juab County                                 C   Millard County  or                                D   Beaver County  and                                ii   two residents of                                 A   Tooele County                                 B   Juab County                                 C   Millard County  or                                D   Beaver County.                                    A member appointed under Subsection     a ,  b , or  d  is appointed to a two year                          term.                                    Notwithstanding the requirements of Subsection    , the governor shall, at the time                          of appointment or reappointment, adjust the length of terms to ensure that the terms of council                          members appointed under Subsection     d  are staggered so that approximately half of the                          council is appointed every two years.                                    The governor shall appoint a chairperson.                                    If a vacancy occurs in council membership for any reason the replacement shall be                          appointed for the unexpired term in the same manner as the vacated member was appointed.                               Section  .    Section      C            is enacted to read                                  C       .  Advisory council duties    Meetings.                                    The council shall                                 a   meet at least annually but may also meet at the call of                                 i   the chair  or                                ii   at least two members of the council                                 b   provide advice to the research team, including recommendations concerning the                          type of data and information gathered by the research team                                 c   review the data and information gathered and reported by the research team,                          including reviewing changes from baseline data collected by the research team  and                                d   act as liaison between the research team and the constituency groups represented by                          the council.                                    A majority of the council members constitute a quorum for the transaction of                             council business.                                    The council may advise the governor and the Legislature on any issue relating to its                          review under Subsection     c .                               Section  .    Section      C            is enacted to read                                  C       .  Compensation of members    Expenses.                                    a   A member who is not a government employee may not receive compensation or                          benefits for the member s service, but may receive per diem and expenses incurred in the                          performance of the member s official duties at the rates established by the Division of Finance                          under Sections       A           and       A          .                                b   A member may decline to receive per diem and expenses for the member s service.                                     a   A state government officer and employee member who does not receive salary,                           per diem, or expenses from the agency the member represents for the member s service may                           receive per diem and expenses incurred in the performance of the member s official duties at                           the rates established by the Division of Finance under Sections       A           and       A          .                                 b   A state government officer and employee member may decline to receive per diem                           and expenses for the member s service.                                     a   A local government member who does not receive salary, per diem, or expenses                           from the entity that the member represents for the member s service may receive per diem and                           expenses incurred in the performance of the member s official duties at the rates established by                           the Division of Finance under Sections       A           and       A          .                                 b   A local government member may decline to receive per diem and expenses for the                           member s service.                                Section  .    Section      C            is enacted to read                                   C       .  Staff.                                The director of the Public Lands Policy Coordinating Office shall provide staff services                           to the council.                                Section  .    Section      C            is enacted to read                                   C       .  Research team.                                     There is created a Snake Valley Aquifer Research Team composed of the following                           eight members                                  a   a representative of the Division of Air Quality, appointed by the executive director                              of the Department of Environmental Quality                                  b   a representative of the Department of Agriculture and Food, appointed by the                           commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and Food                                  c   the executive director of the Department of Natural Resources or the executive                           director s designee                                  d   the following members appointed by the executive director of the Department of                           Natural Resources                                  i   a representative of the Utah Geological Survey                                  ii   a representative of the Division of Wildlife Resources                                  iii   a representative of the Division of Water Rights  and                                 iv   a representative of the Species Recovery Program  and                                 e   a faculty member, appointed by the governor, from a college or university in the                           state with expertise in                                  i   establishing baseline socioeconomic data and information  and                                 ii   measuring adverse socioeconomic impacts.                                     The director of the Public Lands Policy Coordinating Office shall coordinate and                           direct the research team s data and information collection, research, and reporting required by                           Section       C           .
