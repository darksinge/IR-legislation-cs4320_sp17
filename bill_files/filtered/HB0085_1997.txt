Koehn          AN ACT RELATING TO CRIMINAL CODE  PRECLUDING A PERSON CONVICTED OF         CERTAIN DOMESTIC VIOLENCE CRIMES FROM PURCHASING, POSSESSING, OR         TRANSFERRING A FIREARM, AMMUNITION, OR HANDGUN  PROVIDING A         DEFINITION AND PENALTY  PRESCRIBING A PENALTY FOR A PERSON WHO          PROVIDES A FIREARM OR AMMUNITION TO SOMEONE THE PERSON KNOWS OR          REASONABLY BELIEVES HAS BEEN CONVICTED OF A MISDEMEANOR CRIME OF          DOMESTIC VIOLENCE  EXCLUDING CERTAIN PERSONS  AND MAKING          CONFORMING CORRECTIONS.
