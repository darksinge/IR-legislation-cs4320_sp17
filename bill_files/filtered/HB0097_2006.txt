There is created an enterprise fund entitled the  Permanent Community Impact                           Fund.                                     The fund consists of                                 a   all amounts appropriated to the impact fund under Section                                                 b  bonus payments deposited to the impact fund pursuant to Subsection                                                    c  bonus payments deposited to the impact fund pursuant to Section       C                                           d   all amounts received for the repayment of loans made by the impact board under                           this chapter  and                                e   all other monies appropriated or otherwise made available to the impact fund by the                           Legislature.
