DRIVER LICENSE OR IDENTIFICATION                           CARD REQUIREMENT FOR SEX OFFENDERS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor  Paul Ray                            Senate Sponsor       Jon J. Greiner                                                       LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Uniform Driver License Act regarding identification cards to                           provide that a person required to register as a sex offender must maintain a current                           identification card if the person does not have a driver license.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires that sex offenders subject to registration must keep a current state                           identification card if the offenders to not have a driver license                                .    provides that no extension of an identification card beyond the statutory expiration                           date may be granted for sex offenders subject to registration  and                               .    establishes a class A misdemeanor penalty for sex offenders subject to registration                           who fail to maintain an identification card if they do not have a driver license.
