JUDICIARY AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Patrice M. Arent                             This act modifies provisions relating to the Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice                          and the Judicial Code.  It adds the requirement to administer funds for extraditions to the                          duties of the commission and allows the state to recoup expenses from a parent or legal                          guardian for the extradition of a minor from another state.  It also repeals the penalty                          provision against judges for wrongful denial of writ of habeas corpus, allows interest on trust                           funds that are earned in courts not of record to go to the county or municipality, and makes                           technical corrections.
