ENDOWMENT FUNDS FOR UTAH CHARITIES                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   J. Brent Haymond          AN ACT RELATING TO REVENUE AND TAXATION  PROVIDING A TAX CREDIT TO         INDIVIDUALS AND CORPORATIONS FOR CONTRIBUTIONS TO CHARITABLE         ENDOWMENTS WITHIN THE STATE  PROVIDING A REPEAL DATE  AND          PROVIDING FOR RETROSPECTIVE OPERATION.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                     b     , as enacted by Chapters   ,     and    , Laws of Utah               ENACTS                        , Utah Code Annotated                             , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is enacted to read                        .  Charitable endowment tax credit.                   As used in this section                a    Charitable organization  has the same meaning as set forth in Section          .               b    Qualifying endowment donation  means a donation to a charitable organization which          specifies that the donation is to be deposited into a qualifying endowment fund.               c    Qualifying endowment fund  means any endowment fund established for the benefit          of a charitable organization which endowment fund has total assets of   ,   ,    or less, which          is restricted in scope such that all income generated by the endowment fund will be used for          purposes, programs, or projects of the charitable organization within the state.                   For taxable years beginning on or after January  ,     , but beginning before          December   ,     , a taxpayer may claim as provided in this section a nonrefundable credit          against any taxes imposed by this chapter, in an amount equal to     of the aggregate amount of         qualifying endowment donations made by that taxpayer during the taxable year.                  If the amount of credit under this section for which a taxpayer is eligible exceeds the         taxpayer s tax liability for the taxable year, the taxpayer may carry forward for up to five taxable         years the portion of the tax credit that exceeds the taxpayer s tax liability for the taxable year.
