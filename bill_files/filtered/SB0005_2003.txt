Section  .    Section      E           is amended to read                              E      .  Retirement and Independent Entities Committee creation.                               There is created  a Legislative  the Retirement and Independent Entities Committee                      composed of    legislators appointed as follows                            a   five senators, appointed by the president of the Senate, with at least two senators from                      the minority party  and                           b   nine representatives, appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives, with                      at least three representatives from the minority party.                               a   The president of the Senate shall designate one of the Senate appointees as a                      cochair of the committee.                           b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate one of the House of                      Representatives appointees as a cochair of the committee.                               Committee members serve for two years, but may be reappointed by the speaker or                      the president.                               The committee shall meet at least twice each year, but may meet more frequently if                      the chairs determine that additional meetings are needed.                               In conducting all of its business, the committee shall comply with the rules of                      legislative interim committees.                               The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff services                      to the committee.                               Salaries and expenses of legislative committee members shall be paid in accordance                      with                                    a   Section                 and                           b   Legislative Joint Rule   .  .
