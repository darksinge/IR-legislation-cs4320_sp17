Wiley  Scott L Wyatt                                                      LONG TITLE                           General Description                                This bill defines what happens if the Legislature fails to pass, or the governor vetoes,                           one or more appropriation acts to fund state government and to fund other units of                           government for the next fiscal year.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    reappropriates the comparable ongoing appropriation for the current fiscal year                              when the Legislature fails to pass, or the governor vetoes, a bill or act containing an                           appropriation for the next fiscal year.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                       . , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section           .      is enacted to read                                       . .  Comprehensive appropriations acts not enacted    Effect.                                    As used in this section                                 a    Comprehensive appropriation acts  means one or more appropriation bills or acts                          that, taken together, provide all the funding for state government in a fiscal year.                                b    Current fiscal year  means                                 i   the fiscal year for which the Legislature has passed one or more comprehensive                          appropriation acts that appropriate monies to state agencies and other government entities  and                                ii   the fiscal year in which those monies are being spent.                                c    Next fiscal year  means the fiscal year beginning the July   after the current fiscal                          year.                                d    Suspended appropriation item  means each appropriation item providing funding                          for the next fiscal year that does not take effect because the Legislature fails to pass the bill or                          act in which it is contained or because the governor vetoes the bill or act in which it is                          contained.                                    For each suspended appropriation item, the comparable appropriation for the                          current fiscal year that is not designated as one time is duplicated and becomes the                          appropriation for the next fiscal year.                                    Subsection     does not apply if, as a result of applying this section, the state s                          appropriations from the General Fund and Uniform School Fund would exceed the total                             estimated revenue available for those funds during the next fiscal year.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               If approved by two thirds of all the members elected to each house, this bill takes effect                          upon approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah                          Constitution Article VII, Section  , without the governor s signature, or in the case of a veto,                          the date of veto override.
