Enrolled                               LIEN DURATION OF A FOREIGN JUDGMENT                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Thomas V. Hatch                                House Sponsor            Lorie D. Fowlke                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill specifies the time at which a foreign judgment expires.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that a foreign judgment expires eight years after the date of entry by the                           court in the foreign jurisdiction unless the lien is renewed in Utah as required by                           Utah law.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                     a  , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section         a       is amended to read                                      a  .  Lien.                                    A foreign judgment entered in a district court under this chapter becomes a lien as                           provided in Section                 if                                 a   a stay of execution has not been granted                                 b   the requirements of this chapter are satisfied  and                                   c   the judgment is recorded in the office of the county recorder where the property of                           the judgment debtor is located, as provided in Section                .                                    The lien becomes effective at the time and date of recording and expires eight years                          after date of entry by the court in the foreign jurisdiction unless renewed in Utah as required by                          Utah law.
