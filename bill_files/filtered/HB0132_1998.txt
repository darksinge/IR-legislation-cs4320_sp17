MOTOR VEHICLE INSURANCE PREMIUM           RATES                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Trisha S. Beck          AN ACT RELATING TO INSURANCE CODE  PROHIBITING CERTAIN INSURANCE         RATE INCREASES FOR VEHICLES UNDER CERTAIN CIRCUMSTANCES.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                  A       , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    A        is enacted to read                  A       .  Premium increases prohibited for acts of vandalism.                   Each rate, rating schedule, and rating manual filed with the commissioner for property          insurance covering a vehicle with comprehensive insurance coverage may not permit a premium          increase due to                a   telephone calls or other inquiries that do not result in the filing of a claim  or               b   a claim resulting from any incident, including acts of vandalism, in which the person          named in the policy or any other person using the insured motor vehicle with the express or          implied permission of the named insured is not at fault.                   This section is an exception to the provisions of Section   A       .      Legislative Review Note      as of                PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
