LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill repeals use of a firing squad as a means of carrying out the death penalty                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     repeals references to the use of a firing squad  and makes the repeal retroactive  and                                    makes provision for the possibility that the retroactive provision is ruled                           unconstitutional  in which case the defendant who has selected the firing squad may                           be so executed                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses
