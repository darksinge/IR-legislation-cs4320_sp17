FEES TASK FORCE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Scott Daniels                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the Adoption Agency Fees Task Force                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     establishes a legislative task force to study fees associated with private adoptions                                     establishes task force membership  duties  and salaries and designates staff for the                           task force                                     requires the task force to prepare a report  and                                    requires the task force to report its findings to the Health and Human Services                           Interim Committee before November                                 Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates                                            to the Senate                                            to the House of Representatives  and                                            to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel                            Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed on November                                     Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                   Section      Adoption Agency Fees Task Force    Creation    Membership    Interim                           rules followed    Compensation    Staff                                     There is created the Adoption Agency Fees Task Force consisting of the following                          members                                 a   three members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate  no more than                          two of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   three members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives  no more than two of whom may be from the same political party                                     a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed                          under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force                                 b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force                                     In conducting its business  the task force shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees                                     Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force shall be paid in accordance                          with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule                                           The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the task force                                Section      Duties    Interim report                                     The task force shall review and make recommendations on adoption fees and costs                          charged by adoption agencies and attorneys in private adoptions including an accounting of                                 a   consideration paid to birth mothers in adoption situations                                 b   consideration paid to legal and other professionals in adoption situations  and                                c   Medicaid funds used to cover delivery expenses                                     The task force shall review and make recommendations regarding the information                          collected on residence of parties in private adoptions  including                                 a   the state or country the birth mother is a resident of  and                                b   the state or country of which the adoptive parent or parents are residents                                     A final report  including any proposed legislation shall be presented to the Health                          and Human Services Interim Committee before November                                         Section      Appropriation                                   There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year         only                                            to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the                          task force                                            to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses                          of representatives on the task force  and                                            to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for                          staffing the task force                                Section      Repeal date                                This bill is repealed on November
