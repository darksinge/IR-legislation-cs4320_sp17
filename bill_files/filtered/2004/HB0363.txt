  Section       Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER      GOVERNMENT EFFICIENCY AND                            EFFECTIVENESS REVIEW COMMITTEE                                             Government Efficiency and Effectiveness Review Committee                              Creation    Membership    Chairs    Salaries and expenses    Staff support                                     There is created the Government Efficiency and Effectiveness Review Committee                          consisting of the following    members                                 a   seven members from the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker  no                          more than five of whom shall be members of the same political party  and                                b   four members of the Senate appointed by the president  no more than three of                          whom shall be members of the same political party                                     a   The speaker of the House shall designate one of the members from the House of                          Representatives appointed under Subsection     a  to act as a cochair of the committee                                 b   The president of the Senate shall designate one of the members from the Senate                          appointed under Subsection     b  to act as a cochair of the committee                                     In conducting its business  the committee shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees                                     Salaries and expenses of the legislators shall be paid in accordance with Section                                        and Legislative Joint Rule                                           a   The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff                          support to the committee                                 b   The committee may request additional staff support from other legislative staff                          offices as needed                                Section       Section                  is enacted to read                                            Duties                                     The committee shall seek to reduce costs in state government and seek to increase                          the efficiency and effectiveness of state government by reviewing                                 a   the budget of one department within state government in detail each year  to                             determine possible reductions or additions to the budget of that department and to identify                          programs or divisions of the department that could be privatized                                 b   executive branch agencies  for possible reorganization  prioritization  and                          streamlining  including time and motion auditing of executive branch agencies                                 c   government services and programs  to evaluate their potential for possible                          privatization                                 d   occupational and professional licensing boards and processes  for possible                          consolidation or deregulation  and                                e   any other means of reducing the costs of state government and increasing the                          efficiency and effectiveness of state government that the committee determines                                     The committee may recommend                                 a   legislation to implement its conclusions                                 b   privatization studies to the Privatization Policy Board created by Section          a                                       c   modifications to any state agency budget to the Executive Appropriations                          Committee and any appropriations subcommittee  and                                d   modifications to occupational and professional licensing boards and processes to                          the Occupational and Professional Licensure Review Committee created by Section
