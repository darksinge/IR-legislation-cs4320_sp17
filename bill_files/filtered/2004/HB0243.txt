                         provides for membership of the task force and compensation for members                                     specifies duties and responsibilities of the task force  and                                    specifies issues that the task force will review                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates                                              from the General Fund for fiscal year         for a two year task force                            Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed on November                                     Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                    Be it Compensation    Staff                                     There is created the Water Rights Task Force consisting of the following members                                    a   five members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate  no more than                          four of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   eight members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives  no more than six of whom may be from the same political party                                     a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed                          under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the committee                                 b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the committee                                     In conducting its business  the committee shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees                                     Salaries and expenses of the members of the committee shall be paid in accordance                          with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule                                           The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the committee                                Section      Duties    Interim report                                     The committee shall review and make recommendations on the following issues                                 a   instream flow                                 b   water conservation and reuse                                 c   over appropriation of water rights                                 d   management of ground water and surface water  and                                e  water right enforcement and penalties                                     A final report  including any proposed legislation shall be presented before                          November           to the Natural Resources  Agriculture  and Environment Interim                          Committee                                Section      Appropriation                                There
