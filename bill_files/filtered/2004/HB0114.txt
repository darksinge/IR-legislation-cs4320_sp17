   LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Money Management Act to allow the use of investment advisers                          by public treasurers                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     defines the term  certified investment adviser  and investment advisers to make security                           trades in their behalf                                     allows certified investment advisers to make trades with broker dealers not on chapter  and                                    makes technical corrections                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None
