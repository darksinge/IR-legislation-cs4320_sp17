LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the Automobile Crime Prevention Authority  establishes its authority                          and functions  and imposes a fee to fund the Authority                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     establishes the membership  authority  and functions of the Automobile Crime                           Prevention Authority and provides that the Authority is an independent state entity                           housed within the Department of Public Safety                                     imposes on each vehicle registration a    fee that is to be transferred to the authority                           for its use to decrease automobile crime  and                                    authorizes the authority to provide aid to state and local law enforcement agencies                            and other agencies  automobile crime prevention programs                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses created                                     a   There is created an independent state agency and body politic and corporate                          known as the Automobile Crime Prevention Authority   The authority is not a division within                          any department of the state                                 b   The authority is located within the Department of Public Safety   The authority                          shall manage the budget  procurement  and other administrative functions of the authority as                          provided in this part  including the management of employees  under the direction and                          supervision of the director of the department                                 c   The authority shall exercise its authorities and duties as provided in this part                          independently of the department                                     The authority is composed of six members  appointed by the governor                                 a   a representative of motor vehicle commerce doing business in Utah                                 b   a representative of the National Insurance Crime Bureau                                 c   the president of the Utah Sheriffs Association or a designee                                 d   the president of the Utah Chiefs of Police Association or a designee                                 e   the director of the Motor Vehicle Enforcement Division of the Utah State Tax                          Commission  or a designee  and                                f   the commissioner of the Department of Public Safety or a designee                                     a   Members shall be appointed for terms of four years  except that the governor                          shall  at the time of appointment or reappointment  adjust the length of terms to ensure that the                          terms of the members are staggered so that approximately half of the members are appointed                          every two years                                 b   The members shall select a chair and vice chair  whose terms are each for two                             years                                 c   i   Four members of the authority constitute a quorum for the transaction of                          business at a meeting or the exercise of a power or function of the authority                                 ii   A quorum is considered present for voting purposes if the member is present at the                          meeting or is participating through the use of amplified telephonic or telecommunications                          equipment                                 d   When a vacancy occurs in the membership for any reason  the governor shall                          appoint a replacement for the unexpired term                                 e   i   The authority shall meet at the call of the chair or as may be provided in the                          bylaws of the authority                                  ii   Meetings may be held at any location within Utah  as determined by the authority                                 Section                        Monies available to the authority from the account shall be expended in the following                           order of priority                                      first  to pay the costs of administration of the authority  including employees as                           necessary for the effective operation of the authority  and                                     second  to reduce automobile crime in Utah by appropriate measures  including                                  a   provide financial support to state agency automobile crime investigators and local                           law enforcement agencies for their automobile crime enforcement teams                                  b   provide financial support to state or local law enforcement agencies for programs                              designed to reduce the incidents of automobile crime                                  c   provide financial support to local prosecutors for programs designed to reduce the                           incidence of automobile crime                                  d   provide financial support to judicial programs designed to reduce the incidence of                           automobile crime                                  e   provide financial support for neighborhood or community organizations  programs                           and business organizations  programs designed to reduce the incidence of automobile crime                            and                                 f   conduct educational programs designed to inform automobile owners of methods of                           preventing automobile crime and to provide equipment  as part of an experimental program  to                           enable automobile owners to prevent automobile crime                                 Section       Section                  is enacted to read                                             State not liable
