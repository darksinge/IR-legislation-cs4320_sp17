This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            ENACTS                                  A      .  , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A      .       is enacted to read                                  A      .  .  Reading Performance Improvement Awards Program    Participation                              Awards.                                    There is established a Reading Performance Improvement Awards Program to                          recognize and reward qualified elementary schools that demonstrate significant reading                          performance improvement with students in kindergarten through grade three.                                    a   i   The State Board of Education shall select nine schools annually to each receive                            ,    awards for their successful participation in the program.                                ii   The schools shall use their awards to purchase books and other reading materials.                                b  The board shall select the nine schools from schools who have successfully applied to                          participate in the program under rules made by the board in accordance with Title   , Chapter   a,                             Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act.                                c   The board, after consultation with the state s colleges of education and local school                          boards, shall also make rules on what constitutes significant reading performance improvement,                          to include demonstrating both written and oral skill improvement in at least grades one through                          three.                                    The board shall provide the Legislature, through its Education Interim Committee, and                          the governor with an annual evaluation of the program, together with recommendations, if                          required, for modifications in the program.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               Except as provided in H.B.  , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated from                          the Uniform School Fund for fiscal year          ,   ,    to the State Board of Education to                          fund the Reading Performance Improvement Awards Program established under Section                               A      .      .                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
