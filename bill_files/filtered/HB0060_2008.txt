General Description                                This bill modifies provisions related to the Legislature to clarify review of health                           insurance mandates by an interim committee.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the process by which the Business and Labor Interim Committee reviews                           health insurance mandates                                .    removes provisions related to the legislative auditor general assisting with a review                                 .    requires the Department of Insurance to take certain actions in relationship to a                           review  and                               .    makes technical and conforming amendments.
