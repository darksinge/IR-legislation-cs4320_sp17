Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    authorizes voters to petition for and then vote on a repeal of a previously adopted                           optional plan to return the county s form of government to the form the county                           operated under before the optional plan was adopted  and                               .    makes technical changes. Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                            .  Repeal of optional plan.                                    An optional plan adopted under this chapter may be repealed as provided in this                          section.                                    Registered voters of a county that has adopted an optional plan may initiate the                          process of repealing an optional plan by filing a petition for the repeal of the optional plan.                                    A petition to repeal an optional plan may not be filed within three years after the                          election of county officers under Section                  .
