APPROPRIATION FOR UTAH STATE UNIVERSITY           PASTURE AND FORAGE INITIATIVE                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Eli H. Anderson          AN ACT RELATING TO HIGHER EDUCATION  PROVIDING A     ,            APPROPRIATION TO IMPLEMENT THE UTAH PASTURE AND FORAGE INITIATIVE,         A RESEARCH AND EDUCATION PROJECT DESIGNED TO ASSIST LIVESTOCK         PRODUCERS INCREASE THE EFFICIENCY OF PASTURE UTILIZATION  AND          PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act enacts uncodified material.          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .   Appropriation.                   There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        ,     ,    to Utah          State University to be used by the Utah Agricultural Experiment Station as matching funds with          a federal grant for the implementation of the Utah Pasture and Forage Initiative, a research and          education project designed to assist livestock producers increase the efficiency of pasture          utilization.                   The university shall use the appropriation under the initiative for concurrent research          and education projects for grazing based livestock production appropriate to the needs and          conditions faced by Utah producers.              Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .
