STATE SCHOOL BOARD ELECTION                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ronda Rudd Menlove                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the election process for membership on the State Board of Education.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the direct, nonpartisan election of members of the State Board of                           Education                                .    repeals the involvement of the governor and the nominating and recruiting                           committee for the State Board of Education in the selection process  and                                .    makes technical corrections.
