Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the time that triggers a water conservancy district s ability to impose a                           higher tax levy                                .    modifies a provision that allows a water conservancy district to increase its tax rate                           so that it applies to districts that contain land located within the Lower Colorado                           River Basin instead of just districts served by water apportioned by the Colorado                           River Compact to the Lower Basin  and                               .    makes technical changes.
