GIFTS, GRANTS, AND DONATIONS TO                           OFFICE OF THE ATTORNEY GENERAL                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Michael E. Noel                                Senate Sponsor             Beverly Ann Evans                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill creates a restricted account for the receipt and expenditure of certain gifts and                           donations to the Office of the Attorney General.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Attorney General Donations Account as a restricted account in the                           General Fund  and                               .    provides that gifts, grants, devises, donations, and bequests of real or personal                           property or services received by the Office of the Attorney General are deposited                           into the account.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                       , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                   Section  .    Section                is enacted to read                                       .  Attorney General Donations Account    Gifts, grants, devises, donations,                           and bequests to the Office of the Attorney General    Uses.                                    There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the                          Attorney General Donations Account.                                    The Attorney General Donations Account consists of gifts, grants, devises,                          donations, and bequests of real property, personal property, or services from any source made                          to the Office of the Attorney General, which gifts, grants, devises, donations, and bequests                          shall then become the property of the state.                                    Upon appropriation by the Legislature, monies from the account shall be used as                          follows                                 a   if the donor designates a specific purpose or use for the gift, grant, devise, donation,                          or bequest, it shall be used solely for that purpose  and                                b   gifts, grants, devises, donations, and bequests not designated under Subsection                              a  shall be used to carry out the duties of the Office of the Attorney General under this                          chapter.      Legislative Review Note      as of                AM   Based on a limited legal review, this legislation has not been determined to have a high  probability of being held unconstitutional.
