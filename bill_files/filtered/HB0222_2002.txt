SCHOOL AND SCHOOL DISTRICT SIZE                           TASK FORCE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   David N. Cox                             This act creates the School and School District Task Force.  This act provides for the                          membership of the task force, task force chairs, compensation of task force members, the                          task force meeting schedule, staff support, task force duties and responsibilities, and task                          force reporting requirements.  The act requires the task force to follow interim rules. The                           act appropriates a total of    ,    from the General Fund for fiscal year         to the                           Senate, the House of Representatives, and the Office of Legislative Research and General                           Counsel to fund the task force.  This act is repealed November   ,     .                           This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   School and School District Size Task Force    Creation    Membership                              Chairs    Interim rules followed    Compensation    Meetings    Staff.                                    There is created the School and School District Size Task Force consisting of the                          following    members                                 a   five members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          three of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   seven members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the House                          of Representatives, no more than four of whom may be from the same political party.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed under                          Subsection     a  as a cochair of the School and School District Size Task Force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the School and School                          District Size Task Force.                                       In conducting its business, the School and School District Size Task Force shall                          comply with the rules of legislative interim committees.                                    Members of the School and School District Size Task Force shall receive                          compensation and expenses in accordance with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                    The School and School District Size Task Force may meet up to eight times.                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support to                          the School and School District Size Task Force.                               Section  .   Duties and responsibilities    Interim report.                                    The School and School District Size Task Force shall review and make                          recommendations on the following issues                                 a   the ideal size of school districts in this state                                 b   the most cost effective and efficient way to create smaller school districts                                 c   the ideal size of schools in this state  and                                d   the most cost effective and efficient way to create smaller schools.                                    The School and School District Size Task Force shall make a final report to the                          Education Interim Committee on or before the November interim meeting.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year         to pay for the School                          and School District Size Task Force                                        ,    to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the                          School and School District Size Task Force                                        ,    to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses of                          representatives on the School and School District Size Task Force  and                                       ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for staffing                          the School and School District Size Task Force.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This act is repealed November   ,     .
