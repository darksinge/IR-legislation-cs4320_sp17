ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH SCIENTIST                           MODIFICATIONS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Michael E. Noel                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies provisions of the Environmental Health Scientist Act related to                           unprofessional conduct.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    expands the unprofessional conduct provisions of the Environmental Health                           Scientist Act to include providing testimony as an environmental health scientist                           during an administrative hearing while in the employ of or on behalf of an entity                           registered with the Division of Corporations and Commercial Code that has not                           posted a corporate surety bond or cash equivalent required for environmental                           litigation.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                     a    , as enacted by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                                                            Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section         a         is amended to read                                      a    .  Unprofessional conduct.                                Unprofessional conduct  includes                                     acting dishonestly or fraudulently in the performance of professional duties as an                           environmental health scientist or environmental health scientist in training                                     intentionally filing a false report or record in the performance of professional duties                           as an environmental health scientist or environmental health scientist in training   and                                     willfully impeding or obstructing another person from filing a report in the                           performance of professional duties as an environmental health scientist or environmental health                           scientist in training .   and                                    providing testimony, as an environmental health scientist, at an administrative                          hearing that is being held by a state agency and is dealing with environmental issues, while in                          the employ of or acting on behalf of                                 a   an entity registered with the Division of Corporations and Commercial Code that                                 i   is involved in environmental litigation proceedings as defined in Subsection                                 a           b   and                                ii   has not posted a corporate surety bond or cash equivalent as required under                          Subsection         a            or                                b   individuals associated with an entity described in Subsections     a  i  and  ii .
