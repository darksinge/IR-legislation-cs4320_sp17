Cox                                Senate Sponsor             Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Off Highway Vehicles Code by amending provisions relating to                           off highway vehicle user fees.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that an owner or operator operating a nonresident off highway vehicle is                           exempt from the requirement to obtain an off highway vehicle user decal and pay                           the off highway vehicle user fee if the nonresident off highway vehicle is used                           exclusively for the purpose of an off highway vehicle manufacturer sponsored event                           within the state                                .    grants the Board of Parks and Recreation rulemaking authority to establish                           eligibility requirements for an off highway vehicle manufacturer sponsored event                            and                               .    makes technical corrections.
