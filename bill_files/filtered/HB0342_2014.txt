POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE STATE BOARD OF                           EDUCATION                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Dana L. Layton                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the powers and duties of the State Board of Education regarding the                           development and adoption of core curriculum standards.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    specifies procedures for the development and adoption of core curriculum standards                           for English language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, including                                    .    the establishment of a standards development committee consisting of parents,                           teachers, and representatives of school districts, business, and higher education                           to assist the board in developing standards  and                                   .    public review and comment of draft core curriculum standards                                .    requires the State Board of Education to establish a standards review committee                           consisting of    parents of Utah public education students to review proposed core                           curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics, science, and social                           studies                                .    requires the State Board of Education to maintain control of, and the power to                           modify, core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics, science,                           and social studies  and                               .    requires the State Board of Education, on or before July  ,     , to adopt revised                              core curriculum standards for English language arts and mathematics that are developed                           specifically for Utah.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                    A      .   , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A      .      is enacted to read                                  A      . .  Core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics,                           science, and social studies.                                    As used in this section,  board  means the State Board of Education.                                    The board shall develop and adopt core curriculum standards for English language                          arts, mathematics, science, and social studies in accordance with Section      A      .       and this                          section.                                    The board shall develop and adopt core curriculum standards for English language                          arts, mathematics, and science that are aligned with the standards of states and nations whose                          students are among the most proficient on national and international achievement tests.                                    a   The board may adopt an academic standard of another state or nation if the                          board determines that the standard is aligned with the basic knowledge, skills, and                          competencies a student is expected to acquire or master as the student advances through the                          public education system.                                b   Notwithstanding Subsection     a , the board may not adopt, in whole, a set of                          standards for English language arts, mathematics, science, or social studies developed for a                          group of states or the nation as Utah s core curriculum standards.                                    The board shall maintain control of, and the power to modify, core curriculum                          standards for English language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies.                                    a   The board shall establish a standards development committee to assist the board                             in developing core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics, science, and                          social studies.                                b   The board shall appoint to a standards development committee established under                          Subsection     a  individuals with expertise in the subject for which standards are being                          developed, including parents, teachers, school leaders, school district representatives, business                          representatives, and faculty of higher education institutions in Utah.                                c   A standards development committee established under Subsection     a  shall                                 i   hold meetings that are open to the public                                 ii   receive public comment  and                                iii   submit recommendations on core curriculum standards for adoption by the board.                                    a   The board shall establish a standards review committee consisting of    parents                          of Utah public education students to review proposed core curriculum standards for English                          language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies.                                b   The membership of the standards review committee includes                                 i   five parents appointed by the board chair                                 ii   five parents appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives  and                                iii   five parents appointed by the president of the Senate.                                c   The board shall provide staff support to the standards review committee.                                d   Except as provided in Subsection     e , the term of office of each member                          appointed to the standards review committee is four years.                                e   The board chair, the speaker of the House of Representatives, and the president of                          the Senate shall adjust the length of terms to stagger the terms of standards review committee                          members so that approximately one half of the committee members are appointed every two                          years.                                f   No member of the standards review committee may receive compensation or                          benefits for the member s service on the committee.                                g   The standards review committee shall submit comments and recommendations to                          the board on proposed core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics,                          science, and social studies.                                    Before adopting core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics,                          science, and social studies, the board shall                                    a   publicize draft core curriculum standards on the State Board of Education s website                          and the Utah Public Notice website created under Section      F                                            b   invite public comment on the draft core curriculum standards for a period of not                          less than    days  and                                c   conduct three public hearings that are held in different regions of the state on the                          draft core curriculum standards.                                    Taking into consideration the public comment and the comments and                          recommendations of the standards review committee, the board may                                 a   adopt draft core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics,                          science, and social studies as final standards  or                                 b   modify draft core curriculum standards for English language arts, mathematics,                           science, and social studies and adopt the modified standards as final standards.                                      On or before July  ,     , the board shall adopt revised core curriculum standards                           for English language arts and mathematics that are developed specifically for Utah.
