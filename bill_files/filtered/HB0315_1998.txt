Cox          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC OFFICERS  PROVIDING RESTRICTIONS ON         SEVERANCE PAY TO OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES OF GOVERNMENTAL ENTITIES.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                        , Utah Code Annotated                            , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is enacted to read            CHAPTER  .  COMPENSATION                        .  Definitions.              As used in this chapter                     Employee  includes a governmental entity s officers and employees.                    Governmental entity  means                a   the entities included in the definition of  governmental entity  under Subsection                      and               b   the Legislature.                   a    Severance pay  means money or other form of remuneration paid or given to an          employee at the time of termination of employment with a governmental entity.               b    Severance pay  does not include accrued                i   vacation pay                ii   compensatory pay  or               iii   sick pay.              Section  .  Section           is enacted to read                       .  Limitation on severance pay.             A governmental entity may not grant severance pay to an employee that exceeds the         amount of the regular salary or wage received by the employee during the employee s last six         months of employment.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
