There is created a revolving loan fund known as the Petroleum Storage Tank Loan                            Fund.                                     The sources of money for the loan fund are                                  a   appropriations to the loan fund                                  b   principal and interest received from the repayment of loans made by the executive                            secretary under Subsection      and                                 c   all investment income derived from money in the fund.                                     The executive secretary may loan, in accordance with this section, money available                            in the loan fund to a person to be used for                                  a   upgrading a petroleum storage tank                                  b   replacing an underground storage tank  or                                    c   permanently closing an underground storage tank.                                     A person may apply to the executive secretary for a loan under Subsection     if all                            tanks owned or operated by that person are in substantial compliance with all state and federal                            requirements or will be brought into substantial compliance using money from the loan fund.
