EXCELLENCE IN  EDUCATION ACT                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Tammy J. Rowan          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  PROVIDING FOR AT LEAST A BIENNIAL         REVIEW AND UPDATE OF A SCHOOL DISTRICT S EDUCATOR EVALUATION         PROGRAM, WITH A FOCUS ON IDENTIFYING, IMPROVING, AND REWARDING         EDUCATIONAL EXCELLENCE  PROVIDING FOR ANNUAL INFORMAL         EVALUATIONS OF CAREER SERVICE EDUCATORS  AND PROVIDING FOR FORMAL          WRITTEN EVALUATIONS EVERY THREE YEARS.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                  A       , as enacted by Chapter  , Laws of Utah               ENACTS                  A       . , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    A       .  is enacted to read                  A       . .  Review and revision of evaluation program.                   Each local school board shall provide for a periodic review and updating of its          evaluation program established under Section   A       .                   The review shall occur on at least a biennial basis and focus on identifying, improving,          and rewarding educational excellence as an integral part of the program.                   If the district uses an evaluation instrument in its program, it shall                a   determine as part of its review whether the instrument is an effective tool in identifying          excellence in the individual being evaluated  and               b   make revisions in the instrument if those revisions will enhance the program.                   The board may use the joint committee established under Section   A        or a          similar group comprised of classroom teachers and administrators to assist them in their review.             Section  .  Section    a        is amended to read                 A       .  Frequency of evaluations.                  A local school board shall provide for the evaluation of its provisional and         probationary educators at least twice each school year.                  a   A local school board shall provide for informal annual evaluations of its career         service educators.              b   These evaluations shall include an opportunity for each career service educator to set         achievable professional performance goals.                   Each career service educator shall receive a formal written evaluation at least once          every three years.                   The evaluations shall focus on identifying, rewarding, and improving educational          excellence.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 AM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
