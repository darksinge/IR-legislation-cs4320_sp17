STATE FIRE CODE AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   James A. Dunnigan                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the State Construction and Fire Codes Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    adds an exception to the requirement that an automatic sprinkler system be installed                           in certain fire areas.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
