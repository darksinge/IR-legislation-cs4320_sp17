ENROLLMENT GROWTH PROGRAM                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Bradley G. Last                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the funding formula for a program that provides money to school                           districts for school building construction.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    removes the requirement that a school district be a recipient of monies distributed                           under the Capital Outlay Foundation Program to qualify for monies under the                           Enrollment Growth Program  and                               .    requires school districts to have a minimum percentage increase in enrollment to                           qualify for monies under the Enrollment Growth Program.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
