Urquhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill addresses the conveyance of real property and the effect of certain changes to                           recorded documents.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    eliminates language identifying specific types of deeds by which an after acquired                           interest in real property may pass                                .    addresses the effect of recording an affidavit of correction of a recorded document                                .    addresses the effect of reexecuting and rerecording a recorded document  and                               .    makes technical changes.
