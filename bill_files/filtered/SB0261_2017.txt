Section  .  Recovery residence study.                 The Utah Substance Use and Mental Health Advisory Council shall convene a       workgroup to study the licensing and management of recovery residences, as defined in Section         A      .                 The workgroup shall consist of individuals representing              a   the Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health              b   owners and managers of recovery residences              c   the Utah League of Cities and Towns  and             d   other stakeholders, as determined by the council.                 a   The workgroup shall identify the negative impacts of unlicensed or poorly       managed recovery residences on surrounding neighborhoods and clients recovering from       substance use disorder.             b   The workgroup shall identify steps that may be taken by each stakeholder to       promote the licensure of and adoption of management best practices by recovery residences.
