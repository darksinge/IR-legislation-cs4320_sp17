MINING BOND AND SURETY FORFEITURE ACCOUNT                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Michael R. Styler          AN ACT RELATING TO MINES AND MINING  CREATING A RESTRICTED ACCOUNT         KNOWN AS THE BOND AND SURETY FORFEITURE ACCOUNT  SPECIFYING         CONTENTS OF THE ACCOUNT AND USE OF ACCOUNT MONIES  AND PROVIDING         AN EFFECTIVE DATE.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                       , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section          is enacted to read                       .  Bond and Surety Forfeiture Account created    Contents    Use of account           monies.                   There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the Bond and          Surety Forfeiture Account.                   Monies collected by the division as a result of bond or surety forfeitures shall be          deposited in the account.                   Interest earned on monies in the account shall accrue to the account.                   a   Money from each forfeited bond or surety, together with interest, shall be used to          reclaim the mine site or plug the well to which the forfeited bond or surety corresponds.               b   Any money not used for a project shall be returned to the rightful claimant.              Section  .   Effective date.              If approved by two thirds of all the members elected to each house, this act takes effect          upon approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah          Constitution Article VII, Section  , without the governor s signature, or in the case of a veto, the          date of veto override.      Legislative Review Note      as of                  AM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
