Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Governing body in cities of the first class.                                  The  Except as provided under Subsection                     f , the governing body of  cities                           each city of the first class that has not adopted an optional form of government under Part   ,                          Alternative Forms of Municipal Government Act, shall be a commission of five members of which                           one shall be the mayor and the remaining four shall be commissioners.
