LOCAL DISTRICT BOARD OF TRUSTEES                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Janice M. Fisher                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies provisions relating to the board of trustees of mosquito abatement                           districts.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires each member of a mosquito abatement district board of trustees appointed                           by a municipal legislative body to be a resident of the municipality  and                               .    provides that the member need not be a resident of the mosquito abatement district.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  B  a    , as enacted by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  . Mosquito abatement district board of trustees.                                       a   i   Notwithstanding Subsection       B              and subject to Subsection     b ,                           the legislative body of each municipality that is entirely or partly included within a mosquito                           abatement district shall appoint one member to the board of trustees.                                ii   A member of the board of trustees appointed under Subsection     a  i                                  A   shall be a resident of the municipality whose legislative body appoints the member                           and                                B   notwithstanding Subsection       B              a  ii , need not be a resident within the                          boundary of the mosquito abatement district.                                b   If     or more of the area of a mosquito abatement district is within the boundaries                           of a single municipality                                 i   the board of trustees shall consist of five members  and                                ii   the legislative body of that municipality shall appoint all five members of the                           board.                                    The legislative body of each county in which a mosquito abatement district is                           located shall appoint one member to the district s board of trustees if                                 a   some or all of the county s unincorporated area is included within the boundaries of                           the mosquito abatement district  or                                b   i   the number of municipalities that are entirely or partly included within the                           district is an even number less than nine  and                                ii   Subsection     b  does not apply.                                    If the number of board members appointed by application of Subsections     and                               a  is an even number less than nine, the legislative body of the county in which the district                           is located shall appoint an additional member.
