OUTDOOR GUIDES AND OUTFITTERS                           LICENSING ACT                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Evan J. Vickers                                Senate Sponsor             Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill provides for the licensing of outdoor guides and outfitters by the Division of                           Occupational and Professional Licensing.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    enacts the Outdoor Guides and Outfitters Licensing Act                                .    provides definitions, including outdoor guide, outfitter, and outfitting services                                .    creates the Outdoor Guides and Outfitters Licensing Board and provides for its                           duties and responsibilities                                .    provides for the licensing of outdoor guides and outfitters by the division                                .    provides for qualifications for licensure as an outdoor guide or outfitter, for the term                           of the license and its renewal, and for exemptions from licensure                                .    provides grounds for denial of, suspension, revocation, or restrictions on a license                           issued under the act, and for disciplinary proceedings  and                               .    defines unlawful and unprofessional conduct as related to the conduct of licensed                           outdoor guides and outfitters.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                         , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  OUTDOOR GUIDES AND OUTFITTERS LICENSING ACT                            Part  .  General Provisions                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Outdoor Guides and Outfitters Licensing Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               In addition to the definitions in Section                 , as used in this chapter                                      Board  means the Outdoor Guides and Outfitters Licensing Board created in                          Section                  .                                     Compensation  means anything of economic value that is paid, loaned, granted,                          given, donated, or transferred to an outdoor guide or outfitter for or in consideration of personal                          services, materials, or property.                                     Outdoor guide  means an individual who                                 a   offers to guide, lead, or assist another individual in a wildlife related activity on                          public lands for compensation  and                                   b   is retained for compensation by an outfitter.                                     Outfitter  means an individual who offers outfitting services for compensation to                          another individual in the pursuit of a wildlife related activity on public lands.                                     Outfitting services  means providing, for the pursuit of a wildlife activity on                          public lands                                 a   transportation of people, equipment, supplies, or wildlife to or from a location  or                                b   packing, protecting, or supervising services.                                     Public lands  mean any lands owned by the United States, the state, or a political                          subdivision or independent entity of the state that are open to the public for purposes of                          engaging in a wildlife related activity.                                    a    Wildlife related activity  means to hunt, pursue, catch, capture, trap, kill,                          locate, or collect protected wildlife or its parts.                                b    Wildlife related activity  does not include fishing, angling, seining, or                          photographing.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            Part  .  Board                                         .  Board.                                    There is created the Outdoor Guides and Outfitters Licensing Board consisting of                          four persons licensed in accordance with this chapter and one member of the Wildlife Board                          selected by the Wildlife Board or an employee of the Division of Wildlife Resources appointed                          by the Wildlife Board.                                    Except for the Wildlife Board member, the board shall be appointed and serve in                          accordance with Section                 .                                    a   The duties and responsibilities of the board shall be in accordance with Sections                                          and                 .                                b   The board shall also                                 i   designate one of its members on a permanent or rotating basis to assist the division                          in reviewing complaints concerning the unlawful or unprofessional conduct of outdoor guides                          and outfitters  and                                ii   advise the division in its investigations of these complaints.                                    A board member who has, under Subsection     b , reviewed a complaint or                             advised in its investigation may be disqualified from participating with the board when the                          board serves as a presiding officer in an adjudicative proceeding concerning the complaint.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            Part  .  Licensing                                         .  Licensure required    Issuance of licenses.                                    Beginning January  ,     , and except as provided in Sections                  and                                          , a license is required to provide the services of an outdoor guide or outfitter.                                    The division shall issue to an individual who qualifies under the provisions of this                          chapter a license in the classification of                                 a   outdoor guide  or                                 b   outfitter.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Qualifications for licensure.                                     An applicant for licensure as an outdoor guide shall                                  a   submit an application in a form prescribed by the division                                  b   pay a fee determined by the department under Section       J                                            c   produce satisfactory evidence of good moral character                                  d   possess a high degree of skill and ability as an outdoor guide                                  e   successfully complete basic education and training requirements established by rule                           by the division in collaboration with the board  and                                 f   meet with the division and board if requested by the division or board.                                     An applicant for licensure as an outfitter shall                                  a   submit an application in a form prescribed by the division                                  b   pay a fee determined by the department under Section       J                                            c   produce satisfactory evidence of good moral character                                  d   possess a high degree of skill and ability as an outfitter                                  e   successfully complete basic education and training requirements established by rule                           by the division in collaboration with the board  and                                 f   meet with the division and board if requested by the division or board.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Term of license    Expiration    renewal.                                        a   The division shall issue each license under this chapter in accordance with a                           two year renewal cycle established by rule.                                 b   The division may by rule extend or shorten a renewal cycle by as much as one year                           to stagger the renewal cycle it administers.                                     Each license automatically expires on the expiration date shown on the license                           unless the licensee renews it in accordance with Section                 .                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Exemptions from licensure.                                The exemptions from licensure under this chapter are limited to                                      those set forth in Section                   and                                     an employee or subordinate of an outdoor guide or outfitter if                                  a   the employee or subordinate does not use the title of outdoor guide or outfitter or is                           not directly represented to the public to be legally qualified to engage in the practice of being                           an outdoor guide or outfitter before the public in this state  and                                 b   the employee s or subordinate s duties do not include responsible charge.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                             Part  .  License Denial and Discipline                                          .  Grounds for denial of license    Disciplinary proceedings.                                Grounds for refusing to issue a license to an applicant, for refusing to renew the license                           of a licensee, for revoking, suspending, restricting, or placing on probation the license of a                           licensee, for issuing a public or private reprimand to a licensee, and for issuing a cease and                           desist order under this chapter shall be in accordance with Section                 .                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                             Part  .  Unlawful and Unprofessional Conduct                                          .  Unlawful conduct.                                 Unlawful conduct  includes, in addition to the definition in Section                 , using                           the title  outdoor guide  or  outfitter  or any other title or designation to indicate that the                           individual is an outdoor guide or outfitter unless the individual has a current license as an                           outdoor guide or outfitter under this chapter.                                Section   .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Unprofessional conduct.                                    Unprofessional conduct  includes, in addition to the definition in Section                 ,                           and as may be further defined by rule                                      engaging in an activity that would place a licensee s client, prospective client, or                           third party s safety at risk                                      using false, deceptive, or misleading advertising related to providing services as an                           outdoor guide or outfitter  and                                     misrepresenting services, outcomes, facilities, equipment, or fees to a client or                           prospective client.
