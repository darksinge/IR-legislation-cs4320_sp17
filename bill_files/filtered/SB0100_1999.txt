Enrolled                          APPROPRIATION FOR THE UTAH HERITAGE HIGHWAY PROJECT                              GENERAL SESSION                      STATE OF UTAH                       Sponsor   Leonard M. Blackham                       AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS  APPROPRIATING    ,    FROM THE                      GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR           TO THE DIVISION OF BUSINESS                      DEVELOPMENT IN THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC                      DEVELOPMENT FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE UTAH HERITAGE HIGHWAY                      PROJECT  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.                      This act enacts uncodified material.                     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                           Section  .   Intent.                          The Legislature finds that Highway    extending from Utah County south into Arizona                     could be a vehicle for economic development for the entire state, particularly rural Utah, by                     designating the highway the  Utah Heritage Highway  and by developing and promoting a world                     class heritage art and craft merchandising corridor.  The corridor includes many outlets which                     provide heritage products, crafts, and related facilities.  By promoting the Utah Heritage Highway,                     tourism and economic growth would be enhanced.                          Section  .   Appropriation.                          There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,    ,    to the                     Division of Business Development in the Department of Community and Economic Development                     to be used to develop and promote the Utah Heritage Highway Project.                          Section  .   Effective date.                          This act takes effect on July  ,     .
