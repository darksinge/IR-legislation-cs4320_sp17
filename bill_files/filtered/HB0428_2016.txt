Sagers      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies provisions relating to local political subdivision bonding       authorizations.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   authorizes a local political subdivision to issue negotiable bonds to pay claims,       judgments, or settlements in certain circumstances                   specifies a maximum maturity date for bonds issued to pay certain claims,       judgments, or settlements  and                  makes technical and conforming changes.
