SUICIDE PREVENTION REVISIONS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Steve Eliason                                Senate Sponsor             Howard A. Stephenson                                                         LONG TITLE                          Committee Note                               The Education Interim Committee recommended this bill.                           General Description                                This bill amends certain student survey and evaluation provisions related to public                           school suicide prevention.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    under certain circumstances, allows a school employee or agent to intervene and ask                           a student questions regarding the student s suicidal thoughts, self harming behavior,                           or thoughts of harming others, for the purposes of                                     .    referring the student to appropriate prevention services  and                                    .    informing the student s parent                                .    requires a school district or charter school to develop a policy related to school                           employee intervention measures on or before September  ,                                     .    allows school personnel to ask a student questions related to youth suicide                           prevention, intervention, or postvention as part of the school s suicide prevention                           program  and                               .    makes technical changes.
