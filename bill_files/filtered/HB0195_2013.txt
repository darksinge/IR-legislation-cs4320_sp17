Urquhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies a provision of the Budgetary Procedures Act relating to revenue                           volatility reporting.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the revenue volatility report prepared by the Legislative Fiscal Analyst and                           the Governor s Office of Planning and Budget to consider federal funding changes                           and to recommend changes to amounts or limits of reserve funds, if considered                           appropriate.
