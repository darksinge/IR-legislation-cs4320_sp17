Hughes                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill provides for the withdrawal of the state from the Compact for Education.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    repeals provisions relating to the Compact for Education, which is governed by the                           Education Commission of the States and organized for the purpose of facilitating                           the improvement of state and local educational systems  and                               .    directs the governor to notify the party states of Utah s withdrawal from the                           compact. Section  .   Notification of withdrawal from the Compact for Education.                               Pursuant to the withdrawal procedures prescribed in the Compact for Education, Article                          VIII, the governor shall notify the governors of the party states of Utah s withdrawal from the                          Compact for Education.
