Hinkins                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the State System of Public Education Code and the Utah State                                 Retirement and Insurance Benefit Act to amend employee benefit provisions for charter                           schools.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows a charter school that made an election of nonparticipation in the state                           retirement systems for its employees to make a one time, irrevocable retraction of                           the election of nonparticipation  and                               .    makes technical corrections.
