STATE FIRE CODE ACT AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   James A. Dunnigan                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the State Fire Code Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    updates the editions of nationally recognized codes that are incorporated by                           reference                                .    amends provisions related to certain group care facilities and ambulatory surgical                           facilities                                .    requires evacuation fire drills for secondary schools                                .    modifies requirements regarding solar panels                                .    modifies requirements regarding pump and riser rooms                                .    modifies requirements regarding the installation of automatic sprinkler systems  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
