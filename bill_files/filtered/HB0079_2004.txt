RIGHT TO DISPLAY FLAG AT PERSONAL                           RESIDENCE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Carl W. Duckworth                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Real Estate code to provide certain residents with the right to                           display the United States or Utah flag.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides a right to display the United States or Utah flag to                                    .    a renter of a residential rental unit                                    .    a resident of a mobile home park  and                                   .    a condominium unit owner.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                         , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                               CHAPTER   .  DISPLAY OF FLAG                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                     a    Flag means a depiction or emblem                                 i   A   of the flag of the United States as provided in United States Code Title  ,                          Chapter  , The Flag  or                                B   of the state flag of Utah as provided in Section                                                 ii   made from fabric or cloth  and                                iii   with measurements that do not exceed three feet by five feet.                                b    Flag  does not include a depiction or emblem made from                                 i   lights                                 ii   paint                                 iii   roofing                                 iv   siding                                 v   paving materials                                 vi   flora                                 vii   balloons  or                                viii   any other building, landscaping, or decorative component.                                     Resident  means                                 a   a renter as defined in Section                                                 b   a resident as defined in Section                  or                                c   a unit owner as defined in Section               .                                     Residential property management authority  means                                 a   an owner as defined in Section                                                 b   a mobile home park as defined in Section                                                 c   a mobile home park residents  association established in accordance with Section                                                                          d   an association of unit owners as defined in Section                 or                                e   a management committee as defined in Section               .                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Restriction on prohibition of display of flag.                                       A residential property management authority may not prohibit a resident from                          displaying a flag                                 a   consistent with the guidelines in United States Code Title  , Chapter  , The Flag                                 b   within an area over which the resident has exclusive control  and                                c   from a staff, pole, or window.                                    In any action to enforce this section, the prevailing party shall be awarded costs and                          reasonable attorney fees.
