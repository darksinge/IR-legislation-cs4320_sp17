When requested by the director                a  each agency with more than     employees shall annually provide three evaluators who          meet the criteria established in Subsection      or               b   each state agency with     or less employees shall provide one evaluator who meets          the criteria established in Subsection    .                   State employees selected to be an evaluator shall                a   have knowledge about the principles of organizational development and improvement                b   have knowledge about the quality award criteria contained in the National Baldrige          Award or similar quality award programs                c   have knowledge about team process and consensus decision making  and               d   posses demonstrable writing and communication skills.
