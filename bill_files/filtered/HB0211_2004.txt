RENEWAL OF FISHING LICENSE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  J. Morgan Philpot                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends fishing licensing provisions to allow for licenses that are issued for                              days from the date of sale.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends fishing licensing provisions to allow for licenses to be issued for     days                           from the date of sale  and                               .    allows persons under    years of age to fish without a license pursuant to rules,                           proclamations, and orders set forth by the Wildlife Board.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                       , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                                            , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                                REPEALS                                        , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                   Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Licenses issuance    Expiration date.                               The licenses required by this chapter, except lifetime licenses,  shall  may be issued                                     annually, and shall expire on December   st each year .   or                                    on other days of the year, to expire     days from the date of issue.                               Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Fishing license    Limited number of days.                                    A person    years of age or older shall purchase a fishing license before engaging                          in any regulated fishing activity.                                          A person    years of age or older, upon paying the fee prescribed by the                           Wildlife Board, may receive a license to fish                                 a   for one day   or                                 b   for seven consecutive days .                                  c   for     consecutive days from the date of sale  or                                d   annually, and the license shall expire on December   st of each year.                                    A person under    years of age may fish without a license pursuant to rules,                          proclamations, and orders set forth by the Wildlife Board.                                          The effective date shall be indicated on the license.                               Section  .  Repealer.
