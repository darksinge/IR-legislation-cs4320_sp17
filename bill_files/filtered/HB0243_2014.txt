AMENDMENTS TO THE FUND OF FUNDS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jim Bird                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends Title   M, Chapter  , Part   , the Utah Venture Capital Enhancement                           Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends the membership of the Utah Capital Investment Board by adding a member                           of the Senate and a member of the House of Representatives                                .    amends the quorum requirements of the Utah Capital Investment Board                                .    requires that the annual report and the annual audit for the Utah fund of funds be                           completed on or before July   for the previous calendar year                                .    describes additional information required in the annual report                                .    modifies the amount of money required in the redemption reserve before returns                           may be reinvested in the Utah fund of funds                                .    provides that the aggregate outstanding certificates may not exceed a total of                               ,   ,    of contingent tax credits                                .    provides that additional certificates may not be issued after July  ,                                     .    provides that the Utah Capital Investment Corporation is no longer exempt from the                           requirements of Title   , Chapter  , Open and Public Meetings Act, and Title   G,                           Chapter  , Government Records Access and Management Act  and                               .    makes technical changes.
