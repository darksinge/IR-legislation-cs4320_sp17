Legislative Review Note      as of                PM     This legislation raises the following constitutional or statutory concerns    There is a possibility that this law could be challenged as a violation of the constitutional guarantee  of open court s access for redress of injuries under Article  , Section    of the Utah Constitution.    There is no clear precedent as to how a court would rule on this issue.
