SALARY AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF                           LIEUTENANT GOVERNOR                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   LaVar Christensen                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the State Officers and Employees Code to create the Commission on                           Civic and Character Education within the lieutenant governor s office and to amend the                           salary for the lieutenant governor.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Commission on Civic and Character Education within the lieutenant                           governor s office                                .    establishes the duties of the commission to promote and ensure the implementation                           of the provisions of Section   A        related to civic and character education                                .    provides that the lieutenant governor s office shall provide staff support to the                           commission                                .    provides that the lieutenant governor s salary on July  ,      is     of the                           governor s salary  and                               .    makes technical changes. Section        a        is enacted to read                                    a   .  Commission on Civic and Character Education    Membership    Chair                              Expenses.                                    There is created within the lieutenant governor s office the Commission on Civic                          and Character Education.                                    The commission consists of five members appointed as follows                                 a   the lieutenant governor, or a designee                                 b   one member of the House of Representatives, appointed by the speaker of the                          House                                 c   one member of the Senate, appointed by the president of the Senate                                 d   the superintendent of public instruction, or a designee  and                                e   one representative of the judicial branch appointed by the Judicial Council.                                    a   The commission shall annually elect a chairperson from its membership.                                b   The commission shall hold meetings as needed to carry out its duties.  A meeting                          may be held on the call of the chair or a majority of the commission members.                                c   Three commission members are necessary to constitute a quorum at any meeting                          and, if a quorum exists, the action of a majority of members present shall be the action of the                          commission.                                    a   An appointed commission member shall be appointed for a two year term or                          until their successors are appointed.                                b   When a vacancy occurs in the appointed membership for any reason, the                          replacement shall be appointed for the unexpired term.                                    a   Commission members who are not government employees may not receive                             compensation or benefits for the services, but may receive per diem and expenses incurred in                          the performance of their official duties at rates established by the Division of Finance under                          Sections       A           and       A          .                                b   A commission member may decline to receive per diem and expenses for service to                          the commission.                                    The lieutenant governor s office shall provide staff support to the commission.                                    The funding of the commission shall be a separate line item to the lieutenant                          governor s office in the annual appropriations act.                               Section  .
