Section                is amended to read                                        .  Division of Arts and Museums    Creation    Powers and duties.                                    There is created within the department the Division of  Fine  Arts and Museums                           under the administration and general supervision of the executive director or the designee of                           the executive director.                                       The division shall be under the policy direction of the board.                                    The division shall advance the interests of the arts, in all their phases, within the                           state  of Utah , and to that end shall                                 a   cooperate with and locally sponsor federal agencies and projects directed to similar                           undertakings                                 b   develop the influence of arts in education                                 c   involve the private sector, including businesses, charitable interests, educational                           interests, manufacturers, agriculturalists, and industrialists in these endeavors                                 d   utilize broadcasting facilities and the power of the press in disseminating                           information  and                                e   foster, promote, encourage, and facilitate, not only a more general and lively study                           of the arts, but take all necessary and useful means to stimulate a more abundant production of                           an indigenous art in this state.                                    The board shall set policy to guide the division in accomplishing the purposes set                           forth in Subsection    .                                    The division may not grant funds for the support of any arts project under this                           section unless the project has been first approved by the board.
