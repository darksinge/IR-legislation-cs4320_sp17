Hughes                                Senate Sponsor           Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This joint resolution authorizes the Legislature to present an opinion question to the                           registered voters of the state to impose a state sales and use tax to support and enhance                           heritage, arts, culture, and museums throughout the state.
