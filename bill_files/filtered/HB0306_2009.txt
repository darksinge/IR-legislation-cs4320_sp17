Formula for allocation of funds to local substance abuse authorities                             and local mental health authorities.                                      The  board  division shall establish, by rule, formulas for allocating funds to local                             substance abuse authorities and local mental health authorities through contracts, to provide                             substance abuse prevention and treatment services in accordance with the provisions of this                             chapter and Title   , Chapter   , Part  , Local Substance Abuse Authorities, and mental health                             services in accordance with the provisions of this chapter and Title   , Chapter   , Part  ,                             Local Mental Health Authorities.  The formulas shall provide for allocation of funds based on                             need.  Determination of need shall be based on population unless the  board  division                             establishes, by valid and accepted data, that other defined factors are relevant and reliable                             indicators of need.
