Barrus                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Transportation Code by amending provisions relating to                           highways providing access to state parks or sovereign lands.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    prohibits a highway authority from establishing or operating a tollway or imposing                           or collecting a toll on a class B or class C road that provides access to a state park or                           sovereign lands.
