No member of the commission may receive any compensation for that member s                            services. However, each member shall receive a per diem and shall be paid the actual traveling                            and other necessary expenses incurred in the discharge of the commissioner s official duties.                                     The Legislature shall make appropriations to the Office of Legislative Research and                            General Counsel to pay the necessary expenses of the commissioners and to make appropriate                            contribution on behalf of this state to the National Conference of Commissioners on Uniform                            State Laws, including any expenses of a commissioner who has been elected as a life member                           of the National Conference of Commissioners on Uniform State Laws.                                     The commissioners shall keep a full account of their expenditures in the discharge                            of their official duties and shall report the account to the Office of Legislative Research and                               General Counsel.
