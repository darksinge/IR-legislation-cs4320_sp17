Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates a core mission for the Department of Human Services  and                               .    gives the Department of Health responsibility                                    .    to create role transparency and consistency between other human services                           partners                                    .    to clarify performance expectations for each agency or organization                                    .    to further develop job descriptions                                    .    to create performance standards that support the methods and practice of                           collaborative efforts with partner agencies                                    .    to include collaborative practice in reviews, evaluations, or assessments                                    .    to create a system of rewards, incentives, recognition, or career  ladders  based                           on excellence in collaborative practice  and                                   .    to periodically review and revise the above criteria.
