Description                                This bill amends certain provisions related to secondary school students to provide that                           certain students in grade   be treated as secondary school students                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     defines terms                                     provides that a secondary school may impose a fee to secondary students  including                           students in grade   attending a secondary school  and                                    provides that certain remediation programs apply to students in grade   attending a                           secondary school                            Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect July                                    Utah Code Sections Affected
