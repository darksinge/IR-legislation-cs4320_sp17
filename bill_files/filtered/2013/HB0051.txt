applicants applying                           for licensure  licensure renewal  licensure reinstatement  and relicensure                                     allows licensees to designate email probation violations                                     modifies the provisions for diversion to align them with the provisions for                           probation                                     modifies what constitutes unprofessional conduct by including the violation of the                           terms of an order governing a license                                        modifies the requirements to qualify as an approved education program for nursing                           education                                     clarifies who may have access to the Controlled Substance Database                                     reduces the statutory fee for manufactured housing dealers                                     modifies the supervision required for medical assistants to better align the                           requirements with current practice                                     repeals the statute creating the Controlled Substance Precursor Advisory
