DISSOLVING CLARKSTON JUSTICE                           COURT                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jack R  Draxler                                Senate Sponsor             Lyle W  Hillyard                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This joint resolution approves the dissolution of the Clarkston Town Justice Court                            Highlighted Provisions                                This resolution                                     approves the dissolution of the Clarkston Town Justice Court                            Special Clauses                                This resolution provides an effective date                                                     Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                WHEREAS  the town of Clarkston has had a justice court for many years                                WHEREAS  the town of Clarkston has determined that it is no longer feasible for the                           town to operate a justice court                                WHEREAS  with the dissolution of the Clarkston Town Justice Court  the caseload                           from the court will fall upon the First District Court in Cache County                                WHEREAS  the Clarkston Town Council has given notice to the Utah Judicial Council                           of its intent to dissolve the Clarkston Town Justice Court  effective not later than June                                     and                               WHEREAS  Section       A           requires the Legislature to approve by joint resolution                           the dissolution of a justice court                                   NOW  THEREFORE  BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah                           approves the closure of the Clarkston Town Justice Court                                BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the Utah                           Judicial Council and the town of Clarkston                                BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this resolution takes effect upon approval by a                           constitutional majority vote of all members of the House of Representatives and Senate       Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM   Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel  Bill Documents  Bills Directory
