Local Government Communication Task Force    Creation                              Membership    Interim Rules Followed    Compensation    Staff.                         c   four members appointed by the Utah Association of Counties with one designee                          each representing                                 i   the county sheriffs                                 ii   the county fire departments                                 iii   county public works operations  and                                iv   county animal        i   municipal police chiefs                                 ii   municipal fire chiefs                                 iii   municipal public works departments  and                                iv   municipal animal control agencies                                 e   one member representing the University of Utah                                 f   one member representing the Utah Transit Authority                                 g   one member appointed by the State Office of Education, selected from local school                          districts that employ public safety officers for their schools                                 h   one member from the Unified Police Department of Greater Salt Lake                                 i   one member from the Unified Fire Department of Greater Salt Lake                                 j   one member appointed by Salt Lake City  and                                k   one member appointed by board of directors of the Salt Lake Valley Metro Fire                          Agency.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate one of the members of the Senate                          appointed pursuant to Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate one of the members                          from the House of Representatives appointed pursuant to Subsection     b  to serve as a                          cochair of the task force.                                       A majority of the members of the task force constitute a quorum.  The action of a                          majority of a quorum constitutes the action of the task force.                                    a   Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force who are legislators shall                          be paid in accordance with Section                and Legislative Joint Rules, Title  , Chapter  ,                          Expense and Mileage Reimbursement for Authorized Legislative Meetings, Special Sessions,                          and Veto Override Sessions.                                b   A member of the task force who is not a legislator may not receive compensation                          for the member s work associated with the task force, but may receive per diem and                          reimbursement for travel expenses incurred as a member of the task force at the rates                          established by the Division of Finance under Sections       A          .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the task force.                               Section  .   Duties    Meetings    Reports.                                    The task force shall review and make recommendations on local government                          communications systems and infrastructure needed for effective and integrated operation of                          local government communications and dispatch for law enforcement, fire and emergency                          rescue services, public works services, and animal control operations.                                    The task force shall present a final report, including any proposed legislation, to the                          Legislative Management Committee before December   ,     .
