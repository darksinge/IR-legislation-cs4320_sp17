HEALTH INSURANCE BENEFIT DESIGN                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Rebecca D. Lockhart                             This act modifies the Insurance Code.  The act amends provisions related to Accident and                          Health Insurance.  The act permits a carrier to offer less or different coverage than the basic                          benefit package, the minimum standards required by the commissioner of insurance, or any                          other health insurance mandate required by state law when the Department of Health offers                          similar coverage as part of a Medicaid waiver.
