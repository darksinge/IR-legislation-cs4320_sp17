Enrolled                               RETIREMENT AND INDEPENDENT ENTITIES                           COMMITTEE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Curtis S. Bramble                                House Sponsor            David  Clark                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Independent Entities Code by amending the membership of the                           Retirement and Independent Entities Committee.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    increases the number of legislators on the Retirement and Independent Entities                           Committee  and                               .    increases the number of senators on the committee from five to six.                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  E      , as last amended by Chapter  , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      E           is amended to read                                   E      .  Retirement and Independent Entities Committee creation.                                    There is created the Retirement and Independent Entities Committee composed of                                   legislators appointed as follows                                    a    five  six senators, appointed by the president of the Senate, with at least two                           senators from the minority party  and                                b   nine representatives, appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives,                           with at least three representatives from the minority party.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate one of the Senate appointees as a                           cochair of the committee.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate one of the House of                           Representatives appointees as a cochair of the committee.                                    Committee members serve for two years, but may be reappointed by the speaker or                           the president.                                    The committee shall meet at least twice each year, but may meet more frequently if                           the chairs determine that additional meetings are needed.                                    In conducting all of its business, the committee shall comply with the rules of                           legislative interim committees.                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff services                           to the committee.                                    Salaries and expenses of legislative committee members shall be paid in                           accordance with                                 a   Section                 and                                b   Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                               Section  .   Effective date.                               If approved by two thirds of all the members elected to each house, this bill takes effect                          upon approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah                          Constitution Article VII, Section  , without the governor s signature, or in the case of a veto,                          the date of veto override.
