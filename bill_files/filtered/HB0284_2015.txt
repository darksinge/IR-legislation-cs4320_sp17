         MINOR ALCOHOL OR DRUG RELATED OFFENSES AND      DRIVING PRIVILEGES           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Edward H. Redd      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description             This bill modifies provisions relating to sentencing requirements for certain alcohol or       drug related offenses.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   requires a court to order a screening, an assessment, and an educational series or       substance abuse treatment if found appropriate by the screening or assessment for       certain alcohol related offenses committed by minors                   authorizes a court to reduce a driver license suspension period for certain alcohol or       drug related offenses committed by minors                   amends the requirements for a court to reduce a driver license suspension period for       certain alcohol or drug related offenses committed by minors  and                  makes conforming and technical changes.
