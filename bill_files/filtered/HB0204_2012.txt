EXPEDITED JURY TRIAL ACT AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Brian S. King                                Senate Sponsor             Stephen H. Urquhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill removes specific language to prevent confusion.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    removes confusing language regarding a case in which a jury might return a  no                           cause  verdict.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  B      , as enacted by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      B           is amended to read                                   B      .  Agreement.                                    An agreement to participate in an expedited jury trial under this part may be entered                           into only after a dispute has arisen and an action has been filed.                                    Any party to an action may file a motion with the court for an expedited jury trial.                                       Notice of the motion shall be served on all other parties.                                    All parties agreeing to participate in an expedited jury trial and, if represented, their                           counsel shall sign the agreement filed with the court.                                    The agreement to participate in the expedited jury trial process is binding upon the                           parties.                                    The conditions of the agreement shall include                                 a   waiver of the right to appeal the verdict                                 b   waiver of the right to move for a directed verdict                                 c   waiver of the right to file post trial motions, except as permitted by rule and Section                              B            and                                d   an agreed upon range of monetary damages that will be awarded, regardless of the                           verdict , if liability and allocation of fault are proved .                                    The Judicial Council may by rule impose additional reasonable conditions.                                    If the jury verdict is within the agreed range of monetary damages, the court shall                           enter the verdict as rendered.  If the jury verdict is outside the agreed range of monetary                           damages the court shall, as appropriate, increase or decrease the damages to the minimum or                           maximum agreed amount.
