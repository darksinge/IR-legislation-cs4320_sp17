Dee                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends provisions related to a Military Installation Development Authority                           and its ability to petition for annexation of land, create project areas, and issue bonds.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows a Military Installation Development Authority  MIDA  to petition for                           annexation of a project area as if it was the sole private property owner under                           certain circumstances                                .    requires the MIDA to provide notice of the annexation to the military installation                                .    permits the military installation to object to the petition for annexation                                .    amends the land that may be included in a project area                                .    does not require the permission of a county for the creation of a MIDA project area                           if the land in the project area is wholly within a municipality  and                               .    clarifies the bonding process for the project area.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.
