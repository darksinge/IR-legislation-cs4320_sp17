Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies how terms of directors on the board are staggered  and                               .    changes how directors are paid from per diem and expenses approved by the                           Division of Finance to compensation and reasonable expenses approved by the                           board and subject to a cap on compensation.
