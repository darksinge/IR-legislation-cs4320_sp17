Stephenson                                House Sponsor            Steve  Eliason                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends provisions related to the timing of a special election for certain ballot                           propositions.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires an election for a bond, debt, leeway, levy, or tax to take place on the first                           Tuesday after the first Monday in November  and                               .    makes technical and conforming amendments.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
