CLEAN FUEL SCHOOL BUSES AND INFRASTRUCTURE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Stephen G. Handy                                Senate Sponsor             Aaron Osmond                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill provides grants to replace school buses and to create infrastructure for                           alternative fuel vehicles.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows the State Board of Education to award a grant to a school district or charter                           school to                                    .    replace a school bus manufactured before      with a new school bus that uses                           CNG, propane, or clean diesel fuel                                    .    install an alternative fuel fueling station for a school bus that uses alternative                           fuel and that may be accessed by a governmental entity or the public to fuel an                           alternative fuel vehicle  or                                   .    retrofit a bus shop to service and maintain a school bus that uses alternative                           fuel                                .    requires the State Board of Education to make rules related to the awarding of a                           grant, including rules that                                    .    specify criteria for selecting a grant recipient  and                                   .    require a grant recipient to provide funding matches in an amount that is greater                           than or equal to the amount of a grant received  and                               .    requires the State Board of Education to                                       .    facilitate training for bus shop technicians and bus drivers on the operation and                           maintenance of a school bus that uses alternative fuel  and                                   .    make a report to the Legislature.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates in fiscal year                                     .    to the State Board of Education   Utah State Office of Education   Initiative                           Programs as a one time appropriation                                    .    from the Education Fund, One time,    ,   ,   .                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an effective date.                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                    A   a    .   , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A   a    .      is enacted to read                                  A   a    . .  Grants for school buses and alternative fuel infrastructure.                                    As used in this section                                 a    Alternative fuel  means CNG or propane.                                b    Board  means the State Board of Education.                                c    Clean diesel fuel  means diesel fuel with a sulfur content of no more than    parts                          per million  ppm .                                d    CNG  means compressed natural gas.                                    The board may award a grant to a school district or charter school to                                 a   replace a school bus manufactured before      with a new school bus that uses                          CNG, propane, or clean diesel fuel                                 b   install an alternative fuel fueling station                                 i   for a school bus that uses alternative fuel  and                                ii   that may be accessed by a governmental entity or the public to fuel an alternative                          fuel vehicle  or                                c   retrofit a bus shop to service and maintain a school bus that uses alternative fuel.                                       The board shall make rules                                 a   establishing procedures for applying for and awarding a grant                                 b   specifying criteria for selecting a grant recipient                                 c   requiring reporting of grant money expenditures and evidence that a new school bus                          purchased with grant money has replaced a school bus manufactured before       and                                d   requiring a grant recipient to provide matching funds in an amount that is greater                          than or equal to the amount of a grant received under this section.                                    The board shall appoint a committee that includes representatives of school                          districts, charter schools, and the board to review grant applications and make                          recommendations to the board for the award of grants that meet the requirements of this section                          and rules adopted under Subsection    .                                    Through the state superintendent of public instruction, the board shall facilitate                          training for bus shop technicians and bus drivers on the operation and maintenance of a school                          bus that uses alternative fuel.                                    On or before November  ,     , the board shall submit a report to the Public                          Education Appropriations Subcommittee that                                 a   describes the grants awarded under this section  and                                b   includes recommendations regarding continuation of the grant program.
