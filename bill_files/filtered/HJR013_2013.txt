JOINT RESOLUTION HONORING AMBASSADOR JOHN                           PRICE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Brad R. Wilson                                Senate Sponsor             Jerry W. Stevenson                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This joint resolution of the Legislature honors the life and legacy of John Price.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This resolution                                .    honors the life and legacy of John Price as entrepreneur, philanthropist, former                           United States ambassador, humanitarian, and founder of Price Development                           Company, JP Realty, Inc., and Alta Industries Corporation  and                               .    honors John Price as husband, father, grandfather, and friend.                           Special Clauses                                None                                                    Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                WHEREAS, John Price was born in Germany on August   ,                                     WHEREAS, Mr. Price fled Germany with his parents and older brother on April  ,                                                               WHEREAS, Mr. Price graduated from Stuyvesant High School in                                     WHEREAS, Mr. Price attended City College of New York in the evenings and spent                           his days working in a variety of different jobs, each of which taught him different skills and                           talents and eventually led him to study geology                                   WHEREAS, in     , Mr. Price drove across the country in search of geology jobs,                           which were in high demand, at the time, given the country s quest for uranium                                WHEREAS, Mr. Price settled in Salt Lake City after falling in love with the beauty of                           the Wasatch and Oquirrh mountain ranges                                WHEREAS, in     , Mr. Price graduated from the University of Utah with a degree in                           geological engineering                                WHEREAS, Mr. Price married Marcia Lee Poulsen on August   ,                                     WHEREAS, in the autumn of     , Mr. Price purchased three lots in the Avenues of                           Salt Lake City and began J. Price Construction Company                                WHEREAS, Marcia, who was employed by Universal Travel Agency at the time, also                           worked in the evenings assisting John with his new company                                WHEREAS, a determined entrepreneur, Mr. Price founded his second company, John                           Price Associates, Inc.  JPA , in     , which evolved into Price Development Company  PDC ,                           in                                     WHEREAS, the business model was then expanded to include development, design,                           construction, management, and most importantly, ownership of properties                                WHEREAS, with the company s expertise expanding, PDC quickly became one of the                           top    developers in the United States as the dominant retail and industrial builder and                           developer in the Intermountain and western regions of the United States                                WHEREAS, PDC pioneered new integrated building and construction management                           concepts                                WHEREAS, PDC began developing enclosed retail malls, the first being the Cache                           Valley Mall in Logan, Utah                                WHEREAS, over the next    years PDC developed    enclosed malls including Red                           Cliffs Mall in St. George, Provo Towne Center, and Cottonwood Mall in Salt Lake City, with                           more than    retail community centers, and a number of office and industrial park complexes,                           totaling almost   ,   ,    square feet of building space                                WHEREAS, in     , Mr. Price oversaw the Utah s Bicentennial Arts Project as                           construction manager, which included the renovation of the Capitol Theater and construction                           of Abravanel Hall and the adjacent Salt Lake Art Center                                WHEREAS, Mr. Price s ambition and determination led him to many great working                              relationships with both large and small companies that were willing to let him prove himself                           capable of developing at a regional and national level                                WHEREAS, Mr. Price and PDC s reputation for performance, workmanship, and                           competitive pricing opened new doors and the company flourished                                WHEREAS, PDC s construction division included projects for Kennecott Copper,                           Anaconda, Alta High School, and several radio and television station buildings located in the                           first business and industrial parks in the state                                WHEREAS, PDC also constructed landmark office formed a New York Stock Exchange listed Real Estate Investment Trust                            REIT , which merged with General Growth Properties in                                     WHEREAS, many of the thousands of people Mr. Price employed over the years refer                           to their time spent working with Mr. Price as attending  Price University                                 WHEREAS, Mr. Price was an incredible example, mentor, and teacher who would                           expect nothing but the best and would work from dusk until dawn reading every newspaper
