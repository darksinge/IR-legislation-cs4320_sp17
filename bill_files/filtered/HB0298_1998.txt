STATE COURT ADMINISTRATOR SALARY                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  David L. Zolman          AN ACT RELATING TO THE JUDICIAL CODE  CHANGING THE SALARY OF THE         ADMINISTRATOR OF THE COURTS FROM EQUAL TO THE SALARY OF A DISTRICT         COURT JUDGE TO     OF THE SALARY OF THE STATE ATTORNEY GENERAL          GRANDFATHERING IN THE CURRENT HOLDER OF THE POSITION  AND         PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                       , as repealed and reenacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section          is amended to read                       .  Administrator of the courts    Appointment    Qualifications    Salary.                    The Supreme Court shall appoint a chief administrative officer of the council who shall          have the title of the administrator of the courts and shall serve at the pleasure of the council and or          the Supreme Court. The administrator shall be selected on the basis of professional ability and          experience in the field of public administration and shall possess an understanding of court          procedures as well as of the nature and significance of other court services. He shall devote his full          time and attention to the duties of his office, and shall receive a salary equal to that of  a district          judge      the state attorney general.                   The salary provision in this section does not apply to the person holding the position          on January  ,     .              Section  .   Effective date.
