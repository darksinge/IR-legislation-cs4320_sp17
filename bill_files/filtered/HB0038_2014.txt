Arent                                Senate Sponsor             Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies Title   J, Chapter  , Governor s Office of Management and Budget.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires the governor to appoint a sustainability director, to serve within the                           Governor s Office of Management and Budget                                .    describes the duties of the sustainability director                                .    requires the Governor s Office of Management and Budget to assist the                           sustainability director  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                    J        , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                       J        , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapters    and                                       J        , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                  ENACTS                                    J        , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      J           is amended to read                                   J      .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Agency  means a board, commission, department, division, officer, council,                          office, committee, bureau, or other administrative unit of the state, including the agency head,                          agency employees, or other persons acting on behalf of or under the authority of the agency                          head, the Legislature, the courts, or the governor, but does not mean a political subdivision of                          the state, or any administrative unit of a political subdivision of the state.                                           Committee  means the Resource Development Coordinating Committee                           created by this chapter.                                          Executive director  means the chief administrative officer of the Governor s                           Office of Management and Budget appointed as provided in this chapter.                                           Office  means the Governor s Office of Management and Budget created by                           this chapter.                                           Political subdivision  means a county, municipality, local district, special                           service district, school district, interlocal cooperation agreement entity, or any administrative                           subunit of them.                                           State planning coordinator  means the person appointed as planning                           coordinator as provided in this chapter.                               Section  .
