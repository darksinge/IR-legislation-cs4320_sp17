Enrolled                               RISK MANAGEMENT FOR INDEPENDENT ENTITIES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Todd  Weiler                                House Sponsor            Don L. Ipson                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Independent Entities Code by amending requirements for a state                           independent entity to participate in coverage under the Risk Management Fund.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the list of state independent entities included in the definition of                            independent entity  in the Independent Entities Code                                .    provides that an independent entity is not eligible for coverage by the Division of                           Risk Management unless the entity s authorizing statute specifically authorizes                           coverage                                .    modifies the authorizing statutes of certain independent entities to allow for                           participation in risk management coverage                                .    provides that the state risk manager may require participating independent entities                           to obtain additional insurance and comply with other loss prevention measures for a                           commercial activity  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an effective date.
