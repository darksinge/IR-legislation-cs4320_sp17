DISCLOSURE OF CONFLICT OF INTEREST                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ross I. Romero                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the timing requirement for filing a Declaration of Conflict of Interest                           form.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a legislator to file annually a Declaration of Conflict of Interest form.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                        , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Failure of member of Legislature to disclose interest in measure or bill.                                    As used in this section                                 a    Business in which the legislator is associated  means any business in which a                           legislator is a director, officer, owner, member, partner, employee, or is a holder of stocks or                              bonds in the company that have a fair market value of    ,    or more.  This does not include                           business associations by members of the legislator s immediate family.                                b    Conflict of interest  means legislation or action by a legislator that the legislator                           reasonably believes may cause direct financial benefit or detriment to him, a member of the                           legislator s immediate family, or a business in which the legislator is associated, and that                           benefit or detriment is distinguishable from the effects of that action on the public or on the                           legislator s profession, occupation, or association generally.                                c    Immediate family  means the legislator s spouse and children living in the                           legislator s immediate household.                                    In addition to the Declaration of Conflict of Interest form provided for in                           Subsection    , before or during any vote on legislation or any legislative matter in which a                           legislator has actual knowledge that he has a conflict of interest which is not stated on the                           conflict of interest form, that legislator shall orally declare to the committee or body before                           which the matter is pending that the legislator may have a conflict of interest and what that                           conflict is.  This declaration of conflict of interest shall be noted in the minutes of any                           committee meeting or in the Senate or House Journal.                                    a   A legislator shall file annually a Declaration of Conflict of Interest form with                           the Secretary of the Senate if the legislator is a senator or with the Chief Clerk of the House of                           Representatives if the legislator is a representative to satisfy that legislator s disclosure of any                           conflict of interest as required by Subsection    .                                b    This  The Declaration of Conflict of Interest form shall include the businesses in                           which the legislator is associated and the general legislative subject areas in which the                           legislator may have a conflict of interest.                                c    This  The Declaration of Conflict of Interest form is  available to the  a public                          document.                                d    This requirement of disclosure of any conflict of interest does not prohibit                           Nothing in this section prohibits a legislator from voting on any legislation or legislative                           matter.                                     Every  A member of the Legislature  who  is guilty of a class B misdemeanor if                                 a   the legislator has a conflict of interest in any measure or bill proposed or pending                           before the Legislature  of which he is a member and does not                                     b   the legislator fails to disclose the  fact  conflict to the house of which he is a                           member  and  votes thereon is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.                                 c   the legislator votes on the measure or bill.
