Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   amends a definition                   provides that a person who is subject to discrimination in matters of compensation       may receive a remedy in an additional amount equal to the back pay amount already       available  and                  requires the Division of Antidiscrimination and Labor to report to the Business and       Labor Interim Committee annually regarding discrimination in matters of       compensation.
