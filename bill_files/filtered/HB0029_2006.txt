Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the criteria that an unincorporated area is required to meet in order to be                           designated as a township so that an area declared to be a census designated place by                           the United States Census Bureau meets township requirements as an alternative to                           meeting other requirements relating to population or land area and value  and                               .    makes technical changes.
