TANNING FACILITIES TAX AND MELANOMA                           CANCER RESEARCH FUND                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Melvin R. Brown                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Revenue and Taxation portion of the Utah Code to provide for a                           tax on tanning facilities and the creation of a Melanoma Cancer Research Fund.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    imposes a tax on a tanning facility equal to     of amounts paid to, or charged by,                           the tanning facility for an admission fee or a user fee                                .    enacts provisions relating to the payment of the tax imposed by this bill                                .    creates the Melanoma Cancer Research Fund and provides that the Department of                           Health shall use the fund to award grants to organizations for research related to                           melanoma cancer  and                               .    enacts provisions related to record keeping and inspection, collection of the tax                           imposed by this bill, and tax penalties. Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  TANNING FACILITY TAX                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Tanning Facility Tax.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this section                                      Phototherapy device  means equipment that emits ultraviolet radiation used by a                          health care professional in the treatment of disease.                                    a    Tanning device  means any equipment that emits electromagnetic radiation                          with wavelengths in the air between     and     nanometers used for tanning of the skin,                          including                                 i   a sunlamp  and                                ii   a tanning booth or bed.                                b    Tanning device  does not include a phototherapy device.                                     Tanning facility  means any commercial location, place, area, structure, or                          business which provides persons access to any tanning device.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Tax imposed on a tanning facility.                                    A tax is imposed on a tanning facility equal to     of amounts paid to, or charged                             by, the tanning facility for                                 a   an admission fee to the tanning facility  or                                b   a user fee for the use of a tanning device.                                    The tax imposed by this section may not be imposed on any sales and use tax                          collected or paid under Chapter   , Sales and Use Tax Act.                                    The commission shall administer this chapter in accordance with Chapter   , Part                           , Tax Collection.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Payment of tax.                                    Subject to Subsection    , a tanning facility subject to the tax imposed by this                          chapter shall file a return with the commission and pay the tax calculated on the return to the                          commission                                 a   quarterly on or before the last day of the month immediately following the last day                          of the previous calendar quarter if the tanning facility                                 i   is required to file a quarterly sales and use tax return with the commission under                          Section                    or                                ii   is not required to file a sales and use tax return with the commission under Chapter                            , Sales and Use Tax Act  or                                b   monthly on or before the last day of the month immediately following the last day                          of the previous calendar month if the tanning facility is required to file a monthly sales and use                          tax return with the commission under Section                  .                                    In accordance with Title   G, Chapter  , Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                          commission may make rules to establish standards for determining whether an operation is a                          tanning facility.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Melanoma Cancer Research Fund.                                    There is created a restricted special revenue fund called the  Melanoma Cancer                          Research Fund.                                     a   Except as provided in Subsection    , the fund consists of all amounts collected                          by the commission under this chapter.                                b   i   The monies in the fund shall be invested by the state treasurer pursuant to Title                               , Chapter  , State Money Management Act.                                ii   All interest or other earnings derived from the fund monies shall be deposited in the                          fund.                                    Notwithstanding any other provisions of this chapter, the commission may retain an                          amount of tax collected under this chapter of not to exceed the lesser of                                 a    .    or                                b   an amount equal to the cost to the commission of administering this chapter.                                    a   Fund monies shall be used as provided in this Subsection    .                                b   The Department of Health shall use the monies in the fund to award grants to                          organizations for research related to melanoma cancer.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Records.                                     An owner or operator of a tanning facility shall maintain records, statements,                           books, or accounts necessary to determine the amount of tax for which the owner or operator is                           liable to pay under this chapter.                                     The commission may require an owner or operator of a tanning facility, by notice                           served on the person, to make or keep the records, statements, books, or accounts described in                           Subsection     in a manner in which the commission considers sufficient to show the amount                           of tax for which the owner or operator is liable to pay under this chapter.                                     After notice by the commission, the owner or operator of a tanning facility shall                           open the records, statements, books, or accounts specified in this section for examination by                           the commission or an authorized agent of the commission.
