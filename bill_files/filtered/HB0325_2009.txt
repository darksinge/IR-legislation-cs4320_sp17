Section            .      is enacted to read                                        . .  Legislative finding    Prohibition on implementing REAL ID Act.                                    As used in this section,  REAL ID Act  means the REAL ID ACT of      enacted                          by the United States Congress as part of the Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act for                          Defense, the Global War on Terror, and Tsunami Relief Act, Pub. L. No.       .                                    The Legislature finds that the United States Congress  enactment of the REAL ID                          Act into law                                 a   is inimical to the security and well being of the people of this state                                 b   will cause unneeded expense and inconvenience to the people of this state  and                                c   was adopted in violation of the principles of federalism contained in the   th                          amendment to the United States Constitution.                                    a   The state may not participate in the implementation of the REAL ID Act.                                b   The division                                 i   may not implement the provisions of the REAL ID Act  and                                ii   shall report to the governor any attempt by an agency or an agent of the United                          States Department of Homeland Security to secure the implementation of the REAL ID Act                          through the operations of the division.
