Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    allows a consumer to place a security freeze on the consumer s credit report                                .    outlines the method for placing and removing a security freeze                                .    provides exceptions                                .    prohibits the release of a credit report that is subject to a security freeze, except in                           certain instances                                .    allows certain fees to be charged in connection with a security freeze                                .    governs changes to a credit report that is subject to a security freeze                                .    prohibits some uses of a consumer s personal information  and                               .    provides for enforcement.
