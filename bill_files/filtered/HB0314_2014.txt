Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    prohibits a vehicle immobilizer from charging a fee for the immobilization of a                           vehicle for any period in which the vehicle has been towed and custody of the                           vehicle has been transferred to a vehicle impound yard  and                               .    makes technical corrections.
