All employees who perform covered public safety services for any employing unit, except        those withdrawing from coverage as provided by this chapter, shall become members of the        retirement system as follows                  Any employee who is employed to perform public safety services for an employer        covered by this chapter on or after July  ,     , shall become a member of the system effective        on the date of employment.                 a   Any employee engaged in performing public safety services for a department or        political subdivision on the date it becomes a participant in the system under this chapter shall        become a member of the system as of the date of coverage.
