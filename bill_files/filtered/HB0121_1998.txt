Olsen             AN ACT RELATING TO THE STATE SYSTEM OF HIGHER EDUCATION  CREATING THE          STRENGTHENING AT RISK YOUTH AND THEIR FAMILIES  PROGRAM AT UTAH         STATE UNIVERSITY  PROVIDING PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS  APPROPRIATING              ,     AND  PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                  B       , Utah Code Annotated                      B       , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    B        is enacted to read            Part  .  Strengthening At Risk Youth and Their Families Program                  B       .  Program creation    Location.                   There is created the Strengthening At Risk Youth and Their Families Program to be          administered through the Utah State University Extension Family Life and   H Programs.                   The program shall be implemented throughout the state through Utah State University          Extension county offices.              Section  .  Section    B        is enacted to read                  B       .  Program requirements.                   The program shall actively seek and screen adult volunteers to work with high risk          youth and their families.                   The program shall provide training and support to the volunteers to prepare them for         the wide variety of social, cultural, and other environmental factors they will encounter.                  The volunteers shall               a   enroll and involve youth in   H and other community service and activity programs               b   provide academic tutoring, with an emphasis on reading skills, when necessary               c   seek out opportunities for participation in structured recreational activities  and              d   facilitate the families  development of a positive family environment including               i   setting clear expectations and limits in the home               ii   fostering communication skills between family members                iii   encouraging the families  involvement in the school and community  and               iv   assisting in the development of time management skills.              Section  .   Appropriation.                   There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,     ,    to          Utah State University Extension.                   These funds shall be used to expand and implement the  Strengthening At Risk Youth          and Their Families  program throughout the state.  It is the intent of the Legislature that this          appropriation be ongoing.              Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .
