Enrolled                          TRANSFER OF WATER RIGHTS REQUIREMENT                              GENERAL SESSION                      STATE OF UTAH                       Sponsor   Robert M. Muhlestein                       AN ACT RELATING TO WATER AND IRRIGATION  SPECIFYING REQUIREMENTS AND                      PROCEDURES FOR THE TRANSMITTAL BY COUNTY RECORDERS TO THE STATE                      ENGINEER OF DEEDS AND OTHER CONVEYANCES WHICH REFER TO WATER                      RIGHTS.                      This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                       AMENDS                                  , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                           Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                   .  Conveyance of water rights    Deed    Exceptions    Filing and recordation                      of deed.                               a   A water right, whether evidenced by a decree, a certificate of appropriation, a                      diligence claim to the use of surface or underground water, or a water user s claim filed in general                      determination proceedings, shall be transferred by deed in substantially the same manner as is real                      estate.                           b   The deed must be recorded in the office of the recorder of the county where the point                      of diversion of the water is located and in the county where the water is used.                           c   i   A  certified  copy of the deed ,  or other  instrument, transferring the  conveyance                     which contains a reference to a water right number for a water right evidenced by any document                     listed in Subsection                    shall be promptly transmitted by the county recorder to the state                      engineer for filing.                           ii   The state engineer may designate regional offices to receive copies of deeds or other                     conveyances transmitted pursuant to Subsection     c  i .  A county recorder may not be required                     to transmit documents to more than one regional office.                           d   A recorded deed of a water right shall from the time of its filing in the office of the                       county recorder constitute notice of its contents to all persons.                               The right to the use of water evidenced by shares of stock in a corporation shall be                      transferred in accordance with the procedures applicable to securities set forth in Title   A, Chapter                       , Uniform Commercial Code   Investment Securities.
