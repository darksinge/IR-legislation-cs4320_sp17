STATE DISASTER RECOVERY RESTRICTED                           ACCOUNT AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ron Bigelow                                Senate Sponsor             Lyle W. Hillyard                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Public Safety Code regarding the use of state disaster recovery                           funds.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the State Disaster Recovery Restricted Account to authorize the                           Legislature to appropriate funds from the account to address a General Fund budget                           deficit.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.
