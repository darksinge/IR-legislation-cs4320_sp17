Peterson                                Senate Sponsor             Todd Weiler                                                         LONG TITLE                          Committee Note                               The Transportation Interim Committee recommended this bill.                           General Description                                This bill authorizes an In God We Trust freedom celebration support special group                           license plate.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    authorizes an In God We Trust freedom celebration support special group license                           plate for programs that create or support educational and community events within                           the state that celebrate, teach, or honor families, freedom, God, and country                                .    requires applicants for a new plate to make a     annual donation to the In God We                           Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account                                .    creates the In God We Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account                                .    requires the Department of Heritage and Arts to distribute funds in the In God We                           Trust Freedom Celebration Support Restricted Account to certain organizations that                           provide programs that create or support educational and community events within                           the state that celebrate, teach, or honor families, freedom, God, and country  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                              Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on October  ,     .
