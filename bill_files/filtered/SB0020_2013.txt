Reid                                House Sponsor            Paul  Ray                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Medical Assistance Act to require a health care provider to give a                           patient notice that some personal identifying information about the patient may be                           shared with the state s Medicaid and Children s Health Insurance Program eligibility                           database, and amends provisions in the Utah Technology Governance Act related to                           statewide security standards for personal information stored or transmitted on state                           servers. practices to                           patients                                   .    amends the Utah Technology Governance Act to require the state s chief                           information
