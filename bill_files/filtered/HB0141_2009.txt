Frank                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions relating to billboards.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the criteria under which a county or municipality is considered to have                           initiated the acquisition of a billboard structure by eminent domain when the county                           or municipality prevents the billboard owner from relocating the billboard                                .    modifies the height limitation applicable to a billboard erected by an owner who                           modifies, upgrades, or relocates a billboard  and                               .    modifies the allowable height of an outdoor advertising sign whose height is                           adjusted by the owner because of an obstruction due to state agency action.
