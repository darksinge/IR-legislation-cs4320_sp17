UNLAWFUL CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES IN                           CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor  LaVar Christensen                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends a Department of Corrections provision, the Utah Controlled                           Substances Act, and the Criminal Code regarding bringing illegal items into                           correctional facilities.  This bill also provides a penalty for bringing contraband tobacco                           products into a correctional facility for distribution.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the Department of Corrections to advise persons visiting inmates at                           correctional facilities  of prohibited items  that distribution of controlled substances                           to inmates is subject to criminal penalties, including incarceration  and that all                           visitors may be required to submit to a search as a condition of the visit, as required                           by current law                                .    provides for an additional year or more of incarceration for person violating                           controlled substances laws at a correctional facility  and                               .    increases the penalty from a class B misdemeanor to a third degree felony for                           persons bringing contraband tobacco products to a correctional facility in violation                           of state law.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                    This bill provides an immediate effective date.
