General Description                                This bill modifies provisions of the Employment Security Act regarding the eligibility                           of a claimant to receive unemployment compensation benefits.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    clarifies that a claimant for unemployment benefits is not required to personally                           report at an employment office as a condition of ongoing eligibility to receive                           benefits                                .    provides for the waiver of certain filing requirements for unemployment                           compensation benefits if a disaster is declared by the President of the United States                           or the governor  and                               .    makes certain technical changes.
