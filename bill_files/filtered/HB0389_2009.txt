APPLICATIONS FOR A SMALL AMOUNT OF                           WATER                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Patrick Painter                                Senate Sponsor             Dennis E. Stowell                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends a provision relating to an application for a small amount of water.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires an applicant to meet certain local ordinances                                .    requires the state engineer to notify an applicant when proof is due                                .    authorizes an appropriator of a small amount of water to file an affidavit as proof                                 .    provides the time in which an application lapses                                .    authorizes a person to file a request to reinstate a lapsed application  and                               .    requires the state engineer to issue a certificate for a reinstated application if the                           applicant meets certain requirements.
