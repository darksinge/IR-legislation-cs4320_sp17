Enrolled                               COORDINATION WITH TRIBAL LEADERS                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Beverly Ann Evans                                House Sponsor            John G. Mathis                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Utah Division of Indian Affairs Act to address meetings with                           tribal leaders and agency contacts.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    decreases the number of meetings the division is to coordinate and attend with tribal                           leaders                                .    clarifies references to tribes and tribal governments                                .    expands the agencies encouraged to participate in the meetings                                .    modifies the agencies required to provide the division contact information  and                               .    makes technical changes.
