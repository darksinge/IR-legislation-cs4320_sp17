General Description                                This bill  modifies provisions regarding the Department of Corrections and inmate                           capacity.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines levels of capacity regarding inmates in correctional institutions                                .    requires notice to the legislative leadership and the Board of Pardons, in addition to                           the governor, when an overcrowding emergency is approaching  and                               .    provides process for an emergency inmate release, including requiring that inmate                           information be provided to the Board of Pardons and Parole for use in carrying out                           an emergency release.
