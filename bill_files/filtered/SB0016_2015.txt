Vickers      House Sponsor   Michael S. Kennedy             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill amends the Utah Vital Statistics Act related to stillbirths.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   amends the definition of  dead fetus  in the Utah Vital Statistics Act  and                  directs the state registrar to issue a certificate of early term stillbirth to a parent who       requests the certificate under certain circumstances.
