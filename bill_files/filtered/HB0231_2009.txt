UTAH COMMISSION ON VOLUNTEERS                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Douglas C. Aagard                                Senate Sponsor             Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies provisions related to the Utah Commission on Volunteers regarding                           terms of office, quorum requirements, and duties of the commission.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    reduces the term of office for a commission member from four years to three years                                .    provides that approximately one third of the commission is appointed every year                                .    removes a duplicative quorum requirement provision                                .    removes the commission s duty to prepare service learning applications                                .    removes the commission s duty to establish a community volunteer training program                           to assist school districts in implementing certain literacy programs  and                               .    makes certain technical changes.
