Stowell                                                      LONG TITLE                           General Description                                This bill exempts certain radioactive waste disposal facilities from certain approval and                           siting requirements.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    exempts a radioactive waste disposal facility license in effect on or before                           December   ,      from local government planning and zoning approval,                           legislative and gubernatorial approval, and certain siting requirements  and                               .    exempts an amendment to or renewal of a radioactive waste disposal facility license                           in effect on or before December   ,      from local government planning and                           zoning approval, legislative and gubernatorial approval, and certain siting                           requirements unless the amendment or renewal would authorize waste disposal at a                           different geographic location.
