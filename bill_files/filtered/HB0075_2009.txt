Knudson                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill makes modifications to the executive committee for the Utah Communications                           Agency Network.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    increases the number of members on the executive committee from    members to                              members                                .    increases the number of executive committee members that are elected by the board                           at its annual meeting from ten members to    members                                .    requires that                                    .    one of the new committee positions has an initial term of two years, with four                           year terms thereafter  and                                   .    the other new committee position has a four year term from its inception                                .    raises the attendance requirement for a quorum from nine persons to ten persons                            and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  C      , as enacted by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      C           is amended to read                                   C      .  Executive committee established    Terms    Vacancies.                                    The executive committee shall consist of the following         individuals                                 a    ten     member representatives elected by the board at its annual meetings  and                                b   five state representatives.                                    a   i   Four of the state representatives shall be appointed by the governor, with two                            appointed initially for  of the positions having an initial term of two years and two  for                           having an initial term of four years.                                ii   Successor state representatives shall each serve for a term of four years.                                b   The fifth state representative shall be the Utah State Treasurer, who shall serve ex                           officio, or his designee.                                c   A vacancy on the executive committee for a state representative shall be filled for                           the unexpired term by appointment by the governor.                                     a   Five of the initial member representatives of the executive committee elected                          by the board shall serve for two years and five shall serve for four years.                                     a   i   One half of the positions for member representatives elected by the board                          shall have an initial term of two years and one half of the positions shall have an initial term of                          four years.                                ii   Successor member representatives of the executive committee shall each serve for a                           term of four years, so that the term of office  of five  for six of the member representatives                           expires every two years.                                b   The member representatives of the executive committee shall be removable, with or                           without cause, by a majority vote of the board.  A vacancy on the executive committee for a                           member representative shall be filled for the unexpired term by a majority of the remaining                           member representatives of the executive committee.                                       The executive committee shall elect annually one of its members as chair.                                    The executive committee shall meet on an as needed basis and as provided in the                           bylaws.                                    The executive committee shall also elect a vice chair, secretary, and treasurer to                           perform those functions provided in the bylaws.                                a   The vice chair shall be a member of the executive committee.                                b   The secretary and treasurer need not be members of the executive committee, but                           shall not have voting powers if they are not members of the executive committee.                                c   The offices of chair, vice chair, secretary, and treasurer shall be held by separate                           individuals.                                    Each member representative and state representative shall have one vote, including                           the chair, at all meetings of the executive committee.                                     Nine  Ten members of the executive committee constitute a quorum.  A vote of a                           majority of the quorum at any meeting of the executive committee is necessary to take action                           on behalf of the executive committee.
