The board shall enact regulations that require all testimony be given under oath                          during an employee grievance hearing for an employee of an institution of higher education.                                          The board and institutions may enforce these rules and regulations in any                           reasonable manner, including the assessment of fees, fines, and forfeitures, the collection of                              which may be by withholding from money owed the violator, the imposition of probation,                           suspension, or expulsion from the institution, the revocation of privileges, the refusal to issue                           certificates, degrees, and diplomas, through judicial process or any reasonable combination of                           these alternatives.
