COUNSELORS IN ELEMENTARY SCHOOLS                           TASK FORCE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Susan J. Koehn                           AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  CREATING THE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL                          COUNSELORS TASK FORCE  PROVIDING FOR MEMBERSHIP  PROVIDING                          RESPONSIBILITIES AND PROCEDURES FOR THE TASK FORCE  PROVIDING A                          REPORTING DATE  APPROPRIATING    ,    FROM THE GENERAL FUND  AND                           PROVIDING A REPEAL DATE.                           This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Elementary School Counselors Task Force    Creation    Membership                              Interim rules followed    Compensation    Staff.                                    There is created the Elementary School Counselors Task Force consisting of the                          following eight members                                 a   three members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          two of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   five members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the House                          of Representatives, no more than three of whom may be from the same political party.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed under                          Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force.                                    In conducting its business, the task force shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees.                                    Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force shall be paid in accordance with                             Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support to                          the task force.                               Section  .   Duties    Interim report.                                    The task force shall review and make recommendations on the following issues                                 a   the need for and use of counselors in the state s public elementary schools, to include                                 i   academic, family, and socialization issues as related to students  and                                ii   the scope of counselor relationships with students, parents of students, and other school                          personnel  and                                b   a funding source or mechanism that would allow an adequate number of counselors                          to be placed in the state s elementary schools.                                    A final report, including any proposed legislation shall be presented to the Education                          Interim Committee before November   ,     .                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               Except as provided in H.B.  , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated from                          the General Fund for fiscal year                                              ,    to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the task                          force                                      ,    to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses of                          representatives on the task force  and                                      ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for staffing                          the task force.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This act is repealed November   ,     .
