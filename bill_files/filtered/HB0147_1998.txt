LIABILITY FOR MINOR WHO DRIVES                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   John E. Swallow          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC SAFETY  AMENDING PROVISIONS RELATING TO THE         LIABILITY OF CERTAIN ADULTS FOR MINORS WHO DRIVE.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          AMENDS                       , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                            , as renumbered and amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Application of minors    Liability of person signing application              Cancellation of cosigning adult s liability.                     As used in this section,  minor  means any person younger than    years of age who          is not married or has not been emancipated by adjudication.                   a   The application of a minor for a learner permit or provisional license shall be signed          by the parent or guardian of the applicant and verified before a person authorized to administer          oaths.               b   If no person has custody of the minor applicant, then a responsible adult who is willing          to assume the obligation imposed under this chapter may sign the application.                   a   Any negligence or willful misconduct of a minor when driving a motor vehicle          upon a highway is imputed to the person who has signed the application of the minor under          Subsection    .               b   Except as provided under Subsection    , the person who has signed the application          under Subsection    , is jointly and severally liable with the minor for any damages caused by the          minor s negligence or willful misconduct.                c   This liability provision is in addition to the liability provisions in Section         .                   a   If a minor deposits, or there is deposited on his behalf, proof of financial         responsibility for driving a motor vehicle in form and in amounts as required under Title   ,         Chapter   a, Financial Responsibility of Motor Vehicle Owners and Operators Act, the division         may accept the application of the minor when signed before a person authorized to administer         oaths by a parent, guardian of the minor, or a responsible adult.                b   While the proof is maintained, the parent, guardian, or responsible adult is not subject         to the liability imposed under Subsection    .                   If owner s or operator s security for the minor s operation of the motor vehicle is in          effect in amounts as required under Title   , Chapter   a, Financial Responsibility of Motor          Vehicle Owners and Operators Act, the parent, guardian, or responsible adult who signed the          minor s application is not subject to the liability imposed under Subsection    .                   a   A person who has signed the application of a minor under Subsection     may file          with the division a verified written request that the license of the minor be cancelled.               b   The division shall then cancel the license of the minor, and the person who signed the          application of the minor under Subsection     is relieved from the liability imposed under this          chapter regarding any subsequent negligence or willful misconduct of the minor in driving a motor          vehicle.                   a   The division upon receipt of satisfactory evidence of the death of the person who          signed the application of a minor under Subsection     shall cancel the license and may not issue          a new license until a new application, signed and verified, is made under this chapter.               b   This subsection does not apply to an application of a person who is no longer a minor.
