SUPPORT OUR TROOPS SPECIAL GROUP                           LICENSE PLATE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   D. Gregg Buxton                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending provisions related to special                           group license plates.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    authorizes a Support Our Troops special group license plate for troop support                           programs through the Utah National Guard                                .    requires applicants for a new plate to make a     annual donation to the Utah                           National Guard for troop support programs                                .    creates the Community Financial Support for Troops Restricted Account                                .    requires the Adjutant General of the Utah National Guard to distribute contributions                           in the account                                .    grants the Utah National Guard rulemaking authority to make rules establishing a                           procedure for an organization to apply for funds in the Community Financial                           Support for Troops Restricted Account  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   This bill takes effect on October  ,     . Section  .    Section               is enacted to read                                       .  Community Financial Support for Troops Restricted Account    Use of                            contributions.                                     There is created in the General Fund a restricted account known as the Community                           Financial Support for Troops Restricted Account.                                     The account shall be funded by                                  a   contributions deposited into the Community Financial Support for Troops                           Restricted Account in accordance with Section         a                                          b   private contributions                                  c   donations or grants from public or private entities  and                                 d   interest and earnings on account monies.                                     The executive director shall distribute funds in the account to one or more                           organizations that                                  a   are exempt from federal income taxation under Section     c    , Internal Revenue                           Code  and                                 b   distribute money to existing entities which provide assistance to                                  i   families of people serving in the military who are killed or injured to where the                           person cannot provide for the person s family                                  ii   people serving in the military who are injured  or                                 iii   families of people serving in the military who have not been killed or injured but                              need financial help.                                     An organization described in Subsection     may apply to the executive director to                           receive a distribution in accordance with Subsection    .                                     In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                           department may make rules providing procedures and requirements for an organization to                           apply to the executive director to receive a distribution in accordance with Subsection    .
