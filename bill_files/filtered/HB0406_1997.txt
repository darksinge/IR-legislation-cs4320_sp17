MARRIAGE LAW AMENDMENTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Gene Davis          AN ACT RELATING TO HUSBAND AND WIFE  PROVIDING A DEFINITION          MODIFYING SECTION TO ADDRESS CONSENT OF DIVORCED PARENTS FOR         MARRIAGE OF MINOR CHILD  REQUIRING PROOF OF IDENTITY, AND LEGAL         ENTRY BY NONCITIZENS, FOR PERSONS UNKNOWN TO THE CLERK  AND         MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS. For purposes of this section,  minor  means a male or female under    years of age.
