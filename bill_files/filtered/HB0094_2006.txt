BAN ON GIFTS TO ELECTED OFFICIALS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Patricia W. Jones                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Lobbyist Disclosure and Regulation Act by banning certain gifts                           to public officials.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines gifts and prohibits public officials from receiving certain gifts  and                               .    repeals the existing section governing gifts.
