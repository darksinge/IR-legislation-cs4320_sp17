Niederhauser                                House Sponsor            John  Dougall                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Individual Income Tax Act to address return filing requirements.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that for certain returns required to be filed with the state, if the Internal                           Revenue Service provides a due date for a corresponding federal return that is                           different from the state due date, the return shall be filed on or before the due date                           provided by the Internal Revenue Service for filing the return  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.
