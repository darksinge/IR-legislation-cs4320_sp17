Seitz                           AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES  AMENDING THE REPORTING                          REQUIREMENT FOR THE PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION TO REPORT ANNUALLY                          TO VARIOUS ENTITIES.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           AMENDS                                    b  . , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section        b  .      is amended to read                                     b  . .  Report to governor and Legislature.                               Beginning October   ,     , and  biannually  annually thereafter, the commission shall                           submit a report to the governor, Legislature, the Public Utilities and Technology Interim                           Committee, and Information Technology Commission on the state of the telecommunications                           industry and make recommendations for any regulatory changes necessary to achieve the policy                           of the state as set forth in Section         b  .     .  The commission shall determine criteria to be used                           to evaluate the performance of price regulation and the information necessary to conduct the                           evaluation.
