EDUCATOR PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT                           REQUIREMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor  Richard M. Siddoway                           AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  PROVIDING THAT EDUCATORS                          PARTICIPATE IN PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT ACTIVITIES IN ACCORDANCE                          WITH RULES ADOPTED BY THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION IN ORDER TO                          MAINTAIN THEIR STANDARD CERTIFICATES.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                  A      , as enacted by Chapter  , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A           is amended to read                                   A      .  Standard certificates    Period of validity.                                    Unless revoked for cause by the State Board of Education,  an initial  a standard                           certificate is valid if the certificate holder verifies, in accordance with rules of the board,                          participation in professional development activities and at least three years of educational                           employment  of at least three years  during each succeeding five year interval.                                    The board shall establish minimum requirements for recertification of persons who are                           unable to qualify under Subsection    .       Legislative Review Note      as of                  PM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.   Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel     Committee Note     The Education Interim Committee recommended this bill.
