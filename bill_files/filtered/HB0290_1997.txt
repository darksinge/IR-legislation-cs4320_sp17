APPROPRIATION FOR COUNTY LAND USE PLANNING                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Dennis H. Iverson          AN ACT RELATING TO  COUNTIES  APPROPRIATING     ,    TO THE OFFICE OF         PLANNING AND BUDGET TO DISTRIBUTE TO SOUTHERN UTAH UNIVERSITY TO         CONDUCT WORKSHOPS ON COUNTY LAND PLANNING AND TO COUNTIES AS         MATCHING FUNDS FOR TRAINING IN PLANNING.         This act enacts uncodified material.          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .   Intent.              The Legislature finds that the value of property throughout the state is enhanced by          appropriate land use planning by the counties, that revenues to the state depend to a significant          extent on the value of the property generating those revenues, and that the quality of land use          planning improves with training and education.  It is the intent of the Legislature in appropriating          these funds to help meet the statewide need for training and education of county officials involved          in county land planning activities to improve the quality of land use planning by the counties.              Section  .   Appropriation.                   a   There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        ,     ,    to          the Office of Planning and Budget.               b   The Office of Planning and Budget                i   shall distribute up to    ,    of the appropriated funds to Southern Utah University          for use by its Utah Center for Rural Life to conduct workshops on preparation of county land          plans                ii   shall determine, in conjunction with the Utah Association of Counties, the counties          most qualified to receive the remainder of the appropriated funds to assist in developing county          land plans, based on criteria established by the Office of Planning and Budget                iii   may consider the recommendation of the Governor s Local Government         Comprehensive Planning Project Steering Committee in establishing those criteria  and              iv   shall distribute the remaining funds to counties that               A   meet the criteria established by the Office of Planning and Budget               B   match the amount distributed by the Office of Planning and Budget in funds or in kind               C   agree to encourage county officials and county attorneys to attend the workshops  and              D   agree to review and, if necessary, revise the comprehensive general plan at least         annually.              c   In planning and presenting the workshops provided under Subsection     b  i ,          Southern Utah University, through its Utah Center for Rural Life, may involve county officials          who are preparing or have completed a county land plan.                   These appropriations are nonlapsing.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
