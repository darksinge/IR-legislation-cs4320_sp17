UTAH TELEHEALTH COMMISSION                           AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor  Brad L. Dee                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill changes the name of the Utah Telehealth Commission to the Utah Digital                           Healthcare Commission to better reflect the role of the commission in the digitization                           of medicine.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends the name of the Utah Telehealth Commission to the Utah Digital Healthcare                           Commission. Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Utah  Telehealth  Digital Healthcare Commission Act.                                Section  .    Section        f         is amended to read                                     f    .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Commission  means the Utah  Telehealth  Digital Healthcare Commission created                           in Section         f        .                                     Telehealth  means the electronic transfer or exchange of medically related data for                           diagnosis, treatment, consultation, educational, or other related purposes.
