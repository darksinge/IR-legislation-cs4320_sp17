Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   decreases the percentage of sales and use tax revenue received by the Division of       Water Resources  and                  increases the percentage of sales and use tax revenue received by the Division of       Water Rights.       Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             This bill provides a special effective date.
