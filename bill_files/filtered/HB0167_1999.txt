HUNTING LICENSE FEE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   DeMar Bud Bowman                           AN ACT RELATING TO FISH AND GAME  PROVIDING THAT LIFETIME LICENSEES                          ARE ENTITLED TO A     REDUCTION IN THE STANDARD FEE FOR A DEDICATED                          HUNTER CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           AMENDS                                        . , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section            .      is amended to read                                         . .  Lifetime hunting and fishing licenses.                                    As used in this section,  dedicated hunter  means a person who has purchased and                          signed a valid dedicated hunter certificate of registration for the dedicated hunter program, as                          defined by the Wildlife Board.                                          Lifetime licensees born after December   ,     , must be certified under Section                                         before engaging in hunting.                                          A lifetime license shall remain valid if the residency of the lifetime licensee                           changes  to another state or country.                                          a   A lifetime license may be used in lieu of an annual wildlife habitat, small                           game, and fishing license.                                b   Each year, a lifetime licensee is entitled to receive without charge a permit and tag of                            the lifetime licensee s choice for one of the following general season deer hunts                                 i   archery                                 ii   rifle  or                                iii   muzzleloader.                                   c   A lifetime licensee is subject to each requirement for special hunting and fishing                           permits and tags, except as provided in Subsection     b .                                    Lifetime licensees are entitled to a     reduction in the standard scheduled fee for a                          dedicated hunter certificate of registration.                                          The Wildlife Board may adopt rules necessary to carry out the provisions of this                           section.       Legislative Review Note      as of                  AM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
