BACKGROUND CHECK FEE EXEMPTION FOR FIREARM                           PURCHASE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Brad J. Galvez                                Senate Sponsor             Stuart C. Reid                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill provides an exemption to the background check fee requirement for law                           enforcement officers.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows a law enforcement officer currently employed and in good standing to                           purchase a firearm without paying the requisite background check fee as long as the                           dealer verifies the officer s employment and standing with the employing agency.
