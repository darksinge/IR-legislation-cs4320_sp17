         MEDIA PRODUCTION VEHICLE EXEMPTION           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Daniel McCay      Senate Sponsor    Todd Weiler             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies the Traffic Code by amending media production vehicle provisions.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   requires a media production to provide advance notice to law enforcement when       using a media production vehicle on a highway                   provides for notice requirements  and                  makes technical changes.       Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             None       Utah Code Sections Affected        AMENDS                 a     , as enacted by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                   Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah             Section  .  Section     a      is amended to read                 a     . Media production vehicle    Definition    Exemption    Identification.                 As used in this section              a    Media production  means the making of a motion picture, television show, video,       commercial, Internet video, or other viewable programming provided to viewers via a movie       theater or transmitted through broadcast radio wave, cable, satellite, wireless, or Internet.             b    Media production vehicle  means a vehicle used exclusively for media production.                  A  If a media production entity using a media production vehicle provides at least          hours  advance notice as described in Subsection     to the law enforcement agency having       jurisdiction of the highway being used by the media production vehicle, the media production       vehicle is exempt from the restrictions of Section     a      while the vehicle is              a   being used to simulate an authorized emergency vehicle in  a  the media       production  or             b   being driven in transit between the media production location and the media       production vehicle storage location if during transit the vehicle displays a sign prominently on       each front side door of the media production vehicle stating  Media Production Vehicle.                  The notice described in Subsection     shall include              a   the date              b   the time              c   the designated route of travel and location of use  and             d   a description of the media production vehicle.Legislative Review NoteOffice of Legislative Research and General Counsel
