APPLIED TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   A. Lamont Tyler                           AN ACT RELATING TO APPLIED TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION  DESIGNATING THE                          STATE S APPLIED TECHNOLOGY CENTERS TO INCLUDE THE WASATCH FRONT                          SOUTH APPLIED TECHNOLOGY CENTER  PROVIDING FOR THE DIVISION OF                          CURRENT FUNDING FOR THE WASATCH FRONT SOUTH APPLIED TECHNOLOGY                           SERVICE REGION BETWEEN THE WASATCH FRONT SOUTH APPLIED                           TECHNOLOGY CENTER AND SALT LAKE COMMUNITY COLLEGE  AND PROVIDING                           AN EFFECTIVE DATE.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            ENACTS                                  A       . , Utah Code Annotated                                This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A       .      is enacted to read                                  A       . .  Applied technology centers.                               The following applied technology centers are established to assist in providing applied                          technology education in an efficient and cost effective manner throughout the state                                     Bridgerland Applied Technology Center                                     Davis Applied Technology Center                                     Ogden Weber Applied Technology Center                                     Wasatch Front South Applied Technology Center  and                                    Uintah Basin Applied Technology Center.                               Section  .   Intent language.                                       a   It is the intent of the Legislature that for the fiscal year beginning July  ,     , the                          current funding for the Wasatch Front South Applied Technology Service Region shall be divided                          into two parts.                                b   The first part, the public education allocation, shall be used to fund the Wasatch Front                          South Applied Technology Center, which has as its primary role to meet the applied technology                          training needs of secondary students in Salt Lake and Tooele Counties.                                c   The second part, the higher education allocation, shall be used by Salt Lake                          Community College to support applied technology training for adult students.                                    It is the intent of the Legislature that for the fiscal year beginning July  ,     , and for                          each fiscal year thereafter, the Legislature shall provide                                 a   a line item appropriation under public education in the Appropriations Act to fund the                          Wasatch Front South Applied Technology Center  and                                b   a line item appropriation under higher education in the Appropriations Act to fund the                          applied technology training for adult students at Salt Lake Community College referred to in                          Subsection     c .                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
