CONTINGENCY PLANS FOR POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ken Ivory                                Senate Sponsor             Deidre M. Henderson                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts provisions relating to political subdivisions  contingency plans in the                           event of a reduction of federal funds.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a political subdivision that receives federal funds comprising     or more                           of the political subdivision s annual budget to develop and publish a contingency                           plan describing how the political subdivision will operate in the event that the                           federal funds it receives are reduced.
