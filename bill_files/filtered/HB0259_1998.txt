SALE OF VEHICLE                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Brian R. Allen          AN ACT RELATING TO MOTOR VEHICLES  REQUIRING CERTAIN DISCLOSURES FOR         CERTAIN NEW MOTOR VEHICLES  AMENDING CERTAIN SAFETY INSPECTION         PROVISIONS  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          AMENDS                    a    , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                            , as renumbered and amended by Chapters    and    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section      a     is amended to read                    a    .  Safety inspection certificate required for renewal or registration of motor           vehicle    Exemptions.                   A safety inspection certificate, as required by Section         , or proof of exemption          from safety inspection shall be presented at the time of, and as a condition of, registration or          renewal of registration of a motor vehicle.                   a   The safety inspection required under this section may be made no more than two          months prior to the renewal of registration, except as provided in Subsection     b .               b   i   To renew the registration of a used car, the title of which has been transferred or is          to be transferred, a safety inspection certificate received by the previous owner during the past two          months may be presented to the division.               ii   If the transferor is a licensed and bonded used motor vehicle dealer, a safety inspection          certificate issued in a licensed and bonded motor vehicle dealer s name during the previous six          months may be presented to the division.                   The following motor vehicles are exempt from this section                 a   a new motor vehicle when registered the first time,  provided  if               i   the new motor vehicle conforms to federal safety standards for manufacture and         delivery of a new motor vehicle               ii   a new car predelivery inspection has been made by a dealer   and               iii   the dealer provides a written disclosure statement listing any deficiency, existing with         the new motor vehicle at the time of delivery, that would cause the motor vehicle to fail a safety         inspection given in accordance with Section           and              iv   the buyer signs the disclosure statement to acknowledge that the buyer has read and         understands the listed deficiencies  and               b   a motor vehicle required to be registered under this chapter that bears a dealer plate or          other special plate under Title   , Chapter  , Part  , Special Dealer License Plates, except that if          the motor vehicle is propelled by its own power and is not being moved for repair or dismantling,          the motor vehicle shall comply with Section          regarding safe mechanical condition.                   a   A safety inspection certificate shall be displayed on                i   all registered commercial motor vehicles with a gross vehicle weight rating of   ,             pounds or more                ii   a motor vehicle with three or more axles, pulling a trailer, or pulling a trailer with          multiple axles                iii   a combination unit  and               iv   a bus or van for hire.               b   A commercial vehicle under Subsection     a  is exempt from the requirements of          Subsection    .                   A motor vehicle may be sold and the title assigned to the new owner without a valid          safety inspection, but the motor vehicle may not be registered in the new owner s name until the          motor vehicle complies with this section.              Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Safety inspection required    Safety inspection certificate required    Out           of state permits.                   a   Except as provided in Subsection     b , a person may not operate on a highway          a motor vehicle required to be registered in this state unless the motor vehicle has passed a safety          inspection.                      b   Subsection     a  does not apply to               i   vehicles exempt from registration under Section     a      and              ii   off highway vehicles.                  The safety inspection shall               a   be made at least once each year               b   be made by a safety inspector certified by the division at a safety inspection station         authorized by the division   and               c   cover an inspection of the motor vehicle mechanism, brakes, and equipment to ensure         proper adjustment and condition as required by department rules  and               d   include an inspection for the display of license plates in accordance with Section              a    .                   A safety inspection station shall issue a safety inspection certificate to the owner of          each motor vehicle that passes a safety inspection under this section.                   The division may                a   authorize the acceptance in this state of a safety inspection certificate issued in another          state having a safety inspection law similar to this state  and                b   extend the time within which a safety inspection certificate must be obtained by the          resident owner of a vehicle that was not in this state during the time a safety inspection was          required.              Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .
