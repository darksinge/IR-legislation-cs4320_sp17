General Description            This bill provides for the disposition of the remains of a decedent when the decedent       was a disabled adult residing with a guardian        Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   allows for situations where a parent caring for a disabled adult child is divorced or       separated from the other parent and the disabled adult dies  and                  provides for a person who was a guardian of an incapacitated adult to direct the       disposition of the decedent s remains if there is no other person        Money Appropriated in this
