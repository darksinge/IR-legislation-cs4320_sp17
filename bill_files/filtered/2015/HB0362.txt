               requires a political subdivision that receives certain sales and use tax revenue to       submit certain information in audits  reviews  compilations  or fiscal reports     makes technical and conforming changes        Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             This bill provides a special effective date        Utah Code
