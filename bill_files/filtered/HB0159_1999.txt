MENTAL HEALTH INSURANCE PARITY                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Judy Ann Buffmire                           AN ACT RELATING TO INSURANCE  REQUIRING THAT HEALTH INSURANCE                          POLICIES COVER MENTAL HEALTH CONDITIONS ON THE SAME BASIS AS PHYSICAL                          HEALTH CONDITIONS  PERMITTING MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED                          THROUGH MANAGED CARE  REQUIRING THAT SERVICES BE PROVIDED BY                          LICENSED PERSONS AND FACILITIES  EXTENDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY TO                           THE COMMISSIONER OF INSURANCE  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            ENACTS                                  A       , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                                  A       .  Coverage of mental health conditions.                                    This section applies generally to health insurance policies and health maintenance                          organization contracts.                                    A policy or contract                                 a   shall provide coverage for the treatment of mental health conditions  and                                b   may not establish any rate, term, or condition that places a greater financial burden on                          an insured for the treatment of a mental health condition than for the treatment of a covered                          physical health condition.                                    Expenditures for mental health conditions and physical health conditions shall count                          equally toward any deductible or out of pocket limit.                                    A policy or contract may provide coverage for the treatment of mental health                          conditions through a managed care organization or system, regardless of whether the policy or                             contract uses a managed care organization or system for the treatment of physical health                          conditions, provided that the managed care organization or system is in compliance with the rules                          adopted by the commissioner pursuant to Subsection    .                                    a   A policy or contract shall be in compliance with this section if the policy or contract                          offers at least one coverage option that places no greater financial burden on the insured for the                          treatment of mental health conditions than for the treatment of physical health conditions.                                b   The commissioner may disapprove any policy or contract that the commissioner                          determines to be inconsistent with the purposes of this section.                                    To be eligible for coverage under this section treatment for a mental health condition                          must be rendered                                 a   by a mental health professional licensed pursuant to Title   , Chapter   , Mental                          Health Professional Practice Act  or                                b   in a  health care facility licensed to provide mental health service pursuant to Title   ,                          Chapter   , Health Care Facility Licensing and Inspection Act, or  Title   A, Chapter  , Licensure                          of Programs and Facilities, that provides a program for the treatment of a mental health condition                          pursuant to a written plan.                                    The commissioner shall adopt rules to ensure that                                 a   timely and appropriate access to mental health treatment is available  and                                b   administrative and clinical protocols do not serve to reduce access to medically                          necessary mental health treatment for any insured.                                    As used in this section                                 a    Mental health condition  means any condition or disorder involving mental illness that                          falls under any of the diagnostic categories listed in the mental disorders section of the                          International Classification of Diseases, as periodically revised.                                b    Rate, term, or condition  means any lifetime or annual payment limits, deductibles,                          copayments, coinsurance, and any other cost sharing requirements, out of pocket limits, visit                          limits, or any other financial component of health insurance coverage that affects the insured.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
