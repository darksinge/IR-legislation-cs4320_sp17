Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   clarifies provisions regarding municipal annexation of certain unincorporated islands                   modifies annexation procedures to allow a municipality to annex certain       unincorporated islands without allowing or considering protests under certain       circumstances                   provides for a recommendation of annexation from a county legislative body  and                  makes technical and conforming changes.
