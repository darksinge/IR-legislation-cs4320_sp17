         GARNISHMENT AMENDMENTS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Brad L. Dee      Senate Sponsor    Lyle W. Hillyard             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill lifts the ban on certain property from execution under specific circumstances.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   excludes disability and veterans benefits from property exempt from execution if       the recipient has been convicted of a felony and ordered to pay restitution to a       victim.
