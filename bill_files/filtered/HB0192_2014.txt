INITIATIVE AND REFERENDUM PETITION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jon E. Stanard                                Senate Sponsor             Margaret Dayton                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill makes changes to an initiative petition signature sheet and a referendum                           petition signature sheet.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    adds a statement to a statewide or local initiative petition signature sheet stating that                           a signer has read, understands, and agrees to the law proposed by the petition                                .    adds a statement to a statewide or local referendum petition signature sheet stating                           that a signer has read and understands the law the petition seeks to overturn  and                               .    makes technical corrections.
