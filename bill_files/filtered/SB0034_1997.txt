Section  .  Section         is amended to read                    .  Definitions.            As used in this chapter                   Advertisement  means any representation made relative to seeds, plants, bulbs, or        ground stock other than those on the label of a seed container, disseminated in any manner.                  Agricultural seeds  mean seeds of grass, forage plants, cereal crops, fiber crops, sugar        beets, seed potatoes, or any other kinds of seed or mixtures of seed commonly known within this        state as agricultural or field seeds.                          Flower seeds  mean seeds of herbaceous plants grown for their blooms, ornamental        foliage, or other ornamental  parts  plants commonly known and sold under the name of flower seeds        in this state.                  Foundation seed,   registered seed,  or  certified seed  means seed that is produced and        labeled in accordance with procedures officially recognized by a seed certifying agency approved        and accredited in this state.                 a    Hybrid  means the first generation seed of a cross produced by controlling        pollination and by combining   a                i   two or more inbred lines    b               ii   one inbred or a single cross with an open pollinated variety  or   c               iii   two varieties or species, except open pollinated varieties of corn,    Zea mays   .             b   The second generation and subsequent generations from  such  the crosses  shall         referred to in Subsection  a  are not to be regarded as hybrids.             c   Hybrid designations shall be treated as variety names.                  Kind  means one or more related species or subspecies of seed which singly or        collectively is known by one name, for example   , corn, oats, alfalfa, and timothy.                 a    Label  means  all  any written, printed, or graphic  representations  representation        accompanying and pertaining to any seeds, plants, bulbs, or ground stock whether in bulk or in        containers , and .             b    Label  includes representations on invoices, bills, and letterheads   provided, that all        written or printed matter shall appear in English .                  Lot  means a definite quantity of seed identified by a number or other mark, every part        or bag of which is uniform within recognized tolerances.                  Noxious weed seeds  mean weed seeds declared noxious by the commissioner.                   Pure seed,   germination,  or other  such  terms in common use for testing seeds for        purposes of labeling shall have ascribed to them the meaning set forth for such terms in the most        recent edition of  Rules for Seed Testing  published by the Association of Official Seed Analysts.                   Seeds for sprouting  means seeds sold for sprouting for salad or culinary purposes.                                  Sowing  means the placement of agricultural seeds, vegetable seeds, flower        seeds, tree and shrub seeds, or seeds  used  for sprouting  purposes  in a selected environment for        the purpose of obtaining plant growth.                          Treated  means seed that has received an application of a substance to reduce,        control, or repel certain disease organisms, fungi, insects or other pests which may attack the seed        or its seedlings, or has received some other treatment to improve its planting value.                          Tree and shrub seeds  mean seeds of woody plants commonly known and sold        under the name of tree and shrub seeds in this state.                          Variety  means a subdivision of a kind characterized by growth, yield, plant,        fruit, seed, or other characteristic, which differentiate it from other plants of the same kind.                          Vegetable seeds  mean seeds of crops grown in gardens or on truck farms that        are generally known and sold under the name of vegetable seeds, plants, bulbs, and ground stocks        in this state.                          Weed seeds  mean seeds of any plant generally recognized as a weed within this        state.            Section  .  Section         is amended to read                    .
