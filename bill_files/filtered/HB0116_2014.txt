Section      A       .      is amended to read                                   A       . .  School building construction and inspection manual    Annual                           construction and inspection conference    Verification of school construction inspections.                                    a   The State Board of Education, through the state superintendent of public                           instruction, shall develop and distribute to each school district a school building construction                           and inspection resource manual.                                b   The manual shall be provided to a charter school upon request of the charter school.                                    a   The manual shall include                                 i   current legal requirements  and                                ii   information on school building construction and inspections, including the                          standards adopted by the Division of Facilities Construction and Management in accordance                          with Section      A          .                                b   The state superintendent shall review and update the manual at least once every                           three years.                                    The board shall provide for an annual school construction conference to allow a                           representative from each school district and charter school to                                 a   receive current information on the design, construction, and inspection of school                           buildings                                 b   receive training on such matters as                                 i   using properly certified building inspectors                                 ii   filing construction inspection summary reports and the final inspection certification                           with the local governmental authority s building official                                 iii   the roles and relationships between a school district or charter school and the local                           governmental authority, either a county or municipality, as related to the construction and                           inspection of school buildings  and                                   iv   adequate documentation of school building inspections  and                                c   provide input on any changes that may be needed to improve the existing school                           building inspection program.                                    The board shall develop a process to verify that inspections by qualified inspectors                           occur in each school district or charter school.                               Section  .    Section      A           is enacted to read                            Part  .  School Construction Standards                                  A      .  Title.                               This part is known as  School Construction Standards.                                Section  .    Section      A           is enacted to read                                  A      .  Definitions.                                     Division  means the Division of Facilities Construction and Management.                                     Public school construction  means construction work on a new public school.                               Section  .    Section      A           is enacted to read                                  A      .  Division to adopt public school construction standards.                                    The division shall adopt standards for public school construction.                                    Before a school district or charter school may begin public school construction, the                          school district or charter school shall                                 a   review the standards adopted by the division under this section  and                                b   take into consideration the standards when planning the public school construction.                                    In adopting the standards for public school construction, the division shall consider                          the following and adopt alternative standards as needed                                 a   location factors, including whether the school is in a rural or urban setting, and                          climate factors                                 b   variations in standards for significant or minimal projected student population                          growth                                 c   standards specific to schools that serve various populations and grades, including                          high schools, junior high schools, middle schools, elementary schools, alternative schools, and                          schools for people with disabilities  and                                d   year round use.                                    The standards shall address the following                                    a   square footage per student                                 b   minimum and maximum required real property for a public school                                 c   athletic facilities and fields, playgrounds, and hard surface play areas                                 d   cost per square foot                                 e   minimum and maximum qualities and costs for building materials                                 f   design efficiency                                 g   parking                                 h   furnishing                                 i   proof of compliance with applicable building codes  and                                j   safety.
