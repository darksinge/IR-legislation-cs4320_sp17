Blackham                       AN ACT RELATING TO CRIMINAL CODE  CREATING THE CRIME  AND PROVIDING                      A PENALTY OF DISARMING A PEACE OFFICER.                      This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                       ENACTS                                   . , Utah Code Annotated                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                           Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                   . .  Disarming a peace officer.                          A person is guilty of a first degree felony who intentionally takes or removes, or attempts                     to take or remove, a firearm from the person or immediate presence of a person he knows is a                     peace officer                                without the consent of the peace officer  and                               while the peace officer is acting within the scope of his authority as a peace officer.
