         FEES FOR SUPPLEMENTAL HOURS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor  Steve Eliason      Senate Sponsor  Ann Millner             LONG TITLE      Committee Note            The Education Interim Committee recommended this bill.       General Description             This bill addresses provisions relating to supplemental hours of instruction.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides a local school board or charter school governing board with the authority       to offer supplemental hours of instruction to students enrolled in kindergarten       classes  and                  enacts provisions relating to fees for supplemental hours of instruction.
