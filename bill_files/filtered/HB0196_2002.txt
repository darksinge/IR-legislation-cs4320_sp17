DIVORCE AND PARENT TIME REVISIONS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Chad E. Bennion                             This act modifies provisions pertaining to parent time and child support.  It provides for a                          minimum visitation schedule when a parent relocates, either by leaving the state or by                          putting more than     miles between a noncustodial parent and a child.  It also requires that                          the Office of Recovery Services verify information received concerning nonpayment of                          support before acting on a possibly unfounded allegation.
