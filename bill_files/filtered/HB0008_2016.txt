General Description             This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and       operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July  ,      and ending June   ,     .       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides budget increases and decreases for the use and support of certain state agencies       and institutions of higher education                   authorizes certain state agency fees                   authorizes internal service fund rates                   adjusts funding for the impact of Internal Service Fund rate changes  and,                  provides budget increases and decreases for other purposes as described.
