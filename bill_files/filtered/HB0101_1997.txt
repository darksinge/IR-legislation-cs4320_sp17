TEACHER TERMINATION PROCEDURES TASK FORCE                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Tammy J.  Rowan          AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  CREATING THE TEACHER         TERMINATION PROCEDURES TASK FORCE  DEFINING TASK FORCE MEMBERSHIP         AND DUTIES  PROVIDING A    ,    APPROPRIATION  PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE         DATE  AND PROVIDING A REPEAL DATE.         This act enacts uncodified material.          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .   Task force membership.              There is created the Teacher Termination Procedures Task Force consisting of the          following thirteen members                    two members of the Senate, appointed by the president of the Senate                    two members of the House of Representatives, appointed by the speaker of the House          of Representatives                    a member of the State Board of Education or the board s designee                    the president of the Utah PTA or the president s designee                    the president of the UEA or the president s designee                    two school district superintendents, one from a rural district and one from an urban          district, appointed by the Utah School Superintendent s Association                    a local school board member, appointed by the Utah School Boards Association                    the executive director of the AFT or the director s designee                    a school principal appointed by the Utah Association of Elementary Principals and the          Utah Association of Secondary Principals  and                    a school teacher selected by the other members of the task force.              Section  .   Appointment of chairs    Quorum.                   The president of the Senate shall appoint a member of the Senate appointed to the task         force under Section     as Senate chair of the task force.                  The speaker of the House of Representatives shall appoint a member of the House of         Representatives appointed to the task force under Section     as House chair of the task force.                  a   A majority of the members of the task force constitutes a quorum.              b   The action of a majority of the quorum constitutes the action of the task force.             Section  .   Task force duties.                  The task force shall review and make recommendations on teacher evaluation and         termination procedures in the state s public schools as provided for under current state law.                   The task force shall report its findings to the Education Interim Committee before          December   ,     .              Section  .   Salaries and expenses    Staff support.                   a   Salaries and expenses of task force members who are legislators shall be paid in          accordance with Section       .               b   Members of the task force who are not legislators may not receive additional          compensation for their work associated with the task force.                   The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support          to the task force.              Section  .   Appropriation.              There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year                              ,    to the Senate for salary and expenses of senators who are members of the task          force                      ,    to the House of Representatives for salary and expenses of representatives who          are members of the task force  and                      ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for staffing          the task force.              Section  .   Effective date.              If approved by two thirds of all the members elected to each house, this act takes effect          upon approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah          Constitution Article VII, Section  , without the governor s signature, or in the case of a veto, the          date of veto override.                     Section  .   Repeal date.             This act is repealed December   ,     .
