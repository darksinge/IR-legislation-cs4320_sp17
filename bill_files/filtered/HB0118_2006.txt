UTAH HEALTH CARE WORKFORCE                           FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE ACT AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jackie Biskupski                                Senate Sponsor             Beverly Ann Evans                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance Program to add                           radiology technicians and medical laboratory technicians to the list of health care                           professionals who may participate in the program.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends the definition of health care professional                                .    amends the duties of the Department of Health with regard to the Utah Health Care                           Workforce Financial Assistance Program  and                               .    amends the membership of the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance                           Program Advisory Committee.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates                                 .    as an ongoing appropriation subject to future budget constraints,     ,    from the                           General Fund for fiscal year         to the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial                           Assistance Program in the Department of Health.                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                               AMENDS                                         , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                             , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                             , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                  is amended to read                                          .  Definitions.                                     Health care professional  means  a physician, physician assistant, nurse, dentist,                          mental health therapist, or                                  a   a physician licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Utah Medical Practice Act, or                          Chapter   , Utah Osteopathic Medical Practices Act                                 b   a physician assistant licensed under Title   , Chapter   a, Physician Assistant Act                                 c   a nurse licensed under Title   , Chapter   b, Nurse Practice Act                                 d   a dentist licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Dentist and Dental Hygienist Practice                          Act                                 e   a mental health professional licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Mental Health                          Professional Practice Act, or Chapter   , Psychologist Licensing Act                                 f   a radiology technologist or radiology practical technician licensed under Title   ,                          Chapter   , Radiology Technologist and Radiology Practical Technician Licensing Act                                 g   a medical laboratory technician as defined by the department by administrative rule                           or                                h   other health care professional designated by the department by rule.                                     Underserved area  means an area designated by the department as underserved by                           health care professionals, based upon the results of a needs assessment developed by the                           department in consultation with the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance Program                           Advisory Committee created under Section                  .                               Section  .    Section                  is amended to read                                          .  Creation of program    Duties of department.                                    There is created within the department the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial                           Assistance Program to provide professional education scholarships and loan repayment                              assistance to health care professionals who locate or continue to practice in underserved areas.                                    In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                           department shall make rules governing the administration of the program, including rules that                           address                                 a   application procedures                                 b   eligibility criteria                                 c   selection criteria                                 d   service conditions, which at a minimum shall include professional service in an                           underserved area for a minimum period of time by any person receiving a scholarship or loan                           repayment assistance                                 e   penalties for failure to comply with service conditions or other terms of a                           scholarship or loan repayment contract                                 f   criteria for modifying or waiving service conditions or penalties in case of extreme                           hardship or other good cause  and                                g   administration of contracts entered into before the effective date of this act,                           between the department and scholarship or loan repayment recipients under Title   , Chapter  ,                           Part  , Physicians and Physician Assistants Grant and Scholarship Program, Title   , Chapter                            d, Nurse Education Financial Assistance, or Title   , Chapter  e, Special Population Health                           Care Provider Financial Assistance and Retention Act.                                    The department shall seek and consider the recommendations of the Utah Health                           Care Workforce Financial Assistance Program Advisory Committee created under Section                                          as it develops and modifies rules to administer the program.                                    Funding for the program shall be a line item within the appropriations act, shall be                           nonlapsing unless designated otherwise, and may be used to cover administrative costs of the                           program, including reimbursement expenses of the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial                           Assistance Program Advisory Committee created under Section                   . , and publicity for                          the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance Program to increase recruitment and                          outreach to persons in the health care professions.                                    Loan repayments and payments resulting from breach of contract are dedicated                           credits to the program.                                    The department shall prepare an annual report on the revenues, expenditures, and                              outcomes of the program.                               Section  . An appointment to the committee shall be for a four year term unless the member is                            appointed to complete an unexpired term.  The executive director may also adjust the length of                            term at the time of appointment or reappointment so that approximately     the committee is                               appointed every two years.  The executive director shall annually appoint a committee chair                            from among the members of the committee.                                     The committee shall meet at the call of the chair, at least three members of the                            committee, or the executive director, but no less frequently than once each calendar year.                                     A majority of the members of the committee constitutes a quorum.  The action of a                            majority of a quorum constitutes the action of the committee.                                     Members of the committee may not receive compensation for their work associated                            with the committee, but may receive from the department reimbursement for travel expenses                            incurred as a member of the committee, as funds are available, at the rates established by the                            Division of Finance under Section       A          .  Members of the committee may decline                            reimbursement.                                     The committee shall                                  a   make recommendations to the department for the development and modification of                            rules to administer the Utah Health Care Workforce Financial Assistance Program  and                                 b   advise the department on the development of a needs assessment tool for                            identifying underserved areas.                                     As funding permits, the department shall provide staff and other administrative                            support to the committee.                                Section  .
