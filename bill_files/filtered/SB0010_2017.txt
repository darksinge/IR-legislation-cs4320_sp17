The commission may appoint by majority vote of the entire commission up to five       pro tempore members, representing              a   state or local government agencies              b   interest groups concerned with veterans issues  or             c   the general public.                 a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed       under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the commission.             b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House       of Representatives appointed under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the commission.                 A majority of the members of the commission shall constitute a quorum.  The       action of a majority of a quorum constitutes the action of the commission.                 The term for each pro tempore member appointed in accordance with Subsection           shall be two years from the date of appointment.  A pro tempore member may not serve       more than three terms.                 If a member leaves office or is unable to serve, the vacancy shall be filled as it was       originally appointed.  A person appointed to fill a vacancy under this section does not serve the       remaining unexpired term of the member being replaced but begins serving a new term.
