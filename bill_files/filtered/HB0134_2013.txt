PARENTAL NOTIFICATION RELATED TO STUDENT                           SAFETY                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Gage Froerer                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires parental notification of certain safety threats to a parent s student.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a school to notify a parent                                    .     if the parent s student threatens to commit suicide  or                                   .    of an alleged incident of bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, hazing, or                           retaliation involving the parent s student  and                               .    requires a school to have a parent sign a statement acknowledging that the parent                           was notified of the suicide threat or bullying. Section  .    Section      A   a         is enacted to read                                   A   a    .  Parental notification of certain incidents required.                                     For purposes of this section,  parent  includes a student s guardian.                                     A school shall notify a parent                                  a   if the parent s student threatens to commit suicide  or                                 b   of an alleged incident of bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, hazing, or retaliation                           involving the parent s student.                                     If a school notifies a parent of an incident or threat required to be reported under                           Subsection    , the school shall require the parent to sign a statement acknowledging that the                           parent was notified of the incident or threat.                                     The school shall maintain a copy of a parental statement described in Subsection                               for at least four years.                                     At the request of a parent, a school may provide information and make                              recommendations related to an incident or threat described in Subsection    . Bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, hazing, and retaliation                            policy.                                     On or before September  ,     , each school board shall adopt a bullying,                            cyber bullying, harassment, and hazing policy consistent with this chapter.                                     The policy shall                                  a   be developed only with input from                                  i   students                                  ii   parents                                  iii   teachers                                  iv   school administrators                                  v   school staff  or                                 vi   local law enforcement agencies  and                                 b   provide protection to a student, regardless of the student s legal status.                                     The policy shall include the following components                                  a   definitions of bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, and hazing that are consistent                            with this chapter                                  b   language prohibiting bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, and hazing                                  c   language prohibiting retaliation against an individual who reports conduct that is                            prohibited under this chapter   and                                  d   language prohibiting making a false report of bullying, cyber bullying, harassment,                            hazing, or retaliation .   and                                 e   parental notification of an incident of bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, hazing,                           or retaliation involving a parent s student as required in Section       A   a        .                                     A copy of the policy shall be included in student conduct handbooks and employee                            handbooks.                                     A policy may not permit formal disciplinary action that is based solely on an                            anonymous report of bullying, cyber bullying, harassment, hazing, or retaliation.                                     Nothing in this chapter is intended to infringe upon the right of a school employee                            or student to exercise their right of free speech.
