TOURISM AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Sheryl L. Allen                             This act modifies provisions relating to tourism.  The act requires the Division of Travel                          Development to provide the Legislature with a report on coordinated tourist promotion                          activities and to develop a mechanism to measure the economic impact of tourism promotion                          expenditures.  The act provides for an independent annual audit of tourism, recreation,                          cultural, and convention facilities tax funds and a report of the findings to be made to the                           Legislature.  The act makes certain technical changes.
