Section  .  One time appropriation for classroom supplies.                  As used in this section,  classroom teacher  or  teacher  means permanent teacher        positions filled by one teacher or two or more job sharing teachers               a   who are licensed personnel               b   who are paid on the teacher s salary schedule               c   who are hired for an entire contract period  and              d   whose primary function is to provide instructional or a combination of instructional        and counseling services to students in public schools.                  a   The State Board of Education shall distribute money appropriated for teacher        supplies and materials to classroom teachers in school districts, the Utah Schools for the Deaf        and the Blind, and charter schools on the basis of the number of classroom teachers in each        school as compared to the total number of classroom teachers.              b   Teachers shall receive up to the following amounts               i        for a teacher on salary schedule steps one through three teaching in        kindergarten through grade   or preschool handicapped               ii        for a teacher on salary schedule steps one through three teaching in grades          through                  iii        for a teacher on salary schedule step four or higher teaching in kindergarten        through grade   or preschool handicapped  and              iv        for a teacher on salary schedule step four or higher teaching in grades          through   .              c   If the appropriation is not sufficient to provide to each teacher the full amount        allowed under Subsection     b , teachers on salary schedule steps one through three shall        receive the full amount allowed with the remaining money apportioned to all other teachers.                  Teachers shall spend money appropriated for classroom supplies and materials for        school supplies, materials, or field trips under rules adopted by the State Board of Education.
