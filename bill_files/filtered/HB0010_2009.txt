General Description                                This bill addresses the filing and recording of certain information under Title   ,                           Chapter  , Condominium Ownership Act, and Title   , Chapter  a, Community                           Association Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    addresses the contents of an association s notice of lien on a unit                                .    requires an agent for an association to register with the Division of Corporations                           and Commercial Code and provide certain information                                .    requires the filing of a notice of an association s right to claim a lien against a lot                           owner if the association does not record governing documents under Title   ,                           Chapter  a, Community Association Act  and                               .    makes technical changes. Section                is enacted to read                                        .  Registration of agent.                                     If an association of unit owners or management committee appoints an agent to                           receive and collect each owner s share of common expenses, the agent shall register as an agent                           for the association of unit owners or management committee with the Division of Corporations                              and Commercial Code within    days after appointment.                                     Registration as an agent under Subsection     consists of filing a statement with the                           Division of Corporations and Commercial Code including the                                  a   agent s name                                  b   agent s street address                                  c   agent s phone number                                  d   name of the association                                  e   date of the agent s appointment  and                                 f   number of units in the development.                                     If an association of unit owners or management committee replaces an agent or any                           of the information required by Subsection     changes, the agent shall register with the                           Division of Corporations and Commercial Code or update the information within    days after                           the replacement or change.
