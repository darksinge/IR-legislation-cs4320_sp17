Dunnigan                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Insurance Code by amending provisions relating to motor vehicle                           insurance.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that a rental company s owner s or operator s security applies only when                           there is no other valid or collectible insurance                                .    provides that when there is no other valid or collectible insurance for a rental                           vehicle, a rental company is required to meet the minimum requirements for owner s                           or operator s security                                .    clarifies that rental company owner s or operator s security requirements do not                           expand or reduce liability or impair the rental company s right to indemnity,                           contribution, or both  and                               .    makes technical changes.
