INSTRUCTIONAL EXPENSES REQUIREMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Gregory H. Hughes                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill requires school districts to spend a certain portion of General Fund                           expenditures on instructional expenditures.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires each school district to spend at least     of its General Fund expenditures                           on instructional expenditures, with certain exceptions                                .    provides for waivers of the expenditure standards from the State Board of                           Education  and                               .    defines terms.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  A      . , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A      .      is enacted to read                                     A      . .  Instructional expenditures standards.                                    As used in this section                                 a    General Fund expenditures  means expenditures accounted for in a school                          district s General Fund, as defined in the U.S. Department of Education publication,  Financial                          Accounting for Local and State School Systems.                                 b    Instructional expenditures  means expenditures for instruction as defined in the                          U.S. Department of Education publication,  Financial Accounting for Local and State School                          Systems.                                     a   Each school district shall spend at least     of its General Fund expenditures                          on instructional expenditures.                                b   If a school district s instructional expenditures in fiscal year         are less than                              of its General Fund expenditures, the school district shall increase instructional                          expenditures as a percent of General Fund expenditures by two percentage points per year                          beginning in fiscal year        .                                    a   A school district that is unable to meet the expenditure standards established                          under Subsection     may apply to the State Board of Education for a waiver.  The waiver                          request shall specify                                 i   the reasons why the school district is unable to meet the expenditure standards  and                                ii   actions the school district will take to meet the expenditure standards in the                          following year.                                b   The State Board of Education shall grant or deny the waiver request within    days                          of receiving the request.
