DISCLOSURE OF HIGHER EDUCATION                           COSTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Ron Bigelow                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the State System of Higher Education by requiring disclosure to                           students of certain dollar amounts relating to the full cost of instruction.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires each institution within the state system of higher education to plainly                           disclose to its students the full cost of instruction, the portion of that amount                           collected from student tuition and fees, and the difference that is paid by state tax                           dollars and other monies.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  B      , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      B           is enacted to read                                  B      .  Higher education cost disclosure.                                       Each institution within the state system of higher education shall, at the time of                          registration, plainly disclose to all of its students the following amounts, in dollar figures for a                          full time equivalent student                                 a   the full cost of instruction                                 b   the amount collected from student tuition and fees  and                                c   the difference between the amounts described under Subsections     a  and  b .                                    The disclosure under Subsection     c  shall also clearly indicate that this balance                          was paid by state tax dollars and other monies.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This bill takes effect on July  ,     .      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM   A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
