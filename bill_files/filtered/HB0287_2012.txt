Sumsion                                Senate Sponsor             Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill makes minor adjustments to State Board of Education district boundaries to                           reconcile United States Census data with state maps and to resolve certain election                           administration issues.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    makes changes to State Board of Education district boundaries to reconcile United                           States Census data with state maps                                .    makes changes to State Board of Education district boundaries to resolve certain                           election administration issues  and                               .    makes technical corrections.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.
