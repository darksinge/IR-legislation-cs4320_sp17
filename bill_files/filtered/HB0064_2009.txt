General Description                                This bill authorizes the Department of Public Safety to administer and coordinate the                           operation of a multi agency strike force to combat violent and other major crimes                           associated with illegal immigration and human trafficking. Multi agency strike force to combat violent and other major crimes                           associated with illegal immigration and human trafficking.                                    The Department of Public Safety is authorized to administer and coordinate the                          operation of a multi agency strike force to combat violent and other major crimes associated                          with illegal immigration and human trafficking within the state.                                    The department shall invite officers of the U.S.  Immigration and Customs                          Enforcement and state and local law enforcement personnel to participate in this mutually                          supportive, multi agency strike force to more effectively utilize their combined skills,                          expertise, and resources.                                    The strike force shall focus its efforts on detecting, investigating, deterring, and                          eradicating violent and other major criminal activity related to illegal immigration and human                          trafficking.                                    The strike force shall make an annual report on its activities to the governor and the                          Legislature s Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Interim Committee by December  ,                          together with any proposed recommendations for modifications to this section.
