Urquhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Criminal Code regarding penalties for repeat lewdness offenses.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    increases the penalty for lewdness if the offender has been convicted twice before of                           lewdness or lewdness involving a child  and                               .    increases the penalty for lewdness involving a child if the offender has been                           convicted once before of lewdness involving a child.
