EMERGENCY RESPONDER PRIORITY                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Kory M. Holdaway                             This act amends provisions related to the Interlocal Cooperation Act and the Utah                          Emergency Medical Services System Act.  The act requires providers of police, fire,                          emergency medical technicians, and paramedic services to enter into agreements to facilitate                          the closest responder regardless of geographic or political boundaries.  The act limits the                          application of closest responder to counties of the first class.
