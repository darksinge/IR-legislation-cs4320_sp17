REGULATION OF CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY                                GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor   Larry B. Wiley                            Senate Sponsor                                                                           LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies Title   , Chapter   , Utah Construction Trades Licensing Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines  building inspector                                 .    clarifies the owner builder exemption from licensing                                .    allows a building inspector to issue a citation for a violation of Title   , Chapter   ,                           Utah Construction Trades Licensing Act                                .    provides that a fine based on a citation issued by a building inspector is generally                           collected by the entity for which the building inspector performs an inspection                                .    requires a political subdivision to notify the Division of Occupational and                           Professional Licensing of the outcome of any citation issued by the political                           subdivision  and                               .    makes technical changes.
