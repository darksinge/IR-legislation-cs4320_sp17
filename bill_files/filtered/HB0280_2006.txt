GOLF CARTS ALLOWED ON CERTAIN                           STREETS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Carol Spackman Moss                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by authorizing golf carts to be operated on                           certain highways.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines  golf cart                                 .    authorizes a municipality or county to                                    .    allow golf carts to be operated on specified highways under its jurisdiction  and                                   .    require the owner to pay a fee and be issued a permit for the golf cart                                .    prohibits the operation of a golf cart on a highway with a posted speed limit of more                           than    miles per hour                                .    provides that a golf cart, when operated on a highway, is considered a motor vehicle                           for purposes of traffic rules, driver licensing, insurance, and taxation with certain                           exceptions                                .    requires a golf cart operated on a highway to be equipped with stop lamps and                           brakes and with head lamps if used during hours of darkness                                .    exempts golf carts operated on the highway from vehicle registration, safety and                           emissions inspections, vehicle identification numbers, and odometer requirements                                .    provides penalties  and                                  .    makes technical changes.
