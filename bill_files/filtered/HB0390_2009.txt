ABSENTEE BALLOT AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   John G. Mathis                                Senate Sponsor             Kevin T. Van Tassell                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Election Code to amend the date that absentee ballots may be                           applied for and cast in person at the office of the election officer.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires that applications for absentee ballots be applied for no later than the Friday                           before the election date, regardless of whether the absentee ballot will be voted by                           mail or in person                                .    requires that in person absentee ballots be cast no later than the Friday before the                           election date  and                               .    makes technical changes.
