Section   .  Section           is amended to read                       .  Divulging investigative information    False reports prohibited.                  Except as otherwise provided by this chapter, a licensee , associate, registrant, or         apprentice of a licensee  may not divulge or release to anyone other than his client or employer the         contents of an investigative file acquired in the course of licensed investigative activity.  However,         the board shall have access to investigative files if the client for whom the information was         acquired, or his lawful representative, alleges a violation of this chapter by the licensee  or a          registrant, the licensed agency or any employee  or if the prior written consent of the client to          divulge or release the information has been obtained.
