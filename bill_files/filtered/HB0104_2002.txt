REDISTRICTING PROCEDURES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Richard M. Siddoway                             This act creates a new redistricting procedure.  This act requires that the Office of                          Legislative Research and General Counsel prepare redistricting plans and defines the                          process for presenting those redistricting plans to the Legislature for consideration and                          voting.  This act describes standards to be followed by the Office of Legislative Research and                          General Counsel, to the extent practicable, in drawing up redistricting plans.
