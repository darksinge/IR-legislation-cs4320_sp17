PAYMENTS ON CREDIT CONTRACTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Kevin S. Garn          AN ACT RELATING TO FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS  PERMITTING CREDITORS TO         CONTRACT FOR FEES OR SERVICE CHARGES FOR DISHONORED INSTRUMENT.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                     , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section         is enacted to read                      .  Terms regarding dishonored instruments in contracts for the extension of           credit.                   For purposes of this section  extension of credit  means the right to defer payment of          debt or to incur debt and defer its payment.                   a   A contract for the extension of credit may provide for the payment of any fee or          service charge for a check, draft, order, or other instrument received in payment on the contract          for the extension of credit if the check, draft, order, or other instrument is dishonored.               b   Subsection           does not apply to a contract described in Subsection     a .                   If in accordance with Subsection           a holder of a dishonored check, draft, order,          or other instrument provides notice of an action for payment under a contract described in          Subsection     a , the written notice shall state the fee or service charge due under the contract          described in Subsection     a .      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
