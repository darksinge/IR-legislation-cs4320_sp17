Enrolled                          YOUTH MENTORING LEADERSHIP SUMMIT                              GENERAL SESSION                      STATE OF UTAH                       Sponsor   Beverly Ann Evans                       AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS  APPROPRIATING    ,    FROM THE                      GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR           TO THE DIVISION OF BUSINESS AND                      ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT IN THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC                      DEVELOPMENT TO DISTRIBUTE TO SOUTHERN UTAH UNIVERSITY TO DEVELOP A                      YOUTH LEADERSHIP SUMMIT AND TRAINING PROGRAM  PROVIDING THAT THE                      FUNDS BE NONLAPSING  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.                      This act enacts uncodified material.                     Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                           Section  .   Legislative intent.                          The Legislature finds that government within the State of Utah is improved by developing                     leadership qualities and a greater understanding of state and local government processes within                     youth.  It is the intent of the Legislature in appropriating these funds to help meet the statewide                     purpose of developing youth leadership qualities and increasing understanding of state and local                     government processes through conducting a Youth Leadership Summit including workshops and                     speakers.                          Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,    ,    to the                     Division of Business and Economic Development in the Department of Community and Economic                     Development for development of a youth leadership summit and training program.                              a   This appropriation shall be distributed to Southern Utah University for use in                     developing a youth leadership summit and training program.                          b   The objective of the youth leadership summit and training program is to develop                     leadership qualities in participating youth by                           i   providing mentors and insight into the political process through involvement with state                     and local elected officials                            ii   providing a working knowledge of important state and local issues  and                          iii   fostering an interest in public service.                              The money appropriated in Subsection     is nonlapsing.                          Section  .   Effective date.                          This act takes effect on July  ,     .
