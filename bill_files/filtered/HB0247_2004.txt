TASK FORCE STUDYING WATER ISSUES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Michael R. Styler                             James R. GowansDavid Ure                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the Water Issues Task Force and provides for task force membership,                           purposes, and compensation.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Water Issues Task Force                                .    provides for membership of the task force and compensation for members                                .    specifies duties and responsibilities of the task force  and                               .    specifies issues that the task force will review.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates                                .        ,    from the General Fund for fiscal year         for a two year task force.                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed November   ,     .                           Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Water Issues Task Force    Creation    Membership    Interim rules                           followed    Compensation    Staff.                                       There is created the Water Issues Task Force consisting of the following members                                 a   five members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          four of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   eight members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives, no more than six of whom may be from the same political party.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed                          under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force.                                    In conducting its business, the task force shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees.                                    Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force shall be paid in accordance                          with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the task force.                               Section  .   Duties    Interim report.                                    The task force shall review and make recommendations on the following issues                                 a   ground water management                                 b   water right enforcement and penalties                                 c   title for water rights                                 d   administration of ground water and surface water                                 e   instream flow  and                                f   water reuse.                                    A final report, including any proposed legislation shall be presented before                          November   ,     , to the Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim                          Committee.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        , to pay for the                          two year Task Force Studying Water Issues                                        ,    to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the                          task force                                           ,    to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses                          of representatives on the task force  and                                        ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for                          staffing the task force.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This bill is repealed November   ,     .
