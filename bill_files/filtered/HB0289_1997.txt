COUGAR AND BEAR DEPREDATION LOSS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Beverly Ann Evans          AN ACT RELATING TO WILDLIFE DAMAGE COMPENSATION  ALLOWING         COMPENSATION FOR THE FULL FAIR MARKET VALUE OF LIVESTOCK DAMAGED         BY COUGAR AND BEAR  PROVIDING AN EXCEPTION  APPROPRIATING     ,            FROM THE GENERAL FUND FOR COMPENSATION  AND PROVIDING AN         EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                       , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section          is amended to read                       .  Procedure to obtain compensation for livestock damage done by bear or           mountain lion.                   As used in this section                a    Damage  means injury or loss to livestock.               b    Division  means the Division of Wildlife Resources.               c    Livestock  means  calves  cattle, sheep,  or lambs  or turkeys.                   When livestock are damaged by a bear or mountain lion, the owner may receive          compensation for      of  the fair market value of the damage.  To obtain this compensation, the          owner of the damaged livestock shall notify the division of the damage as soon as possible, but          no later than four days after the damage is discovered.  This notification is required each time any          damage is discovered.                   Proof of loss forms, provided by the division, shall be filed no later than    days after          the original notification of damage was given to the division by the claimant.                    a   The division, with the assistance of the Department of Agriculture in making the         determination, shall either accept or deny the claim for damages within    days after the proof of         loss form is filed.   The division shall pay all accepted claims to the extent of money appropriated         by the Legislature for this purpose, except money appropriated from the Wildlife Resources         Account may be used to provide compensation for only up to     of the fair market value of any         damaged livestock.              b   The division  shall  may not pay mountain lion and bear damage claims to livestock         owners who have failed to file with the commissioner of agriculture their completed livestock form         and appropriate fee as outlined in Section        for the immediately preceding and current year.               c   If the division denies a claim, except as provided in Subsection     b , the claimant          may appeal the decision to a panel consisting of one person selected by the claimant, one person          selected by the division, and a third person selected by the first two panel members.  The panel          shall decide whether the division should pay all or a portion of the claim.                   The Wildlife Board is authorized, subject to Title   , Chapter   a,  the  Utah          Administrative Rulemaking Act, to make and enforce rules to administer and enforce this section.              Section  .   Appropriation.              There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        ,     ,    to the          Division of Wildlife Resources to compensate livestock owners for livestock damaged by cougar          and bear.              Section  .   Effective date.              This act takes effect on July  ,     .
