LOCAL SCHOOL BOARD BUSINESS ADMINISTRATOR                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Daniel McCay                                Senate Sponsor             Howard A. Stephenson                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill makes changes to certain procedures related to hiring a business administrator                           of a school district.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    prohibits a local school board from                                    .    appointing a business administrator during an interim vacancy period  and                                   .    entering into a contract on or after May  ,     , that contains an automatic                           renewal provision with a business administrator  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  A      , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                   Section  .    Section      A           is amended to read                                   A      .  Business administrator    Term    Oath.                                     Each  Subject to Subsection    , a local school board shall appoint a business                           administrator.                                    a   The business administrator s term of office is for two years and until, subject to                          Subsection    , a successor is appointed and qualified.                                b   A local school board that appoints a business administrator in accordance with this                          section may not, on or after May  ,     , enter into an employment contract that contains an                          automatic renewal provision with the business administrator.                                     If  Unless a vacancy occurs during an interim vacancy period subject to Subsection                             , if it becomes necessary to appoint an interim business manager due to a vacancy in the                           office of business administrator, then the local school board shall make an appointment during                           a public meeting for an indefinite term not to exceed one year, which term shall end upon the                           appointment and qualification of a new business manager.                                    The business administrator qualifies for office by taking the constitutional oath of                           office.                                    a   As used in this Subsection    ,  interim vacancy period  means the period of                          time that                                 i   begins on the day on which a general election described in Section       A           is                          held to elect a member of a local school board  and                                ii   ends on the day on which the member elect begins the member s term.                                b   i   A local school board may not appoint a business administrator during an interim                          vacancy period.                                ii   Notwithstanding Subsection     b  i                                  A   the local school board may appoint an interim business administrator during an                          interim vacancy period  and                                B   the interim business administrator s term shall expire once a new business                          administrator is appointed by the new local school board after the interim vacancy period has                          ended.                                c   Subsection     b  does not apply if all the local school board members who held                          office on the day of the general election whose term of office was vacant for the election are                             reelected to the local school board for the following term.
