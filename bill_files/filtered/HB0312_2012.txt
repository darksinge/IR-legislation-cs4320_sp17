Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms  and                               .    enacts nonrefundable corporate and individual income tax credits for employing a                           recently deployed veteran who is eligible to collect or has recently exhausted                           unemployment benefits.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill has retrospective operation for a taxable year beginning on or after January  ,                               .
