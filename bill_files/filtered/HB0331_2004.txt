CHOICE IN HEALTH CARE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Katherine M. Bryson                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Unfair Practices Act to prohibit unfair competition by a health                          insurer who owns hospitals and employs health care providers in certain markets.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    establishes what constitutes unfair competition                                .    establishes standing to enforce the act  and                               .    provides for remedies for a violation of the act.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                       , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is enacted to read                                       .  Unfair competition between insurers, health care providers, and                           hospitals.                                       For purposes of this section                                 a    Affiliated company  means a person or group of persons who own a controlling                          interest in another person.                                b    Health care provider  means a physician licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Utah                          Medical Practice Act, or an osteopathic physician licensed under Title   , Chapter   , Utah                          Osteopathic Medical Practice Act.                                c    Hospital  means a general acute hospital licensed under Title   , Chapter   ,                          Health Care Facility Licensing and Inspection Act.                                d    Insurer  means an entity offering accident and health insurance, or health care                          services through a health maintenance organization regulated under                                 i   Title   A, Insurance Code  or                                ii   the federal Employee Retirement Income Security Act.                                e    Market area  means the service area designated for a health maintenance                          organization pursuant to Title  A, Chapter  , Health Maintenance Organizations and Limited                          Health Plans, or the geographic area in which an accident and health insurance plan is offered.                                    a   It is a violation of this chapter for a person to own a controlling interest in an                          affiliated company that                                 i   is an insurer in a market area                                 ii   owns more than     of the available licensed hospital beds in the market area in                          which the insurer offers its product  and                                iii   employs more than     of the health care providers who practice in the health                          care market.                                b   The Legislature finds that a violation of Subsection     a  impairs or prevents fair                          competition, injures the public welfare, and is unfair competition contrary to public policy and                          the policy of this chapter.                                    In addition to any other person with standing to bring an action to enforce the                          provisions of this chapter under Section                , the following health care providers have                          standing to bring an action to enforce this section and the provisions of this chapter                                 a   a health care provider who is not a contracted health care provider for an insurer                          described in Subsection     a  i   and                                b   a health care provider who is not credentialed at a hospital described in Subsection                                 a  ii .                                    In addition to any other remedies provided by this chapter, a court may require the                          person charged with a violation of Subsection     to divest itself of its ownership in affiliated                          companies, such as                                 a   the insurer                                 b   a percentage of the hospitals in the market area  or                                c   the affiliated company that employs health care providers in the market area.
