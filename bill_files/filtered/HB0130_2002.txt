VETERANS  HOMES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Gerry A. Adair                             This act modifies the Veterans Code to provide for the building of one or more veterans                           homes across the state.  The act provides for boards for each home and makes technical                          changes to language to accommodate more than one home within the state.  In addition, this                          act appropriates   ,   ,    from the General Fund as the state s match to build a new                          veterans  nursing home in Weber County.  This act takes effect July  ,     . Utah Veterans  Nursing Home Expendable Trust Fund.                                        There is created the Utah Veterans  Nursing Home Expendable Trust Fund to be                            administered by the division for the benefit of  the  each home and its residents.                                     All cash donations, gifts, or bequests shall be deposited in the trust fund and used                            according to the wishes of the donor.                                     All funds received by the  home  homes from federal or state agencies, individual                            insurance reimbursement, or cash payments shall be deposited  in  into the trust fund.                                Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                         .  Disposition of deceased resident s property.                                       All money or other personal property of a resident held by  the  a home which is left                            on the premises of the home shall, upon the death of the resident, be held in trust to be paid or                            delivered to the spouse, children, grandchildren, or parent of the resident upon the presentation of                            proof of relationship.  Any funds of a deceased resident may be disbursed for the payment of                            funeral expenses or any obligation owed to the home.
