Bramble                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions related to deferred deposit loans.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the reporting requirements for deferred deposit lenders                                .    imposes additional requirements before the extension of a deferred deposit loan                                .    prohibits a deferred deposit contract from modifying statutory venue provisions                                .    requires notice before initiating a civil action                                .    modifies provisions related to extended payment plans  and                               .    makes technical and conforming amendments.
