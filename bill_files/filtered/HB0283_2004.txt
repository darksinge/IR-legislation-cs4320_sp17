DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE AND FOOD                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  David Ure                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Agriculture Code by amending provisions relating to weights and                           measures, licensing fees, and the sale of raw milk.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends conditions under which raw milk may be sold                                .    requires food establishments and entities using weights and measures in commerce                           or trade to be licensed by the Department of Agriculture and Food                                .    enacts conditions for licensure and allows the Department of Agriculture and Food                           to charge licensure fees                                .    designates information regarding food security assessments as a protected record                           under the Government Records Access and Management Act  and                               .    makes technical corrections.
