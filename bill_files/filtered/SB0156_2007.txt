Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows the Public Service Commission to determine by rule what                           telecommunications devices will be distributed to a hearing impaired person                                .    allows distribution of telecommunications devices to customers of wireless                           telecommunications providers  and                               .    makes technical changes.
