RETIRED VOLUNTEER HEALTH CARE                           PRACTITIONER ACT                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Melvin R. Brown                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill enacts the Retired Volunteer Health Care Practitioner Act in the Occupations                           and Professions Code.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    establishes eligibility for a volunteer health care practitioner license                                .    provides a waiver of fees for licensing of a volunteer health care practitioner                                 .    limits the practice of a volunteer health care practitioner to exclusive charity care at                           charity locations  and                               .    requires supervision of a volunteer health care practitioner.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                         , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                                , Utah Code Annotated                                             , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  RETIRED VOLUNTEER HEALTH CARE PRACTITIONER ACT                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Retired Volunteer Health Care Practitioner Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               For purposes of this chapter                                      Board  means the state licensing board created for each of the health care                          practitioners included in Subsection    .                                     Health care practitioner  includes                                 a   a physical therapist licensed under Chapter   a, Physical Therapist Practice Act                                 b   a nurse or advanced practice registered nurse licensed under Chapter   b, Nurse                          Practice Act                                 c   a nurse midwife licensed under Chapter   a, Nurse Midwife Practice Act                                 d   a mental health professional licensed under Chapter   , Mental Health Professional                          Practice Act                                 e   a physician licensed under Chapter   , Utah Medical Practice Act                                 f   an osteopath licensed under Chapter   , Utah Osteopathic Medical Practice Act                                 g   a dentist or dental hygienist licensed under Chapter   , Dentist and Dental                          Hygienist Practice Act  or                                h   a physician assistant licensed under Chapter   a, Physician Assistant Act.                                     Qualified location  means                                 a   a clinic, hospital, church, or organization whose primary purposes is to sponsor,                          promote, or organize uncompensated health care services for people unable to pay for health                          care services  and                                b   is a location approved by the division.                                     Remuneration or compensation  is as defined in Section                .                                        Supervising professional  means a health care practitioner                                 a   who has an active license in the state in good standing                                 b   with a scope of practice that is appropriate for supervising the applicant as                          determined by the board  and                                c   who is practicing at the qualified location.                                     Volunteer  means the individual health care practitioner                                 a   will devote the health care practitioner s practice exclusively to providing medical                          care to the needy and indigent in the state                                 b   will agree to donate at least    hours of professional services in a calendar year in a                          qualified location  and                                c   will not receive remuneration or compensation for the health care practitioner s                          services.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Eligibility for volunteer health care practitioner license.                               A health care practitioner is eligible to apply to the board for a volunteer health care                          practitioner license if the health care practitioner                                     certifies to the board that the applicant will be engaged exclusively in volunteer                          health care services  and                                    completes an application for a volunteer health care practitioner license, which                          includes documentation                                 a   of professional education and graduation                                 b   of practice history                                 c   of a qualified location for which the health care practitioner will be practicing                                 d   of a supervising health care practitioner and the supervising heath care practitioner s                          agreement to accept supervising authority  and                                e   that the applicant has                                 i   previously been issued an unrestricted license to practice in Utah or another state of                          the United States                                 ii   never been the subject of any significant disciplinary action in any jurisdiction  and                                iii   is in good health and is not currently undergoing treatment for any physical or                          mental condition which would impair the health care practitioner s ability to practice with                             reasonable skill and safety to patients.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Volunteer health care practitioner license    Waiver of fees                              Rulemaking authority.                                    The division shall issue a volunteer health care practitioner license to an applicant                          who                                 a   completes an application under Section                    and                                b   meets the requirements of this chapter.                                    A license issued under this chapter shall                                 a   comply with the scope of practice for a similarly situated licensed practitioner,                           except that the license shall limit the health care practitioner s practice to the qualified location                                  b   permit the health care practitioner to apply for a controlled substance registration as                           permitted for other similarly situated licensed practitioners  and                                 c   state that the license is a volunteer health care practitioner license and is subject to                           the restriction of Subsection     a .                                     The division and board shall waive all fees, including application fees, examination                           fees, and annual re registration fees for an applicant who applies for a volunteer health care                           practitioner license and who otherwise meets licensure requirements.                                     The division and a board may adopt rules in accordance with Title   G, Chapter  ,                           Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, to                                  a   approve the charity locations that qualify under Section                                                    b   designate standards for proof of supervision and the terms of supervision under                           Section                    and                                 c   implement the provisions of this chapter.                                     A health care practitioner who is licensed as a volunteer health care practitioner                           under this chapter is entitled to qualified immunity for charity care if the health care                           practitioner meets the requirements of Section                .
