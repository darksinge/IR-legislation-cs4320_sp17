APPROPRIATION FOR CHASE HOME IN                           LIBERTY PARK                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   David M. Jones                           AN ACT RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS  APPROPRIATING     ,    FROM THE                          GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR           TO THE UTAH ARTS COUNCIL IN THE                          DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FOR RENOVATION                          OF THE CHASE HOME MUSEUM OF UTAH FOLK ART  AND PROVIDING AN                           EFFECTIVE DATE.                           This act enacts uncodified material.                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Intent.                               The Legislature finds that because the     year old Brigham Young   Issac Chase Home                          in Liberty Park houses the State Folk Art Collection and plays a significant role in Utah s cultural                          heritage, appropriating funds for the renovation of the home meets a statewide public purpose.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                                    Except as provided in H.B.   , Appropriations Coordination Act, there is appropriated                          from the General Fund for fiscal year          ,     ,    to the Utah Arts Council in the                          Department of Community and Economic Development.                                   The Utah Arts Council shall use the appropriated funds to                                a   match other funds donated for the purpose of completing the exterior renovation of the                          home, with the appropriated funds paying for no more than     of the total renovation costs  and                               b   secure a    year lease to ensure a permanent public home for the State Folk Art                          Collection.                                   Any unused funds shall be returned to the General Fund.                               Section  .   Effective date.                                  This act takes effect on July  ,     .
