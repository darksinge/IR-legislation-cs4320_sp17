IDENTIFICATION NUMBER FRAUD                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Chad E. Bennion                           A. Lamont Tyler                               AN ACT RELATING TO COMMERCE AND TRADE AND THE CRIMINAL CODE                           DEFINING AN IDENTIFICATION NUMBER  CREATING THE CRIME OF                          IDENTIFICATION NUMBER FRAUD  AND PROVIDING A PENALTY.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            ENACTS                                       , Utah Code Annotated                                            , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is enacted to read                                       .  Identification number defined    Use by others.                                     Identification number  means a series of numbers, letters, symbols, a combination of                          them, or any other information used to identify a person, account, business, agency, organization,                          or transaction, which includes                                 a   Social Security numbers                                 b   driver s license numbers                                 c   insurance, bank, or financial institution account numbers                                 d   financial transaction card numbers                                 e   personal identification codes                                 f   personal identification numbers                                 g   digital signatures  and                                h   any other number or information which can be used to access a person s personal                          information or financial resources.                                       A person s identification number or numbers may only be used by another person,                          agency, business, or organization with the person s consent.                               Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  Identification number fraud.                                    As used in this section,  identification number  shall have the same meaning as in                          Section                .                                     A person is guilty of identification number fraud if, not amounting to a violation of                          another provision of law that carries a greater penalty, that person, with intent to defraud or                          deceive, possesses, acquires, uses, or divulges the identification number of another.                                    Identification number fraud is                                 a   a second degree felony if the identification number is used and the value of the                          property, money, or thing obtained or sought to be obtained is or exceeds   ,     otherwise                                b   a third degree felony.                                    Multiple violations within a    day period may be aggregated into a single offense, and                          the degree of the offense is determined by the total value of all property, money, or things obtained                          or sought to be obtained through the multiple violations.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
