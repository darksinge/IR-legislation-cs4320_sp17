Pace                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Alcoholic Beverage Control Act to address unlawful acts by                           minors.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    makes it unlawful for a minor to have any measurable blood, breath, or urine                           alcohol concentration                                .    addresses consumption for medicinal or religious purposes  and                               .    makes technical changes.
