Nelson      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies the Utah Criminal Code regarding sexual offenses against minors.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   adds two additional elements to the class B offense of unlawful sexual activity with       a minor                   provides that the offense of sexual abuse of a minor is a class A misdemeanor if the       defendant cannot establish specified elements  and                  modifies the definition of  position of trust.
