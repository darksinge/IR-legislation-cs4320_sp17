MIDTERM VACANCY AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   John Dougall                                Senate Sponsor            John L. Valentine                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends provisions relating to filling a midterm vacancy in an elected office in                           a municipality or on a local school board.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a municipality and local school board to interview potential appointees in a                           public meeting                                .    prohibits the discussion of filling a midterm vacancy in a closed meeting  and                               .    makes technical changes.
