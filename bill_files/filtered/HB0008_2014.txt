LONG TITLE                           Committee Note                                The Executive Appropriations Committee recommended this bill.                            General Description                                This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and                           operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July  ,      and ending June   ,     .                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides budget increases and decreases for the use and support of certain state agencies                           and institutions of higher education                                .    authorizes certain state agency fees                                .    authorizes internal service fund rates                                .    adjusts funding for the impact of Internal Service Fund rate changes                                .    provides budget increases and decreases for other purposes as described.
