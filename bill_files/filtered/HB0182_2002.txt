TAX REVIEW COMMISSION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Chad E. Bennion                             This act amends the Revenue and Taxation Code to repeal obsolete language relating to the                          duties of the Tax Review Commission and makes technical changes.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           AMENDS                                       , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Duties.                                      The  purpose of the  review commission  is to  shall                                 a   establish an ongoing and comprehensive review of                                 i   the tax laws of this state and  its  the political subdivisions of this state  and  of                                 ii   all issues related to revenue and taxation  and  to                                 b    make recommendations to the governor and the Legislature  as to  on                                 i    specific tax issues  and                                ii   tax policy of the state and  its  the political subdivisions.                                    The review commission , upon request,  may advise the governor, the Legislature, and                           political subdivisions on any proposed change of tax laws or tax policy.                                     The review commission shall study the taxation and regulation of the                          telecommunications industry and make a preliminary report to the governor, the Revenue and                          Taxation Interim Committee, and the Information Technology Commission on or before the                               November interim meeting.         Legislative Review Note      as of                 AM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
