Section   B       is enacted to read               B      . Powdered alcohol.                 As used in this section,  powdered alcohol  means a product that is in a powdered       or crystalline form and contains any amount of alcohol.                 It is unlawful for a person to use, offer for use, purchase, offer to purchase, sell,       offer to sell, furnish, or possess powdered alcohol for human consumption.                 It is unlawful for a holder of a retail license to use powdered alcohol as an alcoholic       product.
