Study regarding entities that may file an application for instream flow.                 Utah Code Section         currently contains a restriction on which entities may       file an application for an instream flow    namely, the Division of Wildlife Resources, the       Division of Parks and Recreation, and certain nonprofit fishing groups.                 Public water suppliers, which are defined in Section       , are currently not able       to apply for an instream flow.                 Some public water suppliers have expressed an interest in exploring the possibility       of expanding the list of entities that can apply for an instream flow.                 This issue is very complex, and formulating a workable alternative to the current       system will require the thoughtful participation of a number of stakeholders.                 The Legislature encourages the Water Development Commission and the Executive       Water Task Force to study the possible options for expanding the potential groups that may file       an application for an instream flow.                 The commission and task force should present their findings and conclusions and       prepare a recommendation, with a conceptual outline of any suggested legislation, to the       Legislature before the      General Session.
