NONRESIDENT TUITION WAIVER FOR                           NATIONAL GUARD MEMBERS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Brad King                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the State System of Higher Education Code by creating a tuition                           waiver for certain Utah National Guard members.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires state institutions of higher education to waive the nonresident portion of                           tuition for each nonresident undergraduate Utah National Guard member who                           performed active duty service under a call by the governor.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  B  e    , Utah Code Annotated                                      B  e    , Utah Code Annotated                                      B  e    , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      B  e         is enacted to read                               CHAPTER  e.  UTAH NATIONAL GUARD MEMBERS  TUITION ACT                                  B  e    .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Utah National Guard Members  Tuition Act.                                Section  .    Section      B  e         is enacted to read                                  B  e    .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter,  Utah National Guard member  means a member of the Utah                          National Guard who performed active duty service under a call by the governor.                               Section  .    Section      B  e         is enacted to read                                  B  e    .  Tuition waivers for Utah National Guard members.                               Beginning in the         academic year, a state institution of higher education shall                          waive the nonresident portion of total tuition charged by public institutions of higher education                          to each nonresident undergraduate Utah National Guard member who                                     is admitted as a full time, part time, or summer school student in an undergraduate                          program of study leading to a degree or certificate  and                                    submits verification that the student qualifies for the waiver.                               Section  .   Effective date.                               This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
