SPOUSE ABUSE AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Gerry A. Adair                             This act modifies the Code of Criminal Procedure to require that domestic violence offenders                          be required to attend and satisfactorily complete a domestic violence treatment program as                          a part of their sentence.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           AMENDS                                       , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Sentencing    Restricting contact with victim    Electronic monitoring                              Counseling    Cost assessed against defendant.                                    When a defendant is found guilty of a crime and a condition of the sentence restricts                           the defendant s contact with the victim, an order may be issued or, if one has already been issued,                           it may be extended for the length of the defendant s probation.  The order shall be in writing, and                           the prosecutor shall provide a certified copy of that order to the victim.                                    In determining its sentence the court, in addition to penalties otherwise provided by                           law, may require the defendant to participate in    a   an electronic monitoring program, as                           described in Section           .     , in accordance with the provisions of that section   and .                                 b   treatment or therapy in a domestic violence treatment program, as defined in Section                               A          , licensed by the Department of Human Services.                                     The court may also require the defendant to pay all or part of the costs of counseling                           incurred by the victim, as well as the costs for defendant s own counseling.                                    The court shall                                 a   assess against the defendant, as restitution, any costs for services or treatment provided                              to the abused spouse by the Division of Child and Family Services under Section       A  a         .                           The court shall   and                                b   order those costs to be paid directly to the division or its contracted provider.                                    The court shall order the defendant to obtain and satisfactorily complete treatment or                          therapy in a domestic violence treatment program, as defined in Section       A          , that is                          licensed by the Department of Human Services.
