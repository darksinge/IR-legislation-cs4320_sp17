Draxler      Senate Sponsor    Margaret Dayton             LONG TITLE      General Description             This bill modifies provisions related to filing a complaint with the Political       Subdivisions Ethics Review Commission.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   addresses the individual with whom a complainant may file a complaint against a       political subdivision officer or employee.
