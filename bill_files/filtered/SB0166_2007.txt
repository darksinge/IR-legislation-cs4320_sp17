Enrolled                               NOTARY PUBLIC AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ross I. Romero                                House Sponsor            David  Litvack                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill addresses criminal liability of a notary public.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    makes it a class B misdemeanor for a notary public to engage in certain conduct,                           including providing legal advice if the notary is not an attorney  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                       , as repealed and reenacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                           , as repealed and reenacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Prohibited acts    Advertising.                                    A nonattorney notary may not provide advice or counsel to another person                           concerning legal documents or legal proceedings, including immigration matters.                                    a   i   A nonattorney notary who advertises notarial services in any language other                              than  English shall include in the advertisement a notice that the notary public is not an                           attorney.                                ii   The notice under Subsection     a  i  must include the fees that a notary may                           charge pursuant to Section                 and the following statement                                 I AM NOT AN ATTORNEY LICENSED TO PRACTICE LAW IN UTAH AND                           MAY NOT GIVE LEGAL ADVICE ABOUT IMMIGRATION OR ANY OTHER LEGAL                           MATTER OR ACCEPT FEES FOR LEGAL ADVICE.                                 b   i   The notice required by Subsection     a  shall be in English and in the language                           of the advertisement and in letters of a conspicuous size.                                ii   If the advertisement is by radio or television, the statement may be modified, but                           must include substantially the same message.                                c   i   Literal translation of the phrase  Notary Public  into any language other than                           English is prohibited if the literal translation implies that the notary is a licensed attorney.                                ii   In this Subsection     c ,  literal translation  means the translation of a word or                           phrase without regard to the true meaning of the word or phrase in the language that is being                           translated.                               Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Liability.                                    A notary may be liable to any person for any damage to that person proximately                           caused by the notary s misconduct in performing a notarization.                                    a   A surety for a notary s bond may be liable to any person for damages                           proximately caused to that person by the notary s misconduct in performing a notarization, but                           the surety s liability may not exceed the penalty of the bond or of any remaining bond funds                           that have not been expended to other claimants.                                b   Regardless of the number of claimants under Subsection     a , a surety s total                           liability may not exceed the penalty of the bond.                                    It is a class B misdemeanor, if not otherwise a criminal offense under this code, for                                 a   a notary to perform an act in violation of Section              or Section                 or                                   b   the employer of a notary to solicit the notary to perform a notarial act in violation of                           this chapter.
