Walsh          AN ACT RELATING TO COUNTIES  REQUIRING COUNTIES OF THE FIRST CLASS TO         HOLD AN ELECTION TO DECIDE WHETHER TO CHANGE THE COUNTY S FORM OF         GOVERNMENT  ALLOWING THE VOTERS OF OTHER THAN FIRST CLASS         COUNTIES TO REQUIRE, BY INITIATIVE, THE COUNTY TO HOLD AN ELECTION TO          DECIDE WHETHER TO CHANGE THE FORM OF GOVERNMENT  PROVIDING A          PROCESS TO IMPLEMENT A CHANGE IN FORM OF GOVERNMENT  PROVIDING          FOR THE ELECTION OF COUNTY EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE BODY OFFICERS          UNDER THE NEW FORM OF GOVERNMENT  AND ALLOWING VOTERS TO DECIDE          WHETHER CERTAIN COUNTY OFFICERS SHOULD BE ELECTED.
