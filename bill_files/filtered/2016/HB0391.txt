Description            This bill enacts provisions relating to law enforcement on public land and on land to       which the federal government has obtained right or title        Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides for the chief executive officer of a political subdivision or a county sheriff       to determine whether                        the Bureau of Land Management or the United States Department of the Interior       is complying with certain provisions of federal law relating to agreements for       local law enforcement to enforce federal law and regulations on public lands  or                       a federal law enforcement official is exceeding the law enforcement official s       jurisdiction in relation to certain land  and                  addresses legal action to enforce the provisions of the bill        Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             None       Utah Code Sections
