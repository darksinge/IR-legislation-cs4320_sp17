Description            This bill allows an emergency service provider to request a blood sample if       significantly exposed to a person s bodily fluids in the course of performing the       provider s duties        Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   allows an emergency service provider to request a court order authorizing a blood       sample from an individual if  during the course of performing the provider s duties        the provider is significantly exposed to the individual s bodily fluids        Money Appropriated in this Bill
