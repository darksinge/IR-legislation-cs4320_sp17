ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT INCENTIVES FOR                           EMPLOYERS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Eric K. Hutchings                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires the Workforce Services and Community and Economic Development                           Interim Committee to study economic development incentives for employers.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the Workforce Services and Community and Economic Development                           Interim Committee to study economic development incentives for employers in the                           state or seeking to locate in the state  and                               .    prescribes the scope of the study.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed on November   ,     .                           Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .  Study on economic development incentives for employers.                                    During the      interim, the Workforce Services and Community and Economic                             Development Interim Committee shall study economic development incentives for employers                                 a   in the state  or                                b   seeking to locate in the state.                                    The study required by Subsection     shall include                                 a   studying the implementation of state income tax credits or other incentives for an                          employer contribution or expense to train employees, including an employer contribution                          toward a training program offered through the Utah College of Applied Technology                                 b   studying current incentives offered to employers described in Subsection     for                          creating jobs within the state, and determining whether additions or modifications to those                          incentives are needed to foster economic development and job growth in the state  and                                c   evaluating the costs as compared to the benefits of existing or proposed state                          income tax credits or incentives that the Workforce Services and Community and Economic                          Development Interim Committee studies.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This bill is repealed on November   ,     .
