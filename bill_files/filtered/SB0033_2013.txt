Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    enacts a sales and use tax exemption for certain sales or leases if the ownership of                           the seller and the ownership of the purchaser are identical or the ownership of the                           lessor and the ownership of the lessee are identical                                .    enacts a sales and use tax exemption for certain purchases of machinery and                           equipment if a certain percentage of the purchaser s sales revenues are certain                           admission or user fees subject to sales and use taxation                                .    grants rulemaking authority to the State Tax Commission                                .    requires the State Tax Commission to report to the Revenue and Taxation Interim                           Committee  and                               .    makes technical and conforming changes.
