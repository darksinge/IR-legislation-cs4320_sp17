Vickers             LONG TITLE      General Description             This bill amends provisions related to investigational drugs and devices.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides that a terminally ill patient may obtain an investigational drug or device       from the drug s or device s manufacturer under certain circumstances  and                  exempts certain conduct from the definition of unlawful and unprofessional conduct       for a physician who administers an investigational drug or uses an investigational       device to treat a terminally ill patient. Section  .  Section           is enacted to read         CHAPTER   .  UTAH RIGHT TO TRY ACT                      . Title.             This chapter is known as the  Utah Right to Try Act.              Section  .  Section           is enacted to read                       . Definitions.             As used in this chapter                    Eligible patient  means an individual who has been diagnosed with a terminal        illness by a physician.                   Physician  means an individual who is licensed under               a   Title   , Chapter   , Utah Medical Practice Act  or              b   Title   , Chapter   , Utah Osteopathic Medical Practice Act.                   Insurer  means the same as that term is defined in Section   A      .                   Investigational device  means a device that               a   meets the definition of  investigational device  in    C.F.R. Sec.    .   and              b   has successfully completed the United States Food and Drug Administration Phase          testing for an investigational device described in    C.F.R. Part    .                   Investigational drug  means a drug that               a   meets the definition of  investigational new drug  in    C.F.R. Sec.    .   and              b   has successfully completed the United States Food and Drug Administration Phase          testing for an investigational new drug described in    C.F.R. Part    .                   Terminal illness  means a condition of a patient that               a   is serious or life threatening               b   as determined by a physician, is likely to pose a greater risk to the patient than the        risk posed to the patient by treatment with an investigational drug or investigational device         and              c   presents the patient with no treatment option that is satisfactory or comparable to        treatment with an investigational drug or device.             Section  .  Section           is enacted to read                       . Right to request investigational drug or device.                  An eligible patient may obtain an investigational drug through an agreement with        the investigational drug s manufacturer and the eligible patient s physician that provides               a   for the transfer of the investigational drug from the manufacturer to the physician         and              b   that the physician will administer the investigational drug to the patient.                  An eligible patient may obtain an investigational device through an agreement with        the investigational device s manufacturer and the eligible patient s physician that provides               a   for the transfer of the investigational device from the manufacturer to the physician         and              b   that the physician will use the investigational device to treat the patient.
