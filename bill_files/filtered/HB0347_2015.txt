Bramble             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies the Utah State Retirement and Insurance Benefit Act by adding a       conversion window for certain employees.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   adds a six month window for employers of certain entities to elect to participate in       the Public Employees  Noncontributory Retirement System and for eligible       employees to elect to participate in that system.
