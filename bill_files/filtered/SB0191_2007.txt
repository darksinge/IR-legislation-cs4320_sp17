Urquhart                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Governmental Immunity Act of Utah by amending provisions                           related to limitations of judgments.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends judgment limitation amounts to reflect amounts currently in effect as                           adjusted by the state risk manager based on changes in the Consumer Price Index                                .    adds language to clarify that judgment limitation amounts specified in the statute are                           periodically adjusted  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
