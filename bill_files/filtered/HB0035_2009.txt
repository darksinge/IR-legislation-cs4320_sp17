General Description                                This bill creates a tax credit for higher education tuition and fees.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows a claimant to claim a nonrefundable tax credit for tuition and mandatory fees                           for the claimant s enrollment in an institution within the state system of higher                           education                                .    limits the nonrefundable tax credit to    of the claimant s tax liability before tax                           credits  and                               .    allows the claimant to carry forward the nonrefundable tax credit if the amount of                           the tax credit exceeds    of the claimant s tax liability before tax credits.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill has retrospective operation for a taxable year beginning on or after January  ,                               .
