Bell                                                         LONG TITLE                           General Description                                This bill amends provisions of the Utah Medical Examiner Act relating to criminal                           investigations and autopsies.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    grants the attorney general or an assistant attorney general the authority to                           investigate certain deaths                                .    grants the attorney general the authority to request an autopsy  and                               .    makes technical changes.
