Hinkins                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the definition of neglect and eliminates a responsibility of the                           Division of Child and Family Services.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    adds willful, wanton, and reckless negligence to the definition of neglect  and                               .    eliminates the division s responsibility to conduct court ordered home evaluations in                           custody proceedings.
