FAILURE TO REPORT CONTRIBUTIONS OR PUBLIC                           SERVICE ASSISTANCE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Craig Hall                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends provisions of the Election Code by imposing a penalty for failure to                           report contributions or public service assistance within the    day period required by                           law.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    imposes a penalty for a state office candidate, a legislative office candidate, a                           political action committee, a political issues committee, a school board office                           candidate, or a judge, that fails to report contributions or public service assistance,                           as applicable, within the    day period required by law  and                               .    provides for publication of information relating to a penalty described in the                           preceding paragraph.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.
