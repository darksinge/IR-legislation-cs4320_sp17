CHILD WELFARE COURT REPORTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Mike Thompson                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Judicial Code to create a disclosure requirement for child welfare                          reports made in juvenile court.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a party to a child welfare hearing to share its report with the parent of the                           child at least five days prior to the proceeding  and                               .    creates an exception for certain drug court hearings.
