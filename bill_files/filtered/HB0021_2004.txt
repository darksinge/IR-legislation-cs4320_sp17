LICENSING   INSURANCE LAW                           AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor  James A. Dunnigan                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Insurance Code to address provisions related to licensing.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies definitions                                .    modifies provisions related to initial and renewal applications for licensure                                .    modifies cross references                                .    repeals certain reporting requirements for appointments in the state                                .    requires a producer to have experience with property and casualty lines of authority                           to be eligible to add surplus lines of authority                                .    modifies reporting requirements for agency designations                                .    repeals the commissioner s authority to issue a single license to each licensed                           adjustor for a single fee                                .    modifies time for retention of records                                .    provides that certain records are private records  and                               .    makes technical changes.
