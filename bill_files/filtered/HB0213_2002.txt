CLASSROOM AMPLIFICATION                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   David L. Hogue                             This act modifies provisions related to the State System of Public Instruction by requiring                          the state superintendent of public instruction to conduct a study on the use of classroom                          amplification devices in Utah classrooms. The provisions enacted by this act are repealed                          July  ,     .                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                     b    , as last amended by Chapters     and    , Laws of Utah                                ENACTS                                  A      , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A           is enacted to read                                  A      .  Study on classroom amplification devices.                                    The state superintendent of public instruction shall conduct a study on the use of                          classroom amplification devices in Utah classrooms.  The study shall include                                 a   an identification of the advantages and disadvantages of using classroom amplification                          devices  and                                b   an evaluation of the effects of using classroom amplification devices on students                           academic performance.                                    By November  ,     , the state superintendent of public instruction shall submit a                          report of the study, along with recommendations regarding the use of classroom amplification                          devices, to the Education Interim Committee.
