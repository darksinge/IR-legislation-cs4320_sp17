Section  .  Section   A         is amended to read               A        . Definitions.            As used in this part                   Authorized entity  means an LEA, qualifying private school, or the State Board of       Education that is authorized to request a background check and ongoing monitoring under this        part.                   Bureau  means the Bureau of Criminal Identification within the Department of        Public Safety created in Section          .                   Contract employee  means an employee of a staffing service or other entity who        works at a public or private school under a contract  between the staffing service and the public        or private school .                   FBI  means the Federal Bureau of Investigation.                         Local education agency  or   LEA   means a school district, charter school,        or the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind.                        a    License applicant  means an applicant for a license issued by the State        Board of Education under Title   A, Chapter  , Educator Licensing and Professional Practices        Act.              b    License applicant  includes an applicant for reinstatement of an expired, lapsed,        suspended, or revoked license.                         Non licensed employee  means an employee of an LEA or qualifying private        school that does not hold a current Utah educator license issued by the State Board of        Education under Title   A, Chapter  , Educator Licensing and Professional Practices Act.                         Personal identifying information  means               a   current name, former names, nicknames, and aliases               b   date of birth               c   address               d   telephone number               e   driver license number or other government issued identification number               f   social security number  and              g   fingerprints.                         Qualifying private school  means a private school that               a   enrolls students under Title   A, Chapter  a, Part  , Carson Smith Scholarships for        Students with Special Needs Act .   and               b  is authorized to conduct fingerprint based background checks of national crime        information databases under the Adam Walsh Child Protection and Safety Act of     , Pub. L.        No.        .                           Rap back system  means a system that enables authorized entities to receive        ongoing status notifications of any criminal history reported on individuals whose fingerprints        are registered in the system.                           WIN Database  means the Western Identification Network Database that        consists of eight western states sharing one electronic fingerprint database.             Section  .
