Harper             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies provisions related to the election of metro township council       members.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides for council members of a metro township with a population of   ,    or       more to be elected by district                   provides for council members of a metro township with a population of less than         ,    to be elected at large                   addresses the status of a candidate s declaration of candidacy for a metro township       council in a metro township with a population of less than   ,     and                  makes technical and conforming changes.       Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             This bill provides a special effective date.            This bill provides revisor instructions.
