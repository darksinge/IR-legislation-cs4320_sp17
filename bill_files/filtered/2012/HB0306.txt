PRESCRIPTION DRUGS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Bradley M  Daw                                Senate Sponsor             Patricia W  Jones                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Pharmacy Practice Act to require the pharmacy board to adopt                           rules regarding the disposal of unused prescription drugs in accordance with federal                           laws and regulations                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     enacts a requirement that the pharmacy board allow a pharmacy to take back unused                           prescription drugs if the federal rules permit a pharmacy take back program  and                                    requires the pharmacy board to enact administrative rules regarding unused drug                           take back programs once federal rules related to drug take back programs are                           published                            Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                     b      Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                   Section       Section         b         is enacted to read                                     b       Disposal of unused prescription drugs                                     A pharmacy may accept unused prescription drugs for disposal in accordance with                          administrative rules adopted by the board                                     The board shall adopt administrative rules regarding a pharmacy accepting unused                          prescription drugs for disposal as permitted by federal law and regulation relating to the                          disposal of unused prescription drugs       Legislative Review Note      as of                PM   Office of Legislative Research
