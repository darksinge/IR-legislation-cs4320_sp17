Trust deeds    Instruments entitled to be recorded    Assignment of a                           beneficial interest.                                  Any trust deed, substitution of trustee, assignment of a beneficial interest under a trust                           deed, notice of assignment of a beneficial interest, notice of default, trustee s deed,                           reconveyance of the trust property, and any instrument by which any trust deed is subordinated                           or waived as to priority, if acknowledged as provided by law, is entitled to be recorded.  The                           recording of an assignment of a beneficial interest  in the  under a trust deed or a notice of                          assignment of a beneficial interest does not in itself impart notice of the assignment to the                           trustor,  his  or the trustor s heirs or personal representatives, so as to invalidate any payment                           made by  any of them  the trustor, or the trustor s heirs or personal representatives, to the                           person holding the note, bond, or other instrument evidencing the obligation by the trust deed.
