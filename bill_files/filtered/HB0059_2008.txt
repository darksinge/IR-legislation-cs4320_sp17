General Description                                This bill makes certain technical changes to reporting requirements for the Governor s                           Office of Economic Development and Department of Community and Culture and                           provides for uniformity in referencing the Office of Rural Development and its director.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    correctly identifies the legislative interim committee to which the Governor s Office                           of Economic Development and the Department of Community and Culture s Olene                           Walker Housing Fund Board make annual reports                                .    provides for uniformity in referencing the Office of Rural Development and its                           director                                .    provides for ongoing rather than one time reporting of activities associated with the                           Economic Development Incentives Act  and                               .    makes certain other technical changes.
