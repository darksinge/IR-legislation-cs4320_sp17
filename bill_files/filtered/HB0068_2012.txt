Bramble                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Powersport Vehicle Franchise Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies a provision relating to a franchisor s obligation to pay a franchisee for                           unsold vehicles when a powersport vehicle franchise is terminated or not continued.
