NATIVE AMERICAN COORDINATING BOARD           MEMBERSHIP                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Beverly Ann Evans          AN ACT RELATING TO COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT  EXPANDING         THE MEMBERSHIP IN THE STATE NATIVE AMERICAN COORDINATING BOARD          AND MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                       , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section          is amended to read                       .  State coordinating board    Chair    Quorum    Creation    Power    Duties.                   There is created an advisory board to be called the  State Native American          Coordinating Board  consisting of the following members                a   the director of the division                b   a representative from the following                i   the Department of Human Services appointed by the executive director of the          Department of Human Services                ii   the Department of Health appointed by the executive director of the Department of          Health                iii   the State Office of Education appointed by the state superintendent of public          instruction                iv   the State Board of Regents appointed by the commissioner of higher education                v   the Department of Community and Economic Development appointed by the executive          director of the Department of Community and Economic Development                 vi   the governor s office appointed by the governor               vii   the office of the attorney general appointed by the attorney general               viii   the Department of Workforce Services appointed by the executive director of the         Department of Workforce Services   and               ix   the Department of Corrections appointed by the executive director of the Department         of Corrections               x   the Department of Environmental Quality appointed by the executive director of the         Department of Environmental Quality               xi   the Department of Natural Resources appointed by the executive director of the          Department of Natural Resources                xii   the Department of Transportation appointed by the executive director of the          Department of Transportation                xiii   the Department of Agriculture and Food appointed by the commissioner of the          Department of Agriculture and Food  and               xiv   the State Tax Commission appointed by the chair of the State Tax Commission.                   a   The director of the division shall be the chair of the board and shall be responsible          for calling meetings of the board.               b   The chair shall coordinate the activities of the board required by this section.                   A majority of the members of the board constitute a quorum of the board for the          transaction of business.                   The board shall meet at least quarterly.                   Board members may not receive any compensation for their service on the board other          than what they receive as state officers and employees.                   The board shall advise the division and the chief administrative officer of other state          agencies regarding how the state may develop a consistent, integrated, and coordinated approach          to implementing laws, services, functions, and governmental programs that serve Indian citizens          of the state.                   The board shall report annually to the Native American Legislative Liaison Committee          of the Legislature.              Legislative Review Note      as of                 AM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
