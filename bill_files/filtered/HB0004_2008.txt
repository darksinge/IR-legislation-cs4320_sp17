LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill supplements or reduces appropriations previously provided for the use and                           operation of state government.  The bill funds compensation changes for state and higher education                           employees for the fiscal year beginning July  ,      and ending June   ,     .                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides budget increases and decreases for the compensation of state and higher                           education employees not including school district personnel                                .    provides for a    cost of living allowance for state and higher education employees not                           including school district personnel                                .    provides for an additional    cost of living allowance for state employees not including                           school district personnel contingent upon changes in health benefit plans                                .    adjusts funding for changes in health plans offered to state employees not including                           school district personnel contingent upon such changes                                .    provides funding for a  .   increase in the premiums charged for higher education                           employee health benefits                                .    provides funding for an increase in the cost of retirement for certain employees                                .    adjusts funding for changes in the cost of Workers  Compensation                                .    provides funding for a    contract increase for certain contract service providers.                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates for fiscal year                                     .       ,   ,    from the General Fund                                .      ,   ,    from the Uniform School Fund                                 .       ,   ,    from the Education Fund                                .       ,   ,    from various sources as detailed in this bill.                               This bill appropriates for fiscal year      contingent upon certain health benefit plan                           changes                                .       ,   ,    from the General Fund                                .        ,    from the Uniform School Fund                                .       ,   ,    from various sources as detailed in this bill.                            Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
