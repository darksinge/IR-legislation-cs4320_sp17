General Description             This bill amends provisions related to the Jury and Witness Act to address jury service       requirements for specific counties.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   exempts counties of the fourth, fifth, and sixth class and counties of the third class       with populations up to   ,    from certain jury service requirements.
