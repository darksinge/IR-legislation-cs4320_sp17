FOSTER PARENT DIRECTOR                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Nora B.  Stephens          AN ACT RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES  DIRECTING THE         DEPARTMENT TO CONTRACT WITH A STATEWIDE ORGANIZATION OF FOSTER         PARENTS  ESTABLISHING PROVISIONS OF THAT CONTRACT  AND PROVIDING AN         EFFECTIVE DATE.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                  A      . , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    A      .  is enacted to read                  A      . .  Foster parent director.              To further protect children in foster care statewide, and to assist foster parents in their roles          as primary caregivers to children in state custody, the department shall contract with a statewide,          private, nonprofit foster parent organization.  The contract shall specify that the organization                    employ a full time executive director                    implement programs to recruit and retain foster parents                    assist and support foster parents in providing care and treatment to children in the          custody of the Division of Child and Family Services                    assist foster parents in solving problems with caseworkers and other employees of the          Division of Child and Family Services                    establish and assist in the operation of  local foster parent organizations                    solicit funds, through donations, and make application for grants to support the          organization and its activities                    publish newsletters, reports, and other materials to provide information to foster          parents  and                  meet additional requirements as the department and the foster parent organization         consider appropriate.             Section  .   Effective date.             This act takes effect on July  ,     .
