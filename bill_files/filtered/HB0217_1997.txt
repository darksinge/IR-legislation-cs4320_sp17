ACCESS OF YOUTH TO TOBACCO PRODUCTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Jordan Tanner          AN ACT RELATING TO HEALTH  PROHIBITING SALE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS BY          ANY SELF SERVICE MEANS, EXCEPT IN SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCES  AND         SPECIFYING PENALTIES.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          AMENDS                         , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                             . , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               ENACTS                         . , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section            is amended to read                         .  Definitions.              As used in this part                     Bar  means any establishment or portion of an establishment where  one can  an          individual may consume alcoholic beverages and hors d oeuvres, but  excluding  excludes any           such  establishment or portion of the establishment with tables or seating facilities where, in          consideration of payment, meals are served.                    Place of business  means any and all  such  places such as shops, stores, factories,          public garages, offices, theaters, recreation and dance halls, poolrooms, cafes, cafeterias, cabarets,          restaurants, hotels, lodging houses, streetcars, buses, interurban and railway passenger coaches and          waiting rooms.                    Smoking  means the possession of any lighted cigar, cigarette, pipe, or other lighted          smoking equipment.                     Tobacco product  means any tobacco cigarette, cigar, pipe tobacco, smokeless         tobacco, snuff, or any other form of tobacco that may be utilized for smoking, chewing, inhaling,         or other manner of ingestion.             Section  .  Section           .  is amended to read                        . .  Prohibition of gift or sale of cigarettes or tobacco through vending          machines    Exceptions    Penalties.                  Except as provided in Subsection    , the gift or sale of cigarettes or tobacco in any         form through vending machines or tobacco product dispensing machines is prohibited in this state.                  a   A bar, or a privately owned and operated club or association that has a private club          liquor license under Title   A, Chapter  , Private Club Liquor Licenses, or that requires          membership and charges a membership fee, may maintain cigarette or tobacco product vending          machines on its premises.               b   A workplace may maintain cigarette or tobacco product vending machines for its adult          employees, in an area not available to the general public.                   Violation of this section is a                a   class C misdemeanor on the first offense , a                 b   class B misdemeanor on the second offense ,   and  a                c   class A misdemeanor on the third and all subsequent offenses.              Section  .  Section           .  is enacted to read                         . .  Sale of tobacco products by any self service means.                   As used in this section                a    Self service merchandising  means the open displaying of tobacco products to which          the public has access without the intervention of an employee.               b    Vendor assisted  means                i   only a store employee has access to the tobacco products  and               ii   the store employee assists the customer by supplying the product to the customer.                   Except under Subsection    , a person may not sell, permit to be sold, offer for sale,          offer as a gift, or display for sale any tobacco product by                a   means of any type of self service merchandising except as allowed in Subsection                   .      or               b   any means other than vendor assisted sales.                          Subsection     does not apply to standard ten pack cartons of cigarettes, standard            carton cases or partial cases of complete cartons of cigarettes, or multicontainer packages of         smokeless tobacco.                  Violation of this section is a               a   class C misdemeanor for the first offense               b   class B misdemeanor for the second offense  and              c   class A misdemeanor for the third and all subsequent offenses.      Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
