Hellewell                                                       LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires notice for certain transfers of money out of utility funds.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires towns, cities, counties, and special districts, to provide notice, under certain                           circumstances, before transferring money out of a utility fund                                .    describes the scope of notice required  and                               .    makes technical changes.
