MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY INSPECTION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jeremy A. Peterson                                Senate Sponsor             Stuart C. Reid                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Motor Vehicle Business Regulation Act by amending provisions                           relating to safety inspection requirements.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    changes the amount of time that a motor vehicle has to receive and pass a safety                           inspection, if required in the current year, for the motor vehicle to be eligible for a                           temporary permit issued by a licensed motor vehicle dealer.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                        , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Temporary permits    Inspections required before issuance.                                    A dealer licensed in accordance with this chapter may not issue a temporary permit                              under Section                  unless                                 a   i   the motor vehicle for which the temporary permit is issued has received and                           passed the safety inspection if required in the current year under Section                  within the                           previous  six     months                                 ii   the safety inspection certificate was issued in the name of a licensed and bonded                           dealer  and                                iii   a copy of the safety inspection certificate is given to the customer  and                                b   the motor vehicle passed the emission inspection test required by Section                                a         .                                    Notwithstanding Subsection     a , a dealer may issue a temporary permit without a                           safety inspection certificate if the motor vehicle complies with the safety inspection as                           provided in Section         a        .                                    Notwithstanding Subsection     b , a dealer may issue a temporary permit without                           proof of an emission inspection if                                 a   the motor vehicle is exempt from emission inspection as provided in Section                                a                                          b   the purchaser is a resident of a county that does not require emission inspections  or                                c   the motor vehicle is otherwise exempt from emission inspections.                                    Notwithstanding Subsection    , a dealer may sell a motor vehicle as is without                           having it safety or emission inspected provided that no temporary permit is issued.
