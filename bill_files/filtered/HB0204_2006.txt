COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH INSURANCE POOL                           AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor   Peggy Wallace                            Senate Sponsor         Curtis S. Bramble                                                       LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Comprehensive Health Insurance Pool Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    removes the term  low cost  from the purpose statement for the high risk pool  and                               .    changes mandatory language to permissive language with regard to coinsurance, and                           maximum out of pocket payments for prescription benefits.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  A       , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                      A       , as last amended by Chapter  , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A            is amended to read                                   A       .  Purpose.                                  The purpose of the Comprehensive Health Insurance Pool Act is to provide  low cost                            access to health insurance coverage to residents of Utah who are denied adequate health                           insurance and are considered uninsurable.                               Section  .    Section      A            is amended to read                                   A       .  Deductibles    Copayments.                                    a  A pool policy shall impose a deductible on a per calendar year basis.                                b  At least two deductible plans shall be offered.                                 c   The deductible is applied to all of the eligible medical expenses as defined in                           Section       A           , incurred by the enrollee until the deductible has been satisfied.  There are                           no benefits payable before the deductible has been satisfied.                                d   The pool may offer separate deductibles for prescription benefits.                                    a  A mandatory coinsurance requirement  shall  may be imposed at the rate of at                           least     of eligible medical expenses in excess of the mandatory deductible.                                b   Any coinsurance imposed under this Subsection     shall be designated in the pool                           policy.                                   The board shall establish maximum aggregate out of pocket payments for eligible                           medical expenses incurred by the enrollee for each of the deductible plans offered under                           Subsection     b .                                    a  When the enrollee has incurred the maximum aggregate out of pocket payments                           under Subsection    , the board may establish a coinsurance requirement to be imposed on                           eligible medical expenses in excess of the maximum aggregate out of pocket expense.                                b   The circumstances in which the coinsurance authorized by this Subsection     may                           be imposed shall be designated in the pool policy.                                c   The coinsurance authorized by this Subsection     may be imposed at a rate not to                           exceed    of eligible medical expenses.                                    The limits on maximum aggregate out of pocket payments for eligible medical                           expenses incurred by the enrollee under this section  shall  may not include out of pocket                           payments for prescription benefits.
