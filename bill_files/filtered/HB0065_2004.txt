TUITION WAIVER FOR PURPLE HEART                           RECIPIENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Gregory H. Hughes                             Eric K. Hutchings                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the State System of Higher Education Code by creating a tuition                           waiver.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires state institutions of higher education to waive the tuition of a Utah resident                           admitted to a program of study leading to a degree or certificate, if the student has                           received a Purple Heart award as a result of military service.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     . Section      B  e         is enacted to read                            CHAPTER  e.  UTAH PURPLE HEART RECIPIENTS  TUITION ACT                                  B  e    .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Utah Purple Heart Recipients  Tuition Act.                                Section  .    Section      B  e         is enacted to read                                  B  e    .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Purple Heart recipient  means any Utah resident who is a military veteran and who                          has earned a Purple Heart award as a result of military service.                                     State institution of higher education  means an institution listed in Section                               B          .                                    a    Tuition  means tuition at the rate for residents of the state.                                b    Tuition  excludes fees.                               Section  .    Section      B  e         is enacted to read                                  B  e    .  Tuition waivers for Purple Heart recipients    Qualifications                              Limitations.                                    Beginning in the         academic year, a state institution of higher education                          shall waive tuition for each Purple Heart recipient who                                 a   is admitted as a full time, part time, or summer school student in a program of                          study leading to a degree or certificate                                 b   is a resident student of the state as determined under Section       B            and                                c   submits verification as provided in Subsection     that the student is a Purple Heart                          recipient.                                    A Purple Heart recipient seeking a tuition waiver shall request the Division of                          Veterans Affairs to provide the verification required by Subsection     c . The Division of                          Veterans Affairs shall provide the verification upon obtaining evidence satisfactory to the                          division that the student is a Purple Heart recipient.
