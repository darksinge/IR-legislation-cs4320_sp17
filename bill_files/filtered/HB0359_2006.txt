PROPERTY TAX EXEMPTION FOR VETERANS                                GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor   Mark A. Wheatley                            Senate Sponsor                                                                           LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Property Tax Act relating to exemptions for veterans.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates a property tax exemption for certain honorably discharged veterans and their                           unmarried surviving spouses                                .    modifies the application process for veterans  exemptions                                .    defines terms  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides a contingent effective date of January  ,      if H.J.R.  ,                                General Session, is approved at the next regular general election.
