Barrus                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill requires legislative approval of certain interstate agreements affecting state                           energy resources and use.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires legislative approval of certain interstate agreements affecting state energy                           resources and use  and                               .    addresses the effect of other interstate compacts and agreements. General Provisions                                  G       .  Title.                               This chapter is known as  Interstate Compacts and Agreements. Section  .    Section      G            is enacted to read                            Part  .  Legislative Approval of Interstate Agreements                                  G       .  Approval of significant interstate agreements    Validity.                                    a   Before legally binding the state by executing a significant interstate agreement                          affecting state energy resources or use, an executive officer shall                                 i   submit the proposed significant interstate agreement to the governor for the                          governor s written approval or rejection  and                                ii   if the governor approves the significant interstate agreement, submit the significant                          interstate agreement to the Legislature for the Legislature s approval or rejection by means of                                 A   a concurrent resolution approving or rejecting the agreement  or                                 B   enacting the agreement into law.                                 b   i   If the Legislature approves the agreement as required by this section, the                           executive officer may execute the significant interstate agreement.                                 ii   If the Legislature rejects the agreement, the executive officer may not execute the                           significant interstate agreement.                                     If an executive officer executes a significant interstate agreement without obtaining                           the governor s approval or the Legislature s approval as required by Subsection    , the                           agreement is void ab initio.                                     The requirements of this section do not apply to a significant interstate agreement                           that is entered into                                  a   before May   ,       or                                 b   by a state institution of higher education as defined in Section       B          .                                Section  .    Section      G            is enacted to read                                   G       .  Other interstate agreements.                                Notwithstanding the provisions of this chapter, the Legislature may enact legislation                           prohibiting the enactment of an interstate agreement even if the agreement is not a significant                           interstate agreement as defined in this chapter.                                Section  .    Section      G            is enacted to read                                   G       .  Application of chapter in emergencies.                                     Notwithstanding the requirements of Section       G           , an executive officer may                           enter into a significant interstate agreement on a temporary basis without legislative approval                              during                                  a   an emergency or major disaster declared by the President of the United States of                           America  or                                 b   a state of emergency declared by the governor under Section       K          .                                     If a significant interstate agreement is entered into without legislative approval                           under Subsection    , the executive officer entering into the agreement shall provide notice of                           the execution of the agreement to the Legislature at the next meeting of the Executive                           Appropriations Subcommittee of the Legislature and shall comply with the requirements of                           Section       G            to thereafter obtain the approval of the Legislature.
