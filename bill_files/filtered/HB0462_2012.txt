                           e    f   the advisability, feasibility, estimated cost, and likelihood of success of                           challenging                                 i   federal court                  B   impact a power or a right reserved to the state or its citizens by the United States                           Constitution, Amendment IX or X  or                                C The council shall                                 a   provide advice to the governor, state planning coordinator, and the public lands                           policy coordinator concerning coordination of                                 i   state and local government rights under R.S.       and                                ii   other public lands issues                                 b   approve a plan for R.S.      rights developed in accordance with Section                              C            and                                c   review, at least quarterly                                 i   financial statements concerning implementation of the plan for R.S.      rights                            and                                ii   financial and other reports from the Public Lands Policy Coordinating Office                           concerning its activities.                                    The council chair may require the attorney general or a designee to provide                           testimony on potential legal actions that would enhance the state s sovereignty or authority on                           issues affecting Utah and the well being of its citizens.
