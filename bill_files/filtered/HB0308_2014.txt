Madsen                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Utah Municipal Code regarding ordinance penalties.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    removes a requirement that a municipality impose a minimum penalty for a                           municipal ordinance, but does not modify the maximum penalty limitation.
