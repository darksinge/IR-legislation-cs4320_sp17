STATEWIDE CRIME REDUCTION PLAN                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Duane E. Bourdeaux                           AN ACT RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS IN GENERAL AND CRIMINAL LAW                           CREATING THE STATEWIDE CRIME REDUCTION PLANNING COMMITTEE WITHIN                          THE COMMISSION ON CRIMINAL AND JUVENILE JUSTICE  DEFINING ITS                          MEMBERSHIP AND DUTIES  CREATING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS FOR STATE                          AND LOCAL CRIMINAL JUSTICE AGENCIES  PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE  AND                           PROVIDING A REPEAL DATE.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                     a    , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                             , as last amended by Chapters   ,     and    , Laws of Utah                                ENACTS                                     a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a    , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section         a         is amended to read                                      a    .  Executive director    Qualifications    Compensation    Appointment                              Functions.                                    The governor, with the advice and consent of the Senate, shall appoint a person                           experienced in the field of criminal justice and in administration as the executive director of the                              Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice.  The governor shall establish the executive                           director s salary within the salary range fixed by the Legislature in Title   , Chapter   , State                           Officer Compensation.                                    a   The executive director, under the direction of the commission, shall administer the                           duties of the commission, including those outlined in Section          a        , and act as the governor s                           advisor on state, regional, metropolitan, and local government planning as it relates to criminal                           justice.                                b  This chapter does not derogate the planning authority conferred on state, regional,                           metropolitan, and local governments by existing law.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                            Part  .  Statewide Crime Reduction Planning Committee                                     a    .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Commission  means the Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice.                                     Committee  means the Statewide Crime Reduction Planning Committee.                                     Executive director  means the executive director of the Commission on Criminal and                          Juvenile Justice.                                     Local criminal justice agency  means each county and municipal law enforcement                          agency.                                     State criminal justice agency  means the Department of Public Safety, Department                          of Corrections, the Division of Youth Corrections, and the Department of Human Services.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Creation    Purpose.                                    The Statewide Crime Reduction Planning Committee is created within the commission.                                    The committee s purpose is to assist local and state criminal justice agencies to create                          crime reduction plans by                                 a   providing leadership, direction, and expertise in crime reduction strategic planning                                 b   providing information on criminal justice strategies proven to prevent and reduce                          crime  and                                c   coordinating and compiling the individual plans into a statewide crime reduction plan.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                        a    .  Membership.                               The Statewide Crime Reduction Planning Committee is composed of the following                          members                                     the membership of the commission                                     the membership of the Utah Law Enforcement Strategic Planning Committee  and                                    one representative appointed by the speaker of the House.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Duties of committee.                               The committee shall                                     hold a strategic planning meeting each year on or before June   to                                 a   evaluate the previous year s crime reduction efforts on state and local levels  and                                b   develop the focus of the statewide crime reduction plan for the coming year                                     disseminate information, confer with, and assist each criminal justice agency in                          developing a crime reduction plan                                     hold a strategic planning meeting each year on or before November   to coordinate the                          statewide crime reduction plan based on the plans submitted by state and local criminal justice                          agencies                                     report each year to the Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Interim Committee                          during its November meeting  and                                    report each year to the Legislature while it is in general session.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .   State and local criminal justice agencies    Reporting requirements.                                    State and local criminal justice agencies shall each submit a crime reduction plan each                          year on or before October   to the executive director containing information about how the agency                          plans to reduce crime victimization in its jurisdiction by     each year and     over the next five                          years.                                    A local criminal justice agency s crime reduction plan shall have the signatures of the                          members of the county or municipal legislative body indicating that the members have read the                          plan.                               Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Notification of requirements.                                  The executive director shall send a letter on or before April  ,      notifying the director                          of each state criminal justice agency and the chief law enforcement official of each local criminal                          justice agency of the statutory requirement to create a crime reduction plan under this part.
