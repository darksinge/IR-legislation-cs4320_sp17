PROVISION FOR EMERGENCY MEDICAL                           SERVICES                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Peggy Wallace                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Municipal Code and the Health Code by amending provisions                           related to emergency medical services provided by municipalities.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a municipality that intends to annex a geographic service area and provide                           emergency medical services to that area to certify to the Department of Health that                           the municipality can meet current emergency medical service levels                                .    requires the Department of Health to amend a municipality s license for emergency                           medical services to include the annexed area after final approval of the annexation                            and                               .    makes technical amendments.
