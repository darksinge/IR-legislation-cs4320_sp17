FALSE VEHICLE TITLE AND                           REGISTRATION PENALTIES                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Paul Ray                                Senate Sponsor             Daniel R. Liljenquist                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Motor Vehicle Act by amending provisions relating to the penalty                           for false evidences of motor vehicle title and registration information.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that it is a third degree felony, rather than a second degree felony, for                           providing false evidences for certain motor vehicle title or registration information.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                    a     , as renumbered and amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                      Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section        a          is amended to read                                     a     .  Third degree felony    False evidences of title and registration.                               It is a  second  third degree felony for a person with respect to a motor vehicle, trailer,                              or semitrailer to                                     fraudulently use a false or fictitious name in an application for registration, a                           certificate of title, or for a duplicate certificate of title                                     knowingly make a false statement or knowingly conceal a material fact in an                           application under this chapter                                     otherwise commit a fraud in an application under this chapter                                     alter with fraudulent intent a certificate of title, registration card, license plate, or                           permit issued by the division                                     forge or counterfeit a document or license plate purporting to have been issued by                           the division                                     alter, falsify, or forge an assignment upon a certificate of title                                     hold or use a document or license plate under this chapter knowing it has been                           altered, forged, or falsified   and  or                                    file an application for a certificate of title providing false lien information, when the                           person named on the application as lienholder does not hold a valid security interest.
