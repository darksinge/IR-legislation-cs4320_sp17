A person need not obtain gubernatorial or legislative approval for the construction of                            a radioactive waste facility for which a license application has been approved by the Department                            of Health or submitted to the federal Nuclear Regulatory Commission and to the Department of                            Health for approval before January  ,     , and which has been determined, on or before October                              ,     , by the Department of Health to be complete in accordance with state and federal                            requirements.
