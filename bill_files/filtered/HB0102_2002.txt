Bourdeaux                             This act modifies the Public Safety Code in both the Bail Bond Recovery chapter and the                          Private Investigator Regulation Act to require that license applicants under these chapters                          submit two sets of fingerprints as part of the application process and that the department                          records and FBI records be searched for criminal history information on the applicant.
