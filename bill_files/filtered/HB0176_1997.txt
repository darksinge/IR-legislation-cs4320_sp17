POLLUTION LIEN FOR COST RECOVERY                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Marda Dillree          AN ACT RELATING TO HEALTH AND ENVIRONMENT  ALLOWING THE         DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL QUALITY TO IMPOSE A LIEN ON A FACILITY         OR  SITE TO RECOVER FROM RESPONSIBLE PARTIES MONEY SPENT IN AN          INVESTIGATION AND ABATEMENT OF A RELEASE OF  HAZARDOUS MATERIALS          PROVIDING PROCEDURES FOR PERFECTION OF THE LIEN  AND PROVIDING          WHEN THE PROVISIONS TAKE EFFECT. Section          .  is enacted to read                        . .   State authority to impose lien to recover mitigation fund spent on facility              Liability of responsible parties.                    As used in this section                a    Action  means an abatement or remedial action authorized by Subsection            ,          Section         , or Section         .               b    Fund  means the Hazardous Substances Mitigation Fund created under Section                  .                   a   The executive director may impose a lien as provided in this section on real          property and fixtures of a facility that is the subject of an action.               b   When the executive director begins to expend fund money on an action, the executive          director may record a notice of intent to impose a lien with the office of the county recorder for          the county  where the facility is located.              c   The executive director shall provide written notice of the recording of the notice of         intent to impose a lien by certified or registered mail to all persons of record holding an interest         in the real property or fixtures of the facility.              d   Any person holding an interest in the real property or fixtures of the facility may         contest the recording of the notice of intent to impose a lien under the hearing procedures of Title           , Chapter   b, Administrative Procedures Act.              e   i   When a notice of intent to impose a lien has been recorded, upon completion of the         action the executive director shall record a notice of completion.               ii   If the executive director terminates the action prior to its completion,  the executive          director shall record a notice of withdrawal of the notice of intent to impose a lien with the office          of the county recorder of the county where the facility is located.  The notice of withdrawal shall          include a cross reference to the notice of intent, including the date the notice of intent was filed.                   To perfect a lien under this section the executive director shall                a   record a notice of intent to impose a lien  under Subsection      and               b   within    days after completion of the action, record a notice of lien with the county          recorder of the county where the facility is located, stating                i   the final amount expended by the executive director from the fund on the action and          the amount of the lien                ii   the name of each person holding an interest of record in any of the real property or           fixtures of the facility                iii   the address of the facility                iv   a legal description of the real property and physical description of the fixtures          sufficient to identify them                v   the date of recording of the notice of lien and identification of the book and page          number of the recording of the notice of intent to impose a lien                vi   the identification number of the facility site, if the department has assigned a number           and               vii   the name, signature, and address of the executive director.                   The executive director shall provide written notice of the lien by certified or registered          mail to all persons of record holding an interest in the real property or fixtures of the facility.                          A lien perfected under this section relates back to, and takes effect on and after the date         and time of recording of the notice of intent to impose a lien under Subsection     and has priority         over               a   any lien, mortgage, or other encumbrance which may have attached subsequently to         the date and time the notice of intent to impose a lien was recorded  and              b   any lien, mortgage, or other encumbrance of which the state had no notice and which         was unrecorded when the notice of intent to impose a lien was recorded.                  The lien against the real property and fixtures of a facility of a responsible party shall         be for the costs expended at the facility from the fund to the extent liability is allocated by          Subsections             and            .                   A lien or notice of intent to impose a lien may not be imposed against the real property          and fixtures of a facility of a person who is not liable for an apportioned share under Subsections                      and            .                   If a party holding an interest of record is aggrieved by the amount of the lien, the party          may                a   submit the matter in any foreclosure proceeding under this section or apportionment          of liability proceeding under Subsection             or              or               b   file a complaint in the district court challenging the imposition and amount of the lien.                   a   Any action for the foreclosure of the lien shall be brought by the state attorney          general within one year after the date the lien is perfected.  The action shall be brought in the name          of the state, and in the district court of the county where the facility subject to the lien is located,          or the lien expires.               b   The state attorney general may separately bring enforcement, apportionment, or cost          recovery actions against responsible parties without first foreclosing on the lien.                    A lien may not be imposed under this section on property and fixtures of the facility          which are only contaminated by an offsite release unless the person who owns the facility has          taken actions which exacerbate the release.                    This section does not prohibit the executive director from issuing a partial or full          release of the lien pursuant to a negotiated agreement.                    Recording of notices or documents under this section shall be accompanied by any          fees required under Section       .                           This section affects and may be applied to only those actions initiated on and after         May  ,     .             Section  .  Section           is enacted to read                       .
