Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    updates the edition of the National Electrical Code, adopted by reference, to the                                edition                                .    modifies an adopted amendment to the National Electrical Code                                .    enacts a new exception to and deletes and replaces a provision of the International                           Building Code relating to concrete durability requirements                                .    enacts new provisions of the International Building Code related to standards                           applicable to concrete elements and minimum clear floor space  and                               .    adopts local amendments to the International Plumbing Code.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .
