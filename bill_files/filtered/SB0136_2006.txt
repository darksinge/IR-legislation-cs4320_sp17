Harper                                Cosponsor Mike Dmitrich                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill decreases the tax rate paid by corporations on certain insurance premiums for                           the purchase of variable life insurance.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    decreases the tax rate paid by corporations on certain insurance premiums for the                           purchase of variable life insurance                                .    amends confidentiality provisions to allow the State Tax Commission to report                           information regarding taxes paid on certain variable life insurance premiums                                .    requires the Revenue and Taxation Interim Committee to study the rate reduction on                           certain variable life insurance premiums  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill has retrospective operation to January  ,     .
