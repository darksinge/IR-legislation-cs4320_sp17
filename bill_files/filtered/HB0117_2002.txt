REVENUE FOR RESTRUCTURING MEDICAID                           SPEND DOWN                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Trisha S. Beck                             This act modifies the Medicaid spend down requirements and creates a funding mechanism                          for the spend down modification.  The act amends the Medical Assistance Act.  The act                          defines terms and directs the Health Department to use      of the federal poverty level as                          the income standard when determining if the aged, blind, or disabled have spent down                           enough excess income to be eligible for Medicaid benefits.  The act increases the beer tax and                           creates a restricted account dedicated to the funding of Medicaid spend down.
