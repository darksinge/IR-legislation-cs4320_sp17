EDUCATION RESOURCES CONSERVATION                           PROGRAM                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jackie Biskupski                                Senate Sponsor             Brent H. Goodfellow                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the State System of Public Education Code by creating a Resource                           Conservation Program for public education.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides definitions                                .    establishes the Resource Conservation Advisory Committee, including its                           composition and responsibilities                                .    specifies school district and charter school responsibilities relating to resource                           conservation, including the requirement to designate a resource conservation                           manager  and                               .    provides State Board of Education rulemaking authority.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  A       , Utah Code Annotated                                         A       , Utah Code Annotated                                      A       , Utah Code Annotated                                      A       , Utah Code Annotated                                      A       , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                            Part   .  Resource Conservation Programs                                  A       .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Resource conservation  means the reduced consumption of electricity, natural gas,                          water, or other resources.                                     Resource conservation management  means a study, audit, operational                          improvement, or training which is designed to provide resource conservation and operational                          cost savings.                                     Resource conservation measure  means an improvement or equipment designed to                          provide resource conservation and operational cost savings at least equivalent to the amount                          expended for the improvement or equipment over a period of not more than    years from the                          date the improvement or equipment is installed or becomes operational.                                     Resource savings performance contract  means an agreement whereby a private or                          nonprofit organization implements and funds resource conservation measures and management                          for a school district or charter school, and the school district or charter school pays for the                          measures and management from long term resource cost savings.                               Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                                  A       .  Resource Conservation Advisory Committee created    Composition                              Duties    Terms of office    Compensation.                                    There is created the Resource Conservation Advisory Committee composed of                                 a   five voting members representing each of the following                                 i   the State Board of Education                                 ii   school districts                                 iii   a representative of the executive branch with expertise in energy and resource                             conservation issues                                 iv   the Division of Facilities Construction and Management  and                                v   the Division of Purchasing and General Services  and                                b   two nonvoting members representing each of the following                                 i   energy services companies  and                                ii   energy consultants.                                    The Resource Conservation Advisory Committee shall advise and provide expertise                          to the State Board of Education, school districts, and charter schools regarding                                 a   resource conservation management                                 b   design standards that minimize buildings  use of resources  and                                c   implementation of resource conservation measures.                                    a   Except as required by Subsection     b , members shall be appointed by the                          State Board of Education to four year terms of office.                                b   The State Board of Education shall adjust the length of terms of the initial members                          appointed to the committee to ensure that the terms are staggered so that approximately half of                          the committee members are appointed every two years.                                c   When a vacancy occurs in the membership for any reason, the replacement shall be                          appointed for the unexpired term.                                    The committee shall elect one member to serve as chair for a term of one year.                                    a   The committee shall meet at the discretion of the chair.                                b   Attendance of a majority of the members at a duly called meeting constitutes a                          quorum for the transaction of official business.                                    a   Members who are not state government employees shall receive no                          compensation or benefits for their service on the committee, but may receive per diem and                          expenses incurred in the performance of the member s official duties at the rates established by                          the Division of Finance under Sections       A           and       A          .                                b   Members may decline to receive per diem and expenses for their service.                               Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                                  A       .  School districts and charter schools to implement resource                           conservation measures and management.                                    School districts and charter schools shall                                    a   implement resource conservation measures and management  and                                b   seek technical assistance from the Resource Conservation Advisory Committee                          created in Section       A           .                                    School districts and charter schools may enter into resource savings performance                          contracts.                                    Small school districts and charters schools may cooperate or enter into joint                          agreements to provide resource conservation and operational cost savings.                                    School districts shall include students in developing and implementing resource                          conservation measures.                               Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                                   A       .  Resource conservation manager    Duties.                                     Each school district and charter school shall designate a resource conservation                           manager.                                     A resource conservation manager shall                                  a   track the resource costs and resource conservation measures, if any, of the school                           district or charter school                                  b   make resource conservation reports as requested by the State Board of Education                                  c   be involved in the management of the school district s or charter school s resources                           and advise the school district or charter school about the management of its resources  and                                 d   be involved in the development of plans for the construction or remodeling of the                           school district s or charter school s facilities.                                Section  .    Section      A            is enacted to read                                   A       .  State Board of Education    Rulemaking authority.                                     In accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                           State Board of Education may make rules which provide standards and guidelines for resource                           conservation and resource conservation measures within the public education system.                                     The rules under Subsection     may incorporate State Building Board or federal                           standards.
