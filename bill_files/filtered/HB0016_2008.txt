General Description                                This bill amends the Medical Assistance Act in the Health Code.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the state Medicaid program to allow the use of telemedicine for certain                           services that are otherwise reimbursable under the state Medicaid plan  and                               .    provides guidelines for reimbursing for telemedicine services.
