Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends the Farmland Assessment Act to authorize a county legislative body to                           deposit certain revenues received under the Farmland Assessment Act into a                           conservation and preservation fund                                .    establishes the purposes for which revenues deposited into a conservation and                           preservation fund may be expended  and                               .    provides that a conservation and preservation fund is subject to Title   , Chapter                             , Uniform Fiscal Procedures Act for Counties.
