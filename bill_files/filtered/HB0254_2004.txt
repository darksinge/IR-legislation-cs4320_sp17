SOLID WASTE LANDFILL AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  D. Gregg Buxton                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill requires that the Solid and Hazardous Waste Control Board within the                          Department of Environmental Quality adopt rules that prohibit construction and                           demolition waste facilities within     mile of incompatible structures unless allowed by                           local zoning.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires that the Solid and Hazardous Waste Control Board establish siting criteria                           that prohibit construction and demolition facilities within     mile of residential                           areas, schools, churches, or other incompatible structures unless allowed by local                           zoning ordinances.
