RECREATIONAL LAND USE IMMUNITY                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   DeMar Bud Bowman                           AN ACT RELATING TO CITIES AND TOWNS  LIMITING THE LIABILITY OF A                          MUNICIPALITY UNDER CERTAIN CIRCUMSTANCES  AND MAKING CONFORMING                          CHANGES TO IMMUNITY PROVISIONS.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           AMENDS                                       , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                                           , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                                ENACTS                                        , Utah Code Annotated                                            , Utah Code Annotated                                            , Utah Code Annotated                                            , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                            Part  .  Limitation on Municipal Liability                                        .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Person  includes an individual, regardless of age, maturity, ability, capability, or                          experience, and a corporation, partnership, limited liability company, or any other form of business                          enterprise.                                     Undeveloped  means                                 a   substantially unchanged from its natural condition  or                                b   developed but not suitable for the sport or recreational activity without further                             development, improvement, or alteration of condition.                                    a    Without charge  means that a person is not required to pay any sum of money to                          use the land owned by the municipality for the sport or recreational activity.                                b   The determination under Subsection     a  shall be made without regard to any taxes                          or fees a person pays to the municipality for a reason other than the use of the land for the sport                          or recreational activity.                               Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  Limitation on liability.                                    A municipality that allows a person to develop, improve, or alter the condition of                          undeveloped land owned by the municipality so that it may be used by the public without charge                          for a sport or recreational activity may not be held liable for any personal injury or property                          damage resulting from the use of the land for the sport or recreational activity.                                    A municipality does not lose the protection against liability provided by Subsection                              by                                 a   issuing a building permit for an improvement on the land  or                                b   approving an improvement on the land as complying with applicable building code                          requirements.                               Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  No affect on person s obligation to use due care.                               Nothing in this part may be construed to relieve a person using the recreational facilities                          from an obligation that the person would have in the absence of this part to exercise due care in                          the use of the recreational facilities or from the legal consequences of a failure to use due care.                               Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  No affect on obligation of person or firm that improves land.                               Nothing in this part may be construed to relieve the person that develops, improves, or                          alters the condition of land owned by a municipality from an obligation that the person would have                          in the absence of this part to exercise due care in developing, improving, or altering the condition                          of the land.                               Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Waiver of immunity for injury caused by defective, unsafe, or dangerous                           condition of highways, bridges, or other structures.                                  Unless the injury arises out of one or more of the exceptions to waiver set forth in Section                                          or               , immunity from suit of all governmental entities is waived for any injury                           caused by a defective, unsafe, or dangerous condition of any highway, road, street, alley,                           crosswalk, sidewalk, culvert, tunnel, bridge, viaduct, or other structure located on them.                               Section  .    Section                is amended to read                                        .  Waiver of immunity for injury from dangerous or defective public building,                           structure, or other public improvement    Exception.                               Unless the injury arises out of one or more of the exceptions to waiver set forth in Section                                          or               , immunity from suit of all governmental entities is waived for any injury                           caused from a dangerous or defective condition of any public building, structure, dam, reservoir,                           or other public improvement.
