Section       Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER      MERCURY SWITCH REMOVAL ACT                                            Title                                This chapter is known as the  Mercury Switch Removal Act                                 Section       Section                  is enacted to read                                            Definitions                                As used in this chapter                                      Board  means the Solid and Hazardous Waste Control Board created in Section                                                                               Executive secretary  means the executive secretary of the Solid and Hazardous                          Waste Control Board appointed under Section                                                       Manufacturer  means the last person in the production or assembly process of a                          vehicle                                      Mercury switch  means a mercury containing capsule that is part of a convenience                          light switch assembly installed in a vehicle s hood or trunk                                      Person  means an individual  a firm  an association  a partnership  a corporation                           the state  or a local government                                      Plan  means a plan for removing  collecting  and recovering mercury switches                             from vehicles                                      Vehicle  means any passenger automobile or car  station wagon  truck  van  or                          sport utility vehicle that may contain one or more mercury switches                                Section       Section                  is enacted to read                                            Mercury switch collection plan                                     a   i   Except as provided by Subsection     c   each manufacturer of any vehicle                          sold within this state shall submit a plan  accompanied by a fee  to the executive secretary                                 ii   The executive secretary shall establish a fee to cover the costs of the plan s review                          and approval by following the procedures and requirements of Section                                                   b   A manufacturer may develop and submit a plan individually or in cooperation with                          other manufacturers                                 c   If the manufacturer does not know or is uncertain about whether or not a switch                          contains mercury  the plan shall presume that the switch does contain mercury                                     The plan shall include                                 a   the make  model  and year of any vehicle  including current and anticipated future                          production models  sold by the manufacturer that may contain one or more mercury switches                                 b   the description and location of each mercury switch for each make  model  and year                          of vehicle                                 c   education materials that include                                 i   safe and environmentally sound methods for mercury switch removal  and                                ii   information about hazards related to mercury and the proper handling of mercury                                 d   a method for storage and disposal of the mercury switches  including packaging and                          shipping of mercury switches to an authorized recycling  storage  or disposal facility                                 e   a procedure for the transfer of information among persons involved with the plan to                          comply with reporting requirements  and                                f   a method to implement and finance the plan  which shall include the prompt                          reimbursement by the manufacturer of costs incurred by                                 i   a person removing  collecting  or recovering mercury switches  and                                ii   the Division of Solid and Hazardous Waste for administering this chapter                                     In order to ensure that the costs of removal  collection  and recovery of mercury                          switches are not borne by any other person  the manufacturers of vehicles sold in the state shall                             pay                                 a   a minimum of    for each mercury switch removed by a person as partial                          compensation for the labor and other costs incurred in removing the mercury switch                                 b   the cost of packaging necessary to store or transport mercury switches to recycling                           storage  or disposal facilities                                 c   the cost of shipping mercury switches to recycling  storage  or disposal facilities                                 d   the cost of recycling  storage  or disposal of mercury switches                                 e   the cost of the preparation and distribution of educational materials  and                                f   the cost of maintaining all appropriate record keeping systems                                     Manufacturers of vehicles sold within this state shall reimburse a person for each                           mercury switch removed  collected  and recovered without regard to the date on which the                           mercury switch is removed  collected  or recovered                                 Section       Section
