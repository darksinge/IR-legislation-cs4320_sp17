Rosalind J  McGee                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Utah Medical Practices Act and the Utah Osteopathic Medical                           Practice Act to establish limitations on the use of noncompetition clauses in physician                           contracts                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     beginning with contracts signed after July          invalidates contracts to the                           extent that the contract                                         imposes a restriction on the right of a physician to practice medicine within a                           particular geographic area  unless the contract is for certain specific                           circumstances  or                                        imposes financial penalties on a physician for terminating a professional                           relationship                                     creates limited exceptions to the prohibition against financial penalties  and                                    permits a contract to limit a departing physician s ability to recruit patients from an                           existing practice                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                           Utah Code Annotated
