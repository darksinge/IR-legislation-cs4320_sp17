 members of which                                 a   eight members to be appointed as follows                                 i   five members from the House of Representatives  appointed by the                         reappointed under Subsection     c  every two years                                 c   when a vacancy occurs among members appointed by the governor  the replacement                          shall be appointed for the unexpired term  and                                d   one of the two members appointed by the governor shall be from a Utah industry
