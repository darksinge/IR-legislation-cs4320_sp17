                   This bill modifies the Air Conservation Act by authorizing an aggrieved person to file a                           motion for a stay of an order of the Air Quality Board if the person also posts a bond                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     authorizes aggrieved parties to file a motion requesting a stay if they post a bond                                     establishes standing requirements for a stay                                     authorizes the Board of Air Quality to enact rules governing the bond                                     directs that the bond be paid to the order recipient if the order is determined to have                           been properly issued  and                                    authorizes aggrieved parties to seek judicial review of a final order of the Board of                           Air Quality                            Monies
