                This bill modifies the State Officers and Employees Code to create the Commission on                           Civic and Character Education within the lieutenant governor s office and appropriates                           certain ongoing General Fund monies to the lieutenant governor s office for funding the                           activities of the commission                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     creates the Commission on Civic and Character Education within the lieutenant                           governor s office                                     establishes the duties of the commission to promote and ensure the implementation                           of the provisions of Section   A        related to civic and character education                                     provides that the lieutenant governor s office shall provide leadership to the                           commission                                     appropriates from the General Fund to the lieutenant governor s office as an ongoing                           appropriation  subject to future budget constraints          for fiscal year                                   for funding of the Commission on Civic and Character Education including                           leadership of the commission
