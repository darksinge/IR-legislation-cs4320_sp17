                    General Description                                This bill allows certain certified dispatchers to be covered in the public safety                           retirement systems                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     provides definitions                                     requires the state to cover its certified dispatchers under the public safety retirement                           systems                                     authorizes other participating employers to elect to cover its certified dispatchers                           under the public safety retirement systems  and                                    makes technical corrections                            Monies
