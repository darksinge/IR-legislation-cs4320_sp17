CARE FOR INMATES                                GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Chief Sponsor   Jackie Biskupski                            Senate Sponsor         Darin G  Peterson                                                       LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the chapter titled  Department of Corrections    State Prison  to                           require the department to provide appropriate health care for terminally and chronically                           ill patients  and to require that the department provide notice to any health care facilities                           where these offenders may become residents                            Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                     provides definitions                                     requires the Department of Corrections to provide for the health care needs of an                           inmate who is chronically or terminally ill  and                                    provides that if an inmate is given an early release  pardon  or parole due to a                           chronic or terminal illness  and is then subsequently to be admitted as a resident of a                           health care facility due to that illness  the department shall provide written notice to                           the administrator of the facility prior to the offender s admission                                         advising of the offender s conviction and status with the department  and                                        stating that the administrator shall provide this information to residents and staff                              days prior to admission of the offender                            Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                            Utah Code
