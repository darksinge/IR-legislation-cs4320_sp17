GOVERNMENT RECORDS ACCESS AND                           MANAGEMENT ACT REVISIONS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Douglas C. Aagard                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Government Records Access and Management Act to address                           issues related to access to and classification of records.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that a governmental entity is not required to manipulate electronic data to                           provide compilations, summaries, or other records not maintained by the                           governmental entity                                .    requires governmental entities to charge fair market value for records under certain                           circumstances                                .    allows for the sharing of private records with a private entity under a contract                           between the governmental entity and the private entity                                .    provides for certain information to be a private record  and                               .    makes technical changes.
