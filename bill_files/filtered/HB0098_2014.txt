Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends licensure provisions for optometrists                                .    amends licensure by endorsement provisions for optometrists                                .    amends the conditions and diseases for which an optometrist may prescribe an oral                           antibiotic                                .    authorizes an optometrist to continue to administer or prescribe a hydrocodone                           combination drug, even if the drug is reclassified as a Schedule II controlled                           substance under the Utah Controlled Substances Act  and                               .    makes technical changes.
