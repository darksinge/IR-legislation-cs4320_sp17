         LOCAL ELECTIONS AMENDMENTS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Merrill F. Nelson      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill prohibits a county from electing the county s elected officers through a       nonpartisan election.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   prohibits a county that provided for the election of the county s elected officers       through a partisan election in the      general election from changing to a process       that provides for the election of the county s elected officers through a nonpartisan       election.
