Enrolled                           AREA HEALTH EDUCATION CENTER AMENDMENTS                             GENERAL SESSION                      STATE OF UTAH                         Sponsor  Peter C. Knudson                         This act modifies the Health Code to remove the Department of Health from the                      responsibility of establishing area health education centers.  The act establishes five regional                      centers and makes technical changes.  The act appropriates     ,    from the General Fund                      for fiscal year         to the University of Utah Health Science Center to expand the Area                      Health Education Center Program.                      This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                       AMENDS                                 , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                          Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                           Section  .    Section               is amended to read                                  .  Rural health development initiatives.                               a   The University of Utah Health Science Center  and the department  shall use any                      appropriations it receives for developing area health education centers to establish and maintain                      an area health education center program in accordance with this section.                           b   Implementation and execution of the area health education center program is                      contingent upon appropriations from the Legislature.                               a   The area health education  centers  center program shall consist of a central                      program office , and one or more area health education centers established throughout the state.                      The purpose of the program is to establish and operate a statewide, decentralized, regional health                     professions and training program, with special emphasis on addressing rural education and training                     needs  at the University of Utah Health Science Center.  The program office shall establish and                     operate a statewide, decentralized, regional program with emphasis on addressing rural health                     professions workforce education and training needs.                           b   The area health education center program shall have five regional centers serving the                     following geographic areas                             i   the northern center serving Box Elder, Cache, Rich, Weber, and Morgan counties                            ii   the crossroads center serving Salt Lake, Wasatch, Summit, Tooele, Utah, and Davis                     counties                            iii   the central center serving Juab, Millard, Piute, Sanpete, Sevier, and Wayne counties                            iv   the eastern center serving Carbon, Daggett, Duchesne, Emery, Grand, San Juan, and                     Uintah counties  and                           v   the southwest center serving Beaver, Garfield, Iron, Kane, and Washington counties.                                The University of Utah Health Sciences Center is responsible for the development and                     maintenance of the area health education centers program.  The department shall provide assistance                     to the University of Utah Health Sciences Center for the development and maintenance of the area                     health education centers program.                                      The area health education  centers  center program shall attempt to acquire funding                      from state, local, federal, and private sources.                                     Each area health education center shall provide community based health professions                      education programming for  a defined  the geographic area described in Subsection     b  of this                     section.                          Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year         only,     ,    to                     the University of Utah Health Science Center.                              It is the intent of the Legislature that this appropriation shall be used for the Area Health                     Education Center program established in Section               .
