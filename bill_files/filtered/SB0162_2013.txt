Ipson                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions related to charging partial tuition for concurrent                           enrollment courses.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    removes a provision allowing the waiver of partial tuition when a student elects not                           to receive higher education credit                                .    allows an institution of higher education to charge a student partial tuition for                                    .    technology intensive concurrent enrollment courses  and                                   .    gateway career and technology education courses  and                               .    eliminates a provision allowing a student to pay a reduced partial tuition rate for                           each subsequent concurrent enrollment course the student takes after the student                           pays the partial tuition for the first concurrent enrollment course.
