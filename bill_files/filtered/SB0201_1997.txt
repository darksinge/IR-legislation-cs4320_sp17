Blackham         AN ACT RELATING TO HIGHER EDUCATION  PROVIDING A     ,           APPROPRIATION TO UTAH STATE UNIVERSITY TO BE USED BY THE        BIOTECHNOLOGY CENTER TO CARRY OUT AGRICULTURAL        BIOTECHNOLOGY RESEARCH PROJECTS TO ENHANCE THE STATE S        AGRICULTURAL INDUSTRIES  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.        This act enacts uncodified material.        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah             Section  .   Appropriation.                 There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year        ,     ,    to Utah        State University for the Biotechnology Center located in the Utah Experiment Station for the        purpose of carrying out agricultural biotechnology research projects to enhance the state s        agricultural industries.                 The Biotechnology Center shall use the appropriation to               a   fund projects in agricultural biotechnology              b   provide educational career and technical information training              c   offer outreach services in research techniques and procedures to researchers,        governmental agencies, and extension agents              d   develop and transfer data and technology to industry, educational institutions, and the        public  and             e   purchase equipment.            Section  .   Effective date.            This act takes effect on July  ,     .
