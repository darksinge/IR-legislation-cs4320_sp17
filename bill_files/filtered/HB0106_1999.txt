STANDARDS AND ASSESSMENT PROCESSES                           TO MEASURE STUDENT PERFORMANCE                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Susan J. Koehn                           AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC EDUCATION  REQUIRING THE STATE BOARD OF                          EDUCATION TO DEVELOP AND ESTABLISH ACADEMIC STANDARDS FOR STUDENT                          PERFORMANCE TOGETHER WITH ASSESSMENT TECHNIQUES AND INSTRUMENTS                          TO MEASURE ACHIEVEMENT OF THOSE STANDARDS  PROVIDING FOR A BASIC                           SKILLS COMPETENCY TEST TO BE ADMINISTERED IN THE TENTH GRADE WHICH                           A STUDENT MUST PASS IN ORDER TO RECEIVE A BASIC HIGH SCHOOL DIPLOMA.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                  A      , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                ENACTS                                  A      , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A           is amended to read                                   A      .  Construction of part.                                  Nothing  Except as otherwise provided in Section       A          , nothing in this part shall                           be construed to mean or represented to require that graduation from a high school or promotion                           to another grade is in any way dependent upon successful performance of any test administered                           as a part of the testing program established under this part.                                Section  .    Section      A           is enacted to read                                  A      .  Standards and assessment processes to measure student performance                             Basic skills competency test.                                    The Legislature recognizes the need for the State Board of Education to develop and                             implement standards and assessment processes to ensure that student progress is measured and that                          school boards and school personnel are accountable.                                    a   In addition to its responsibilities under Sections       A           through       A          , the                          State Board of Education, through the State Superintendent of Public Instruction, shall                                 i   develop and establish academic standards for student performance within the state s                          public education system  and                                ii   develop appropriate assessment techniques and instruments to measure the                          achievement of performance standards established under Subsection     a  i .                                b   i   As part of the assessment program required under Subsection     a  ii , the state                          board shall design a basic skills competency test to be administered in the tenth grade.                                ii   A student must pass the basic skills competency test in order to receive a basic high                          school diploma of graduation.                                iii   The state board shall include in the test, at a minimum, components on                                 A   English language arts                                 B   mathematics                                 C   science  and                                D   social studies.                                iv   A student who fails to pass all components of the test may not receive a basic high                          school diploma but may receive a certificate of completion or alternative completion diploma                          under rules made by the State Board of Education in accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah                          Administrative Rulemaking Act.                                v   The state board shall make rules to allow students who initially fail the test to retake                          all or part of the test.                                vi   The state board shall make separate provisions for testing students with disabilities,                          to include the awarding of special diplomas by local school boards to students with disabilities                          who complete the requirements of their individualized education programs.                                c   The state board shall consult with local school boards and district superintendents in                          designing and implementing the standards and assessment program required under Subsections                              a  and  b .                                    The State Board of Education shall implement the standards and assessment program                          established under this section, including the tenth grade basic skills competency test, no later than                             the beginning of the           school year.                                    The state board shall design and operate the assessment program to provide the                          Legislature, the governor, local school boards, educational administrators, teachers, parents of                          students, and the general public with timely and accurate information on student performance                          within the state s public education system.                                    The requirements of this section are to be complementary to the other achievement                          testing provisions of this part.
