Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the definition of the practice of acupuncture and changes the designation                           of  acupuncturist  to  licensed acupuncturist                                 .    modifies provisions regarding the licensing and the renewal of a license of an                           acupuncturist                                .    makes conforming changes in the manner in which the title  licensed acupuncturist                            may be used with an individual s name and practice                                .    modifies a provision dealing with presumptions related to third party insurance                           payments to a person practicing acupuncture  and                               .    makes certain technical changes.
