Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .     creates the third degree offense of assaulting a peace officer or military                           servicemember in uniform if the offender is a repeat offender or causes substantial                           bodily injury  and                               .    creates the second degree offense of assaulting a peace officer or a military                           servicemember in uniform while the offender is using a dangerous weapon or force                           likely to cause serious injury or death.
