A  After a lien claimant provides a written notice pursuant to Section                , a                           lien claimant shall file an action to enforce the lien filed under this chapter within                                 a     months from the date of final completion of the original contract not involving a                           residence as defined in Section                    or                                b       days from the date the lien claimant last performed labor and services or last                           furnished equipment or material for a residence, as defined in Section                  .                                    a   Within the time period provided for filing in Subsection     the lien claimant                           shall file for record with the county recorder of each county in which the lien is recorded a                           notice of the pendency of the action, in the manner provided in actions affecting the title or                           right to possession of real property, or the lien shall be void, except as to persons who have                           been made parties to the action and persons having actual knowledge of the commencement of                           the action.                                b   The burden of proof shall be upon the lien claimant and those claiming under him                           to show actual knowledge. Section                is enacted to read                                       .  Lien notification.                                    Before filing a mechanics lien pursuant to Section                , the person or entity                          claiming a lien shall give ten days written notice by certified mail to the person or entity to                          whom the lien claimant supplied labor, materials, equipment, or services for the improvement                          of real property, stating the amount of any claim and from whom it is due.                                    There is a rebuttable presumption that failure to file written notice as required in                          Subsection     results in a waiver of the right to file a lien pursuant to Section                .
