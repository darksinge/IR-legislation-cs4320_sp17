DESIGNATION OF FREQUENT CRASH                           HIGHWAYS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Glenn L. Way                           AN ACT RELATING TO TRANSPORTATION  PROVIDING FOR THE DESIGNATION OF                          FREQUENT CRASH HIGHWAYS.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           ENACTS                                        , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  Frequent crash highway designation.                                    A highway authority may designate a highway or portion of a highway as a frequent                          crash highway for the purpose of                                 a   informing motorists that crashes have occurred with above average frequency or                          severity on the designated highway or portion of the highway  and                                b   urging users to exercise caution while traveling on the designated highway or portion                          of the highway.                                    A highway authority shall be designated a frequent crash highway by erecting signs on                          the highway or portion of the highway.  The signs shall comply with the purposes under Subsection                             .        Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
