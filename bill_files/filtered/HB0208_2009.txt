MODIFICATION OF EXEMPTION FROM                           NONRESIDENT TUITION                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Richard A. Greenwood                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies eligibility criteria for an exemption from the nonresident portion of                           total tuition at a state institution of higher education.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    limits an exemption from the nonresident portion of total tuition at a state institution                           of higher education to students who are not employed or do not earn income in the                           United States during the calendar year without federal authorization  and                               .    makes technical corrections.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  B      , as enacted by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      B           is amended to read                                      B      .  Resident tuition    Requirements    Rules.                                     If allowed under federal law, a  A student, other than a nonimmigrant alien within                           the meaning of paragraph      of subsection  a  of Section      of Title   of the United States                           Code, shall be exempt from paying the nonresident portion of total tuition if the student                                 a   attended high school in this state for three or more years                                 b   graduated from a high school in this state or received the equivalent of a high                           school diploma in this state  and                                c   registers as an entering student at an institution of higher education not earlier than                           the fall of the         academic year.                                    In addition to the requirements under Subsection    , a student without lawful                           immigration status shall file an affidavit with the institution of higher education stating that the                           student has filed an application to legalize his immigration status, or will file an application as                           soon as he is eligible to do so.                                    a   Beginning on January  ,     , a student may not be employed or earn income in                          the United States during a calendar year that the student claims the exemption under                          Subsection     unless the student s employment is authorized by an appropriate federal agency.                                b   If a student is employed or earns income in the United States during a calendar year                          that the student claims the exemption in violation of the provisions under Subsection     a , the                          student shall be permanently ineligible for the exemption under Subsection    .                                c   A student who claims the exemption under Subsection     shall, prior to registering                          for each term or semester, file an affidavit with the institution of higher education stating that                          the student has not been employed or earned income in the United States during the calendar                          year in accordance with the requirements under this Subsection    .                                          The State Board of Regents shall make rules for the implementation of this                           section.                                          Nothing in this section limits the ability of institutions of higher education to                           assess nonresident tuition on students who do not meet the requirements under this section.
