Wheatley                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill requires a legislator to report the receipt of certain gifts, meals, and                           admissions.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines  gift                                 .    requires legislators to quarterly report the receipt of                                    .    gifts received from someone other than a relative                                    .    food and beverage for which the legislator pays less than fair market value  and                                   .    certain admissions for which the legislator pays less than fair market value                                .    applies administrative penalties to a legislator who violates gift reporting                           provisions  and                               .    makes technical changes. Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                          .  Legislator reporting of certain gifts, meals, and admissions.                                     A legislator shall file a quarterly report with the lieutenant governor on or before                           the dates listed in Subsection    .                                     a   A quarterly report required by this section is due                                  i   April   , for the period of January   through March                                     ii   July   , for the period of April   through June                                     iii   October   , for the period of July   through September     and                                 iv   January   , for the period of October   through December   .                                 b   If the due date for a quarterly report falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday,                           the report is due the following business day.                                     a   A quarterly report required by this section shall contain                                  i   a listing of any gift the legislator received during the reporting period, including the                           name of the person giving the gift and an estimate of the value of the gift                                  ii   a listing of any food and beverage received during the reporting period for which                           the legislator did not pay fair market value, including the name of the person providing the                           food and beverage and an estimate of the value of the food and beverage  and                                 iii   a listing of any admission to a sporting, recreational, or artistic event, whether as a                           spectator or participant, for which the legislator did not pay fair market value, including the                           name of the person providing the admission and an estimate of the value of the admission.                                    b   If a legislator receives no gifts, food and beverage, or admissions required to be                           reported under Subsection     a , the legislator shall file a quarterly report indicating that fact.                                     Subsection     does not apply to a gift, food and beverage, or admission provided                           by a relative.                                Section  .
