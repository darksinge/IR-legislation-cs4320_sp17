FILLING MIDTERM VACANCIES IN THE           LEGISLATURE                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  David Ure          AN ACT RELATING TO ELECTIONS  MODIFYING PROCEDURES FOR FILLING         MIDTERM VACANCIES IN THE LEGISLATURE  AND MAKING TECHNICAL         CORRECTIONS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                  A      , as enacted by Chapter  , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    A       is amended to read                  A      .  Midterm vacancies in the Legislature.                   As used in this section ,                 a    Central committee  means                 a    i   the state central committee, when the legislative district encompasses more than          one county  and                b    ii   the county central committee, when the legislative district is entirely within one          county.               b   Legislative party leader  means                i   the speaker of the House, for a representative who is a member of the majority political          party                ii   the House minority leader, for a representative who is a member of the minority          political party                iii   the Senate president, for a senator who is a member of the majority political party  or               iv   the Senate minority leader, for a senator who is a member of the minority political          party.                  When a vacancy occurs for any reason in the office of representative in the Legislature,         the  governor  legislative party leader shall fill the vacancy by appointing a person who meets the         qualifications for the office from three persons nominated by the central committee of the same         political party as the prior officeholder.                  a   When a vacancy occurs for any reason in the office of senator in the Legislature,         it shall be filled for the unexpired term at the next regular general election.              b   The  governor  legislative party leader shall fill the vacancy until the next regular         general election by appointing a person who meets the qualifications for the office from three          persons nominated by the central committee of the same political party as the prior officeholder.       Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
