AMENDMENTS TO LEGISLATIVE SALARY                           PROCESS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Merlynn T. Newbold                             This act modifies the title addressing the Legislature to require that the Legislature acts                          before legislative salaries can be increased.  The act rejects the salary increase recommended                          by the Legislative Compensation Commission and maintains the initial salary amounts paid                          effective January  ,     .  The act requires the Legislature by joint resolution to approve,                           lower, or reject the Legislative Compensation Commission s recommendation or the                           recommendation is considered rejected.  This act also makes technical changes.
