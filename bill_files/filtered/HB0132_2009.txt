SEXUAL ASSAULT VICTIM PROTOCOLS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Jackie Biskupski                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts Sexual Assault Victim Protocols within the Utah Health Code.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires a health care facility to provide to a victim of sexual assault                                     .    information regarding emergency contraception  and                                   .    emergency contraception, upon request by the victim                                .    requires a health care facility to                                    .    maintain a protocol, prepared by a physician, for the administration of                           emergency contraception at the health care facility to a victim of sexual assault                            and                                   .    develop and implement a written policy to ensure that a person is present at the                           facility, or on call, who has authority and training to comply with the                           requirements of this bill                                .    grants rulemaking authority to the Department of Health  and                               .    provides for the enforcement of the provisions of this bill by the Department of                           Health. Section         b         is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   b.  SEXUAL ASSAULT VICTIM PROTOCOLS                            Part  . General Provisions                                     b    .  Title.                               This chapter is known as  Sexual Assault Victim Protocols.                                Section  .    Section         b         is enacted to read                                     b    .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Emergency contraception  means the use of a substance or device, approved by the                          United States Food and Drug Administration, to prevent pregnancy after sexual intercourse.                                     Health care facility  is as defined in Section                .                                     Sexual assault  means any criminal conduct described in Title   , Chapter  , Part                           , Sexual Offenses, that may result in a pregnancy.                                     Victim of sexual assault  means any person who presents to receive, or receives,                          medical care at a health care facility in consequence of being subjected to sexual assault.                               Section  .    Section         b         is enacted to read                            Part  .  Emergency Contraception Services                                     b    .  Emergency contraception services for a victim of sexual assault.                               A health care facility shall provide the following services to a victim of sexual assault                                     provide the victim with written and oral medical information regarding emergency                          contraception that is unbiased, accurate, and generally accepted by the medical community as                             being scientifically valid                                     orally inform the victim of sexual assault that the victim may obtain emergency                          contraception at the health care facility                                     offer a complete regimen of emergency contraception to a victim of sexual assault                                     provide, at the health care facility, emergency contraception to the victim of sexual                          assault upon her request                                     maintain a protocol, prepared by a physician, for the administration of emergency                          contraception at the health care facility to a victim of sexual assault  and                                    develop and implement a written policy to ensure that a person is present at the                          facility, or on call, who                                 a   has authority to dispense or prescribe emergency contraception, independently, or                          under the protocol described in Subsection    , to a victim of sexual assault  and                                b   is trained to comply with the requirements of this section.                               Section  .    Section         b         is enacted to read                            Part  .  Investigation and Enforcement                                     b    .  Investigation and enforcement.                                    The department may make rules, in accordance with Title   G, Chapter  , Utah                          Administrative Rulemaking Act, to enforce the provisions of this chapter.                                    The department shall, in an expeditious manner, investigate any complaint received                          by the department regarding the failure of a health care facility to comply with a requirement of                          this chapter.
