EXPUNGEMENT OF JUVENILE RECORDS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Scott  Daniels                             This act modifies the Juvenile Court Act by clarifying the steps necessary to expunge a                          record in the Juvenile Court.  It requires that the petitioner be over    years of age, have                          completed all court requirements, and not have an adult criminal record.   The statute is                          further modified by requiring notification of victims and allowing for expungement of                          nonjudicial closures.
