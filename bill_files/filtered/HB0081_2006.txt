Bell Teaching Incentive                           Loans program                                .    gives the State Board of Regents sole authority to make standard awards                                .    modifies prioritization criteria                                .    allows the State Board of Education to select certain recipients, subject to program                           appropriation levels  and                               .    makes technical changes.
