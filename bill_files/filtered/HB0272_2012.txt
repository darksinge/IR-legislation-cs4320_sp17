PILOT PROGRAM FOR INSURANCE COVERAGE FOR                           AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDERS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor  Ronda Rudd Menlove                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Public Employees  Benefit and Insurance Program Act to create a                           pilot program to provide coverage for treatment of autism spectrum disorders.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires the Public Employees  Benefit and Insurance Program to establish a pilot                           program to provide coverage for treatment of autism spectrum disorders                                .    describes minimum coverage amounts and limits for the autism coverage required                           by this bill                                .    provides for annual adjustment of the coverage amounts described in the preceding                           paragraph, based on the Consumer Price Index during the pilot program  and                               .    sunsets the pilot program on July  ,     . Beginning July  ,     , an employee benefit plan that provides a health insurance                          benefit shall provide coverage for treatment of autism spectrum disorders in accordance with                          the requirements of this section.                                    The office shall set minimum standards of coverage for the treatment of autism                          spectrum disorders which establish durational limits, amount limits, deductibles, and                          coinsurance for the treatment of autism spectrum disorders that are similar to, or identical to,                          the coverage provided for other illnesses or diseases.                                    Subject to Subsection    , an accident or health insurance policy that provides a                          health insurance benefit shall provide coverage for the provision of early intensive behavior                          therapy for a person with an autism spectrum disorder of at least                                 a      ,    annually for a child who is less than nine years old  and                                b      ,    annually for a child who is at least nine years old, but less than    years                          old.                                    Beginning on July  ,     , the commissioner has the authority to annually adjust                          the amounts described in Subsection     by a percentage equal to the percentage difference                          between the consumer price index for the current calendar year and the consumer price index                          for the preceding calendar year.
