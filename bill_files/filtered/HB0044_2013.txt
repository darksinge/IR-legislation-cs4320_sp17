General Description                                This bill enacts and amends provisions in Title   A, Chapter   , Campaign and                           Financial Reporting Requirements, regarding polls.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires the disclosure of the person who pays for a poll regarding a candidate or                           ballot proposition  and                               .    makes technical changes.
