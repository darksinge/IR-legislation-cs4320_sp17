Stuart Adams                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill addresses partial releases and reconveyances of real property.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    expands the definition of  delivery  applicable to reconveyances and releases of real                           property                                .    eliminates the need to record a copy of a release or reconveyance of a mortgage or                           trust deed                                .    outlines the method of partially reconveying or partially releasing real property                           subject to a trust deed or mortgage                                .    provides forms for certain documents required to partially reconvey or partially                           release real property subject to a trust deed or mortgage  and                               .    makes technical changes.
