Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Administrator  means the executive director of the Department of Community and                           Economic Development or the executive director s designee.                                     Board  means the Board of Business and Economic Development.                                     Company creating an economic impediment  means a company that discourages                           economic development within a reasonable radius of its location because of                                 a   odors                                 b   noise                                 c   pollution                                 d   health hazards  or                                e   other activities similar to those described in Subsections     a  through  d .                                     Economic opportunities  means unique business situations or community                          circumstances which lend themselves to the furtherance of the economic interests of the state                          by providing a catalyst or stimulus to the growth or retention, or both, of commerce and                          industry in the state.                                           Economically disadvantaged rural area  means a geographic area designated                           by the board under Section                 .                                           Fund  means the restricted account known as the Industrial Assistance Fund                           created in Section                 .                                           Replacement company  means a company locating its business or part of its                           business in a location vacated by a company creating an economic impediment.                                           Targeted industry  means an industry or group of industries targeted by the                           board, under Section                 , for economic development in the state.                                  Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Industrial Assistance Fund created.                                    There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the                           Industrial Assistance Fund of which                                 a   up to     shall be used in economically disadvantaged rural areas .   and                                b   up to     may be used to take timely advantage of economic opportunities as they                          arise.                                    The fund shall be administered by the administrator under the policy direction of                           the board.                                     The administrator may hire appropriate support staff.                                    The cost of administering the fund shall be paid from monies in the fund.                                    Interest accrued from investment of monies in the fund shall remain in the fund.                               Section  .
