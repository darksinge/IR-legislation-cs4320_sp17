Dee                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Criminal Code and requires identification and related procedures                           for the sale of specified metals and imposes penalties for participating in transactions                           involving these metals without providing appropriate identification.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines  regulated metals                                 .    specifies identification that must be provided in order to sell regulated metals to                           dealers                                .    requires that dealers post a sign advising sellers that they must provide identification                           in compliance with state law                                .    requires that dealers keep a written or electronic log of identification and sales for                           not less than one year                                .    requires authorization from a governmental entity in order to sell certain regulated                           metals such as manhole covers                                .    requires authorization of the owner in order to sell specified regulated metals that                           are defined as  suspect metals,  such as copper and grave site vases                                .    allows a law enforcement agency to place a hold up to    days if the agency                           believes the metal is stolen                                .    provides an exemption for businesses with established accounts who maintain                           specified records with the dealer                                 .    provides that violation of these requirements by the dealer or the seller is a class C                              misdemeanor  and                               .    repeals the current identification and records requirements for dealers in junk other                           than regulated metals.
