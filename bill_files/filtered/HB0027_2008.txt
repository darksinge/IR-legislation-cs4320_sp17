General Description                                This bill modifies the State Officers and Employees title by amending powers of the                           lieutenant governor.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    allows the lieutenant governor and designated employees to administer oaths.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides an immediate effective date.
