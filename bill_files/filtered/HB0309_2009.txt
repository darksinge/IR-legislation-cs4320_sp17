COUNTY FISCAL PROCEDURES AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Christopher N. Herrod                                Senate Sponsor             Curtis S. Bramble                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies provisions relating to county fiscal procedures.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies a provision relating to transferring an unencumbered or unexpended                           appropriation balance or incurring an excess expenditure to                                    .    eliminate the need for the budget officer s consent and require instead the                           officer s review  and                                   .    require the approval of the county legislative body                                .    modifies a provision requiring county officers to be paid monthly to allow officers                           to be paid monthly, semi monthly, or bi weekly, as determined by the county                           legislative body  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                        , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                              , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Salaries paid out of general fund.                               The salaries of county officers  must  shall be paid monthly, semi monthly, or                          bi weekly, as determined by the county legislative body, out of the general fund or the salary                           fund, as the case may be, of the county upon the order of the county legislative body.                               Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Transfer of unexpended appropriation balance by department.                                With the consent of                                     After review by the budget officer ,  and with the approval of the county legislative                          body, any department may                                 a   transfer any unencumbered or unexpended appropriation balance or any part from                           one expenditure account to another within the department during the budget year ,   or                                b   incur an excess expenditure of one or more line items  may be permitted  provided,                          that .                                    A transfer or expenditure under Subsection     may not occur if the transfer or                          expenditure would cause the total of all excess expenditures or encumbrances  does not  to                           exceed the total unused appropriation within the department at the close of the budget period.
