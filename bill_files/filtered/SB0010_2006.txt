Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that a voter whose identity or right to vote is challenged shall be issued a                           provisional ballot and be permitted to vote                                .    removes the procedure requiring an affidavit to be signed in order to obtain a ballot                           when a voter s identity or right to vote is challenged                                .    removes criminal penalties associated with the affidavit requirement                                .    provides a criminal penalty for knowingly providing false information when voting                           using a provisional ballot  and                               .    makes technical changes.
