Perry             LONG TITLE      General Description             This bill increases the administrative fee for impounding a vehicle.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   increases the administrative fee for impounding a vehicle due to an arrest, citation,       or referral for administrative action for driving under the influence or reckless       driving  and                  allocates the funds from the increased impound fee to the Department of Public       Safety Restricted Account.
