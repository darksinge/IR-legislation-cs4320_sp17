TASK FORCE STUDYING NATURAL                           DISASTERS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Todd E. Kiser                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill creates the Task Force Studying Natural Disasters.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Task Force Studying Natural Disasters                                .    specifies membership, duties, and powers of the task force                                .    appropriates money from the General Fund to fund the task force  and                               .    repeals the task force on November   ,     .                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates for fiscal year      only                                 .      ,    to the Senate  and                                .       ,    to the House of Representatives.                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed November   ,     .                           Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Studying Natural Disasters     Creation    Membership    Interim rules                              followed    Compensation    Staff.                                    There is created the Task Force Studying Natural Disasters consisting of the                          following eight members                                 a   three members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          two of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   five members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives, no more than four of whom may be from the same political party.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed                          under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force.                                    In conducting its business, the task force shall comply with the rules of legislative                          interim committees.                                    Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force shall be paid in accordance                          with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the task force.                               Section  .   Duties    Interim report.                                    The task force shall review and make recommendations on the following issues                                 a   statewide insurance coverage for a natural disaster                                b   state and local government preparedness for a natural disaster  and                               c   the economic impact of a natural disaster.                                    The task force shall submit a final report, including any proposed legislation, to the                          Business and Labor Interim Committee before November   ,     .                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year         only                                       ,    to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the                          task force  and                                       ,    to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses                          of representatives on the task force.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                                  This bill is repealed November   ,     .
