GERIATRIC CARE MANAGERS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Rebecca D. Lockhart                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Nurse Practice Act to certify licensed practical nurses who have                          additional training in long term care nursing as geriatric care managers and to repeal                           provisions in the Nurse Practice Act related to health care assistants.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines a geriatric care manager and the practice of geriatric care management                                .    creates a pilot program for certification of geriatric care managers                                .    specifies qualifications for geriatric care managers                                .    repeals provisions related to health care assistants                                .    amends provisions related to unlawful conduct to incorporate geriatric care                           managers                                .    establishes standards for geriatric care manager training  and                               .    sunsets the Geriatric Care Manager Pilot Program on July  ,     .
