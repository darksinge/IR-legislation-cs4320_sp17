Membership        legislators       non legislators                                   Legislative Vote       voting for      voting against      absent                           General Description                                This bill modifies provisions relating to development exactions of counties and                           municipalities.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    enacts a definition of  water interest                                 .    places limitations and restrictions on a county or municipality s imposition of an                           exaction for a water interest  and                               .    requires culinary water authorities to provide the basis for its calculations of                           projected water right requirements.
