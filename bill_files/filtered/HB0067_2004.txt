CULTURAL AFFAIRS TASK FORCE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Sheryl L. Allen                             Ralph  BeckerDavid  Clark                                                    LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the Cultural Affairs Task Force.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    establishes the membership of the task force and provides for compensation of                           legislative members                                .    outlines the duties of the task force                                .    provides for staffing of the task force  and                               .    requires the task force to give a final report to the Workforce Services and                           Community and Economic Development Interim Committee.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates for fiscal year         only                                .      ,    to the Senate                                .      ,    to the House of Representatives  and                               .       ,    to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel. Uncodified Material Affected                            ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIAL                                                       Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Cultural Affairs Task Force    Creation    Membership    Quorum                              Compensation    Staff.                                    There is created the Cultural Affairs Task Force consisting of the following                             members                                 a   two members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          one of whom may be from the same political party                                 b   four members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives, no more than two of whom may be from the same political party                                 c   two members from the Department of Community and Economic Development                                 i   the first being the department s executive director or the director s designee  and                                ii   the second being an individual designated by the executive director  and                                d   three members from the general public with backgrounds or expertise in the culture                          and arts industry appointed by the governor.                                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed                          under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force.                                    a   A majority of the members of the task force constitute a quorum.                                b   The action of a majority of a quorum constitutes the action of the task force.                                    a   Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force who are legislators shall                          be paid in accordance with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                b   A member of the task force who is not a legislator may not receive compensation                          for the member s work associated with the task force, but may receive per diem and                          reimbursement for travel expenses incurred as a member of the task force at the rates                          established by the Division of Finance under Sections       A           and       A          .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the task force.                               Section  .   Duties    Interim report.                                    The task force shall review and make recommendations on the following issues                                 a   an inventory of the state s arts and culture industry                                    b   the economic impact of the arts and culture industry on the state                                 c   strategic approaches to coordinating and enhancing the arts and culture industry,                          including tourism and the film industry, through increased focus, consideration, and                          investment  and                                d   other issues that may be recommended by the task force including the merits of                          available prior and ongoing studies and reports done by others.                                    A final report, including any proposed legislation shall be presented to the                          Workforce Services and Community and Economic Development Interim Committee before                          November   ,     .
