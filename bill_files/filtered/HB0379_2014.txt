PROPOSED TAX INCREASE AND BOND PROPOSITION                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Ryan D. Wilcox                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill provides requirements for certain ballot propositions and tax increases.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    provides for the submission and posting of arguments in favor of and against certain                           ballot propositions or tax increases                                .    requires a governing body of a taxing entity to conduct a public meeting to allow                           interested parties to                                    .    present arguments in favor of and against certain ballot propositions or tax                           increases  and                                   .    provide oral testimony regarding the ballot proposition or tax increase  and                               .    requires a governing body of a taxing entity to provide remote access to the public                           meeting. Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                            Part   .  Proposed Tax Increase and Bond Proposition Act                                         .  Title.                               This part is known as the  Proposed Tax Increase and Bond Proposition Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                     a    Ballot proposition  means                                 i   an opinion question or other question concerning a tax increase submitted to voters                          for their approval or rejection  or                                ii   a question submitted to voters concerning the issuance of bonds under Section                                          .                                b    Ballot proposition  does not include an initiative or referendum authorized under                          Title   A, Chapter  , Issues Submitted to the Voters.                                     Determination date  means the earlier of the date                                 a   of an election at which a ballot proposition is considered by voters  or                                b   a governing body adopts a tax increase by a resolution or ordinance.                                     Election officer  is as defined in Section      A           .                                     Governing body  is as defined in Section                 .                                     Remote access  or  remotely accessed  means the ability of an individual to access                          the proceedings of a public meeting described under this part via the Internet when the                          individual is not physically present at the public meeting.                                     Tax increase  means                                 a   for a property tax levied under Chapter  , Property Tax Act, a tax rate that exceeds                          the taxing entity s certified tax rate, as defined in Section                   or                                b   for a sales and use tax imposed under Chapter   , Sales and Use Tax Act, a sales                          and use tax rate that                                 i   is not currently imposed  or                                ii   exceeds the sales and use tax rate that is currently imposed.                                     Taxing entity  means                                 a   a taxing entity as defined in Section                   or                                   b   a county, city, or town authorized to impose a sales and use tax under Chapter   ,                          Sales and Use Tax Act.                                     Eligible voter  means a person who                                 a   has registered to vote in accordance with Title   A, Chapter  , Voter Registration                           and                                b   is a resident of a voting district or precinct within the taxing entity that is holding                          an election to consider a ballot proposition.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Applicability of part.                               A taxing entity may not submit a ballot proposition or adopt a tax increase by resolution                           or ordinance unless the taxing entity complies with this part.
