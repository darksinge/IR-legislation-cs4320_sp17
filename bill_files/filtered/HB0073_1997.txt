REGULATION OF SALE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Wayne A.  Harper          AN ACT RELATING TO HEALTH AND COMMERCE  ALLOWING LOCAL AUTHORITIES         TO PROHIBIT OR REGULATE THE SALE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                 B      , Utah Code Annotated                      B      , Utah Code Annotated                      B      , Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section    B       is enacted to read            TITLE   B.  TOBACCO CONTROL ACT            CHAPTER  .  LOCAL REGULATION FOR TOBACCO SALES                  B      .  Definitions.              As used in this chapter                     Cigarette  means any roll for smoking made wholly or in part of tobacco, irrespective          of size or shape, and whether or not such tobacco is flavored, adulterated, or mixed with any other          ingredient, the wrapper or cover of which is made of paper or any other substance or material          except tobacco.                    Local authority  means                a   the county legislative body of the county if the premises are located in an          unincorporated area of a county  or               b   the governing body of the city or town if the premises are located in an incorporated          city or town.                    Retailer  means the holder of a permit issued by a local authority to allow the holder          to engage in the sale of tobacco products to consumers.                   Tobacco products  means all products made of, or containing tobacco, including         cigarettes, that are intended for smoking, inhaling, chewing, or any other form of ingestion.             Section  .  Section    B       is enacted to read                 B      .  Local permitting for sale of tobacco products.                  A local authority may               a   i   prohibit the retail sale of tobacco  or              ii   require a permit for the retail sale of tobacco and may issue, renew, suspend, and         revoke the permit based on enacted local ordinances  and               b   otherwise regulate the retail sale of tobacco products.                   A local authority s suspension or revocation of a permit issued under this section          prohibits the retailer whose permit is suspended or revoked from continuing to operate under any          other license, permit, or other authority granted by the local authority or the state.                   A local authority may not grant a permit under this chapter to sell tobacco within the          proximity of any church or school.  The local authority shall define the proximity, and shall apply          the definition in a uniform and consistent manner.              Section  .  Section    B       is enacted to read                  B      .  Effect of chapter.              This chapter does not affect the state taxation or licensure regarding tobacco products under          Title   , Chapter   ,  Cigarette and Tobacco Tax and Licensing Act.       Legislative Review Note      as of                PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
