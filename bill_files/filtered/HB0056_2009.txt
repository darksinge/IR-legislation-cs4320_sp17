General Description                                This bill modifies the Election Code by amending the dates for filing a declaration of                           candidacy for general elections.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends dates for filling midterm vacancies for county or district attorney                                .    changes filing a declaration of candidacy and a petition of nomination dates for                           officers elected during a general election from between March   and March    to                           between the second Friday and the third Friday in March  and                               .    makes technical changes.
