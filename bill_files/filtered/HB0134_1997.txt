SODOMY AMENDMENTS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  David Ure          AN ACT RELATING TO THE CRIMINAL CODE  PROVIDING THAT SODOMY BETWEEN         CONSENTING MARRIED PERSONS IS NOT A CRIMINAL OFFENSE  AND PROVIDING         THAT SODOMY IS A THIRD DEGREE FELONY UNLESS THE VICTIM IS    YEARS         OF AGE OR OLDER OR THE PERPETRATOR IS MORE THAN THREE YEARS OLDER         THAN THE VICTIM.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                        , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Sodomy    Forcible sodomy.                  A person commits sodomy when the actor engages in any sexual act with a person who          is not that person s spouse, and who is    years of age or older involving the genitals of one person          and mouth or anus of another person, regardless of the sex of either participant.                   A person commits forcible sodomy when the actor commits sodomy upon another          without the other s consent.                   a    Sodomy is a class B misdemeanor. Forcible sodomy is a felony of the first degree.           Forcible sodomy is a first degree felony.               b   Sodomy is a third degree felony except when, at the time of the offense, the victim was             years of age or older, or the actor was not more than three years older than the victim, in which          case it is a class B misdemeanor.                   Evidence that the actor was not more than three years older than the victim at the time          of the offense shall be raised by the defendant.       Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
