Enrolled                               EXPLOITATION OF A VULNERABLE ADULT                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Lyle W. Hillyard                                House Sponsor            Scott L Wyatt                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Utah Human Services Code to provide that a court may, under                           certain circumstances, award court costs and reasonable attorney fees to a prevailing                           party in an action for exploitation of a vulnerable adult.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that, if the plaintiff prevails in an action for exploitation of a vulnerable                           adult, the court may order the defendant to pay the court costs and reasonable                           attorney fees of the plaintiff  and                               .    provides that, if the defendant prevails in an action for exploitation of a vulnerable                           adult, the court may order the plaintiff to pay the court costs and reasonable attorney                           fees of the defendant, if the action was frivolous, unreasonable, or taken in bad                           faith.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  A      , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                            Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A           is amended to read                                   A      .  Private right of action    Estate asset    Attorney fees.                                    A vulnerable adult who suffers harm or financial loss as a result of exploitation has                           a private right of action against the perpetrator.                                    Upon the death of a vulnerable adult, any cause of action under this section shall                           constitute an asset of the estate of the vulnerable adult.                                    If the plaintiff prevails in an action brought under this section, the court may order                          that the defendant pay the costs and reasonable attorney fees of the plaintiff.                                    If the defendant prevails in an action brought under this section, the court may                          order that the plaintiff pay the costs and reasonable attorney fees of the defendant, if the court                          finds that the action was frivolous, unreasonable, or taken in bad faith.
