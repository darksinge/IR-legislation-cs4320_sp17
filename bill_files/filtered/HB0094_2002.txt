SPECIAL FUEL TAX AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Ben C. Ferry                             This act modifies the Revenue and Taxation Code by amending reporting and special fuel                          user permit requirements for certain users of special fuel.  This act exempts a user of special                          fuel from reporting requirements if the tax has already been paid and or the tax is paid                          under the interstate fuel tax agreement.  This act takes effect January  ,     .
