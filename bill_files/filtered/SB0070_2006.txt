Stuart Adams                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies legislative and gubernatorial approval requirements for the disposal                           of certain wastes.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies the legislative and gubernatorial approval requirements for the disposal of                           certain commercial radioactive wastes, hazardous wastes, and nonhazardous solid                           wastes                                .    requires that legislative approval be provided by statute                                .    provides that gubernatorial approval is given if the statute giving legislative                           approval is not vetoed  and                               .    provides that gubernatorial approval is not required if the governor vetoes the                           statute giving legislative approval and the veto is overridden.
