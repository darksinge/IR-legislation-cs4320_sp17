JOINT RESOLUTION ON JAIL FACILITIES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Richard A. Greenwood                                Senate Sponsor             Ralph Okerlund                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This joint resolution of the Legislature supports contracting for beds for state inmates at                           correctional facilities in the counties of Tooele, Weber, Davis, Iron, Kane, Emery,                           Grand, and Garfield.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This resolution                                .    supports jail expansion as beneficial to both the state and the counties through a                           contract with the Utah Department of Corrections  and                               .    approves contracting for beds dedicated to housing state inmates in the counties of                           Tooele, Weber, Davis, Iron, Kane, Emery, Grand, and Garfield.                           Special Clauses                                None                                                    Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                WHEREAS, counties in Utah contract with the Utah Department of Corrections to                           provide prison bed space for state prisoners                                WHEREAS, this contracting arrangement has proved beneficial to both the state and                           the counties and saved taxpayers from the burden of building new state correctional facilities
