PROPERTY TAX MODIFICATIONS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   John G. Mathis                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill makes changes to Farmland Assessment Act rollback tax provisions.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    removes the Farmland Assessment Act rollback tax requirement under certain                           conditions related to a mineral rights owner exercising the right to extract minerals                            and                               .    makes technical and conforming changes.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill has retrospective operation to January  ,     .
