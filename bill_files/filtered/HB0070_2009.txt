General Description                                This bill amends the Cigarette and Tobacco Tax and Licensing Act relating to                           exemptions from the state tax on cigarettes.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    amends a citation to the Internal Revenue Code relating to federal law exemptions                           that also apply to the state tax on cigarettes  and                               .    makes technical changes.
