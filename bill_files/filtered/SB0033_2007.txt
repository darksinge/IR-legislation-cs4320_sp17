Newbold                                                         LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by creating a special group license plate for                           the parents, spouse, or siblings of a person who is killed while serving in the United                           States armed forces.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates a special group license plate for the parents, spouse, or siblings of a person                           who is killed while serving in the United States armed forces  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on October  ,     .
