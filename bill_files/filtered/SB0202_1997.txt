Enrolled                PROCUREMENT CODE   DESIGN BUILD AMENDMENTS                GENERAL SESSION         STATE OF UTAH          Sponsor  Lane Beattie         AN ACT RELATING TO PROCUREMENT  CLARIFYING PROCEDURES FOR        CONTRACTS FOR DESIGN BUILD HIGHWAY PROJECTS  MAKING TECHNICAL        CORRECTIONS  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.        This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows         AMENDS                      . , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah             Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah             Section  .  Section          .  is amended to read                      . .   Procurement of design build highway project contracts.                 As used in this section              a    Department  means the Department of Transportation.             b    Design build highway project contract  means the procurement of both the design and        construction of a highway project in a single contract with a company or combination of        companies capable of providing the necessary engineering services and construction.                 The Department of Transportation may              a   award a design build highway project contract by following the requirements of this        section  and             b   make rules, by following the procedures and requirements of Title   , Chapter   a,        Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, establishing requirements for the procurement of        design build highway project contracts in addition to those required by this section.                 a   Before entering a design build highway project contract, the department may issue        a request for qualifications to prequalify potential contractors.             b   Public notice of the request for qualifications shall be given in accordance with policy        board rules.             c   The department shall require, as part of the qualifications specified in the request for          qualifications, that potential contractors at least demonstrate their              i   construction experience              ii   design experience              iii   financial, manpower, and equipment resources available for the project  and             iv   experience in other design build highway projects with attributes similar to the project        being procured.             d   The request for qualifications shall identify the number of eligible competing  bidders         proposers that the department will select to submit a proposal, which must be at least two.                 a   The department shall              i   evaluate the responses received from the request for qualifications              ii   select from their number those qualified to submit proposals  and             iii   invite those respondents to submit proposals based upon the department s request for        proposals.             b   If the department fails to receive at least two qualified eligible competing  bidders         proposers, the department shall readvertise the project.                 The department shall issue a request for proposals to those qualified respondents that              a   includes a scope of work statement constituting an information for  bid  proposal that        may include              i   preliminary design concepts              ii   design criteria, needs, and objectives              iii   warranty and quality control requirements              iv   applicable standards              v   environmental documents              vi   constraints              vii   time expectations or limitations              viii   incentives or disincentives  and             ix   other special considerations              b   requires submitters to provide                      i   a sealed cost proposal              ii   a critical path matrix schedule, including cash flow requirements              iii    bid  proposal security  and             iv   other items required by the department for the project  and             c   may include award of a stipulated fee to be paid to submitters who submit unsuccessful         bids  proposals.                 The department shall              a   evaluate the submissions received in response to the request for proposals from the        prequalified  bidders  proposers   and              b   comply with rules relating to discussion of proposals, best and final offers, and        evaluations of the proposals submitted  and              b     c   after considering price and other identified factors, award the contract to the         lowest  responsible  bidder  proposer whose proposal is most advantageous to the state.            Section  .   Effective date.            If approved by two thirds of all the members elected to each house, this act takes effect upon        approval by the governor, or the day following the constitutional time limit of Utah Constitution        Article VII, Section  , without the governor s signature, or in the case of a veto, the date of veto        override.
