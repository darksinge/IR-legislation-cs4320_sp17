LAW ENFORCEMENT DRUG TASK FORCE                           FUNDING                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Eric K. Hutchings                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies Title   , Chapter   a, Criminal Justice and Substance Abuse, to                           create a restricted account to fund grants for law enforcement task forces.  This bill also                           appropriates   ,   ,    from the General Fund to the restricted account for the                                   fiscal year only.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Law Enforcement Drug Task Force Restricted Account to fund law                           enforcement drug task forces in conjunction with available federal funding                                .    provides that    of the criminal fines surcharge be allocated to the Law                           Enforcement Drug Task Force                                .    creates the Statewide Law Enforcement Drug Task Force Grant Program within the                           Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice and requires that the commission                           administer the program and establish procedures for application and awarding the                           task force grants                                .    requires grant recipients to report to the commission on the use of the grants  and                               .    requires that the commission report to the Legislature annually regarding the grant                           program.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                   This bill appropriates                                .    for fiscal year         only,   ,   ,    from the General Fund to the Law                           Enforcement Drug Task Force Restricted Account.                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on July  ,     .                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                     a    , Utah Code Annotated                                         a   , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section         a         is enacted to read                                     a    .  Statewide Drug Law Enforcement Task Force Grant Program                              Funding and administration.                                    As used in this section                                 a    Commission  means the Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice.                                b    Task force  means a multijurisdictional law enforcement task force or other                          agency comprised of persons who are employed by or acting under the authority of different                          governmental authorities, including federal, state, county or municipal governments, or any                          combination of these agencies, to enforce state controlled substance laws.                                c    Task force grant program  means the Statewide Law Enforcement Drug Task                          Force Grant Program created in this section.                                    a   There is created within the commission the Statewide Law Enforcement Drug                          Task Force Grant Program to provide grant funding for the operation of task forces.                                b   The commission shall, in accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah                          Administrative Rulemaking Act, make rules establishing requirements, forms, and procedures                          for the application for and the awarding of task force grants under this section.                                    The funding for the task force grant program shall be                                 a   federal funding provided to the state for the purpose of operating task forces  and                                b   an allocation to the Law Enforcement Drug Task Force Restricted Account created                          and funded under Section          a       .                                       The commission shall use funding under Subsection                                     a   to award to task forces for operating expenses under the task force grant program                           and                                b   to provide necessary staff support and administrative costs of administering the task                          force grant program.                                    Each agency or political subdivision that receives a task force grant shall submit to                          the commission, no later than    months after receipt of the grant, a report in a form specified                          by the commission.  The report shall include regarding each grant                                 a   the agency s name                                 b   the amount of the grant                                 c   the date of the grant                                 d   how the grant has been used, including functions of the task force  and                                e   a statement signed by both the agency s or political subdivision s executive officer                          or designee and by the agency s legal counsel, that the task force grant was used for the law                          enforcement purposes specified in the grant application.                                    The commission shall report in writing to the legislative Law Enforcement and                          Criminal Justice Interim Committee annually on or before December   regarding the grants                          made under this section.
