MOBILE HOME PARK RESIDENCY ACT                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Carl W. Duckworth                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Mobile Home Park Residency Act.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides a right of first refusal to a mobile home park residents  association prior to                           the sale of a mobile home park.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                        , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  Sale of a mobile home park.                                    If a mobile home park residents  association has been formed within a mobile home                          park pursuant to Section                 , the owner of the mobile home park shall comply with                          Subsection     prior to selling the mobile home park.                                       An owner of a mobile home park described in Subsection     shall                                 a   provide written notice, including the sale price, terms, and conditions, through                          certified mail to the mobile home park residents  association if the owner receives an offer to                          purchase the mobile home park  and                                b   sell the mobile home park to the mobile home park residents  association if, within                             calendar days after the day on which the owner sent the notice described in Subsection                              a  by certified mail, the mobile home park residents  association provides to the owner                                 i   a fully executed purchase and sale agreement with a sale price, terms, and                          conditions at least as favorable to the owner as the offer contained in the notice described in                          Subsection     a   and                                ii   a deposit of money equal to at least    of the purchase price.
