PEHP RISK POOL AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   David Clark                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Public Employees  Benefit and Insurance Program Act by                           amending provisions related to the establishment of certain risk pools.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires the Public Employees  Benefit and Insurance Program  PEHP  to include                           employees of higher education, public education, and employees of an independent                           entity in the state employee s risk pool.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                         , as renumbered and amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                  is amended to read                                          .  Establishment of separate risk pools.                                    The program shall establish  separate  the following risk pools  for                                     a    state  one for employees   and  of                                 i   the state                                 ii   the state system of higher education designated under Section       B                                           iii   the state s system of public education under Title   A, State System of Public                          Education  and                                iv   independent entities as defined in Section       E            and                                b   one for the Utah Children s Health Insurance Program.                                    The program shall create risk pools for other covered employers separate from                           those created in Subsection     as determined by the program.
