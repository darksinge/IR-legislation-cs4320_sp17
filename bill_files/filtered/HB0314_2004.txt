CONSUMER PROTECTION REVISIONS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  John Dougall                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill enacts the Electronic Privacy Act within the Commerce and Trade Code.                          Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines terms                                .    requires a person who manufactures, distributes, or sells a product with a unique                           electronic identifier or product location information to provide notice that meets                           certain criteria  and                               .    provides a civil cause of action for a violation.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill takes effect on January  ,     . Section                  is enacted to read                            CHAPTER   .  ELECTRONIC PRIVACY ACT                            Part  .  General Provisions                                         .  Title.                               This chapter is known as the  Electronic Privacy Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this chapter                                      Identified product  means a product that is available for retail purchase and                                 a   contains or bears a unique electronic identifier  or                                b   provides product location information.                                     Product location information  means information based on a unique electronic                          identifier                                 a   providing                                 i   global positioning information of an identified product  or                                ii   relative positioning information of an identified product  and                                b   that may be                                 i   transmitted external to an identified product  or                                ii   determined using remote product location techniques that are based on a unique                          electronic identifier.                                     Unique electronic identifier  means an identification number that                                 a   may be electronically transmitted external to an identified product  and                                b   is                                 i   universally unique  or                                ii   unique within the context in which the unique electronic identifier is used. Consumer disclosure requirements.                                    A person shall comply with Subsection     if the person manufactures, distributes,                          or sells an identified product.                                    A person described in Subsection     shall provide notice                                    a   i   on an identified product  or                                ii   A   for a person who sells an identified product, conspicuously posted on the                          premises at which a consumer purchases an identified product  or                                B   for a person who manufactures or distributes an identified product, by including                          with the identified product a notice that a seller may use to satisfy Subsection     a  ii  A   and                                b   informing a consumer who purchases an identified product                                 i   of the existence of a unique electronic identifier on or within the identified product                                 ii   how the consumer may obtain additional information about the unique electronic                          identifier, including                                 A   if possible, how the consumer may discard, disable, or remove the unique                          electronic identifier  or                                B   if the unique electronic identifier A person does not violate this section if the person                                 a   sells an identified product to a consumer in violation of this section  and                                b   allows the consumer to return the identified product for a full refund of the                          purchase price.                                    A person commits a single violation of this section with respect to identified                          products                                 a   sold to the same consumer  and                                b   that are identical or substantially similar.
