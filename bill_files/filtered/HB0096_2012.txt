Section                  is enacted to read                            Part   .  Utah Automated Sales Suppression Device Act                                         .  Title.                               This part is known as the  Utah Automated Sales Suppression Device Act.                                Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this part                                      Amnesty  means a person is not subject to the criminal and civil penalties detailed                          in Section                  .                                     Automated sales suppression device  means                                 a   a software program that falsifies the electronic records of electronic cash registers                          or any other point of sale systems, including transaction data and transaction reports  or                                b   a general reference to a device that allows for, creates, or supports an automated                          sales suppression system or any kind of phantomware.                                     Commission  means the Utah Tax Commission.                                     Electronic cash register  means any device, wherever located, that maintains a                          transaction register or supporting documents by means of an electronic device or computer                          system designed to record transaction data for the purpose of computing, compiling, or                             processing retail, wholesale, or any other sales transaction data.                                     Person  means an individual, business, or entity.                                     Phantomware  means a programming option that                                 a   is pre installed, installed at a later time, or otherwise embedded in the operating                          system of an electronic cash register or hardwired into the electronic cash register  and                                b   can be used to create a virtual alternate register or to eliminate or manipulate                          transaction records that may or may not be preserved in digital formats in order to represent a                          manipulated record or records of transactions in the electronic cash register.                                     Transaction data  includes items purchased by a customer, the price for each item,                          a taxability determination for each item, a segregated tax amount for each of the taxed items,                          the amount of cash or credit tendered, the net amount returned to the customer in change or in a                          refund, the date and time of the purchase, the name, address, and identification number of the                          vendor, and the receipt or invoice number of the transaction.                                     Transaction report  means a report that includes the sales, taxes collected, media                          totals, and discount voids at an electronic cash register and that is generated at the end of a day                          or shift.  The report is printed on cash register tape or is stored electronically.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Possession, sale, or use of automated sales suppression device unlawful                              Penalties.                                    It is a third degree felony to willfully or knowingly sell, purchase, install, transfer,                          use, or possess in this state any automated sales suppression device or phantomware, except                          that any second or subsequent violation of this Subsection     is a second degree felony.                                    Notwithstanding Section                 , any person convicted of violating Subsection                              shall pay a fine of at least    ,    but not more than     ,   .                                    Any person convicted of a violation of Subsection                                     a   is liable for all applicable taxes, penalties under Section                 , and interest                          under Section                  that would otherwise be due, but for the use of the automated sales                          suppression device or phantomware to evade the payment of taxes  and                                b   shall disgorge all profits associated with the sale or use of an automated sales                          suppression device or phantomware.                                    An automated sales suppression device and any device containing an automated                             sales suppression device is contraband and subject to forfeiture under Title   , Chapter  , Utah                          Uniform Forfeiture Procedures Act.                               Section  .    Section                  is enacted to read                                         .  Amnesty.                                    Notwithstanding Section                  , before any criminal action is initiated under                          this part the commission may grant amnesty to any person who voluntarily corrects and                          accurately reports any previously underreported sales records prior to January  ,     ,                          provided that the correction and resulting payment are not the subject of an audit or                          investigation.                                    This section may not be construed to restrict or affect a person s liability for taxes                          and interest due or for any other liabilities arising from the underreporting of sales.                                     Any amnesty the commission grants to a person under this section remains in effect                           if that person remits to the commission the taxes, fees, and charges that are due and is not                           found to be in violation of Section                   for any new or previously unreported offenses for                           a period of five years subsequent to the date payments are made under the granting of amnesty.                                     The commission may not grant a person amnesty under this section if, with respect                           to a tax, fee, or charge for which the person would otherwise be granted amnesty under                           Subsection    , the person has committed an intentional misrepresentation of a material fact,                           separate from the use of any automated suppression device or phantomware as defined in this                           part.
