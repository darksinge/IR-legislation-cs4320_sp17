During the pendency of a general adjudication suit, a claimant or group of        claimants may petition the court to direct the state engineer to  survey and  prepare a proposed        determination and hydrographic survey map for a limited area within the general adjudication        area in which the claimant or group of claimants has a claim.                  The court may grant a petition under Subsection     if               a   the claimant or group of claimants will suffer prejudice if the petition is not        granted               b   the matters raised by the claimant or group of claimants are proper for        determination in a general adjudication               c   granting the petition will not unduly burden the state engineer s resources  and              d   granting the petition will not unduly interfere with the state engineer s discretion to        allocate resources for the preparation of another proposed determination.                  If the court grants a petition under this section, the state engineer shall comply with        this chapter in satisfying the court s order.             Section   . Repealer.
