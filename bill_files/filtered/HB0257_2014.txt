A person commits sexual abuse of a child if, under circumstances not amounting to                           rape of a child, object rape of a child, sodomy on a child, or an attempt to commit any of these                           offenses, the actor touches the anus, buttocks, or genitalia of any child, the breast of a female                           child, or otherwise takes indecent liberties with a child, or causes a child to take indecent                           liberties with the actor or another with intent to cause substantial emotional or bodily pain to                           any person or with the intent to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person regardless of                           the sex of any participant.                                    Sexual abuse of a child is  punishable as  a second degree felony.
