General Description             This bill modifies the Utah State Retirement and Insurance Benefit Act by amending       certain death benefits.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides that a former spouse of a member who dies before retiring may receive a       death benefit in certain circumstances  and                  makes technical changes.
