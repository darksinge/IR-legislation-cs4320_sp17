NAVAJO REVITALIZATION FUND BOARD                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Keele  Johnson          AN ACT RELATING TO COMMUNITY AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT  ALLOWING         PER DIEM AND EXPENSES FOR THE NAVAJO REVITALIZATION FUND BOARD          AND MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          AMENDS                        , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section           is amended to read                        .  Navajo Revitalization Fund Board created    Members    Terms    Chair              Expenses.                   There is created within the division the Navajo Revitalization Board composed of five          members as follows                a   the governor or the governor s designee                b   the two members of the San Juan County commission whose districts include portions          of the Navajo Reservation                c   the chair of the Navajo Utah Commission or a member of the commission designated          by the chair  and               d   the chair of the Utah Dineh Committee, as created in Section          , or a member          of the committee designated by the chair.                   The terms of office for the members of the board shall run concurrently with the terms          of office for the governor, county commissioners, member of the Navajo Utah Commission, and          member of the Utah Dineh Committee.                   The governor, or the governor s designee, shall be the chair of the board and the chair           is responsible to call necessary meetings.                  a   i    Members of the board shall serve without compensation except the members         shall receive travel expenses as determined by the Division of Finance in Section   A      .          These travel expenses  Members who are not government employees of the state or local         government shall receive no compensation or benefits for their services, but may receive per diem         and expenses incurred in the performance of the member s official duties at the rates established         by the Division of Finance under Sections   A       and   A      .              ii   Members may decline to receive per diem and expenses for their service.              b   i   State government officer and employee members who do not receive salary, per          diem, or expenses from their agency for their service may receive per diem and expenses incurred          in the performance of their official duties from the committee at the rates established by the          Division of Finance under Sections   A       and   A      .               ii   State government officer and employee members may decline to receive per diem and          expenses for their service.               c   i   Local government members who do not receive salary, per diem, or expenses from          the entity that they represent for their service may receive per diem and expenses incurred in the          performance of their official duties at the rates established by the Division of Finance under          Sections   A       and   A      .               ii   Local government members may decline to receive per diem and expenses for their          service.               d   The per diem and expenses permitted under this Subsection     may be included as          costs of administration of the fund.                   Four board members are a quorum.                   Any board decisions related to monies in or disbursed from the fund requires the          affirmative vote of each member of the board present at a meeting when a quorum is present.              Legislative Review Note  as of                 PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
