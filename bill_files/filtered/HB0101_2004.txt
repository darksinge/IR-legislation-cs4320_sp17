MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES COORDINATOR                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  David L. Hogue                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the position of statewide coordinator for mental health services for the                          deaf and hard of hearing and funds the position.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the position of statewide coordinator for mental health services for the deaf                           and hard of hearing within the Department of Human Services, Division of                            Substance Abuse and Mental Health.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                This bill appropriates                                .       ,    from the General Fund to the Department of Human Services for fiscal year                                   only, for the purposes described in Section       A       .     .                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                  A       . , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A       .      is enacted to read                                  A       . .  State coordinator for mental health services for the deaf and hard                              of hearing.                                    a   The executive director shall appoint a state coordinator for mental health                          services for the deaf and hard of hearing.                                b   The coordinator should be knowledgeable in interpretive services for the deaf and                          hard of hearing as well as mental health issues, norms, and culture of the deaf and hard of                          hearing community.                                    The coordinator shall assist the deaf and hard of hearing community in the state                          with locating and receiving mental health services available from state agencies, local mental                          health authorities, federal programs, and private providers, including interpretive services                          necessary to access mental health services.                               Section  .   Appropriation.                               This bill appropriates    ,    from the General Fund to the Department of Human                          Services for fiscal year         only, to be used to fund the statewide coordinator for mental                          health services for the deaf and hard of hearing    .
