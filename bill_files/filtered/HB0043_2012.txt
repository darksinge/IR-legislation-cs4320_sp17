General Description                                This bill adds an exemption from licensure in the Barber, Cosmetologist Barber,                           Esthetician, Electrologist, and Nail Technician Licensing Act for a person assisting in                           the production of a motion picture.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    adds an exemption from licensure in the Barber, Cosmetologist Barber, Esthetician,                           Electrologist, and Nail Technical Licensing Act for a person assisting in the                           production of a motion picture under certain circumstances.
