Section  .    Section      A       .      is amended to read                              A       . .  Property damage protection.                               At the request of the named insured, every motor vehicle liability policy of insurance                      under Sections       A            and       A            or combination of policies purchased to satisfy the                      owner s or operator s security requirement of Section          a         which policy does not provide                      insurance for collision damage shall provide coverage for property damage to the motor vehicle                      described in the policy for the benefit of covered persons, as defined under Section       A           , who                      are legally entitled to recover damages from the owner or operator of an uninsured motor vehicle,                      as defined under Subsections       A                a ,  c , and   c    d , arising out of the operation,                      maintenance, or use of an uninsured motor vehicle.                               The coverage provided under this section shall include payment for loss or damage to                      the motor vehicle described in the policy, not to exceed the motor vehicle s actual cash value or                        ,   , whichever is less.  Property damage does not include compensation for loss of use of the                      motor vehicle.                               The coverage provided under this section shall be payable only if                            a   the occurrence causing the property damage involves actual physical contact between the                      covered motor vehicle and an uninsured motor vehicle                                    b   the owner, operator, or license plate number of the uninsured motor vehicle is identified                       and                           c   the insured or someone on his behalf reports the occurrence within ten days to the insurer                      or his agent.                               The coverage provided under this section shall be subject to a      deductible and shall                      be excess to any other insurance covering property damage to the motor vehicle described in the                      policy.                               The insurer providing coverage under this section may make available additional                      deductibles at appropriate premium rates.                               No rating surcharge may be applied to any policy of motor vehicle insurance issued in                      this state as a result of payment of a claim made under this section.
