sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           ENACTS                  B         Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section     Section    B        is enacted to read            Part     Center for the School of the Future                  B          Center for the school of the future    Purpose                    There is hereby established a Center for the School of the Future at Utah State          University                    The center shall contribute to the creation and maintenance of a public education          system within the state that continually and systematically improves by building upon the most          effective existing practices by                a   identifying the best current approaches  techniques  and technologies in public          education                b   supporting the adoption of these innovations by the state and their adaptation to local          circumstances  and               c   helping to build communities of teachers and parents that support each other in          continuing to improve their education                    The activities of the center shall include                a   gathering  evaluating  and synthesizing the most effective and up to date educational         practices and techniques from across the nation to bring them to bear on the school of the future               b   establishing a teacher academy to help teachers improve their instructional ability and         implement more effective learning techniques and tools               c   establishing a family academy to involve and educate families and communities in         adopting changes to improve the health and education of their children  and              d   establishing a design institute to consolidate ideas about effective schools and practices         and implement those ideas into effective  replicable models of education innovation              Section      Appropriation                    There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year                   to Utah          State University to help fund the activities of the Center for the School of the Future at the          university and to allow the center to challenge industrial sources to provide matching funds                    For fiscal year           and for each fiscal year thereafter  the State Board of          Regents shall include in the higher education system budget a requested line item appropriation          to help fund the activities of the center               Section      Effective date               This act takes effect on July               Legislative Review Note      as of                  PM    A limited legal review of this bill raises
