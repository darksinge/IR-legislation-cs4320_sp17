PROVIDING FOR RETROACTIVE APPLICATION UNDER CERTAIN          CIRCUMSTANCES  AND MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                          as repealed and reenacted by Chapter      Laws of Utah                      A        as renumbered and amended by Chapter      Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section     Section           is amended to read                           Electric utility service in annexed area                    a   If the electric consumers of the area being annexed are receiving electric utility          services from sources other than the annexing municipality or an electric service district organized          under Section   A        the municipality may not  without the consent of the electric utility           furnish its electric utility services to the electric consumers until the municipality has reimbursed          the electric utility company which previously provided the services for the fair market value of          those facilities dedicated to provide service to the annexed area                      b   If the annexing municipality and the electric utility cannot agree on the fair          market value  it shall be determined by the state court having jurisdiction                    Subsection   A          applies if a city or town that provides electric services to its          residents annexes an area that is included within an electric service area organized under Section            A                    Section     Section    A       is amended to read                 A         Electric service districts    Public Service Commission jurisdiction             Exceptions                   An electric service district may only include an area where               a   no retail electricity has been provided to commercial  industrial  residential  and other         users of electricity from an investor owned utility within any part of an area certificated by the         Public Service Commission or an area adjacent to that area  municipal agency  or electric         cooperative within the five years immediately preceding September          and               b   electric service is provided to at least one user of electricity within the electric service          district as of September                            An electric service district organized under this part is a public utility and subject to          the jurisdiction of the Public Service Commission   Nothing in this part may be construed to give          the Public Service Commission jurisdiction over any improvement district  other than an electric          service district organized under this part  or over any municipality or association of municipalities          organized under the Interlocal Cooperation Act   Before an electric service district serves any          customer  the electric service district shall obtain a certificate of public convenience and necessity          from the Public Service Commission                    Section         does not apply to rate changes of an electric service district subject          to the following                a   the electric service district is organized for the purpose of distributing electricity to          customers within the boundaries of the electric service district on a not for profit basis                b   the schedule of new rates or other change that results in new rates has been approved          by the board of directors of the electric service district                c   prior to the implementation of any rate increases  the electric service district first holds          a public meeting for all its customers to whom mailed notice of the meeting is sent not less than          ten days prior to the meeting  and               d   the electric service district has filed the schedule of new rates or other change with the          commission   These documents shall be made available by the commission for public inspection                    If an application for certification is not filed by an electric service district organized          under this part and approved by the Public Service Commission by September          all                 provisions in this part relating to electric service districts are repealed                   a   As used in this Subsection       fair market value  means replacement cost less         depreciation               b   If a city or town that provides electric services to its residents annexes an area included         within an electric service district organized under this part               i   the area is  upon annexation  excluded from the electric service district               ii   subject to Subsection     d   the city or town shall pay the electric service district the         fair market value of               A   the electric service district s tangible assets located within the annexed area                B   the electric service district s tangible assets located outside the annexed area that are          required to provide service to customers within the annexed area  and               C   the electric service district s tangible assets located outside the annexed area that are          rendered entirely useless to the electric service district due to the annexation and the transfer of          electric service district assets to the city or town                iii   the city or town may provide electric service to the annexed area immediately upon          annexation  and               iv   the electric service district shall  upon payment of the fair market value  transfer to the          city or town title to and ownership of the assets for which payment was made                c   Subject to Subsection     d   the electric service district is entitled only to payment of          the fair market value of           d   Notwithstanding Subsections     b  ii  and  c   if the electric service district has no          customers in the annexed area  the electric service district is not entitled to any
