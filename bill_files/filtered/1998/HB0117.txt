TASK FORCE                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Beverly Ann Evans          AN ACT RELATING TO THE LEGISLATURE  CREATING THE INTERNAL SERVICE         FUND REVIEW TASK FORCE  PROVIDING FOR MEMBERSHIP  DELINEATING         RESPONSIBILITIES AND PROCEDURES  PROVIDING A REPORTING DATE          APPROPRIATING         FROM THE GENERAL FUND  AND PROVIDING A REPEAL         DATE           This act enacts uncodified material           Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section      Internal Service Fund Review Task Force    Creation    Membership              Interim rules followed    Compensation    Staff                    There is created the Internal Service Fund Review Task Force consisting of the          following eight members                a   three members from the Senate  appointed by the president of the Senate  no more than          two of whom may be from the same political party  and               b   five members from the House of Representatives  appointed by the speaker of the          House of Representatives  no more than three of whom may be from the same political party                    a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed under          Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force                    In conducting its business  the task force shall comply with the rules of legislative          interim committees                    Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force shall be paid in accordance with          Section        and Legislative Joint Rule                          The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support         to the task force              Section      Task force duties    Interim report                   The task force shall               a   determine the level  scope  and dollar amount of internal service funds in agencies and         programs of the state               b   review procedures and controls governing internal service funds  and              c   review any other issues relating to internal service funds in Utah                    A final report  including any proposed legislation  shall be presented to the          Government Operations Interim Committee before November                        Section      Appropriation               There is appropriated from the General Fund for fiscal year                                   to the Senate to pay for the compensation and expenses of senators on the task          force                           to the House of Representatives to pay for the compensation and expenses of          representatives on the task force  and                           to the Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel to pay for staffing          the task force               Section      Repeal date               This act is repealed November                Legislative Review Note      as of
