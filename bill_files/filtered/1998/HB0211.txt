HEARING           REQUIREMENTS                  GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Evan L  Olsen          AN ACT RELATING TO THE LEGISLATURE  ESTABLISHING CERTAIN NOTICE AND         PUBLIC HEARING REQUIREMENTS FOR SPECIAL SESSIONS          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows          ENACTS                           Utah Code Annotated                               Utah Code Annotated                               Utah Code Annotated               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section     Section            is enacted to read            CHAPTER      SPECIAL SESSIONS            Part     General Provisions                            Title               This chapter is entitled  Special Sessions                Section     Section            is enacted to read                            Definitions               As used in this chapter                     Legislative body  means the Legislature meeting in joint convention  the Senate  the          House of Representatives  a standing committee of the Legislature  an interim committee of the          Legislature  or any other committee or group composed exclusively of legislators that is formally          appointed by the president of the Senate  the speaker of the House of Representatives  or both of          them                     Notice  means                a   posting written notice at the building where the meeting is to be held  and              b   providing notice to at least one newspaper of general circulation within Utah or to a         local media correspondent                    Public hearing  means a formal meeting by a legislative body where               a   the public is given at least    hour notice of the agenda  date  time  and place of the         meeting  and              b   comments from the public will be accepted                    Special session  means an extraordinary session of the Legislature called by the         governor under the Utah Constitution  Article VII  Section                      a    Special session legislation  means each bill or constitutional resolution that has          been approved for introduction in the special session by a legislative sponsor                b    Special session legislation  does not mean substitute bills or amendments prepared          during the course of the special session               Section     Section            is enacted to read            Part     Public Hearing for Special Session Legislation                            Public hearing for Special Session legislation                    a   Except as provided in Subsection  b   before formally debating or passing any          special session legislation  the Legislature shall ensure that a legislative body holds a public          hearing on that special session legislation                b   When the governor has declared a state of emergency according to the procedures and          requirements of Section     a    the Legislature need not hold a public hearing on any special          session legislation drafted to respond to the state of emergency                    a   The Legislature shall ensure that written minutes are kept of the public hearing                b   Those minutes shall include                i   the date  time  and place of the public hearing                ii   the names of members of the legislative body who are present and absent                iii   the substance of all matters proposed  discussed  or decided  and a record  by          individual member  of votes taken  if any                iv   the names of all citizens who appeared and the substance in brief of their testimony                v   any other information that any member of the legislative body requests be entered in          the minutes                       c   The minutes are public records and shall be available within a reasonable time after the         public hearing               d   All or any part of the public hearing may be recorded by any person in attendance if         the recording does not interfere with the conduct of the meeting
