Section            is enacted to read            CHAPTER   .  PRIVATIZATION OF QUASI GOVERNMENTAL ENTITIES            Part  .  General Provisions                         .  Title.              This chapter shall be known as the  Privatization of Quasi Governmental Entities Act.               Section  .  Section            is enacted to read                         .  Definitions.              For purposes of this chapter                     Asset  means property of all kind, real and personal, tangible and intangible, and          includes                a  cash                 b   stock or other investments                c   goodwill               d   real property               e   an ownership interest               f   a license  and              g   a cause of action.                   Authorizing statutes  means the statutes creating an entity as a quasi governmental         entity.                   Business interest  means               a   holding the position of trustee, director, officer, or other similar position with a          business entity  or               b   the ownership, either legally or equitably, of at least     of the outstanding shares of          a corporation or     interest in any other business entity, being held by                i   an individual                ii   the individual s spouse                iii   a minor child of the individual  or               iv   any combination of Subsection     b  i  through  iii .                    Interested party  means a person  holding the position of trustee, director, officer, or          other similar position with a quasi governmental entity.                    Lobbyist  is as defined in Section          .                    Privatized  means the repeal of the authorizing statute of a quasi governmental entity          and the revision to state laws to                a   terminate the relationship between the state and the quasi governmental entity  and               b   provide for the operation of a successor to the quasi governmental entity                i   that is unaffiliated to the state  and               ii   receives any asset of the quasi governmental entity.                    Quasi governmental entity  means the                a   Dairy Commission created in Title  , Chapter   , Dairy Promotion Act                b   Utah Technology Finance Corporation created in Title  , Chapter  , Part  , Utah          Technology Finance Corporation Act                c   Heber Valley Railroad Authority created in Title  , Chapter  , Part  , Heber Valley          Historic Railroad Authority                       d   Utah Science Center Authority created in Title  , Chapter  , Part  , Utah Science         Center Authority               e   Utah Housing Finance Agency created in Title  , Chapter  , Part  , Utah Housing         Finance Agency               f   Utah State Fair Corporation created in Title  , Chapter  , Part   , Utah State Fair         Corporation Act               g   Workers  Compensation Fund of Utah created in Title   A, Chapter   , Workers          Compensation Fund of Utah               h   Utah State Retirement Office created in Title   , Chapter  , Part  , Retirement Office          and Board                i   School and Institutional Trust Lands Administration created in Title   C, Chapter  ,          Part  , School and Institutional Trust Lands Administration                j   Crime Victims Reparations Office created in Title   , Chapter   a, Part  , Crime          Victims  Reparations Act  and               k   Utah Communications Agency Network created in Title   C, Chapter  , Utah          Communications Agency Network Act.
