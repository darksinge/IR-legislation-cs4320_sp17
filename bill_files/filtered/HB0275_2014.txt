VIETNAM WAR MEMORIAL DAY AMENDMENT                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Curtis Oda                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill declares a day to be remembered as the Vietnam War Memorial Day.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    designates March    as Vietnam War Memorial Day.                           Money Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                    G        , as last amended by Laws of Utah     , Chapter                                                        Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      G           is amended to read                                   G      .  Commemorative periods.                                    The following days shall be commemorated yearly                                 a   Bill of Rights Day, on December                                    b   Constitution Day, on September                                    c   Yellow Ribbon Day, on the third Monday in May, in honor of men and women who                              are serving or have served in the U.S. armed forces around the world in defense of freedom                                 d   POW MIA Recognition Day, on the third Friday in September                                 e   Indigenous People Day, the Monday immediately preceding Thanksgiving   and                                 f   Utah State Flag Day, on March   .   and                                g   Vietnam War Memorial Day, on March   .                                    The Department of Veterans  and Military Affairs shall coordinate activities, special                           programs, and promotional information to heighten public awareness and involvement relating                           to Subsections     c  and  d .                                    The month of October shall be commemorated yearly as Italian American Heritage                           Month.                                    The month of November shall be commemorated yearly as American Indian                           Heritage Month.                                    The month of April shall be commemorated yearly as Clean Out the Medicine                           Cabinet Month to recognize the urgent need to make Utah homes and neighborhoods safe from                           prescription medication abuse and poisonings by the proper home storage and disposal of                           prescription and over the counter medications, and to educate citizens about the permanent                           medication disposal sites in Utah listed on useonlyasdirected.org that allow disposal throughout                           the year.                                    The first full week of May shall be commemorated yearly as State Water Week to                           recognize the importance of water conservation, quality, and supply in the state.                                    The second Friday and Saturday in August shall be commemorated yearly as Utah                           Fallen Heroes Days to                                 a   honor fallen heroes who, during service in the military or public safety, have                           sacrificed their lives to protect the country and the citizens of the state  and                                b   encourage political subdivisions to acknowledge and honor fallen heroes.
