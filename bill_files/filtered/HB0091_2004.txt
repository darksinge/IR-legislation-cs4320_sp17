UTAH ADMINISTRATIVE RULEMAKING ACT                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor  Wayne A. Harper                                                      LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill requires state agencies to provide an electronic copy of a proposed rule to the                           sponsor of the bill that provided the authorization for the proposed rule.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires state agencies that file a proposed rule with the Division of Administrative                           Rules to send an electronic copy to the sponsor of the legislation that established the                           requirement and that is cited by the agency as authority for the rule                                .    requires that the agency send the electronic copy of the proposed rule to the bill                           sponsor on the same day as the filing of the proposed rule with the Division of                           Administrative Rules and provides an alternative regarding emergency rules                                .    requires that the electronic copy of the rule be sent only if the legislation creates an                           express, implied, or perceived obligation on the part of an agency to draft a rule, is                           cited by the agency as authority for the rule, and is enacted no more than two years                           prior to the date that the rule was submitted to the division  and                               .    clarifies that members of the Legislature that are concerned with the manner in                           which a bill is being implemented may express those concerns to the agency that                           made the rule and request a hearing before the Legislature s Administrative Rules                           Review Committee.
