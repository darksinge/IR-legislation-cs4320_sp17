A person may not operate a motor vehicle on a highway if a fuel supply tank of themotor vehicle contains dyed diesel fuel, unless  a   permitted under federal law  b   i   the motor vehicle is used on the highway only to travel from one parcel of landowned or operated by the owner to another parcel of land owned or operated by the owner  and ii   the motor vehicle s travel on the highway is necessary for furtherance of agriculturalpurposes  or c   the motor vehicle is special mobile equipment, as defined in Section         a        ,including off road motorized construction or maintenance equipment, that is only incidentallyoperated or moved on a highway in connection with a construction project.     A person who violates Subsection     shall pay a penalty assessed by the commissionas follows  a   the greater of      or    per gallon of dyed diesel fuel within each fuel supply tank ofthe motor vehicle, based on the maximum storage capacity of each fuel supply tank  or b   for a second and subsequent offense, the greater of   ,    or     per gallon of dyeddiesel fuel within each fuel supply tank of the motor vehicle, based on the maximum storagecapacity of each fuel supply tank.     The penalty imposed under this section  a   is in addition to any other taxes, interest, or penalties imposed under this chapter  and b   shall be deposited in the Transportation Fund.
