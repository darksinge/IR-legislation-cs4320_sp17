Section  .  Section   C       is amended to read                C      . Fund created    Donations.                  There is created an expendable special revenue fund entitled the  State Capitol        Fund.                   The fund consists of money generated from the following revenue sources               a   any donations, deposits, contributions, gifts, money, and items of value received        from private persons, foundations, or organizations                b   gift shop profits                 c    b   appropriations made to the fund by the Legislature  and               d    c   money received by the board from the federal government.                  a   The fund shall earn interest.              b   All interest earned on fund money shall be deposited into the fund.                  The board may use fund money to               a   acquire historical and other items to furnish the capitol hill facilities               b   pay for the repair and maintenance of the capitol hill facilities and capitol hill        grounds               c   pay for the rehabilitation of the capitol hill facilities and capitol hill grounds  and              d   fund all costs incurred in complying with this chapter.
