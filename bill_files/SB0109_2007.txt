 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions related to transfers of structured settlements, including
            
10

    
rights to periodic payments under workers' compensation.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    addresses transfers of payment rights under workers' compensation;
            
14

    
    .    removes payment rights under workers' compensation from the application of the
            
15

    
Structured Settlement Protection Act; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical and conforming changes.
