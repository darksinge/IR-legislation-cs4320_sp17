 General Description:
            
13

    
    This bill amends Title 76, Chapter 6a, Pyramid Scheme Act.
            
14

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
15

    
    This bill:
            
16

    
    .    defines "compensation" to exclude commissions earned for the sale of goods or
            
17

    
services for actual consumption;
            
18

    
    .    makes participation in a pyramid scheme only by receiving compensation for
            
19

    
recruiting another person a class B misdemeanor; and
            
20

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
21

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
22

    
    None

 Other Special Clauses:

    None
