INCREASE IN CIGARETTE TAX                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor   Gene  Davis          AN ACT RELATING TO REVENUE AND TAXATION  INCREASING THE TAX RATE         LEVIED ON CIGARETTES  PROVIDING THE PURPOSES FOR WHICH THE REVENUES         GENERATED BY THE CIGARETTE TAX INCREASE MAY BE USED  CREATING A         RESTRICTED ACCOUNT TO BE USED BY THE DIVISION OF SUBSTANCE ABUSE          MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                         , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section            is amended to read                         .  Tax basis    Rate    Future increase.                   There is levied a tax upon the sale, use, or storage of cigarettes in the state.                   The rates of the tax levied under Subsection     are                a     .      .    cents on each cigarette, for all cigarettes weighing not more than three          pounds per thousand cigarettes  and               b     .      .    cents on each cigarette, for all cigarettes weighing in excess of three          pounds per thousand cigarettes.                   The tax levied under Subsection     shall be paid by the manufacturer, jobber,          distributor, wholesaler, retailer, user, or consumer.                   The tax rates specified in this section shall be increased by the commission by the same          amount as any future reduction in the federal excise tax on cigarettes.                   Beginning on July  ,     , the revenues generated by the increase in the cigarette tax          under this section enacted during the      Annual General Session shall be annually distributed          as follows               a       of the revenues shall be deposited into the Comprehensive Health Insurance Pool         Enterprise Fund created in Section   A        to be used as provided in Title   A, Chapter   ,         Comprehensive Health Insurance Pool Act  and              b       of the revenues shall be deposited into the restricted account created in Subsection            .                  a   There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the          Cigarette Tax Restricted Account.               b   The Division of Substance Abuse created in Section   A       shall expend the funds          in the restricted account for                i   education on preventing tobacco use                ii   tobacco use cessation  and               iii   a public information campaign on preventing tobacco use.               c   The Division of Substance Abuse shall annually report to the Legislature regarding the          use of the funds and the resulting impact on tobacco use prevention and cessation.
