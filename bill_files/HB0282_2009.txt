TASK FORCE ON LEGISLATIVE REFORM                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Neil A. Hansen                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill creates the Task Force on Legislative Reform.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    specifies membership, duties, and powers of the task force  and                               .    repeals the task force on November   ,     .                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill is repealed on November   ,     .                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .   Task Force on Legislative Reform    Creation    Membership                              Quorum    Compensation    Staff.                                    There is created the Task Force on Legislative Reform consisting of the following                             members                                 a   four members of the Senate appointed by the president of the Senate, no more than                          two of whom may be from the same political party  and                                b   eight members of the House of Representatives appointed by the speaker of the                          House of Representatives, no more than four of whom may be from the same political party.                                       a   The president of the Senate shall designate a member of the Senate appointed                          under Subsection     a  as a cochair of the task force.                                b   The speaker of the House of Representatives shall designate a member of the House                          of Representatives appointed under Subsection     b  as a cochair of the task force.                                    a   A majority of the members of the task force constitutes a quorum for the                          conduct of business.                                b   The action of a majority of a quorum present is considered the action of the task                          force.                                    Salaries and expenses of the members of the task force shall be paid in accordance                          with Section                and Legislative Joint Rule   .  .                                    The Office of Legislative Research and General Counsel shall provide staff support                          to the task force.                               Section  .   Duties    Ability to create subcommittees.                                    The task force shall study                                 a   the effectiveness of and potential changes to                                 i   the appropriations process, including an evaluation of using a ways and means                          committee                                 ii   the interim process                                 iii   campaign finance reform                                 iv   gift reform  and                                v   the length of the general session                                 b   the current status of constituent services and recommendations for improvement                                 c   the current status of legislative staff and its role in legislators  efficiency and                          effectiveness                                 d   funding of the legislative branch, legislator compensation, and legislator travel                          expenses  and                                e   major areas of negative impact on the public perception of legislator integrity.                                    In conducting the study, the task force shall review how other states, particularly                          other western states, address studied issues.                                    The task force may create subcommittees.                                    The task force may request reports and recommendations from the Legislative                             Compensation Commission.                               Section  .   Repeal date.                               This bill is repealed on November   ,     .
