ELECTED OFFICIALS SALARIES                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   Michael R. Styler                           AN ACT RELATING TO THE LEGISLATURE  MODIFYING PROVISIONS RELATING TO                          COMPENSATION  PROHIBITING INCREASES IN COMPENSATION FROM TAKING                          PLACE UNTIL AFTER THE NEXT ELECTION  PROVIDING AN EXCEPTION FOR                          COST OF LIVING INCREASE  AND MAKING TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            AMENDS                                      , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section               is amended to read                                       .  Compensation of members set by Legislature based on recommendations of                           Legislative Compensation Commission.                                The       a   The Legislature shall establish the salary of members of the Legislature  shall                          be set  at an annual general session based upon recommendations of the Legislative Compensation                           Commission.                                b   The Legislature may accept, reject, or lower the salary recommendation, but may not                           increase the recommendation.                                    a   Except as provided in Subsection     b , a salary increase approved by the                          Legislature may not take effect until after the next regular general election.                                b   Subsection     a  does not apply to a cost of living increase in salary, not to exceed                          an amount equal to the decrease in the purchasing power of the dollar since the last increase, as                          measured by the Consumer Price Index prepared by the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics.        Legislative Review Note      as of                 PM     A limited legal review of this legislation raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
