HATE CRIMES AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   David  Litvack                             This act modifies the Criminal Code by providing for the court s consideration of the                          defendant s selection of the victim because of the defendant s perception of a group or                          individual as an aggravating circumstance.  This act provides that the defendant s mere                          beliefs, associations, or expressions may not be considered in the court s evaluation of the                          weight to give this aggravating circumstance.  This act allows the court to impose alternative                           sentence provisions in cases with this aggravating circumstance.  This act repeals the current                           state  hate crimes  statute.
