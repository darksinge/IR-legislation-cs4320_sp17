    This bill reauthorizes, for one year, the Transportation Planning Task Force created in
            
10

    
2003.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    establishes a legislative task force to study transportation needs and funding for the
            
14

    
next 10 and 20-year time frame;

16

    
task force;
            
17

    
    .    requires the task force to prepare a report; and
            
18

    
    .    requires the task force to report its findings to the Transportation Interim Committee
            
19

    
on a specified date.

23

    
2004-05 to pay for task force expenses.
