H.B. 22









1     DESIGNATION OF STATE HIGHWAYS




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor: Dave Hogue


5    AN ACT RELATING TO HIGHWAYS; AMENDING CERTAIN HIGHWAYS ON THE STATE

6    SYSTEM.

7    This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated 1953 as follows:

8    AMENDS:

9         27-12-38.1, as last amended by Chapter 18, Laws of Utah 1995

10    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

11        Section 1.  Section  27-12-38.1 is amended to read:

12         27-12-38.1.  State highways -- SR-71 to SR-80.  

13        The following named roads are designated as state highways:

14        (1)  SR-71.  From Route [68] 154, Bangerter Highway in Riverton easterly to Seventh East

15    Street in Draper; thence northerly via Seventh and Ninth East Streets to Route 186 at Seventh East

16    Street and Fourth South Street in Salt Lake City.

17        (2)  SR-72.  From Route 24 in Loa northerly to a junction with Route 70 and Route 10 near

18    Fremont Junction.

19        (3)  SR-73.  From Route 36 northeast of St. John Station southeasterly via Five Mile Pass;

20    thence northeasterly via Lehi to Route 89 east of Lehi.

21        (4)  SR-74.  From Route 89 in American Fork northerly to Alpine.

22        (5)  SR-75.  From Route 15 northwest of Springville easterly to Route 89 near Ironton.

23        (6)  SR-76.  From Route 70 easterly to old Fremont Junction on Route 72.

24        (7)  SR-77.  From Route 147 north of Benjamin north via Barney Corner; thence easterly

25    to Route 89 in Springville.

26        (8)  SR-78.  From Route 15 at the Mills Junction Interchange northerly to west of Levan;

27    thence east to Route 28 in Levan.


1        (9)  SR-79.  From Route 126 easterly via Thirty-first Street in Ogden to the lane separation,

2    thence on eastbound lane only to Route 89; then easterly via Thirtieth Street to Route 203;

3    commencing again at Thirtieth Street and Route 89 then westerly on the westbound lane only to

4    merge with eastbound lanes.

5        (10)  SR-80.  From the Utah-Nevada state line in Wendover to the Utah-Wyoming state

6    line west of Evanston, Wyoming, traversing the alignment of Interstate 80.
