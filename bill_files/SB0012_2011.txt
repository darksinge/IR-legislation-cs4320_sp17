 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Alcoholic Beverage Control Act to correct omissions or errors
            
11

    
made as part of the recodification of that act.

 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    reorders language;
            
15

    
    .    corrects or clarifies terminology, including defining the term "container";
            
16

    
    .    clarifies a formula related to the Alcoholic Beverage Enforcement and Treatment
            
17

    
Restricted Account;
            
18

    
    .    clarifies references to specific licenses or sublicenses;
            
19

    
    .    relocates a provision addressing advertising;
            
20

    
    .    makes the provision related to a package agency posting a bond more consistent
            
21

    
with other bond requirements in the act; and
            
22

    
    .    makes technical and conforming amendments.
