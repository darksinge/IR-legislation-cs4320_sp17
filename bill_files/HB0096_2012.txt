 General Description:

    This bill modifies the Criminal Code by prohibiting the use, purchase, installation,
            
12

    
transfer, or sale of any automated sales suppression device or phantomware.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    provides definitions, including defining automated sales suppression device and
            
16

    
phantomware;

    .    prohibits a person from knowingly selling, purchasing, installing, transferring,

26
