CHILD IMMUNIZATION AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Rebecca D. Lockhart                             This act modifies provisions related to the State System of Public Education by removing the                          requirement that a form claiming an exemption from immunization based on a personal                          belief against immunization be witnessed by a local health department representative and                          allowing a person to obtain the form at a local health department or public school.
