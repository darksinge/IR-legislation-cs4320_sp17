    This bill modifies the Individual Income Tax Act.

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    modifies the state taxable income brackets and amounts of tax;
            
14

    
    .    requires the Utah State Tax Commission to adjust the state taxable income brackets

and amounts of tax for inflation or deflation and to make certain other adjustments

    .    modifies the personal exemption amount that a resident or nonresident individual is

required to add to federal taxable income;

    .    repeals a subtraction from federal taxable income for federal income tax paid that is

    .    provides for a nonrefundable state earned income tax credit that is equal to a certain
            
22

    .    makes technical changes.

    None
