SMOKE FREE WORKPLACE LAW                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Scott  Daniels                             This act modifies the Utah Indoor Clean Air Act. The act amends the exclusions to the act.                           The act requires taverns and private clubs to comply with the clean air act.
