 General Description:

    This bill modifies the Governor's Office of Economic Development chapter by

amending Industrial Assistance Fund provisions.

    This bill:

    .    moves the funding and administration of the Business Expansion and Retention
            
14

    
Initiative from the Rural Fast Track Program to the Industrial Assistance Account
            
15

    
administered by the director of the Governor's Office of Economic Development;

18

    
business and economic development in rural Utah with the Business Expansion and
            
19

    
Retention Initiative; and

    .    makes technical changes.

    None
