Hansen                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending provisions relating to                           motorcycle operator protective headgear requirements.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides that a person may not operate or ride on a motorcycle or motor driven                           cycle on a highway in this state unless the person is wearing protective headgear if                                    .    another person under    years of age is also riding on the motorcycle or                           motor driven cycle                                    .    the operator of the motorcycle holds a motorcycle learner permit  or                                   .    the motorcycle is being operated within the city limits of a city of the first,                           second, or third class with an exception                                .    provides that a person under    years of a age, rather than    years of age, may not                           operate or ride on a motorcycle or motor driven cycle on a highway in this state                           unless the person is wearing                                    .    protective headgear with a continuous solid chin bar  and                                   .    protective glasses, goggles, or a transparent face shield unless the motorcycle or                           motor driven cycle is equipped with a protective windshield                                .    grants the Department of Public Safety rulemaking authority to establish standards                           for approved protective headgear, glasses, goggles, or transparent face shields  and                                  .    makes technical changes.
