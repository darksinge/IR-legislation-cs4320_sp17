SALES AND USE TAX AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Wayne A. Harper                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill amends the Sales and Use Tax Act and other provisions relating to sales and                           use taxation.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    modifies state and local sales and use tax rates                                .    repeals certain local sales and use taxes                                .    repeals voter approval requirements for certain local sales and use taxes                                .    requires certain adjustments to the property tax certified tax rates of counties, cities,                           or towns imposing certain local sales and use taxes                                .    provides that if a county, city, or town does not impose certain sales and use taxes,                           the state shall impose the taxes                                .    defines terms and modifies definitions                                .    designates that certain state sales and use tax revenues shall be used for purposes                           relating to                                    .    botanical, cultural, recreational, and zoological organizations or facilities  or                                   .    rural health care facilities                                .    creates restricted special revenue funds consisting of state sales and use tax                           revenues, addresses the administration of those restricted special revenue funds, and                           establishes the purposes for which the revenues deposited into the restricted special                              revenue funds may be expended                                .    provides for the appointment of delegates to the governing board of the Streamlined                           Sales and Use Tax Agreement                                .    modifies sales and use tax provisions relating to a repossessed vehicle that is resold                                .    addresses the distribution of certain local sales and use taxes to counties, cities, and                           towns                                .    modifies provisions relating to determining the location of sales and use                           transactions                                .    repeals provisions relating to determining the location of a florist delivery                           transaction                                .    addresses the circumstances under which a city or town may impose a resort                           communities tax                                .    modifies provisions governing the imposition, enactment, or repeal of certain local                           sales and use taxes                                .    modifies the amount of the sales tax refund for qualified emergency food agencies                                .    modifies a tax imposed by cities and towns for highways or to fund a system for                           public transit to allow counties to also impose the tax                                .    creates the Additional Highways or Public Transit System Tax Act                                .    creates the Single Statewide Rate State Sales and Use Tax Act                                .    repeals legislative intent language                                .    grants rulemaking authority to the State Tax Commission                                .    repeals obsolete language  and                               .    makes technical changes.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides effective dates.                               This bill provides revisor instructions. Prepaid Sales and Use Tax Construction Account    Use of account                             funds.                                 There is created a Prepaid Sales and Use Tax Construction Account as a special                             suspense account within the state General Fund.  All revenues collected or received by the                             State Tax Commission from the prepayment of sales or use taxes under this chapter shall be                             deposited with the state treasurer  in accordance with Section                   daily and credited by                             the state treasurer to the Prepaid Sales and Use Tax Construction Account.  This account shall                             be used to finance state related public improvements, including but not limited to highways                                and related facilities and schools and related facilities.  Funds from this account shall only be                             disbursed or drawn upon after proper authorization and only after appropriation of these funds                             by the Legislature.
