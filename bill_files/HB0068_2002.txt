SUBDIVISION OF AGRICULTURAL LAND                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Brent D. Parker                             This act modifies the County Code relating to the definition of subdivision.  The act exempts                          the division or partition of agricultural land meeting certain requirements from the                          definition of subdivision and makes technical changes.
