 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions of Title 9, Community and Culture, related to the
            
10

    
Permanent Community Impact Fund and the Permanent Community Impact Fund
            
11

    
Board.

 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    provides that the Permanent Community Impact Fund Board shall elect one of its
            
15

    
members to serve as its chair for a two-year term;
            
16

    
    .    modifies the definition of subdivision for purposes related to the receipt of loans or
            
17

    
grants made from the Permanent Community Impact Fund; and

    .    provides that 75% of new monies received into the Permanent Community Impact
            
19

    
Fund shall be used for grants.
