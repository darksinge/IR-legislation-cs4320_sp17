 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions regarding school supplies for elementary school students.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    allows an elementary school or elementary school teacher to provide to a student's
            
13

    
parent or guardian a suggested list of supplies for use during the regular school day;
            
14

    
and
            
15

    
    .    requires that a suggested list of supplies include specified language explaining that:
            
16

    
        .    the list is compiled solely to provide information to parents and guardians who
            
17

    
wish to voluntarily furnish supplies for student use; and
            
18

    
        .    equivalent supplies will be furnished by the school to all students free of charge
            
19

    
as necessary.
