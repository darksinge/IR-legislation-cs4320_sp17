H.B. 18









1     COMPUTERS FOR HIGHWAY PATROL




2    1998 GENERAL SESSION


3    STATE OF UTAH


4     Sponsor:  Gerry A. Adair


5    AN ACT RELATING TO PUBLIC SAFETY AND APPROPRIATIONS; APPROPRIATING

6    $300,000 FOR FISCAL YEAR 1998-99 FROM THE GENERAL FUND TO THE UTAH

7    HIGHWAY PATROL DIVISION OF PUBLIC SAFETY FOR COMPUTER EQUIPMENT

8    AND RELATED SUPPORT COSTS FOR FIELD TROOPERS; AND PROVIDING AN

9    EFFECTIVE DATE.

10    This act enacts uncodified material.

11    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

12        Section 1.   Appropriation.

13        (1)  There is appropriated for fiscal year 1998-99 from the General Fund to the Utah

14    Highway Patrol Division of the Department of Public Safety $300,000 to provide computer

15    equipment, training, and the hardware and software, including servers, scanners, and all other

16    related equipment.

17        (2)  It is the intent of the Legislature that this appropriation, as part of ongoing upgrade of

18    troopers' computer access, shall be applied to the highway patrol field troopers serving in Utah and

19    Juab counties, due to the populations in need of increased service in these counties.

20        Section 2.   Effective date.

21        This act takes effect on July 1, 1998.
