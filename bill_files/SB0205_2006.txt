 General Description:

    This bill addresses partial releases and reconveyances of real property.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    expands the definition of "delivery" applicable to reconveyances and releases of real
            
14

    
property;

    .    eliminates the need to record a copy of a release or reconveyance of a mortgage or
            
16

    
trust deed;

    .    outlines the method of partially reconveying or partially releasing real property
            
18

    
subject to a trust deed or mortgage;
            
19

    
    .    provides forms for certain documents required to partially reconvey or partially
            
20

    
release real property subject to a trust deed or mortgage; and
            
21

    
    .    makes technical changes.
