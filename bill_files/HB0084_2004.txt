 General Description:
            
8

    
    This bill modifies the process for selecting candidates for membership on the State
            
9

    
Board of Education.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    modifies the number of nominating and recruiting committees that select candidates
            
13

    
for membership on the State Board of Education;
            
14

    
    .    makes the members of local school boards within state board districts the members
            
15

    
of nominating and recruiting committees; 
            
16

    
    .    removes the governor from the candidate selection process; and
            
17

    
    .    modifies the qualifications used by the nominating and recruiting committees to
            
18

    
select candidates.
