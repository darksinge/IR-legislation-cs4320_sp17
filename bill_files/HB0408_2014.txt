 LONG TITLE

 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends portions of the Election Code that relate to a write-in candidate.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    amends certain portions of the Election Code to require a ballot to contain a space
            
13

    
for a write-in candidate only if a write-in candidate is qualified for the election;
            
14

    
    .    changes the deadline for a write-in candidate to file a declaration of candidacy for a
            
15

    
regular general election; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
17
