 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies the Innkeeper's Rights Act to address the conditions under which an
            
10

    
innkeeper may refuse or deny accommodations, facilities, or privileges.
            
11

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
12

    
    This bill:
            
13

    
    .    modifies the provision under which an innkeeper may refuse or deny
            
14

    
accommodations, facilities, or privileges related to a person bringing in firearms or
            
15

    
explosives; and
            
16

    
    .    makes technical changes.
