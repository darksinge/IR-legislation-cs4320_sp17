USE OF BEAR RIVER BIRD REFUGE                           SETTLEMENT MONIES                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Sponsor   Ben C. Ferry                             This act modifies State Affairs in General.  The act creates two restricted accounts  the                          Wetlands Protection Restricted Account and the Recreational Trails Enhancement and                          Protection Restricted Account which are funded by payments resulting from a                               Settlement Agreement between the United States Department of Interior through the Fish                           and Wildlife Service and the state through the Department of Natural Resources.  The act                           provides an immediate effective date. Section           .      is enacted to read                                       . .  Wetlands Protection Account.                                    There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the Wetlands                          Protection Account.                                    The account shall be funded by a    ,   ,    payment resulting from a                               Settlement Agreement between the United States Department of Interior through the Fish and                          Wildlife Service and the state through the Department of Natural Resources.                                    Funds in the Wetlands Protection Account may be used for the purposes of wetland                          or open space preservation, enhancement, creation, and acquisition in and near the Great Salt Lake.                                  Section  .    Section           .      is enacted to read                                       . .  Recreational Trails Enhancement and Protection Account.                                    There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the                          Recreational Trails Enhancement and Protection Account.                                    The account shall be funded by a   ,   ,    payment resulting from a      Settlement                          Agreement dated between the United States Department of Interior through the Fish and Wildlife                          Service and the state through the Department of Natural Resources.                                    Funds in the Recreational Trails Enhancement and Protection Account may be used                          for the development, improvement, and expansion of motorized and nonmotorized recreational                          trails on public and private lands in the state.
