FEE ON LUBRICATING OIL                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   David L. Zolman                           AN ACT RELATING TO THE USED OIL MANAGEMENT ACT  DECREASING THE                          RECYCLING FEE IMPOSED UPON THE SALE OF LUBRICATING OIL  MAKING                          TECHNICAL CHANGES  AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.                          This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                           AMENDS                                        , as enacted by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Recycling fee on sale of oil.                                      On and after October  ,     , a recycling fee of   .     .   per quart or   .     .                             per gallon is imposed upon the first sale in Utah by a lubricating oil vendor of lubricating oil. The                           lubricating oil vendor shall collect the fee at the time the lubricating oil is sold.                                    A fee under this section  shall  may not be collected on sales of lubricating oil                                 a   shipped outside the state                                 b   purchased in five gallon or smaller containers and used solely in underground mining                           operations  or                                c   in bulk containers of    gallons or more.                                    This fee is in addition to all other state, county, or municipal fees and taxes imposed                           on the sale of lubricating oil.                                    The exemptions from sales and use tax provided in Section                   do not apply to                           this part.                                    The commission may make rules to implement and enforce the provisions of this                           section.                                   Section  .   Effective date.                               This act takes effect on July  ,     .
