UTAH SCENIC BYWAY DESIGNATION                           AMENDMENTS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Christopher N. Herrod                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill modifies the Designation of State Highways Act by amending scenic byway                           designation provisions.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    provides definitions                                .    provides that the Legislature shall designate highways as state scenic byways                                .    provides that the Legislature shall approve state highway and state scenic byway                           nominations for National Scenic Byway or All American Road designations                                .    beginning on January  ,     , prohibits a non scenic area from being designated as                           a state scenic byway or nominated for designation as a National Scenic Byway or                           All American Road                                .    provides that non scenic areas of existing state scenic byways shall be segmented                                .    provides that the Utah State Scenic Byway Committee shall apply for segmentation                           of non scenic areas on existing National Scenic Byways or All American Roads                                .    provides that the Utah State Scenic Byway Committee shall recommend to the                           Legislature highways for state scenic byway designation or removal of a state scenic                           byway designation on a highway  and                               .    makes technical changes.                              Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                This bill provides revisor instructions. Section                 is amended to read                                         .  Definitions.                               As used in this part ,  committee                                       Committee  means the Utah State Scenic Byway Committee created in Section                                        .                                     Non scenic area  means                                 a   any property that is unzoned or zoned for commercial or industrial use adjoining a                          highway that does not contain at least one of the intrinsic qualities described in Subsection                                             b  immediately upon the property  or                                b   any property that is unzoned or zoned for commercial or industrial use that contains                          an intrinsic quality described in Subsection                     b  immediately upon the property but                          the intrinsic quality does not represent the primary characteristic and use of the property.                                     Segmentation  means                                 a   removing the scenic byway designation from a portion of an existing scenic byway                          that adjoins a non scenic area  or                                b   excluding a portion of a highway from a scenic byway designation where the                          highway adjoins a non scenic area.                               Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                        . .  Designation of highways as scenic byways.                                       The Legislature shall designate highways as state scenic byways.                                    A highway or state scenic byway may not be nominated for designation as a                          National Scenic Byway or All American Road unless the nomination is approved by the                          Legislature.                                    Beginning on January  ,     , a non scenic area may not be                                 a   designated as a state scenic byway  or                                b   nominated for designation as a National Scenic Byway or All American Road.                                    a   A non scenic area that was designated as a state scenic byway prior to the                          effective date of this bill shall be segmented.                                b   The committee shall apply for segmentation of a non scenic area that was                          designated as a National Scenic Byway or All American Road prior to the effective date of this                          bill. In accordance with Title   G, Chapter  , Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                            department shall make rules in consultation with the committee                                  a   for the administration of a scenic byway program                                  b   establishing the criteria that a highway shall possess to be designated as a scenic                            byway, including the criteria described in Subsection     b                                   c   establishing the process for nominating a highway to be designated as a state scenic                            byway                                  d   specifying the process for hearings to be conducted in the area of proposed                            designation prior to the highway being designated as a scenic byway                                  e   identifying the highways within the state designated as scenic byways  and                                 f   establishing the process and criteria for removing the designation of a highway as a                            scenic byway.
