REGULATION OF PRIVATE INSTRUCTION                           SWIMMING POOLS                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Gregory H. Hughes                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                                This bill amends the Utah Health Code to provide for regulation of a private instruction                           pool.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    defines the term,  private instruction pool                                 .    grants authority to the Department of Health to make rules for a private instruction                           pool that are necessary to protect public health and safety                                .    prohibits a local health department from imposing or enforcing any rule or                           regulation relating to a private instruction pool that is not made by the department                                .    requires that rules made by the department under the provisions of this bill shall                                    .    be the least restrictive rules necessary to protect public health and                                        safety  and                                   .    provide flexibility to allow an owner of a private instruction pool to                                            implement adequate alternative measures to comply with a rule  and                               .    describes the authority granted to, and the restrictions placed on, the department in                           making rules under the provisions of this bill.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                              Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            ENACTS                                        , Utah Code Annotated                                                         Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section                 is enacted to read                                        .  Regulation of health and safety for a private instruction pool.                                    As used in this section,  private instruction pool  means a swimming pool located                          at a private residence that                                 a   is primarily for private use                                 b   except as provided in Subsection     c , is not used by any person in exchange for a                          fee  and                                c   is used to conduct swimming lessons, in exchange for a fee, less than    days per                          calendar year.                                    Subject to Subsection    , the department may make rules for a private instruction                          pool that are necessary to protect public health and safety.                                    a   A private instruction pool is not a public swimming pool.                                b   The department may only make rules, and may only enforce rules, relating to a                          private instruction pool, that are consistent with the provisions of this section.                                c   A local health department may not impose or enforce any rule or regulation relating                          to a private instruction pool that is not made by the department, by rule, in accordance with the                          provisions of this section.                                d   In making rules that are applicable to a private instruction pool, the department                          shall                                 i   enact the least restrictive rules necessary to protect public health and safety  and                                ii   provide flexibility to allow an owner of a private instruction pool to implement                          alternative measures that substantially comply with the intent of a rule and do not pose a threat                          to public health or safety.
