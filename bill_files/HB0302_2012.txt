 General Description:

    This bill amends provisions related to certain civil penalties established by a
            
10

    
municipality or county.

 Highlighted Provisions:

    This bill:

    .    requires that a municipality or county provide:
            
14

    
        .    notice of an ordinance violation to the owner of record;

16

    
        .    a schedule of the civil penalties for an ordinance violation; and
            
17

    
    .    makes technical corrections.
