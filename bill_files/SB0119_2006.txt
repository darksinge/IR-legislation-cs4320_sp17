 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending off-highway vehicle
            
11

    
registration provisions.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    repeals the requirement that the Motor Vehicle Division assign an off-highway
            
15

    
vehicle registration number to each registered off-highway vehicle;
            
16

    
    .    repeals the requirement that an owner of an off-highway vehicle affix and display
            
17

    
the off-highway vehicle registration number assigned by the Motor Vehicle
            
18

    
Division;
            
19

    
    .    repeals the requirement that the Board of Parks and Recreation make rules for the
            
20

    
display of an off-highway vehicle registration number; and
            
21

    
    .    makes technical changes.
            
22

    
 Monies Appropriated in this Bill:
            
23

    
    None
            
24

    
 Other Special Clauses:

sale for any off-highway vehicle;
