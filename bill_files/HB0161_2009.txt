MOTOR VEHICLE INSURANCE FEE                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Larry B. Wiley                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the Motor Vehicles Code by amending provisions relating to a motor                           vehicle insurance fee.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    creates the Automated License Plate Recognition System Restricted Account                                .    provides that the Motor Vehicle Enforcement Division shall use funds in the                           Automated License Plate Recognition System Restricted Account to administer an                           automated license plate recognition system program                                .    imposes a  .   annual fee on every vehicle covered under a policy of insurance                           purchased to satisfy the owner s or operator s security requirement                                .    requires insurers issuing insurance policies covering motor vehicle risks to pay the                           fees to the State Tax Commission on or before March    of each year                                .    provides that the revenues from the fee shall be deposited into the Automated                           License Plate Recognition System Restricted Account  and                               .    grants the State Tax Commission rulemaking authority to make rules to implement                           and enforce collection of the fee.                           Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                   This bill takes effect on July  ,     . Section        a          is enacted to read                                    a     .  Automated License Plate Recognition System Restricted Account.                                    As used in this section,  automated license plate recognition system  means a                          computerized camera system mounted on a vehicle or permanently mounted over a highway                          that recognizes license plates to alert law enforcement of violations, including                                 a   Amber Alerts                                 b   stolen vehicles                                 c   stolen license plates                                 d   wanted persons                                 e   revoked license plates                                  f   expired license plates  and                                g   any other criminal violation that could be identified by the automated license plate                          recognition system.                                    a   There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as the                           Automated License Plate Recognition System Restricted Account.                                 b   The account consists of monies generated from the following revenue sources                                 i   monies deposited into the fund in accordance with Section             .       and                                ii   appropriations made to the account by the Legislature.                                    a   The account shall earn interest.                                b   All interest earned on account monies shall be deposited into the account.                                    The Motor Vehicle Enforcement Division shall use monies deposited into the                          account to                                 a   purchase additional automated license plate recognition systems                                 b   support and update existing automated license plate recognitions systems and                             equipment  and                                c   fund automated license plate recognition system program management and                          personnel costs.                               Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                        . .  Fee on certain insurers to pay for automated license plate recognition                           system program.                                    Each insurer providing coverage for motor vehicle liability, uninsured motorist, and                          personal injury protection shall pay to the State Tax Commission on or before March    of                          each year, an annual fee of  .   on each vehicle covered under a policy covering motor vehicle                          risks in this state that satisfies the requirement for owners or operators security under Section                                  a        .                                    All monies received by the state under this section shall be deposited into the                          Automated License Plate Recognition System Restricted Account created by Section                                 a          for the purpose of providing funds to pay for any costs and expenses incurred by                          the Motor Vehicle Enforcement Division to administer an automated license plate recognition                          system program in accordance with Section         a         .                                    In accordance with Title   G, Chapter  , Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the                          commission may make rules to implement and enforce this section.
