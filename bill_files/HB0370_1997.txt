RECORDING FEDERAL TAX LIENS                 GENERAL SESSION          STATE OF UTAH           Sponsor  Richard M. Siddoway          AN ACT RELATING TO LIENS, FEDERAL TAX LIENS  AMENDING PROVISIONS ON         RECORDING FEDERAL TAX LIENS  PROVIDING FOR GRANTOR AND GRANTEE         INDEX  REQUIRING RECORDING OF THE CERTIFICATE OF DISCHARGE  AND         MAKING TECHNICAL CHANGES.         This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows           AMENDS                      , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                          , as last amended by Chapter    , Laws of Utah                          , as last amended by Chapter   , Laws of Utah               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah               Section  .  Section         is amended to read                      .  Notice by recording with county recorder.              Notices of liens for taxes payable to the United States of America, other federal liens          created under acts of Congress or regulations adopted pursuant to those acts, and certificates          discharging those liens, shall be  filed  recorded in the office of the county recorder of the county          within which any property subject to the lien is situated.               Section  .  Section         is amended to read                      .  Recorder s duties.              When a notice of the lien is  filed  recorded, the county recorder shall forthwith enter the          notice of lien in an alphabetical federal lien index, or by grantor and grantee index, showing , on          one line,  the name and residence of the taxpayer named in the notice, the collector s serial number          of the notice, the date and hour of  filing  recording, and the amount of the lien with interest,          penalties, and costs.   The county recorder shall  file and keep all original notices so filed in          numerical order in a file designated  record the  Federal Lien Notices    , mail the original         document to the person recording the notice, and, if any notice so filed contains a legal description,          it shall be indexed  index the lien against the real property described in the file.              Section  .  Section         is amended to read                     .  Recording of certificate of discharge.             When a certificate of discharge of any federal lien issued by the proper officer is  filed          recorded in the office of the county recorder where the notice of lien is  filed  recorded, the         recorder shall enter the certificate of discharge with date of  filing  recording in the federal lien         index  on the line where notice of the lien so discharged is entered and shall permanently attach          the certificate of discharge to the notice of lien.  or grantor and grantee index and return the          original document to the person recording.  If the lien, when  filed,  recorded, contained a legal          description of real property, the recorder  may  shall require that the certificate of discharge          contain the legal description.  The recorder shall index the certificate of discharge to that property.       Legislative Review Note      as of                 AM    A limited legal review of this bill raises no obvious constitutional or statutory concerns.
