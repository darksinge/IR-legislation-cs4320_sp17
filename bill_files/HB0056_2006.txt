 General Description:
            
10

    
    This bill requires the registration of a sex offender who is committed to the Division of
            
11

    
Juvenile Justice Services for secure confinement and who remains in the division's
            
12

    
custody 30 days prior to the offender's 21st birthday.
            
13

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
14

    
    This bill:
            
15

    
    .    modifies the responsibilities of the Division of Juvenile Justice Services to require
            
16

    
that the division register a sex offender with the Department of Corrections prior to
            
17

    
the offender's release from custody;

    .    amends sex offender registration law to include those who have been adjudicated
            
19

    
delinquent for one of the specified offenses and remain in the custody of the
            
20

    
division 30 days prior to their 21st birthday;

    .    requires lifetime registration if an offender who is convicted as an adult has been
            
22

    
previously convicted or required to register as a sex offender; and
            
23

    
    .    requires the Division of Juvenile Justice Services to provide the following
            
24

    
information when available:
            
25

    
        .    crimes for which the offender was adjudicated delinquent; and

        .    a description of the sex offender's primary and secondary targets.
