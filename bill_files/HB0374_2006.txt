HIGH SCHOOL DIPLOMA AMENDMENTS                                 GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                             Chief Sponsor   Kory M. Holdaway                                Senate Sponsor                                                                                 LONG TITLE                          General Description                               This bill modifies the State System of Public Education Code s requirements for                           receiving a high school diploma.                           Highlighted Provisions                                This bill                                .    requires a student to pass the basic skills competency test and satisfy other                           graduation requirements to receive a high school diploma that states the student                           passed all components of the basic skills competency test                                 .    provides that, in accordance with State Board of Education rules, a student who fails                           to pass all components of the basic skills competency test may receive                                         a diploma that states the components of the basic skills competency test passed                           or that the student did not pass all components of the basic skills competency                           test  or                                        a certificate of completion  and                               .    directs the State Board of Education to make rules providing for                                          reciprocity in awarding diplomas for students who pass a high school exit exam                           in another state                                         adult education students to take the basic skills competency test  and                                        adult education students who pass the GED to receive a diploma that states the                           student passed a basic skills test.                              Monies Appropriated in this Bill                                None                           Other Special Clauses                                None                           Utah Code Sections Affected                            AMENDS                                  A      , as last amended by Chapter  , Laws of Utah     , Fifth Special Session                                                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section      A           is amended to read                                   A      .  Standards and assessment processes to measure student performance                              Basic skills competency test.                                    The Legislature recognizes the need for the State Board of Education to develop                           and implement standards and assessment processes to ensure that student progress is measured                           and that school boards and school personnel are accountable.                                    a   In addition to its responsibilities under Sections       A           through       A          ,                           the State Board of Education, through the state superintendent of public instruction, shall                           design a basic skills competency test to be administered in the tenth grade.                                 b   A student must pass the basic skills competency test, in addition to the established                          requirements of the state and local board of education of the district in which the student                          attends school, in order to receive a basic high school diploma of graduation.                                  c    b   The state board shall include in the test, at a minimum, components on                           English language arts and reading and mathematics.                                 d   A student who fails to pass all components of the test may not receive a basic high                          school diploma but may receive a certificate of completion or alternative completion diploma                          under rules made by the State Board of Education in accordance with Title   , Chapter   a,                          Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act.                                     Except as provided in rules made in accordance with Subsection     c , a student                          shall pass the basic skills competency test and satisfy graduation requirements established by                          the State Board of Education and local board of education or charter school to receive a high                          school diploma that states the student passed all components or subtests of the basic skills                             competency test.                                    In accordance with rules made by the State Board of Education, a student who fails                          to pass all components or subtests of the basic skills competency test may receive                                 a   a high school diploma that states                                 i   the components or subtests passed by the student  or                                ii   the student did not pass all subtests or components of the basic skills competency                          test  or                                b   a certificate of completion.                                 e        The state board shall make rules                                  i    a   to allow students who initially fail the test to retake all or part of the test   and                                  ii    b  that take into account and are consistent with federal law relating to students                           with disabilities in the administration of the test .                                  c   that provide for reciprocity in awarding diplomas for a student who passed a high                          school exit exam in another state                                 d   that provide for adult education students to participate in administrations of the                          basic skills test  and                                e   that provide for an adult education student who exited the public education system                          and passed the GED test to receive a high school diploma that states the student passed a basic                          skills test.                                          The state board shall implement the tenth grade basic skills competency test,                           no later than the beginning of the         school year.                                          The requirements of this section are to be complementary to the other                           achievement testing provisions of this part.
