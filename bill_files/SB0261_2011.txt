 LONG TITLE
            
8

    
 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill modifies provisions relating to trust deed foreclosures.
            
10

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
11

    
    This bill:
            
12

    
    .    imposes civil liability on a person who violates specified trustee provisions;
            
13

    
    .    modifies a provision relating to successor trustees;
            
14

    
    .    imposes a duty on a trustee who exercises a power of sale; and
            
15

    
    .    requires a beneficiary or the beneficiary's agent to provide notice to a trustor on a
            
16

    
residential property if the beneficiary or agent does not intend to instruct a trustee to
            
17

    
defer a notice of sale despite a temporary reduced payment arrangement and
            
18

    
imposes civil liability for a failure to do so.
