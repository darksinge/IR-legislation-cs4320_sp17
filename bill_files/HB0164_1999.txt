ANNUAL LEAVE ACCRUAL FOR STATE                           EMPLOYEES                                  GENERAL SESSION                          STATE OF UTAH                           Sponsor   James R. Gowans                           AN ACT RELATING TO STATE OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES  ALLOWING STATE                          EMPLOYEES TO CONVERT EXCESS ACCUMULATED ANNUAL LEAVE INTO                          DEFERRED COMPENSATION PLAN CONTRIBUTIONS  AND GRANTING RULEMAKING                          AUTHORITY TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT TO                           IMPLEMENT THE PROGRAM.                           This act affects sections of Utah Code Annotated      as follows                            ENACTS                                        . , Utah Code Annotated                               Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah                                Section  .    Section            .      is enacted to read                                        . .  Accumulated annual leave    Conversion to deferred compensation plan.                                    The division shall make rules in accordance with Title   , Chapter   a, Utah                          Administrative Rulemaking Act, governing procedures whereby, after an employee has                          accumulated     hours of unused annual leave, the employee may, in each calendar year, convert                          up to    hours of annual leave into a deferred compensation plan qualified under Section     k                           or Section     of the Internal Revenue Code which is selected by the employee and sponsored by                          the Utah State Retirement Board.                                    Any hours converted under Subsection    , shall be converted at the hourly pay rate                          of the employee at the time of conversion.                                    An employee may not convert any annual leave hours under Subsection     which                          would                                 a   exceed the monetary amount set by federal law which may be contributed to a deferred                             compensation plan  or                                b   cause the employee s balance of accumulated annual leave to drop below     hours.
