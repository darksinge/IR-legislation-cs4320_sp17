    This bill modifies hazardous waste fees and repeals Title 19, Chapter 9, Hazardous

    .    defines "demilitarization waste";

    .    repeals Title 19, Chapter 9, Hazardous Waste Facilities Management Act; and

    .    makes technical changes.
