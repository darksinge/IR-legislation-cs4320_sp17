    This bill  modifies the Pawnshop and Secondhand Merchandise Transaction

    This bill:

    .    provides that a pawn or secondhand business is required to return property to an

original victim only if the original victim files a police report and files a statement

under the Rules of Criminal Procedure regarding hearsay statements used in a
