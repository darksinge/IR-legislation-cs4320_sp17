 General Description:
            
9

    
    This bill amends the licensing of non-Medicaid nursing care facility beds for a facility
            
10

    
with 100 or more beds and provides continuity of care provisions for patients who have
            
11

    
exhausted Medicare benefits.
            
12

    
 Highlighted Provisions:
            
13

    
    This bill:
            
14

    
    .    authorizes the department to license non-Medicaid nursing care facility beds for a
            
15

    
facility that will have at least 100 beds;
            
16

    
    .    if a facility has 100 or more beds, authorizes the department to convert a limited
            
17

    
number of non-Medicaid certified beds in the nursing care facility to Medicaid
            
18

    
certified beds for continuity of care for patients who have exhausted personal
            
19

    
resources and Medicare reimbursements; and
            
20

    
    .    extends the moratorium on licensing of non-Medicaid certified beds until July 1,
            
21

    
2018.
